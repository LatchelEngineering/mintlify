## **Document Highlights:**

- People Operations Department manages the hiring process and works closely with the Finance Department on approvals of staffing requests.
- Once approved, the hiring manager sends the People Operations Manager the full job description. With the specifics, People Operations will make the job posts and open the role in our ATS.
- People Operations will work with the hiring manager to determine the preferred hiring process flow and stakeholders.
- Every external hire will have a minimum of three interviews. All interviewers will complete a [<u>feedback form</u>](https://forms.gle/j1h1gp4z752HEJko6 "https://forms.gle/j1h1gp4z752HEJko6") and participate in a debriefing before an offer is made.
- People Operations will be responsible for sending all offer letters and onboarding for all new employees/contractors.

 

## **Policy**

The People Operations department is responsible for managing the approval of staffing needs and the hiring processes. The release of all employees/contractors must be coordinated through the People Operations Department directly with the agency when applicable.

The People Operations department will work closely with the Finance department to secure financial approval for the department budget for additional staffing requests. If the requestor does not have room in their budget, the Finance and People operations will advise the requester to build a business case to present to the COO to request the funds.

## **Procedures**

The department supervisor/manager will contact People Operations and Finance via Slack requesting a new hire. The request will be reviewed based on the following criteria: available funds in the department budget and reason for need. (backfill or new job tasks).

If approved, the department supervisor/manager will provide the People Operations Department with the date the employee/contractor is needed to start, job description, job responsibilities, requirements, and shift, if applicable. If it is a new position, People Operations with work with the hiring manager to determine the pay band for the position.

The People Operations Manager will take the specifics of the position and make the job posting, and begin to track candidates in our applicant tracking system (ATS). People Operations will schedule a meeting with the hiring manager to discuss the hiring process. The process is at the discretion of the hiring manager, and People Operations provides support and assist where preferred/needed. All external hires will require a minimum of three interviews with appropriate stakeholders. and each interviewer will fill out this feedback[<u> form</u>](https://forms.gle/m2fmjQQrFNwZB7cV6 "https://forms.gle/m2fmjQQrFNwZB7cV6"). After each candidate's series of interviews, an appointed person will lead a debriefing meeting. All interviewers must attend this meeting and submit feedback before the meeting.

People Operations will be responsible for sending all offer letters and onboarding for all new employees/contractors.

If any performance concerns with the employee arise, the department supervisor/manager must notify the People Operations department so performance plans can be put into place or schedule terminations.
