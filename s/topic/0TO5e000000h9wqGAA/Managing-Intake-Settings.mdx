### **Creating a New Rule Set**

Access the **Intake Settings** via your **Account Settings** page. From there, click **Manage Rule Sets**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 27 2026 09 48 AM](/images/Latchel-05-27-2026_09_48_AM.png)
</Frame>

Next, click **Create New**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 27 2026 01 17 PM](/images/Latchel-05-27-2026_01_17_PM.png)
</Frame>

**Enter the name** of your new rule set. Then, click **OK**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 27 2026 01 18 PM](/images/Latchel-05-27-2026_01_18_PM.png)
</Frame>

After this step, the system will take you to the new rule set page where you can create your first rule!

### **Creating New Rules**

Click **Create First Rule**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 27 2026 01 21 PM](/images/Latchel-05-27-2026_01_21_PM.png)
</Frame>

Then, edit the rule by writing a plain-language rules that describe how the issue must be classified. 

<Tip>
  **For example:**

  _AC issues are only emergencies if outside temperature is above 90°F"_ or _"If the AC brand is LG, classify as emergency regardless of temperature."_
</Tip>

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 27 2026 01 23 PM](/images/Latchel-05-27-2026_01_23_PM.png)
</Frame>

As you create or update rules in your Intake Settings, the system will automatically review your wording, flag anything that could be misinterpreted, and suggest improvements—so your rules work the way you intend them to.

You can take the suggestions provided by choosing **I Want to Make Changes**, or choose **Next** to proceed.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 27 2026 01 46 PM](/images/Latchel-05-27-2026_01_46_PM.png)
</Frame>

Next, select the **Outcome**. This sets the severity of the rule you determined.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 27 2026 01 50 PM](/images/Latchel-05-27-2026_01_50_PM.png)
</Frame>

Finally, click **Save rule** to complete.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 27 2026 01 52 PM](/images/Latchel-05-27-2026_01_52_PM.png)
</Frame>

### **Fallback Outcome**

Instead of evaluating rules in a fixed order, our system now considers all available rules at once and automatically matches your request to the most relevant and specific one — ensuring faster, more accurate routing every time.

**If no rule applies**, Latchel can create a task for your approval, though this can be customized. To set a fallback outcome when no rules match, scroll down to the **“If no rules match”** section and select the **desired outcome by choosing from these two options:**

1. **Set the work order to a specific severity.**

<Frame>
  ![Image 05 27 2026 02 04 PM](/images/image-05-27-2026_02_04_PM.png)
</Frame>

2. **Notify the PM via email or call.**

<Frame>
  ![Image 05 27 2026 02 05 PM](/images/image-05-27-2026_02_05_PM.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Your rules can be **updated anytime**, giving you full flexibility.
</Tip>

### **How to Edit your Current Rules**

**Locate the rule** **set** you want to edit. Then, click on **the pencil icon (** <Icon icon="pencil" /> **)** across the rule you want to edit to open the rule details.

<Frame>
  ![Image 05 28 2026 07 57 AM](/images/image-05-28-2026_07_57_AM.png)
</Frame>

In the Edit Rule box, you can **update or change the Rule Text**. Click **Next** to proceed the revisions stage, then **change the Outcome** if needed. If all is set, click **Save rule** to complete.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 28 2026 08 00 AM](/images/Latchel-05-28-2026_08_00_AM.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  You can go through the same steps to **Delete** a rule if deemed unuseful by clicking on the trash icon ( <Icon icon="trash-can" /> ).
</Tip>
