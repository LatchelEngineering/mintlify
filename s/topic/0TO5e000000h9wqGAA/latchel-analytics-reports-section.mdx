---
title: "Reports Section"
---

This section allows property managers to quickly export key data such as resident lists, invoices, and property records. Having this information readily available makes it easier to review portfolio activity, share reports with stakeholders, and maintain accurate records for operational or financial tracking.

### **Types of Reports**

Click **Reports**, then choose from **Work Orders, Residents, Properties, and Invoices.**

### **Work Orders**

This provides etailed list of all work orders, including key information such as portfolio, owner, job ID, work order number, property address, and associated residents for easy tracking and reference.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel Analytics › Work Orders 03 17 2026 08 06 PM](/images/Latchel-Analytics-›-Work-Orders-03-17-2026_08_06_PM.png)
</Frame>

### **Resident Section**

This provides detailed information about active residents, including their contact information, associated properties, and property owner details.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel Analytics › Residents 03 17 2026 08 15 PM(1)](/images/Latchel-Analytics-›-Residents-03-17-2026_08_15_PM\(1\).png)
</Frame>

### **Properties Section**

This provides comprehensive property information including address, owner details, active residents, and integration data. However, this only includes active properties.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel Analytics › Properties 03 17 2026 08 17 PM](/images/Latchel-Analytics-›-Properties-03-17-2026_08_17_PM.png)
</Frame>

### **Invoices Section**

This provides detailed invoice information including line items, associated work orders, properties, and vendor details. It is useful for financial reporting and invoice tracking.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel Analytics › Invoices 03 17 2026 08 18 PM](/images/Latchel-Analytics-›-Invoices-03-17-2026_08_18_PM.png)
