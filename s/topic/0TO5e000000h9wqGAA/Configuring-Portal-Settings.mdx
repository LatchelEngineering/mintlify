title: "Configuring Portal Settings"
description: "Portal Settings are Latchel account settings where you can customize your company logo and color, and most importantly, this is where you can add your maintenance URL and Resident Review URL."
---

### **Where to Access Portal Settings**

Click on the **Account Icon** on the top right corner of your Dashboard and select **Account Settings**.

<Frame>
  ![Latchel 05 28 2026 09 12 AM](/images/Latchel-05-28-2026_09_12_AM.png)
</Frame>

Once you're on the Account Settings page, look for the **Portal Settings** dropdown menu.

<Frame>
  ![Rta Image 663 05 29 2026 06 18 AM](/images/rtaImage-663-05-29-2026_06_18_AM.png)
</Frame>

Under the Portal Settings section, you will find other options to configure your settings.

### **Residents Work Order Submission URL**

Residents can use this link to submit online maintenance requests.

<Frame>
  ![Rta Image 664 05 29 2026 06 20 AM](/images/rtaImage-664-05-29-2026_06_20_AM.png)
</Frame>

### **Resident Benefits Access URL**

Residents can use this link to access their Resident Benefits.

<Frame>
  ![Rta Image 665 05 29 2026 06 20 AM](/images/rtaImage-665-05-29-2026_06_20_AM.png)
</Frame>

### **Website URL**

You may also add your company's website URL.

<Frame>
  ![Rta Image 666 05 29 2026 06 21 AM](/images/rtaImage-666-05-29-2026_06_21_AM.png)
</Frame>

### **Resident Review URL**

Add your social media URL to your Latchel account page. If your resident leaves a 4 or 5-star review, Latchel sends a prompt asking them to share their positive experience publicly on your social media page. This is where you can customize it.
