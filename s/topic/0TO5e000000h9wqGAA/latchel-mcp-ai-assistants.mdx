title: "Latchel MCP for AI Assistants"
---

**Latchel MCP (Model Context Protocol)** connects Latchel to AI tools like Claude, giving you instant access to help guides, workflows, and answers without leaving your workflow. Instead of searching manually, simply ask a question and get accurate, Latchel-specific guidance in seconds.

### **What is Latchel MCP?**

The **Latchel MCP connector** lets you talk to your Latchel account directly from AI assistants like Claude and ChatGPT. Ask in plain English and the assistant can pull and act on your real work-order data — no copy-pasting, no switching tabs.

Everything is scoped to your PM company and gated behind your own Latchel login, so the assistant only ever sees what you're allowed to see.

### **What You Can Do**

Once connected, you can interact with your Latchel account conversationally. Here's what's supported:

- **Work orders** — list them, get full details (including open tasks), review activity history, and create new ones.
- **Tasks** — list, view details, create, resolve, and reschedule (defer) tasks on a work order.
- **Lookups** — search your properties, see the residents on a property, and browse your company's users and user groups.

<Tip>
  **A typical request just works conversationally**, e.g. _"Open a work order for a leaking faucet at 123 Main St for the current tenant" or "What's still open on work order #4821?"_
</Tip>

### **Before You Begin**

You'll need the following to connect:

<Icon icon="circle-1" iconType="duotone" color="#ff9b8a" /> An **active Latchel account** with your PM company credentials

<Icon icon="circle-2" iconType="duotone" color="#ff9b8a" /> A **compatible AI assistant plan** (see requirements below per platform).

<Icon icon="circle-3" iconType="duotone" color="#ff9b8a" /> The **Latchel MCP connection** URL: `https://app.latchel.com/mcp/user-tools`

<Note>
  You'll be prompted to sign in with your Latchel credentials the first time you connect via standard OAuth — no API keys to manage.
</Note>

### **How to Install in Claude (web or desktop)**

<Danger>
  If you would like to use an email address other than your Latchel admin account, please log out of the Latchel Console before proceeding with these steps.
</Danger>

Open **Settings** by clicking your **Account icon**.

</Frame>

From the settings window go to **Connectors**, then click **Customize**.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 06 01 2026 12 09 PM](/images/Desktop-screenshot-06-01-2026_12_09_PM.png)
</Frame>

Click the “**\+**” button next to **Connectors**. Then select, **Add custom connector**.

<Frame>
  ![Image 06 01 2026 12 18 PM](/images/image-06-01-2026_12_18_PM.png)
</Frame>

Place a **name** for your custom connector, then paste the URL `https://app.latchel.com/mcp/user-tools` in the box for **Remote MCP server URL**. Then click **Save**.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 06 01 2026 12 24 PM](/images/Desktop-screenshot-06-01-2026_12_24_PM.png)
</Frame>

Once saved, click **Connect**.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 06 01 2026 12 29 PM](/images/Desktop-screenshot-06-01-2026_12_29_PM.png)
</Frame>

In the new browser on your screen, sign in to your Latchel admin account when prompted to authorize access.

<Frame>
  ![Sign In Latchel 06 15 2026 02 24 PM](/images/Sign-In-Latchel-06-15-2026_02_24_PM.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>

</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![Image 06 15 2026 02 34 PM](/images/image-06-15-2026_02_34_PM.png)

### **How to Install in Chatgpt**

<Danger>
  If you would like to use an email address other than your Latchel admin account, please log out of the Latchel Console before proceeding with these steps.
</Danger>

Open **Settings** by clicking your **Account icon**.

<Frame>
  ![Image 06 01 2026 12 51 PM](/images/image-06-01-2026_12_51_PM.png)
</Frame>

Go to **Apps**, then click **Create App** to create a custom MCP application.

<Note>
  Depending on the Chatgpt plan, the exact menu names may vary.
</Note>

<Frame>
  ![Chat GPT 06 01 2026 12 58 PM](/images/ChatGPT-06-01-2026_12_58_PM.png)
</Frame>

Add your new app **name**, click the check box for Custom MCP Server, then **paste** the URL `https://app.latchel.com/mcp/user-tools` in the **Connection** box. Click **Save** when done.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 06 01 2026 01 05 PM](/images/Desktop-screenshot-06-01-2026_01_05_PM.png)
</Frame>

**Sign in** to your Latchel account when prompted.

<Frame>
  ![Desktop Screenshot 06 01 2026 01 11 PM](/images/Desktop-screenshot-06-01-2026_01_11_PM.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Enable the connector in your conversation (and Composer/Deep Research where applicable).
</Tip>

Complete the authorization process by logging in to your Latchel admin account, then **authorizing Chatgpt** to use the MCP server.

<Frame>

</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![Authorize Application Latchel 06 15 2026 02 40 PM](/images/Authorize-Application-Latchel-06-15-2026_02_40_PM.png)
</Frame>
