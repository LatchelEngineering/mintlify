---
title: "How to Save Latchel to Your Home Screen (iPhone & Android)"
---

Latchel works as a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means you can add it to your phone's home screen for quick, app-like access — no App Store or Play Store download required.

<Note>
  This adds a shortcut to the Latchel web app — it is not a download from the App Store or Google Play. There is currently no native Latchel app in either store.
</Note>

### **iPhone / iPad (Safari)**

Open Safari and go to [app.latchel.com](http://app.latchel.com). Log in if prompted.

<Frame>
  ![IMG 4588 06 26 2026 03 13 PM](/images/IMG_4588-06-26-2026_03_13_PM.png)
</Frame>

Tap the **Share icon** (the square with an arrow pointing up) in the bottom toolbar.

<Frame>
  ![IMG 4589 06 26 2026 03 15 PM](/images/IMG_4589-06-26-2026_03_15_PM.png)
</Frame>

Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.

<Frame>
  ![IMG 4590 06 26 2026 03 15 PM](/images/IMG_4590-06-26-2026_03_15_PM.png)
</Frame>

Edit the name if you'd like, then tap **Add** in the top-right corner.

<Frame>
  ![IMG 4591 06 26 2026 03 15 PM](/images/IMG_4591-06-26-2026_03_15_PM.png)
</Frame>

The Latchel icon will now appear on your home screen — tap it any time for quick access.

### **Android (Chrome)**

Open Chrome and go to [app.latchel.com](http://app.latchel.com). Log in if prompted.

<Frame>
  ![Chrome App 1 1](/images/chrome-app-1-1.png)
</Frame>

Tap the **three-dot menu** in the top-right corner.

<Frame>
  ![Chrome App 2](/images/chrome-app-2.png)
</Frame>

Tap **Add to Home screen** (you may see this as **Install app** depending on your Android/Chrome version).

<Frame>
  ![Chrome App 3](/images/chrome-app-3.png)
</Frame>

Confirm by tapping **Add** or **Install**.

<Frame>
  ![Chrome App 4](/images/chrome-app-4.png)
</Frame>

The Latchel icon will now appear on your home screen — tap it any time for quick access.

### **Why Add Latchel to Your Home Screen?**

- **Quicker access** — no need to type the URL or dig through browser bookmarks
- **Full-screen, app-like experience**
- **Notifications and updates** work the same as logging in through your browser
