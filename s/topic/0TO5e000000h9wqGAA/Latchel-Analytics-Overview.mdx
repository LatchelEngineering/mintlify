### **The Expanded Analytics**

Latchel Analytics has recently undergone a major update, including new functionality, UI changes, powered through Google Looker Studio. These changes significantly improve how property managers access, filter, and understand their data across their Latchel account.

The Analytics dashboard is organized into different sections to help you monitor and evaluate your maintenance performance.
