## Content

**What happened prior to this step?**

Contacts have been created in Salesforce and are categorized in Tiers. See the Glossary Page to learn about those definitions.

### SDR & Account Stage Assignment

Depending on the type of the lead, it will or not be assigned to an SDR via a Round Robin logic and SDRs will be notified in a Slack Channel.

The inbound leads that are not auto-assigned to SDRs are expected to be continued to be nurtured by Marketing, so when they become a T1 or T2, they will be assigned.

| **Lead Type** | **Auto Assigned to SDR?** | **Salesforce Assigned Account Status** | **Notifications of new leads** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tier 1 | YES | **Lifecycle Stage:** Lead Assigned / **Lifecycle Substage:** Lead Assigned | [#tier-1-notifications](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/tier-1-notifications) |
| Tier 2 | YES | **Lifecycle Stage:** Lead Assigned / **Lifecycle Substage:** Lead Assigned | [#tier-2-notifications](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/tier-2-notifications) |
| Tier 3 | NO | **Lifecycle Stage:** Prospecting / **Lifecycle Substage:** Inbound Unassigned | #other-tier-notifications |
| Tier 4 | NO | **Lifecycle Stage:** Prospecting / **Lifecycle Substage:** Inbound Unassigned | #other-tier-notifications |
