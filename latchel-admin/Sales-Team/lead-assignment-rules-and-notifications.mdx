## SDR & Account Stage Assignment

**What happened prior to this step?**

Contacts have been created in Salesforce and are categorized in Tiers. See the [Glossary Page](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2902917222) to learn about those definitions.

Depending on the type of the lead, it will or not be assigned to an SDR via a Round Robin logic and SDRs will be notified in a Slack Channel.

The inbound leads that are not auto-assigned to SDRs are expected to be continued to be nurtured by Marketing, so when they become a T1 or T2, they will be assigned.
