## Purpose

<Note>
  This guide will give you a foundation on what and how we want to communicate with prospects on social media platforms.
</Note>

<Note>
  **WHEN we do we reach out to someone on LinkedIn?**

  - if we can't get in contact with them via phone, email, or text
  - if we talk to them and they book a discovery call
</Note>

## LinkedIn

### Finding the Correct Person

We talk to a lot of "salt of the earth" type people. They may or may not have a LinkedIn Profile. You're more likely to find your prospect on LinkedIn if they are Mid-Market, Enterprise, or SMB (but more technologically aware).

With super common names you will need to also add part of the company name in your search

You will find more success in using only part of the company name in case they have a different name on their profile. A GREAT option is skipping the company name completely and trying "property management" .

### Request to Connect

You can add a quick note along the lines of:

> _Hey NAME, you requested a demo of Latchel so I wanted to connect on here. I look forward to chatting!_
>
> _Hey NAME, Sent you an email or two and wanted to put a face with a name._

You don't need to add a note with your invite if you don't want to.

### Additional Reach Out

Depending on how accessible the contact is via phone & email - you may or may not need to private message them.

If they haven't responded to call, email, or text please try LinkedIn. Something along the lines of:

> _"Hey NAME, you reached out to request a demo of Latchel but I haven't been able to catch you to schedule some time. Are you still interested?"_

### Post Interactions

Once you're connected with a contact, or follow them you will see their posts in your feed - please DO NOT make everything you post or comment be about Latchel.

It may take some time, but your industry knowledge is where you will really be able to shine here. Learn about what is going on - make thoughtful comments on the posts, whether it be about a contact moving to a new position, or them sharing about how their company is poised for an acquisition.

### Message Ideas

- _What made you go with X software instead of Y? I talk with a lot of managers and I'm always curious_
- _Just wanna know your thoughts - Where do you think the property management industry is going re: boutique vs VC backed players?_
- _(If owner/operator) "How long did it take to work up the courage to buy that first property?"_
- _'Spoke with (employee name), just wanted to let you know I talk to a lot of people answering phones and \_\_\_\_ is phenomenal"_
