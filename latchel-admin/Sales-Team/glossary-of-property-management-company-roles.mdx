**Accounting** - does invoicing activities and everything in Appfolio including realizing revenue for work that is completed

**Business Development Manager** - closes the business and goes to meetups to be part of the real estate community locally.

**Client Success Manager (CSM)** - responsible for client success. Manages the Pod.

**Eviction Specialist** - provides eviction updates to the clients and the client success manager.

**Field Inspector** - responsible to be the eyes of the organization. Does move ins, move outs, property reviews, fire marshal inspections, section 8 inspections, etc.

**Integrator** - This role oversees the Sales/Marketing Manager, Operations Lead, Finance Manager, and the Special Projects Manager. Responsible to review budgets, and help wherever the business needs him/her.

**KPI and Audit Specialist** - pulls all the data weekly for KPIs. Performs daily/weekly/monthly audits on our technology to make sure we are not paying for things we are not using and that other issues that we identify do not continue to be an issue going forward.

**Leasing Specialist** - responsible for doing everything from marketing descriptions, CMA generation (under the supervision of the licensed person), communications with pre-applicants who want to see a property, applicants, etc. Requests missing information and fills out application scoring sheet.

**Maintenance Coordinator** - responsible for finding new vendors, scoring vendors, praising and rebuking vendors, dispatching vendors, communicating with residents and providing information to CSM so owner's can stay informed.

**Operations Lead** - Responsible for escalations from each pod and to oversee and manage each CSM for success. Responsible for KPI generation and that pod is operating efficiently.

**Office Staff** - responsible to receive and scan mail, go to the bank for deposits, and mail letters for residents that are not technological savvy.

**Resident Experience Specialist** - Responsible for all interactions with residents. Responsible for Section 8, lease renewals, and provide great service to residents.

**Sales Assistant** - Responsible for cold calling, agent referral programs and warming up leads for the BDM

**Marketing Assistant** - Responsible for facebook/linkedin/instagram/tiktok posting, editing videos, developing blogs, supporting lead magnets, building CRM nurture sequences, etc

**Sales/Marking Manager** - responsible to oversee the sales/marketing team. Responsible for KPI generation and that team is operating efficiently.

**Special Projects Manager** - responsible for Objectives and Key Results, project management, and working on special projects to move the company forward. This position is basically an integrator in training and reports directly to the integrator.

**Onboarding Manager** - manager who transitions a new client from the BDM to the CSM. Onboarding manager would be responsible to get everything going until the property is operating well and the CSM can take control. This is the CSM when not a lot of new clients are signed each month.

**Finance Manager** - responsible to oversee the finance and special projects team. Responsible for KPI generation and that the team is operating efficiently. Also responsible for the moving and paying of money.

**PM persona Assistant** - person that assists PM on projects that may be one off or not related to property management (flips, etc)

**Licensed Person** - responsible person to communicate with owner when it requires a licensed activity. Oversee the marketing, applicant approval, rent price negotiation with owner/resident, activities supported by the leasing specialist.
