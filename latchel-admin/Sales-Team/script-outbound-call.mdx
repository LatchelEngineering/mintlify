## When to Use this Script?

This script is to be used when we are contacting prospects, which are potential customers that have not reached out to us or engaged with us. This happens when we get a list of potential customers with their contact information and we call them to introduce ourselves and try to sell. Therefore, this is a Cold Calling script.

## Script

> _INTRO: "This is (your name) from Latchel. You're actually not expecting this call, it's the first time I've tried to reach you. Mind if I share why I'm calling and you can let me know if it's relevant or not?"_

…then…

> _Ok perfect, well I know you're busy, so I'm not looking to take any of your time now, but let me give you just a 30-second overview and if that interests you we'll set-up another time in the future to talk, if not – I'll let you go, sound fair?_

_(Common brush offs and how to handle them)_

**NOT INTERESTED**

> _I know you get sales calls all the time, I can cover what we do in less than 60 seconds, and if it interests you, I can call you back, if not – at least I only took a minute of your time, do you have just 60 seconds?_

_GET BACK TO SCRIPT_

**TOO BUSY**

> _I believe it, so let me give you a one-minute description of how we can help you and if it interests you, we can set up another time to talk in the future, if not – at least I only took a minute of your time, so really quickly..._

_GET BACK TO SCRIPT_

**WHERE ARE YOU CALLING FROM?**

> _So, we've actually been around since 2017 now. It's Latchel, and really quickly..._

_GET BACK TO SCRIPT_

**NO TIME**

> _I certainly want to be respectful of your time so I can call you back, but all I was looking for was just 60 seconds to explain what we do, and if it interests you, I can call you back, if not – at least I only took a minute of your time, do you have just 60 seconds?_

_GET BACK TO SCRIPT_

## Qualification

Once you were able to stablish a conversation with the prospect, you will ask questions to verify if the prospect meets our qualification criteria - [Qualification Meeting Questions](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2868248756)

If the prospect meets the qualification, you will set an appointment for the Discovery Meeting and set expectations for the meeting.

## Book Discovery Meeting
