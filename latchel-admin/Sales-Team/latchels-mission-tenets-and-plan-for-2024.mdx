## **2024 Goals & Plan**

In [<u>the Latchel 2024 Rearview document </u>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1aZewUdVAhSSh6lG6iR0oxOCkkSX4pODQ6qaHGcFaDBc/edit "https://docs.google.com/document/d/1aZewUdVAhSSh6lG6iR0oxOCkkSX4pODQ6qaHGcFaDBc/edit") you will find our 2024 plan wrote in a Press Release style that outlines the main accomplishments that we are expecting to achieve in 2024, so we can continue to work on our mission to **Empower Property Managers to deliver unbeatable customer experiences**!

**Our 2024 Objectives**:

- Increase our rate of new sales to \$750k CARR/quarter
- Make benefits a product that our customers would choose to use, even without maintenance
- Reduce variable COGS to \<\$0.75/unit while maintaining or improving PM NPS and maintaining or decreasing fixed COGS spend
- For a work order in any given status, enable PM users to complete the happy path action and most common exception action in only 2 clicks or less
