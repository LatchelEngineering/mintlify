### SDR Qualification Descriptions:

- **Unit Count** - we can talk to all unit counts, there is only a few caveats to keep in mind!
  - if the property manager has under 50 (fifty) units, they need to be willing to pay the onboarding fee as if they had 50 units. If they are not interested in paying the onboarding fee please refer them to our free maintenance tool.
  - This applies UNLESS the property manager is using Rent Manager as their property management software, or a part of RPM or a part of KeyRenter!
  - If the property manager has under 50 units but is highly engaged and with expansion plans, we will move forward with them in the process
- **Structure Type** - single family residences (SFR), or multi-family (MF)
- **What is their current maintenance process?** - do they have a call center for after hours, do they have someone on call, do they have a phone they pass around to whoever is on call? This will be different for each company, but you may start to notice re-occurring themes.
- **Asset Ownership** - Owner/Operator (O/O), 3rd Party Fee Manager, Mixed - Majority 3rd Party, Mixed - Majority O/O
- **Who am I speaking with?** - (Acceptable Titles for DM: President, CEO, COO, Director of Ops, VP of Ops., Broker/Owner, Broker/Owner President, Property Manager (sub 500 units); Acceptable Titles for Champion: Property Manager (above 500 units), Asst./Assoc. Property Manager, Maintenance Manager, other C-Suite
- **What percentage of your portfolio is Section 8 housing?** - must be 25% or less

### Disqualifying Criteria:

- Section 8/Government Assistance/LIHTC (Low Income Housing Tax Credit)/Section 42 portfolio (over 25%)
- HOA Only
- Commercial properties only
- Non-Independent Living - Elderly
- No long term rentals - only short term/vacation rentals
- less than 50 Units (depends on expansion or reduction plans, or willingness to pay for 50 unit threshold even though they are below)

For situations not included in this list, like student housing, lease by bed, mobile homes, please refer to this FAQ - [SDR FAQ](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2888597549)
