## Purpose

<Note>
  **Purpose of this document**

  The purpose of having a script and templates is to provide guidance and tips on how to communicate certain key selling points and collect key information from the lead.

  The goal is not to read the script, but to ensure the key items are included in the communication. For email templates, although you can copy and paste the template, you should make some small adjustments to add your personal touch!
</Note>

<Note>
  **Who should read this document?**

  SDRs
</Note>

## When to use these Templates?

This script is to be used when the SDR receives a notification that they have have a Tier 3 lead assigned. Tier 3 leads are the ones that we have enough information to contact them, but they may not know us and we don't know if they are qualified. For Tiers definitions, check the [Glossary Page](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2902917222).

SDRs should only be worked on when they have worked all of their Tier 1s and Tier 2s, as these are low engagement types of leads, that are still being worked on (enriched / increasing engagement level) by Marketing.
