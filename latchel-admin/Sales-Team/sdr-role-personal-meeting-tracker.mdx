<Warning>
  It's always a good idea to keep track of your meetings booked through out the month instead of relying on our reports catching everything.
</Warning>

Every month you will be sent your commission statement to review it is correct. How you want to track your meetings is completely up to you. If you don't know a system that works for you, we have a template you can use!

[https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/18MGUjs5mJQez6\_D4qqJqAgFKFYqcsqZQFoD8U9aCCkE/edit#gid=2108688722](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/18MGUjs5mJQez6_D4qqJqAgFKFYqcsqZQFoD8U9aCCkE/edit#gid=2108688722)

<Danger>
  Please do NOT edit this template - under file click "make a copy" and make your edits on the copy version.
</Danger>
