## Contract Types & When to Use them

- **Assisted Ledgering Platform Agreement**: When the customer wants to enroll in Mandatory benefits \+ a maintenance product using assisted ledgering process. They must pay the platform fee on all units day 1 and then units are added at new lease and renewal to their bill.
- **Latchel Standard Platform Agreement**: When the customer wants to enroll in Mandatory benefits \+ a maintenance product without the assisted ledgering process. Instead, they would like a ramp up to the full price to simplify billing. They must pay the platform fee on all units day 1 and then their bill will ramp up to full price.
- **Maintenance ONLY Standard Agreement**: When a customer only wishes to use one of our maintenance products and no benefits product. They would have to pay platform and cost of service day 1.
- **Optional Benefits \+ Maintenance Agreement**: When the customer wants to utilize one of our maintenance products \+ our optional benefits program. They will have to pay the platform AND maintenance cost for product day 1, but only pay for benefits as they are opted into by the resident.
- **Optional Benefits ONLY Agreement**: When the customer only wants to offer Latchel's optional benefit program and no maintenance product. They would have to pay the platform fee starting day
- **Hub Only Agreement**: When the customer wants to use Latchel's mandatory benefits product. This contract the platform fee is due day 1 and then assisted ledgering is used to add units at new lease and renewal. WE CURRENTLY DO NOT HAVE A STANDARD VERSION OF THIS CONTRACT.
- **Master Third Party Addendum**: When a customer wants to use our marketplace benefits, this contract addendum must be used. This can only be added on to an additional benefits service.
