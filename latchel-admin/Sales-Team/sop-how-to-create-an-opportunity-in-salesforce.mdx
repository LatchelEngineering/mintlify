## Step-by-Step Guide

**What happened prior to this step?**

The SDR run the Qualification Meeting with the Lead and defined the company meets our criteria to move forward - [Qualification Meeting Questions](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2868248756/Qualification+Meeting+Questions)

### Process Overview

1. Go to the account in Salesforce
2. Scroll down to find the Opportunity Section of the page on the right side
3. Click the drop down arrow and select new
4. Select Sales Pipeline
5. Enter Opportunity Name
6. Select Stage - Appointment Scheduled
7. SDR Owner - your name
8. Software - the software the lead said they are using
9. Discovery Meeting - the time the lead said they are available
10. Close date - 1 month out from the Discovery Meeting date
11. Price per unit - use the current standard
12. Contracted units - the number of units the lead mentioned they have
13. Save it.
14. Go back to the account and the opportunity section
15. Change the Opportunity Owner to the AE
16. Add Contact Roles - add the names of the people that will be in the meeting
