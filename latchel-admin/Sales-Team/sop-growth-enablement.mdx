## Sales

We are currently in the process of documenting and creating a space on confluence to house all sales related items (for both SDR & AE positions). We will be working together to determine what projects we will work on next, what takes priority, and what changes need to be made to create new standards moving forward.

**SDR**

We will have a weekly 30 min Content/Library Management meeting where assignments will be given to each creator for items that either need to be created in confluence or corrected/updated. The other half of this meeting will be updates from Marketing. This is to ensure we are up to date in our messaging with prospects.

Each item that is assigned needs to be owned IN COMPLETION by the assignee. If you need help, it is your job to schedule more time/get help before the next update meeting.

<Warning>
  This meeting is NOT for help, this meeting is for assignment/marketing updates only - additional meetings are available if a working meeting/additional help is needed.
</Warning>

**AE**

Similar to the SDR role, we are currently consolidating information to be put in to confluence. All the above for SDR's owning their projects applies here as well.
