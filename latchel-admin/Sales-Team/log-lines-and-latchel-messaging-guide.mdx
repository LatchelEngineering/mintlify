## Latchel Log Lines

### Overall Company

<Tip>
  Renters today expect better service, more perks, and faster maintenance than most property managers can afford to provide.

  Latchel is a software platform that enables property managers to be more responsive and offer better perks while also creating a new revenue stream so they don't have to choose between happy tenants and a healthy bottom line
</Tip>

### Benefits

<Tip>
  When it is unclear if a renter or owner is responsible for a repair related expense, the only way to create a wow moment is to pay the bill yourself.

  Latchel's resident benefits enable you to deliver wow moments without paying the bill yourself by providing reimbursements to residents for these disputable issues, all while creating a new revenue stream
</Tip>
