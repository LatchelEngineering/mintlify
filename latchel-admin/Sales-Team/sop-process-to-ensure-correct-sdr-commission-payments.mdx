## Process

### Process Overview

In summary, the AE needs to move the Opportunities through each step of the Opportunity Pipeline in Salesforce.

This needs to happen even if that specific deal "skipped" a step in the process. For example, if the Discovery is Completed and a demo is not required, the Opportunity in Salesforce still needs to be moved into Demo Completed, then moved to the next step.
