<Note>
  **Purpose of this document**

  To provide guidelines on how to connect the Latchel Gmail Account to Salesforce, so the team member can send emails from Salesforce, which is a pre-requisite to use Salesforce Cadences.
</Note>

<Note>
  **Who should read this document?**

  SDRs and anyone wanting to send emails from Salesforce
</Note>

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Remove Latchel Gmail account from your phone Contacts Sync

<Warning>
  **Contacts Syncing to Your Phone**

  As you sync Salesforce and your Gmail account, your Contacts Settings in your phone may be set-up to sync contacts from our latchel account into your phone.

  To avoid adding all of those contacts to your phone, you will have to go to your phone contacts app, select "Manage Contacts", "sync contacts" and remove your Latchel email address.

  If you didn't do this step before connecting your Salesforce Account and Gmail account, you will need to go to the Contacts App, go the the menu, select the Latchel email account, and delete all contacts from that account from your phone
</Warning>
