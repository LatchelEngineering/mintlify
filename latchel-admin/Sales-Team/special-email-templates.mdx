### Template 1: Referral Partner Meeting Reminder

**Template Reason / When to send it** – Remind referral partner of upcoming discovery call. Referral partner is someone that has referred a PM to Latchel.

**Subject Line:** Our meeting is today!

**Content:**

> _Hey (first name referral partner),_
>
> _Just wanted to send a quick reminder of our video meeting today at \[3:30 PM EST\]_
>
> _This will be a brief and casual conversation, no more than 15 to 20 minutes. (AE name) and I are really excited to explain how Latchel helps property managers with managing maintenance coordination._
>
> _We'll also touch on resident amenities (benefits) and how our property management clients generate additional monthly revenues per door using our platform._
>
> _Is there any particular information that you would like to hear from us?_
>
> _Video call link: [https://meet.google.com/cmd-tesb-aub](https://meet.google.com/cmd-tesb-aub)_
>
> _If you have any questions before the meeting, let me know, and if not we'll see you shortly!_
>
> _Thanks,_

### Template 2: Where do we stand?

**Template Reason / When to send it** – prospect has gone silent on us/not showed for previously scheduled meeting

**Subject Line:** Where do we stand?

**Content:**

> _Hey (first name),_
>
> _Hope everything is ok, haven't heard from you!_
>
> _Want to make sure we are on a timeline that works for you. Our onboarding team gets booked up fast but if we make moves we can still get everything in place for you by (Month)._
>
> _Conversely, we can take you off the list if the timing is off. Is implementing a maintenance solution still a priority on your end?_
