## Purpose

<Note>
  **Purpose of this document**

  The purpose of having a script and templates to provide guidance and tips on how to communicate certain key selling points and collect key information from the lead.

  The goal is not to read the script or blindly copy and paste the email template, but to ensure the key items are included in the communication. You can add your personal touch
</Note>

<Note>
  **Who should read this document?**

  SDRs
</Note>

## When to Use this Script?

After confirming that a lead has met our qualification criteria ([Qualification Meeting Questions](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2868248756)), you will book a Discovery Meeting with the Account Executive. Use the script below as a guideline to set expectations for the meeting and the email templates to confirm the meeting schedule and remind the lead prior the meeting.

## Script

> _I'm putting down a 30 min time slot with my colleague (Account Executive) for a video chat, most of these end up being a quick 10-15 min conversation and I'll leave some time to run over. The objective is for you to leave that call with a clear picture of how Latchel can help you be more responsive to maintenance, offer better perks, and drive an increase to bottom line revenue._
>
> _So in order to accomplish this we'll want to know how you're operating, where you're coming into friction, and your goals for this year._
>
> _If during the call you feel like we can add value to (PM Company name), then we can schedule a Demo so you can see the software in action. Who else do you think should be on this call?_
>
> _Is there anything we can make sure we bring to the table, or any specific questions we can answer for you? Ok that sounds great! See you there_

Confirm appointment time and send emails below…..
