## Overall Industry Information

The best way to learn more about the property management industry and Latchel's customers is to talk to property managers! Attend conferences, bring your A\+ sales game to the phones (listen to what they are saying) and keep up to date on widely read industry reports.

[Buildium State of the Industry Reports](https://www.buildium.com/resource/2024-property-management-industry-report/?reference=pmir-banner) (available here) is a must read.

The [Latchel Property Management Podcast](https://podcast.latchel.com/) is a must listen

Also you must sign-up for our internal Slack Channel that the team posts articles and news about the industry - #indus-tea

Other good resources to reference as you become more entrenched in the industry:

1. Property Brew Newsletter (sign up [here](https://latchel.com/blog/))
2. [Yardi's blog](https://www.yardibreeze.com/blog/)
3. [Real Trends](https://www.realtrends.com/?s=property+management)
4. [Rental Housing Journal](https://rentalhousingjournal.com/)
5. [Real Pages' blog](https://www.realpage.com/blog/)

## Competitors

Latchel is truly unique. Latchel is the only end-to-end platform that automates maintenance coordination and delivers best in class resident amenities. To learn more about how to talk about Latchel, visit the [Log Lines & Latchel Messaging Guide](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2848522265) page.

Because Latchel is unique, prospects try to understand Latchel in the context of more common services in the industry. Until we can change our customers' frame of reference, you will need to understand Latchel's competitors so that you can differentiate Latchel and sell its value.

Notes on competitors:

- Never "bash" a competitor or speak of them by name to a prospect, unless a prospect makes a direct ask.
- We're not selling their products so give them as little air time as possible. When you talk about a competitor, you add credibility to the competitor.
- Better to say "We are unlike many other software/call centers out there. Let me explain how we provide a solution."
