Welcome to your team space!

The purpose of this space is to be a central location for the Sales Team to access all of the resources the team needs to:

- Keep everyone informed and on the same page regarding Company Leadership Direction and Sales Strategy, Department and Team Management & Performance
- Perform the day-to-day tasks and responsibilities for every job role in the Sales Team

In this Space, you will also find shortcuts to other resources owned by other departments within Latchel that could also be useful for the sales team.

Some of the Sales Team resources will be files that may not be a good fit to display in Confluence or were built in a format that the team decided would be better than a Confluence Page. In those cases, they will all be stored in the Sales Team Google Drive, but will also be accessible via a link from a Confluence Page.
