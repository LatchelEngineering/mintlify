## Purpose

<Note>
  **Purpose of this document**

  The purpose of having a script and templates is to provide guidance and tips on how to communicate certain key selling points and collect key information from the lead.

  The goal is not to read the script, but to ensure the key items are included in the communication. For email templates, although you can copy and paste the template, you should make some small adjustments to add your personal touch! 🙂
</Note>

<Note>
  **Who should read this document?**

  SDRs
</Note>

## When to use these Templates?

This script is to be used when the SDR receives a notification that someone has filled out the demo request on [http://Latchel.com](http://Latchel.com). For Tiers definitions, check the [Glossary Page](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2902917222).

SDRs will receive their name, phone, email, and small information about them (estimate unit count). From there, the SDR should immediately call them and ask the qualifying questions / set up a time on the AE's calendar to move forward.

This script can be used inside or outside a cadence, or any time we are contacting a lead for the first time.

## Prior to Calling…

**PREPARE!**

Learn about them and their history with us, if any:

- View data provided in the lead assignment, or attempt to gather some information online:
  - Unit count
  - Lead Name
  - Company Name, info
  - Date they Reached Out
  - Lead Tier
  - Property Management Software
  - Source of the lead (referral, rent manager integrations page, rentredi, etc)
- Check Salesforce for potential past engagement, like meetings, calls, emails. Add note summarizing past engagement to top of the page.
- Check if it is a duplicate account and merge if needed.
- Check website info to learn about them
- Do any data clean up, if needed (add/adjust websites, numbers, or direct numbers)

Prepare for questions:

**Key questions!**

- What do we do? --- check the [Log Line Page](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2848522265/Log+Lines+Latchel+Messaging+Guide)
- How much does it cost? ----- CHECK UPDATED INFO FOR THIS

They may have very specific questions that mean a lot to them - but aren't important to answer on the first day. You can use the blurb below to answer those:

> _"I'll grab a specialist to help you answer that! I want to get the right person on the phone so before I do that I need to ask you a few questions: ([Qualification](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2868248756))_

Prepare for challenges:

- Bad or missing Contact info – look at tips in the [Lead Research section in this page](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2888499262/SDR+Role+-+Organize+your+day#Lead-Research-\(~1hr\))
- Unreachable Decision Maker (DM) - how to overcome this?
  - Company main phone number, try to get direct Cell Phone numbers
  - It is easier to get in touch directly, specially if that is the Decision Maker's number!
  - Can be reached outside of business hours
- Be careful with what you say,
  - They can be shifty at this stage in the game, avoid answering questions - Let the AE explain things
  - For T1s, the phone conversations can be short. Set expectations that you are there to get them in touch with a specialist.

## Script for Call

**Opener - Option 1:**

> _Hey (first name), This is (your name) with Latchel, did I catch you at a bad time?_
>
> _Your name came across my desk and you wanted to learn more about Latchel. I have a couple questions for you to better understand your portfolio and your interest in Latchel before setting up a time to go more in depth with one of my colleagues._

**Opener - Option 2:**

> _Hey this is NAME From Latchel. I got your demo request on our website and wanted to reach out to understand what you wanted to learn more about, and schedule that demo..._

Try asking the [Qualification Questions](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SAL/pages/2868248756):

Thanks for answering those, it helps us better tailor the meeting to your situation specifically, now I have (name drop who you are going to book them with, for example- "my director of sales, Shannon's") calendar pulled up to find a time. How does x date at x time work for you?

Great, I'll send over the calendar invite for x date at x time, is [example@company.com](mailto:example@company.com) the best email to send that to?

I'm sending over the calendar invite now, the video meeting link will be in the calendar invite, if you could accept the invite it will be on your calendar. This will be a meeting to dive deeper into your situation and how Latchel can help you and then we will set additional meetings as needed.

Thank you.

<Note>
  When scheduling the meeting, schedule it as soon as possible. Push for later that day/tomorrow/or that week

  If they are scheduling the meeting for a later date, it is essential to get their buy-in so they show up for the meeting.
</Note>

## Script for Voicemail

> _Hey Name, this is NAME from Latchel. Giving you a call because I just got a demo request from you on our website. I was hoping to chat more about that and understand how we can help! Give me a call when you see this: PHONE NUMBER._
