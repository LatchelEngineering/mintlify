## Process Overview

There are multiple sections in Salesforce for notes, which makes it hard to find the notes information if we don't have a standard.

When working on an Opportunity, the notes should be left within the Opportunity, not at the Account.

In this video ([https://www.loom.com/share/97023d3cb98c4f89b6837cf847cfae14?sid=a861115b-299c-4c5c-97ba-a57a5640399f](https://www.loom.com/share/97023d3cb98c4f89b6837cf847cfae14?sid=a861115b-299c-4c5c-97ba-a57a5640399f)), Matt shows the standard location the AEs should leave notes after each meeting type and for contracts, which should be added in the Internal Notes section of the corresponding step or meeting in the pipeline.

### Contract Notes

Any notes related to Contract Terms, such as subscription, special set-up discussions, pricing, etc, or anything Customer Success must know to configure the account correctly, should go under the "Contract Sent" stage, "Contract" section"

<Frame>
  ![Salesforce Contract stage showing opportunity Rent-LBK details](/images/Sales-174.png)
</Frame>

Scroll all the way down to see the notes section:

<Frame>
  ![Salesforce Contract section scrolled down to show the notes area](/images/Sales-175.png)
</Frame>

The same rationale applies to all other steps of the pipeline, i.e. go to the stage of the Opportunity when those notes were taken and leave the notes in the notes section.
