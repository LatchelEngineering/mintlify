## Step-by-Step Guide

### Process Overview

In order to keep track of requests for the change management into Confluence for the Growth Org we will be using Jira to create tickets for any process or information in confluence to be created or updated.

<Warning>
  If there is not a ticket for it in Jira, the request does NOT exist!
</Warning>

---

### Step 1 - Go to the #sales-change-request slack channel

this is a public channel, you should be able to find it by searching for it on Slack

  ![](/images/Sales-114.png)
</Frame>

---

### Step 2 - Locate the Sales Content Request button

It is located at the top of the screen, right under the channel name

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Sales-115.png)
</Frame>

---

### Step 3 - Start Workflow

You will be prompted to connect your Atlassian account if you have not already, once you have, click "Start Workflow"

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Sales-116.png)
</Frame>

Please complete all fields then submit your ticket.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Sales-117.png)
</Frame>

---

### What happens after I submit my ticket?

There is a weekly meeting with both the SDR's & AE's to determine which ticket will be worked on & by whom - the collective sales team determines which tickets takes precedence. Please supply any information to the Jira ticket that you feel is relevant to determining its importance.
