## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Complete the required fields in on the Company and Deal for quote generation.

<Note>
  Video overview of this step (5 mins): [Completing your Deal with required fields for Quotes](https://www.loom.com/share/dbd531a21b3b48259ff329720678fed1)
</Note>

<Accordion title="Required fields for ALL contract types">
  On the Company:

  1. Street address
  2. Street address 2 (if applicable)
  3. City
  4. State/Region
  5. Postal Code

  On the Deal:

  6. Contracted Units
  7. Price Per Unit
  8. Contract Renewal Date
     - Select the 12 month anniversary of the Billing Start Date. Note: BI is working on adding an Initial Term End Date field to Deals which will replace this as a required field in the future – stay tuned for the update!
  9. Billing POC Name
  10. Billing POC Title
  11. Billing POC Email
  12. Billing POC Phone
  13. Subscription
  14. Appfolio slug (if customer is on Appfolio)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Additional required fields on Assisted Ledgering deals">
  - Contract Start Date
    - Note: We're using this as proxy for Guarantee Period Start Date
  - Guarantee Period End Date
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Additional required fields on Optional Benefits + Maintenance deals">
  - Optional Latchel Opt-In Rate
  - Resident Charge Amount
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Additional required fields on Optional Benefits Only deals">
  - Platform Fee
  - Optional Latchel Opt-In Rate
  - Resident Charge Amount
</Accordion>

<Note>
  Video overview of this step (7 mins): [Quote creation process overview](https://www.loom.com/share/eede264da497433da4c4debff9cd5e39)

1. From the deal, click the \+ Add link in the Quotes card (bottom right of the page)

<Frame>
  ![The \+ Add link in the Quotes card on a HubSpot deal](/images/Sales-181.png)

2. **Buyer Information**
   - If the company and contact(s) are already associated with the deal, these fields will auto populate. You can add/remove contacts with the checkboxes, or by clicking the \+ Add another contact link and searching for the contact(s) you want to be included on the quote.

**Your Information**

This will be auto populated with your information.

Add all other line items using the same process as the implementation fee

Update the Billing Start Date, Billing Frequency and Quantity on each line item

**Implementation Fee**

- Quantity: 1
- Billing Frequency: One-time
- Billing Start Date: At payment

**All other products**

- Quantity: Update this to the number of Initial Units (Contracted Units on deal)
- Billing Frequency: Monthly
- Billing Start Date: Custom Date → Update this to the Billing Start Date

**Special note on Marketplace Benefits**

The Third Party Addendum was deprecated in October 2024. Marketplace Benefits pricing is now added via HubSpot line items. Marketplace Benefits terms are included in the [MSA](https://latchel.com/terms-of-use-msa/).

**Special note on Optional Benefits or any item for which we don't have a specific quantity yet**

When adding the **Home Assistant Opt-Ins (Benefits Only)**, **Home Assistant Opt-Ins (with Maintenance)** line items or any item for which we don't have a specific quantity yet, leave the Quantity as 1.

Add a note in the Comments section: e.g. This quote includes an Implementation Fee, which is due upon acceptance. The other items (Home Assistant Opt-Ins, Platform Fee, and Marketplace: Pinata) are listed for reference but will be charged separately starting on Month DD, AAAA. The quantity for these items is set to 1 as a placeholder, as the actual quantity will depend on usage and be determined at that time.

_We are so excited to partner with you! Here is the link to pay your implementation fee: \[insert correct link\]. Once it's paid, we can get your kick-off meeting scheduled! ### Step 3: After the quote is sent

<Note>
  Video overview of this step (2 mins): [What happens after a Quote is sent](https://www.loom.com/share/392a65014d3542239423565bc72a4d9e)
</Note>

- Signers will receive an email to verify their identity and sign the quote.
- Once they sign, the AE and Jen Sells will receive an email notification.
- When Jen Sells countersigns, the AE and the customer will receive an email notification.
- Once the AE receives this notification, they are responsible for updating the Contract Effective Date on the deal to the date of the countersignature

<Frame>
  ![Updating the Contract Effective Date on the deal to the date of the countersignature](/images/Sales-193.png)
</Frame>

- The customer's notification includes a PDF attachment of the countersigned contract.

### Step 4: Changes to quotes after signing

Creating a new quote updates the deal amount to whatever is included in the most recent quote. Therefore, if we make any contract changes post-signing, create a new deal and a new quote on that deal to send to the customer.

Example: A customer decides to add Marketplace Benefits after signing a contract for Latchel Services. Create a new deal and a new quote on that deal for their current Latchel Services \+ Marketplace Benefits so that everything is included in the new quote. Make it clear to the customer that when they add a new service, they are resigning with Latchel for 12 months.
