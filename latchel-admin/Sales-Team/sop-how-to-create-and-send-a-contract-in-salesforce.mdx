## Step-by-Step Guide

### Process Overview

In order to create a contract in Pandadoc., you need to navigate to the Account's Opportunity page and select the contract template.

If the applicable template has not been added to the template selection, you can also import one and add the necessary addendums. To do that, you need to download the contract as a PDF and upload it into Pandadoc. Once you open it in Pandadoc, you will need to place fields (e.g. check boxes, text boxes, signature fields), and assign them to the applicable parties (Account and AE).

Finally, you can send the contract via email and check the record in Salesforce.
