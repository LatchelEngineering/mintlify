### 🧠 What This Process Is Trying to Solve

<Note>
  This SOP ensures that Harmonia Work Orders are reviewed consistently, accurately, and in alignment with PM rules and AI Intake processing. By following this process, auditors can:

  - Identify potential gaps or errors in the AI Intake process as well as Work Order handling, preventing issues such as missed follow-ups or improper escalation
  - Provide a standardized method for auditors to conduct reviews, improving audit reliability, reducing confusion, and supporting better outcomes for Latchel and our customers.

  In short, this process ensures accuracy, accountability, and quality control in the WO lifecycle while giving auditors clear guidance on how to review efficiently and consistently.
</Note>

### 📋 Step-by-Step Process

#### Step 1 - Access Your Audit Assignments

1. Open the [**Harmonia Audits**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1hXLukPR_bI7HiC5_fMfC_V-np94gBR9YtIkaw20Y6VE/edit?gid=1482822428#gid=1482822428) sheet.
2. Navigate to the **Assignments** tab.
3. Locate your name in the **Auditor** column.
4. Check the **Company Name** column to identify the Property Manager (PM) you are assigned to audit.

#### Step 2- Select a Sample Work Order

1. Go to the **Samples** tab in the Harmonia Audits sheet.
2. Randomly select a Work Order from the **Job URL** column (D) corresponding to your assigned PM.
   1. **Prioritize** samples flagged as **“Low”** in the **Skill Match Status** column (H).
   2. If there are no “Low” samples, select any available sample.
3. Open the Work Order (WO) and verify the following:
   1. The **Property Manager** listed is correct (found in the **Details** tab).
   2. The WO was submitted using **AI Intake** (check for an Intake summary in the **Intake** tab).
4. Go to the **Activity** tab and **sort by “oldest first”**.
   1. Confirm that a **Troubleshooting call** was conducted.
   2. If no Troubleshooting call is present, return to the sample sheet and select a different WO.

#### Step 3 -Conduct the Audit

1. Open the [**Harmonia Work Order Audit Form**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd2uS-2jOAePnukrCZcCYD93rPtl-Z2Pfp1ePBzEaqLyA2vqA/viewform).
2. Start your review by following these steps:
   1. Review the **Harmonia Intake Processing Summary** in the **Activity** tab.
   2. Listen to the **Troubleshooting call** and review all notes, WO updates, and follow-up actions.
   3. Answer all audit form questions in alignment with the **Harmonia Work Order Audit guidelines**.
3. Submit the completed audit form.

#### Step 4 -Document Your Audit

1. After submission, add your name to the **Auditor** column (L) in the **Samples** tab.
   1. This ensures tracking and prevents duplicate audits.

### 🎯 When to Break This SOP

This SOP covers 80–90% of cases. But no SOP is perfect. Break the SOP and escalate when:

- _You encounter an unusual AI Intake or WO edge case that the SOP doesn’t address._
- _You believe an alternate audit action would more accurately assess the WO or better support Latchel and the Property Manager._
