### 🧠 What This Process Is Trying to Solve

<Note>
  This SOP ensures that emergency work orders are handled efficiently and accurately by guiding troubleshooters to gather all relevant information, assess severity, and take appropriate immediate action. The goal is to prevent missed or delayed responses, reduce property damage, and ensure residents and property managers receive timely, confident guidance; saving time, avoiding unnecessary follow-ups, and improving overall customer experience.
</Note>

<Check>
  📍**Before taking action, reflect on the following:**

  - Does this reduce friction for the customer, or create more?
  - Am I communicating clearly, or leaving them to guess?
  - Am I truly solving the problem, or just applying a temporary fix?
  - Did I address the customer’s real question?
  - Will this interaction leave a memorable, positive impression?
  - If this issue recurs, am I escalating it appropriately, or merely reacting?

  At Latchel, we don’t just support customers—we improve the way support is delivered.
</Check>

<Note>
  These audits are based on [How to: Properly Diagnose Emergency Situations](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OPS/pages/2475851825)
</Note>

<Warning>
  Note for auditors: Only audit troubleshooting calls for the purpose of this Work Order Audit. If the assigned agent has no troubleshooting calls for the week, enter **“No TS call”** in the **“Reviews of the Week”** tab in place of your name.
</Warning>
