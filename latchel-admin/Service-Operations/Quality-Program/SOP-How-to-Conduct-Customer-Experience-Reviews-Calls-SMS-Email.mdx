## Purpose

<Note>
The purpose of this document is to guide Quality auditors on how to properly conduct customer experience reviews in alignment with [Job Aid: Customer Experience Review Calibration Guidelines](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OPS/pages/2150039592)
</Note>

<Note>
Estimated time to complete an audit: 5-10 minutes.
</Note>

<Note>
**Who should read this document?**

- Ops Trainers
- Ops Quality Associate
- Search & Rescue Associates
- Ops Supervisors
</Note>

## Content

### Step-by-Step Guide for Reviewers

1. Open the Customer Experience Review Google sheet and consult the **“Reviews of the Week”** tab to identify which agent(s) you’ve been assigned to audit this week.  If you see your name in Column G, you’ve been assigned to review the agent on the same line in Column A. <br/><br/>
1. Complete 3 Cx reviews for every assigned agent in red (bottom performers), and 1 Cx review for every assigned agent in white.<br/><br/>
1. Once you have an agent to audit, identify the agent’s role and go to the tab designated for each type of sample. We aim to review a sample associated with the associate’s role to audit their main task, for those assigned for more than one review we can use at least two from their main role and for the third review use a different sample category to complete their performance evaluation.For Call Center “CC” check the [Inbound Call sample](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/11g_qx-XbGTujAK04kS-rI8qtHLz6ZqrsFbWLis3knAE/edit#gid=989517436) For Troubleshooters “OA-T” use the troubleshooting call you used to audit WO audit, if more reviews are needed check the [Outbound call sample](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/11g_qx-XbGTujAK04kS-rI8qtHLz6ZqrsFbWLis3knAE/edit#gid=990445649) For the “SMS” associates check the [Webchat sample](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/11g_qx-XbGTujAK04kS-rI8qtHLz6ZqrsFbWLis3knAE/edit#gid=2112145643) and/ or [SMS sample](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/11g_qx-XbGTujAK04kS-rI8qtHLz6ZqrsFbWLis3knAE/edit#gid=762777134)For “Task” team we can use any of the sample tab above or the [Email sample](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/11g_qx-XbGTujAK04kS-rI8qtHLz6ZqrsFbWLis3knAE/edit#gid=502761781)<br/><br/>
1. The “**Reviews of the week**” tab is updated on Monday mornings and reviews can be completed anytime before the end of the day on Saturday.<br/><br/>
