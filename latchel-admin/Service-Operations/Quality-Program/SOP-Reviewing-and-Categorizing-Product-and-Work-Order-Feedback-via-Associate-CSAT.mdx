### 🧠 What This Process Is Trying to Solve

<Note>
  The Product Support review is to systematically analyze Associate CSAT feedback to identify deeper work order issues and product-related insights that extend beyond individual service quality. Product Support is responsible for uncovering patterns, risks, and opportunities that warrant further investigation through Work Order Deep Dives, as well as surfacing actionable product feedback, such as feature requests and bugs, to the Product team. This ensures that systemic operational issues and product gaps are documented, tracked, and addressed with clear ownership and traceability.
</Note>

### Product Support Review & Deep Dive Identification

#### Purpose

In addition to Quality Assurance review, Product Support is responsible for scanning Associate [CSAT](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1PG_UqlMVMyB7XgQdnoHYWJfGY2E4LFVnMyjupsXww0U/edit?gid=0#gid=0) feedback to identify:

- **Work Order Deep Dive opportunities**, and
- **Product feedback**, including feature requests and bugs.

#### Procedure

##### **A. Scanning Feedback**

1. In tandem with QA processing, Product Support reviews all CSAT entries in the **Weekly QA Review** tab.
2. Identify feedback that indicates:
   1. A need for a **Work Order Deep Dive**, or
   2. **Product-related feedback** (feature gaps, usability issues, defects, or bugs).

##### **B. Work Order Deep Dives**

1. For each identified Work Order Deep Dive:
   1. Create a **Deep Dive ticket** following Product Support’s standard intake process.
   2. Capture the **Deep Dive ticket URL** in the designated column in the spreadsheet.
2. Ensure each Deep Dive is clearly linked back to the originating CSAT feedback for traceability.

##### **C. Product Feedback (Features & Bugs)**

1. For product-related feedback:
   1. Route the feedback to the appropriate **Product feature request** or **bug ticket**.
   2. Capture the **feature or bug ticket URL** in the designated column in the spreadsheet.
2. If the feedback spans multiple issues, document each relevant URL.

##### **D. Tracking & Documentation**

1. Product Support must track:
   1. All **Deep Dive URLs**, and
   2. All **Product feature or bug URLs** directly in the spreadsheet to maintain a single source of truth.
2. Use notes fields as needed to provide additional context, trends, or rationale for escalation.

### Quality & Governance

- This dual-review process ensures operational quality issues, systemic work order risks, and product improvement opportunities are consistently identified and owned.
- Complete and accurate URL tracking is required to support reporting, trend analysis, and cross-functional accountability.
- Any ambiguity in ownership or routing should be flagged in **Special Notes** and discussed during weekly WBR syncs.
