## Purpose

<Note>
**Purpose of this document**

The purpose of this document is to provide Quality Associates with a clear, step-by-step guide for completing the weekly setup process for Customer Experience, Failed escalations, and Work Order audits. This includes updating response sheets and dashboards, and ensuring all audit-related information is accurate and ready for the week.
</Note>

<Note>
**Who should read this document?**

Quality team: Quality associates and manager.
</Note>

## Content

### Step-by-Step Guide

<Note>
**What happened before this step?**

Before starting the weekly setup process, all customer interactions from the previous week (calls, SMS, emails, troubleshooting, and failed escalations) have been completed and recorded. Supervisors have imported any required initial data feeds into Assembly or related platforms, and the audit cycle is ready to begin for the new week.
</Note>

Update Formulas for Score Columns (for each response sheet):Review Submission Dates:Check the submission date in the “Timestamp” column (A)If there are submissions dated on Sunday (which would count as the current week), update those dates to align with the previous week’s range (Sunday through Saturday) to ensure accurate reporting.Go to the score-related columns at the end of the sheet (from Date/ Yes count to Markdowns, column labels may vary depending on the sheet).Drag down the formulas to ensure all rows have the correct calculations applied.Make sure all formulas are properly updated, as this directly reflects in the weekly results shown in the Dashboard.**For Calls Only **– Rename Inbound Call Links:In the Calls response sheet, locate column F: “_Twilio mp3 file (if inbound call)_”.Open each link and rename the file by deleting the last part of the filename (this is usually the auditor’s name).After renaming, copy and paste the links from column F into column E: “_Link to Latchel Console Call Information (if outbound call)_”. #### Step 3 -Assign Reviewers

1. **CX Assignments** ([Video Guide](https://www.loom.com/share/19c7858d1489480c876a882d5b9f58c5?sid=4bbef3c2-1921-4078-ad54-d111557175a5))Open the [Bottom Performers by Week](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/11g_qx-XbGTujAK04kS-rI8qtHLz6ZqrsFbWLis3knAE/edit?gid=709986194#gid=709986194) tab to find the calculated CX Reviews Goal.In the “Reviews of the Week” tab, update the “Total Reviews to Complete this Week” column (P) in the auditors’ list.Click the **Randomizer button**:This will randomly assign reviews and automatically paste the previous week's assignments into the corresponding table.<br/><br/>Open Perform a final verification:Ensure each auditor has the correct number of assignments.Check that the assigned agents are not the same as the previous week.Confirm that agents’ roles do not conflict with the auditors’ access credentials:Example: Coaches should not review inbound calls due to Twilio access restrictions.Make manual adjustments as needed.<br/><br/><br/>
1. **Email Assignments****Filter the **[**Email Sample (Assembly) **](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/11g_qx-XbGTujAK04kS-rI8qtHLz6ZqrsFbWLis3knAE/edit?gid=1330611190#gid=1330611190)**tab**In **Column A (week_of_year)**, filter to display only the current week.**Sort the agents alphabetically**In **Column G (Start User)**, sort the Agent names from **A to Z**.**Assign agents in the “Reviews of the Week” tab**Copy and paste each Agent’s name from the sample list into the **Email Assignments** section of the **“**[**Reviews of the Week**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/11g_qx-XbGTujAK04kS-rI8qtHLz6ZqrsFbWLis3knAE/edit?gid=1657910582#gid=1657910582)**”** tab (Delete the email address in brackets and keep only the Agent’s full name.)**Assign the corresponding auditor**Copy and paste the assigned Auditor’s name based on the **first Cx list assignments**.**Adjust assignments if needed**Review each Auditor’s total assigned volume.If the email assignments cause an Auditor to exceed their goal, reassign the excess reviews evenly among the QA Associates to maintain balanced workloads.<br/><br/>
1. **WO Audit Assignments** ([Video Guide](https://www.loom.com/share/8594eea0ada84357aea2fdedb396a316?sid=05a3e375-2c43-49a4-9ee1-704b7c0458c0))Go to the **Reviews of the Week** tab in the **CX Review spreadsheet**.Sort the assignments list by **Name** and **Role**.Identify auditors assigned to agents with **OA-T roles** (Troubleshooters).Copy and paste those assignments into the **Reviews of the Week** tab in the [**Work Order Audits**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1pDBRevftU7iNqV7Syc4mb5AeGG8lMmwcjxic88TwI-A/edit?gid=0#gid=0) spreadsheet.<br/><br/><br/>
