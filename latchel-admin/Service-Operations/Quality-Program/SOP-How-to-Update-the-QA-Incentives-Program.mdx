### 📋 Step-by-Step Process

<Callout icon="notes" iconType="duotone" color="#b19cf8">
  The QA Incentives Program is updated monthly using data from the Customer Experience Review form, self-assessments, and attendance records. The process begins with validating and exporting performance data, then populating and verifying the Stars Tracking Sheet. Points are awarded based on CX score, perfect attendance, and 5-star TS reviews. Once all qualifying agents are identified, a raffle is conducted for recognition. Results are shared via Slack to close the cycle.
</Callout>

#### 1. Prepare Customer Experience Review Data

1. Open the **Individual Results: Customer Experience Review (Responses)** sheet.
2. Go to the **Form Responses 1** tab.
3. Make sure Columns **B, C, Q, and R** have updated formulas. If not, drag the formulas down to update.

#### 2. Complete Reassessment Verifications

1. Confirm that all reassessments for the relevant month have been completed.
2. Ensure updated scores are reflected in the sheet.

#### 3. Create the Monthly Tab in the Stars Tracking Sheet

1. Open the **Stars Tracking Sheet**.
2. Create a copy of the last month’s tab.
3. Rename the new tab using the current month and year (e.g., **June 2025**).

#### 4. Populate Performance Data

1. Open the **Supervisor CX Experience Review Dashboard** and go to the **CX Monthly Performance** tab.
2. Filter the data by **year and month**.
3. Export and paste the data into the new tab in the **Stars Tracking Sheet**.
4. Fill in Columns **A to H**:
   1. RoleAgent NameS
   2. upervisor
   3. week\_per\_month
   4. Total CX Reviews
   5. Average CX Score
   6. Number of Self-Assessment Submissions
   7. Average Self-Assessment Compliance

#### 5. Update Formulas

1. Column I – "Perfect attendance points"

- Go to the final part of the formula to adjust:
  - The **week numbers**
  - The **total weeks** to consider for that month's calculation
- Example: (23, 24, 25, 26) and =4

2. Column J – "TS 5-star rating \+ Comment"

- Change the **month number** and verify the **year** is correct in this portion of the formula:Example (“6” and “2025”)

`IMPORTRANGE("1hMT5oOMmZjl0ennUWmW1AJ9lrzU811qX0R1z9MQ2C7g", "Troubleshooting data!K:K"), "6", IMPORTRANGE("1hMT5oOMmZjl0ennUWmW1AJ9lrzU811qX0R1z9MQ2C7g", "Troubleshooting data!L:L"), "2025" `

#### 6. Review Star Calculations and Determine Qualifiers

1. After all data is updated, the following columns will auto-populate:
   1. **Performance Star** (Column K)
   2. **TS-feedback Star** (Column L)
   3. **TOTAL Stars Count** (Column M)
2. Based on these results, identify the associates qualifying for each award, aligned with the criteria outlined in the **Latchel Performance Recognition & Reward Programs**.

#### 7. Conduct Raffle & Share Recognition

1. For each reward category:
   1. Input the list of qualifying associates into [https://wheelofnames.com/](https://wheelofnames.com/)  to select a random winner.
   2. Record a **Loom video** of each draw for transparency.

##### Post in Slack

- Share the results in the **#service-operations** Slack channel.
- The post should include:
  - Links to the Loom recordings
  - The reward for each winner
  - Individual tags for each winner

### 🎯 When to Break This SOP

<Callout icon="notes" color="#b19cf8">
  This SOP covers 80–90% of the monthly QA Incentives update process. However, exceptions may occur. Associates should use their judgment and escalate or pause the process when the following situations arise:
</Callout>

- **Incomplete Data or Missing Submissions**<br />If self-assessment data, CX review results, or attendance records are incomplete or delayed, hold off on updating the sheet until all data sources are confirmed as final. Escalate to a Supervisor or Manager if the delay may impact reward timelines.
- **Discrepancies in Data**<br />If any formulas return unexpected values, star awards seem incorrect, or agent results do not align with historical patterns, pause the process and review the source formulas. Escalate to the QA Program Lead if verification cannot be completed independently.
- **Changes to Award Criteria**<br />If updates to the Performance Recognition & Reward Programs criteria have been announced (e.g., new scoring thresholds, different award categories, changes to eligibility rules), confirm the new process with the QA Program Lead before proceeding.
- **Technical Issues in Sheets or Dashboards**<br />If key spreadsheets or dashboards (e.g., Customer Experience Review, Stars Tracking Sheet, or Troubleshooting Tracker) are unavailable or return errors, escalate to the relevant point of contact (e.g., Ops Analytics or the sheet owner) before continuing.
- **Team Structure Changes**<br />If an agent’s role, team, or supervisor information has changed during the month and it's unclear which team the points should be attributed to, confirm with the QA Supervisor before proceeding with the update.
