## Purpose

<Note>
**Purpose of this document**

The Quality Department is responsible for evaluating interactions between Latchel associates and customers, including residents, property managers, service providers, and owners. The primary goal is to ensure these interactions align with established SOPs and guidelines while consistently delivering exceptional customer service. By identifying strengths and areas for improvement, the department provides constructive feedback and actionable insights. This empowers associates to enhance their performance, improve customer experiences, and maintain Latchel's high standards of service excellence.
</Note>

<Note>
**Who should read this document?**

This document is intended for all Latchel associates involved in customer interactions, including those working with residents, property managers, service providers, and owners. It is also essential for team leaders, managers, and members of the Quality Department who oversee performance evaluations and provide feedback. By understanding the quality evaluation process and its objectives, all stakeholders can align their efforts to deliver exceptional customer service and continuous improvement.
</Note>

### **Auditing Process**

The Quality Department receives random samples from the previous week and assigns these samples to a group of auditors. The assignment process is as follows:

1. **Sample Selection Process**A database automatically generates samples for each communication channel (calls, SMS, webchat, and emails), updated weekly to show random samples for each associate’s interactions on the given dates.Troubleshooting and PM outbound communication (Failed escalations) samples are generated based on the date and Work Order severity (emergency and non-emergency, respectively). For all sample types, auditors randomly select from the available samples provided in each database.
1. **Audit Assignment Distribution:**Names of associates are assigned to auditors randomly based on the weekly samples provided, with the auditor changing week over week.If no samples are available for a particular associate, no audit will be conducted for that associate in the given week. The auditor must notify the QA team before the end of the week.
1. **Audit Completion:**Auditors are required to complete their assigned audits within the same week they were assigned.The process repeats every Monday with the start of a new auditing cycle.
1. **Acknowledgment of Feedback:**Associates must review and acknowledge their feedback from the previous week by filling out the [Self-assessment form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScGVQ_QfUNY9XlWV_82WR-ZBlBWOeSDoICkHFDosCBIlkhPuA/viewform) within the same week.If unable to complete the Self-assessment form, associates must inform their direct supervisor and provide a reason for the delay.Supervisors must notify the QA team about instances when an Associate should be exempt from acknowledgement (e.g., the associate was OOO for the week, etc.).
1. [**Responding to Markdowns or Questions:**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/~5eebcfadb364ce0abe6e0234/pages/edit-v2/3310551080?#Criteria-for-Score-Updates)During the acknowledgment process, associates can include comments or questions regarding markdowns they received.These comments will be reviewed by the Quality Department. For more information, refer to the [Reassessment Process ](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/~5eebcfadb364ce0abe6e0234/pages/edit-v2/3310551080#Reassessment-process-and-criteria.)section below.

### **Reassessment Overview**

The reassessment process allows associates to respond to feedback from auditors, particularly in cases where a markdown has been applied. Associates can provide additional context to explain their actions during the interaction in question.

### **Process Steps:**

1. **Feedback Review:**The Quality Department reviews the interaction, markdowns, and any feedback submitted by the associate.Relevant SOPs and guidelines are referenced to ensure the assessment aligns with company standards.
1. **Timeline:**Reassessments are reviewed on the following Monday.The goal is to resolve reassessments within one week of receiving the associate’s comments.
1. [**Decision Making:**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/~5eebcfadb364ce0abe6e0234/pages/edit-v2/3310551080?draftShareId=273ea0c4-0ffd-46b6-90dc-d7e6d012e662#Criteria-for-Score-Updates)Following a detailed review, the Quality Department determines whether to update the score or maintain the deductions. Updates to scores are accompanied by revised comments explaining the decision. If deductions are upheld, comments clarify the rationale.If uncertainties remain, the case is escalated to the manager for further evaluation.
1. **Communication:**Associates are informed of the reassessment outcome. Responses include explanations for the decision and, where applicable, suggestions for improvement or clarification of process gaps. Once the reassessment has been completed, the associate will be able to view the outcome in their [dashboard](https://lookerstudio.google.com/reporting/f7b64bbc-6b72-4a82-af6c-fc4304e8c36c/page/p_hkpj6byg1c) along with the comments.

## **Reassessment process and criteria.**

This policy establishes a systematic process for associates to request a reassessment of their weekly audit results. The goal is to ensure transparency and fairness while maintaining alignment with Latchel's quality standards.

### **Timing for Reassessment Requests**

Reassessment requests must be submitted within the week the audit results are uploaded or by the following week at the latest.

- Example: If the request is submitted in Week 50, it will only be reviewed if it pertains to Week 50 audits or Week 49 at the earliest.
- Requests submitted more than two weeks after the audit publication, will not be eligible for review.

### **Criteria for Score Updates**

**Reassessment requests will be considered valid if the following apply:**

- The auditor made a clear error, such as selecting an incorrect evaluation (e.g., marking “No” when it should be “Yes” or “Not Applicable”).
- The associate followed documented instructions provided in a public Slack channel after escalating the issue between teams
- The associate met the expectations outlined in Latchel’s SOPs, guidelines, and other documentation.
- There is no documentation to support the markdown, or insufficient coaching material is available.

**Reassessment requests will not be approved if the following apply:**

- The argument is based on assumptions and lacks validation by SOPs, documentation, or extraordinary instructions following escalation.
- The request is submitted more than two weeks after the audit publication.

##### **How to Submit a Reassessment Request**

1. Complete the [Self-Assessment Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScGVQ_QfUNY9XlWV_82WR-ZBlBWOeSDoICkHFDosCBIlkhPuA/viewform).
1. Under the question, “_**Are you dissatisfied with any of your audit results?**_”, provide a detailed explanation of your argument and sample details.
1. The form must be submitted within 2 weeks of receiving the feedback.

##### **Reassessment Review Process**

- The Quality team will review all reassessment requests submitted within a week.
- Feedback and results (applicable or not applicable) will be posted in the associate’s dashboard by the following Monday.
- Associates will also receive a Slack notification confirming the reassessment outcome as soon as it’s completed.

The Quality Department welcomes associates to reach out directly for any guidance or process improvements they might need. Our team is always open and ready to assist without any hesitation.
