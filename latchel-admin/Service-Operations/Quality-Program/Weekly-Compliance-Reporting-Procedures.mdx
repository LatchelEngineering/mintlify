<Note>
  Purpose of the SOP: The purpose of this SOP is to **outline the steps for updating the tracking spreadsheet** used to confirm with Supervisors **which of their Associates have reviewed the**[Weekly Operations Newsletter.](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OPS/pages/3539566594)This process ensures that all team members stay informed of the latest updates posted every Friday in the **Service Operations channel**.
</Note>

### Reporting compliance for the Weekly operations newsletter

How to Update the Newsletter Review Tracker and Notify Supervisors

**1.- Open the Spreadsheet**

Locate and open the spreadsheet titled **["Operations Weekly Newsletter Review Form](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1cgpoi-_4qpf3AgVqwjsjl3mZBZ2AmL0144CxFgQpKCg/edit?gid=1309447518#gid=1309447518)"**.

**2. -Open the tab “Compliance.”**

Access the pivot table and locate the filter that says  “Newsletter Reviewed.”

**3.-Filter by date**

In the "Newsletter Reviewed" filter, **select the date of the previous Friday** (the day the newsletter was posted).

**4.- Filter by supervisor and sort A-Z**

Apply the **Supervisor filter** and **sort the list A–Z** to organize associates by their respective supervisors.<br />Associates marked with **“#N/A”** in the Supervisor column are those who **have not confirmed they reviewed the newsletter**.

**5.-Edit template to send via slack**

Identify team members marked with “#N/A” and send the Slack message template to their Supervisor **every Wednesday**.

**TEMPLATE:**

> “_Hello @SupervisorName☀️ Happy Wednesday!_
>
> _Quick reminder for the agents on your team who haven’t submitted their form to acknowledge the **Operations Weekly Newsletter for \[Insert Date\]**._
>
> _Let’s please remind them to complete it before **Sunday end of shift (EOS)**._
>
> _Thank you! 😊”_
