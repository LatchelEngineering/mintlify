### 🧠 What This Process Is Trying to Solve

<Note>
  This SOP outlines the process for updating the Top Opportunities presentation slides for our weekly All Hands meetings held on Wednesdays and Thursdays. During these meetings, we share with our associates the top strengths and key improvement opportunities identified from the previous week’s audits.
</Note>

### 📋 Step-by-Step Process

<Callout icon="notes" iconType="duotone" color="#b19cf8">
  List each major step in the process clearly and logically. **Use bullets or numbers.**
</Callout>

#### Step 1 - Open the “QA - Weekly Top Opportunities” presentation file.

1. [QA- Weekly Top Opportunities](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1hyzmq7fO-cxIOMwX8FhaSwqkWpECpgCl-5HVXIHoo0U/edit?slide=id.g34f82bd124d_4_0#slide=id.g34f82bd124d_4_0)

#### Step 2- Slide 1: Shoutouts

1. Include the top performers from the [**Customer Experience** ](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/11g_qx-XbGTujAK04kS-rI8qtHLz6ZqrsFbWLis3knAE/edit?gid=420588568#gid=420588568)sheet, under the **Weekly Reporting** tab.
2. Also, if there are any 5-star troubleshooting rating comments from the previous week, add them here. These are found in the [**Troubleshooting Ratings** ](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1hMT5oOMmZjl0ennUWmW1AJ9lrzU811qX0R1z9MQ2C7g/edit?gid=0#gid=0)sheet, under the **Troubleshooting Data** tab. Look for 5-star ratings and positive feedback in the comments for the week being reported.

#### Step 3 - Slide 2: Top Opportunities

1. This slide includes a screenshot of our **WoW Trends – Top Strengths** and **WoW Trends – Opportunities** from the weekly audit reports. The screenshot is taken from the [**Customer Experience Review**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/11g_qx-XbGTujAK04kS-rI8qtHLz6ZqrsFbWLis3knAE/edit?gid=420588568#gid=420588568) sheet, **Weekly Reporting** tab, rows 22–26. Focus on highlighting what we did well and areas where we can improve. Use this slide to clearly advise associates on strengths to continue and specific opportunities to address for better performance.

#### Step 4 - Slide 3: Top Opportunities

This slide is dedicated to sharing practical tips and guidance on how to improve our main top opportunity from the previous week. For example, if the lowest score was related to “relaying the call recording reminder,” we will provide clear examples, best practices, and what associates should focus on to improve in that area. This slide allows creativity—feel free to include short text, graphics, or other engaging elements that help make the information easy to understand and memorable for associates.
