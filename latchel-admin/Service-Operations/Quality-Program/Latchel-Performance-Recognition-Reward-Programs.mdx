## Purpose

<Note>
  **Purpose of this document**

  At Latchel, we are committed to providing exceptional customer service to property managers, service providers, and residents. Our team members are the driving force behind this success. As a way to recognize and reward those who demonstrate sustained excellence in performance and service, this SOP outlines the structure, criteria, and eligibility for the “**QA Stars: Monthly recognition**” program.
</Note>

<Note>
  **Who should read this document?**

  This document is intended for **Service Operations Associates**, **Supervisors**, **Managers**, and the **Quality Team** involved in implementing, tracking, or participating in the “QA Stars” Program.
</Note>

## **QA Stars: Monthly Top Performers Recognition**

The **QA Stars** program recognizes associates who consistently exceed expectations in delivering exceptional customer service. Each month, top performers are rewarded for their outstanding contributions.

### **Tier 1: Top Shoutout/Customer Review**

**Requirements:**

- During the month, receive a shoutout from a resident, Service Provider, or Property Manager.
- The winner will be selected through an automatic raffle system.

**Rewards:**

- A \$25 USD bonus
- An early log-out, 2 hours before the end of the shift.

### **Tier 2: Silver Level**

**Requirements:**

- Achieve a global audit score of 97% or above for the month.

**Rewards:**

- A \$50 USD bonus
- An early log-out, 4 hrs before the end of the shift.

### **Tier 3: Gold Level**

**Requirements:**

- Achieve a global audit score of 100% for the month.

**Rewards:**

- A paid day off.
- A \$75 USD bonus

## **Eligibility Criteria**

To be eligible for any recognition or reward, the following must be met:

1. **Minimum QA Score:** Associates must have a global QA score of 97% or higher.
2. **Performance Review Completion:** Weekly self-assessment forms must be submitted. Failure to submit the required reviews will disqualify the participant from earning a star for the month.
3. **Perfect Attendance:** Associates must have no unapproved absences or late logins during the month. Any violations will disqualify the agent from star eligibility for that cycle.

## **Recognition & Announcements**

- **QA Stars Winners:** 
  - Monthly winners will be celebrated and announced during the **All Hands** meeting in the second week of the following month. They will also be recognized in the **#Service Operations** Slack channels.
  - **One associate** from each category will earn this reward. If there are multiple eligible associates in a category, a raffle system will be used to randomly select one winner.
- **PTO Usage:** The PTO earned by the winners will be subject to supervisor approval.
