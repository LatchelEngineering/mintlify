<Note>
This SOP provides structured and professional templates for auditors to communicate reassessment outcomes and feedback to associates. The templates are designed to ensure consistency, clarity, and constructive feedback while fostering transparency and professionalism.
</Note>

### Step by step guide for communicating the outcomes of reassessment requests to associates.

#### **Definitions**

- **Reassessment Request:** A formal request submitted by an associate to review an audit outcome.
- **Audit Type:** The specific category of audit being reviewed (e.g., call, SMS, email, etc.).
- **CX Calibration Guidelines:** Standards used to evaluate the quality of customer experience delivery.

#### **Process Flow**

1. **Review the reassessment request** thoroughly, referencing the relevant audit type and feedback.
1. **Determine the reassessment outcome** as either "Applicable" or "Not Applicable."
1. **Use the appropriate template** below to communicate the outcome.
1. **Customize the template** by inserting specific details (e.g., associate’s name, audit type, week).
1. **Provide detailed feedback** for the associate using the feedback template.
1. **Document the communication** for record-keeping purposes.

#### **Reassessment Notification Templates**

##### 1. Reassessment Outcome – Applicable

**Subject:** Updated Score – Reassessment Completed

_“Hi [Associate's Name],_

_Your reassessment request for **[Audit Type and Week]** has been reviewed. The updated score is now reflected in your _[_dashboard_](https://lookerstudio.google.com/reporting/f7b64bbc-6b72-4a82-af6c-fc4304e8c36c/page/p_hkpj6byg1c)_ under the **Reassessment Feedback** section._

_If you have further questions, please discuss them during your next 1:1 with your supervisor._

_Thank you,_<br/>_[Quality Team]”_

##### 2. Reassessment Outcome – Not Applicable

**Subject:** Reassessment Outcome – No Change

_“Hi [Associate's Name],_

_Your reassessment request for **[Audit Type and Week]** has been carefully reviewed. After thorough consideration, the original score remains unchanged. Detailed feedback can be found in the **Reassessment Feedback** section of your _[_dashboard_](https://lookerstudio.google.com/reporting/f7b64bbc-6b72-4a82-af6c-fc4304e8c36c/page/p_hkpj6byg1c)_._

_If you would like to discuss this further, please bring it up during your next 1:1 with your supervisor. For urgent matters, you may forward this message to your supervisor to request an ad-hoc meeting._

_Thank you,_<br/>_[Quality Team]”_

#### **Reassessment & Review Feedback Template**

**Introduction. **E.g.:

- _“Hi [Associate's Name],_

_Thank you for submitting your reassessment request and demonstrating your commitment to improving processes.”_

**Feedback**

- **Strengths:** E. g._"You demonstrated excellent paraphrasing skills and effectively validated the customer’s concerns."_
- **Areas for Improvement:** E.g._"In setting expectations, clarity was missing as you did not concisely explain who would handle the next steps. Since this was a non-emergency request, you should have clarified that it would be addressed by the PM."_

**Outcome**

- _"Your score has been adjusted accordingly..."_
- _"The original markdown remains valid..."_

**Closing Remarks**

- _“We appreciate your dedication to refining your performance. Your proactive approach reflects your commitment to delivering outstanding service._

_Best regards,_<br/>_[Quality Team]”_

#### **Style Tips for Auditors**

- Maintain a **neutral and constructive tone**.
- Use **precise language** to avoid ambiguity.
- Focus on **actionable feedback** that associates can use to improve.
- Ensure all templates are customized appropriately for the context and individual associate.
