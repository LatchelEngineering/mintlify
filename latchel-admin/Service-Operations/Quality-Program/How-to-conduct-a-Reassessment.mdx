## Content

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### How to update reassessment data

1. To identify the “Reassessment requests”, access to the [**Individual Results: Customer Experience Review (Responses)**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/13iotz2uwAImCSs49goI-0h1a20Bwfl9yZh5L0QhToJo/edit#gid=34806261) Google sheet in the “**Form Responses 1**” tab. Filter selecting only the previous and current “**Week reviewed**” (column B)Then filter the question “Optional: If you have any questions about the feedback you received in either CX Reviews or WO Audits…” (column E), to select only those responses with a request/ question (clear blanks). Once you have identified all the submissions, select “time stamp”, “name”, and “Questions about the feedback received…” (columns A, B, and E).Copy these data to the [**Disputes data**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/13iotz2uwAImCSs49goI-0h1a20Bwfl9yZh5L0QhToJo/edit#gid=756298559) tab to fill in the “Date”, “Agent”, and “Reassessment request” blanks (columns A, B, and G). Drag down the formulas to fill in “Supervisor”, “month”, “week”, and “year” (columns C: F).

#### How to conduct a reassessment

1. Select the request you will handle, read it carefully, and **identify the type of sample** to reassess; fill in the “sample type” blank.
1. **Access the Responses Google sheet**, according to the sample you will review (WO, call, SMS, or Email).
1. **Find the sample** to reassess in the response sheet. Considering the week of the request submission, most likely the audit should pertain to the week before.
1. Copy and paste the auditor’s name to the [Reassessments data](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/13iotz2uwAImCSs49goI-0h1a20Bwfl9yZh5L0QhToJo/edit#gid=756298559) tab “Auditor” column, then read the feedback provided and identify the markdowns. The auditor’s feedback should explain clearly if there was a markdown, which was, and why it was applicable. The link to the SOPs associated with that decision should be included, too.
1. Copy and paste the **markdowns** and the **previous score** from the responses sheet to the Reassessments data tab.
1. Review the sample and **validate the auditor’s conclusions**. **If the reassessment is applicable** for score updates:Mark as **Applicable** in the [Reassessment data](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/13iotz2uwAImCSs49goI-0h1a20Bwfl9yZh5L0QhToJo/edit#gid=756298559) tab those audits where all the markdowns are disputed will be disregarded and the score updated accordingly. Add notes to validate the resolution, explaining where the auditor failed and listing any applicable SOP that validates the reassessment.Mark as **Partial **those reassessments where the audit score was updated, but not all the markdowns were applicable. Add notes to validate the resolution, explaining where the auditor failed and listing any applicable SOP that validates the reassessment.To complete the applicable or partially applicable dispute, the markdowns must be updated in the corresponding response sheet ([WO audit](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1u7MLxMlhEoCmDqXW-MKBivhakBo8wU8ZBYiXH2CvnU0/edit#gid=1752332690), [Call](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/15em2mYJmciuOLHZDyZ-ukvLkVE3aPrMYsUgsFzc-oz0/edit?resourcekey#gid=1867553785), [SMS](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1vUxcCmutIZOFr1GccKSVF9Sx_eCjnJASmkfG8-0j9ZI/edit#gid=872676639), [Email](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1mWE0GQgmk400STrZ6FiFZDjmcTFzXy4xSVTG70saCFM/edit#gid=2132911568)). This will automatically adjust the score, and the new result should be added to the “Updated score” column in the [Reassessment tab](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/13iotz2uwAImCSs49goI-0h1a20Bwfl9yZh5L0QhToJo/edit#gid=756298559).**If the reassessment is not applicable** for score updates:Mark as Not-Applicable those reassessments where none of the markdowns disputed were applicable, and the score will remain the same. Add notes to validate the resolution, explain how the associate failed at the guidelines, provide improvement suggestions (adding detail to the auditor’s feedback), and list any applicable SOP that validates the reassessment.
