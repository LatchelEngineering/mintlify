## Purpose

<Note>
  **Purpose of this document**

  This document outlines the process for generating two weekly quality reports based on Work Order audit data:

  - The **Troubleshooting Report** focused on analyzing troubleshooting quality across associates.
  - The **Harmonia Report**, centered on identifying Intake process improvement opportunities and Product feedback.

  It includes guidance on how to review audit results, identify trends and opportunities, flag performance concerns, verify QA auditor compliance, and publish insights in the #quality-programs and Harmonia-beta Slack channels. This process supports transparency, accountability, and continuous improvement across both service lines.
</Note>

<Note>
  **Who should read this document?**

  Quality associates & Managers
</Note>

## Background and Deviation Guidelines

<Note>
  **What is this process trying to solve?**

  **Troubleshooting Report**<br />This report helps surface patterns in how effectively associates troubleshoot maintenance issues. It identifies strengths, recurring knowledge gaps, and associates who may need further coaching. It also promotes consistency in handling issues before dispatch and ensures that feedback loops drive improvements in training and support.

  **Harmonia WO report**<br />This section of the process is designed to track the quality and effectiveness of the Work Order (WO) Intake process. It identifies missed opportunities to collect key information that may impact the accuracy of the WO severity classification. The report provides the Product Team with detailed feedback to enhance the Harmonia Intake process and strengthen the overall customer experience.
</Note>

<Warning>
  **When to break this SOP?**

  This SOP may be adjusted or paused if:

  - Work Order auditing is paused or significantly restructured.
  - A new reporting framework is introduced that replaces or consolidates these reports.
  - Weekly audit results are unavailable due to system downtime or auditing team reassignment.

  In such cases, the deviation should be documented and communicated in the #quality-programs Slack channel, along with any temporary workarounds or next steps.
</Warning>

## Process Overview

This guide outlines the steps to generate and share two weekly reports based on Work Order audit data.

### Step-by-Step guide

#### Troubleshooting Audit Weekly Report

1. Open the [WO Audit](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1pDBRevftU7iNqV7Syc4mb5AeGG8lMmwcjxic88TwI-A/edit?gid=0#gid=0) spreadsheet.
2. Update the tables in the “Weekly Reporting” tab ([video guide](https://www.loom.com/share/dea7357e54c844e8bdb62c68ef13b7b5?sid=c809d330-33be-47bf-9385-bfd538518f62)).
3. **Open** the [QA Reports doc 2026](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1U6CE9mEryRg1cadMMytHixLNNhsaNrsQ6OlmigHZ7yE/edit?tab=t.0) .<br />3.1. Locate the **WO Audit Report** section.<br />3.2. Copy and paste the report from the previous week as a template.<br />3.3. Update the data using the new week’s results from the **Weekly Reporting** tab of the Work Order sheet.
4. **Publish** the updated report in the [**#quality-programs**](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C022ZQQD056) Slack channel.<br />4.1. Include **screenshots of each updated table** referenced in the report ([example](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C022ZQQD056/p1753725659699029)).

#### Harmonia WO Weekly Report

1. **Open** the [**WO 1&2 – Responses**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1u7MLxMlhEoCmDqXW-MKBivhakBo8wU8ZBYiXH2CvnU0/edit?gid=431814009#gid=431814009) spreadsheet.
2. **Review** the data in the **“Form Responses 1”** tab.Focus on the **pink section** (Columns **AI–AJ** for Harmonia intake feedback).Review the cases when the Work Order remained Emergency and those when it was de-escalated
3. **Open** the [**QA Reports Doc 2026**](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1U6CE9mEryRg1cadMMytHixLNNhsaNrsQ6OlmigHZ7yE/edit?tab=t.0).<br />3.1. Copy and paste last week’s report as a template.<br />3.2. Update the report using the current week’s data. For outstanding product feedback, if similar cases appear across different WOs, consolidate them into one main topic and include a brief summary description.
4. **Publish** the report in the **#Harmonia-beta** Slack channel.
