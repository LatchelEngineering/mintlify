## Purpose

<Note>
  The purpose of this document is to align Customer Experience reviewers on how to assess the Operations Team’s interactions with our customers. This document also serves as a reference for Ops Associates & Ops Troubleshooters to understand Latchel’s expectations and provide an excellent customer experience.
</Note>

##  **Grading**

The score depends on the number of questions we're evaluating for each form as seen above. Each question on the review will share the same value, depending on the number of total questions being evaluated.

Example: The outbound call form has 14 questions. Therefore:

100 / 14 = 7,14

This means that for outbound calls, each question would be worth 7,14 points. As a consequence, each markdown would represent a minus 7,14 on the score.

Please note that SMS, WebChat, Email, and Call scores represent an average of all the Customer Experience Reviews received in that category.
