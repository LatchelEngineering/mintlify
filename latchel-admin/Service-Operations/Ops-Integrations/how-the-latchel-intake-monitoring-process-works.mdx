title: "How: the Latchel Intake monitoring process works"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to detail the end-to-end automated process that detects, classifies, alerts, and helps resolve low Latchel Intake adoption by PMs, using automated emails and Slack notifications.
</Note>

<Tip>
A script monitors the [**Work Order Submission Monitoring**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1tUzHMzy3eJUZg_5orOYPnj4CNPtwgiMW89YPbt4Ccd4/edit?gid=111199548#gid=111199548) tab. **<u>When a PM reaches 15, 30, or 45 consecutive days below the 90% adoption target</u>**, the script creates an entry in the [**PM Risk Alert Hub **](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1tUzHMzy3eJUZg_5orOYPnj4CNPtwgiMW89YPbt4Ccd4/edit?gid=555935922#gid=555935922)tab and triggers email and Slack notifications to the appropriate stakeholders.
</Tip>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Issue Detection

a. Whenever a problem is identified in the **Work Order Submission Monitoring** tab, a matching row is created in the **PM Risk Alert Hub** tab.

b. Columns **A – G** are automatically filled with the data pulled from **Work Order Submission Monitoring.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Intg-109.png" alt="image-20250629-202019.png" />
</Frame>

c. For each issue, an email guide is sent to the PM and their team—except for **“15 days below target,”** which is sent to the Primary member only.

d. Once the email is sent, **columns H and I** are updated automatically.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Intg-110.png" alt="image-20250629-202358.png" />
</Frame>

e. Slack workflows are triggered for stakeholders’ review after the email is sent to the Property Manager. **Columns J–L** are updated automatically.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Intg-111.png" alt="image-20250629-202644.png" />
</Frame>

### **Step 2: Notification Logic**

a. Depending on the number of consecutive days a PM is below the 90% adoption target, the following actions will be triggered.

**<u>The issues currently flagged are:</u>**

| **Milestone** **(Days Below Target)** | **Email Recipients** | **Slack Notification** | **Slack Channel** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **15 days** | Primary email only | CSM (⚠️PM Risk Alert) | [#customer-success](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/CLB7U1KRC) |
| **30 days** | Primary + Other member contacts | CSM (⚠️PM Risk Alert) | [#customer-success](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/CLB7U1KRC) |
| **45 days** | Primary + Other member contacts | CSM (🚨PM Risk: Action Required) | [#customer-success](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/CLB7U1KRC) |

<Note>
_The template email includes hyperlinks to job-aid guides for rerouting requests to Latchel and will be sent from _[_success@latchel.com_](mailto:success@latchel.com)_. If a PM replies, the PS team should escalate the case to the CSM immediately._
</Note>

Below are the templates that will be sent to the PM and the relevant stakeholders:

_**Body:**_

Just wanted to let you know about your account. We're seeing quite a few work orders continue to be created through Appfolio. To keep things running smoothly and avoid delays, it's best if residents submit their requests directly through Latchel.

Here are a few quick guides to help you update your settings:
