---
title: "How to Grant Report Permissions to Latchel in AppFolio"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide for AppFolio President/ Admin Role on how to grant the necessary report access permissions to the Latchel team. Completing these steps is crucial to ensure correct data synchronization and the proper functioning of the integration.
</Note>

<Tip>
Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

- Access to AppFolio with a _President/Admin_ role.
- Knowledge of the role name assigned to the **Latchel team** (**Example: **_Onsite Manager_).
</Tip>

## Step by step guide

### Step 1: Access General Settings

a. **Log in to AppFolio** with your **President/admin account**.

b. In the top-right corner, click on your profile name.

c. From the drop-down menu, **select General Settings**.

### **Step 2: Navigate to Role Management**

a. Within **General Settings**, scroll down to the **Users** section.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Intg-80.png" alt="image-20251023-193339.png" />
</Frame>

b. Click on the **Manage Roles** option.

### **Step 3: Select the Latchel Role**

a. In the list of roles, find and click on the role that has been assigned to the Latchel team (e.g., **Onsite Manager**).
b. This will open the access permissions window for that role (`Onsite Manager Access Permissions`).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Intg-81.png" alt="image-20251023-193619.png" />
</Frame>

### **Step 4: Configure Report Permissions**

a. In the permissions window, scroll down to the **Reporting** section and click to expand it.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Intg-82.png" alt="image-20251023-193858.png" />
</Frame>

b. For each category listed below, expand the section and **set the permission to Full Access** for the corresponding reports:

- **Maintenance Reports** → _Work Order_
- **Property and Unit Reports** →_ Unit Directory, Property Directory, Property Group Directory, Rent Roll (Itemized)_
- **Tenant Reports **→ _Tenant Directory_
- **Owner Reports** → _Owner Statement, Owner Statement (Enhanced)_
- **Accounting Reports **→ _General Ledger_

<Tip>
**<u>To ensure</u>**<u> the</u>**<u> daily data sync runs</u>**<u> and </u>**<u>the financial ledgering</u>**<u> information are error-free</u>, the specific reports above must be enabled. **<u>The General Ledger report and the Rent Roll (itemized) aren’t needed for sync purposes but are used for financial ledgering.</u>**
</Tip>

<Note>
If the PM does not grant access to owner reports, this is acceptable for the time being, as these reports are being gradually enabled by the engineering team.
</Note>

<Warning>
**IMPORTANT NOTE:** Ensure you locate each report on this list and successfully change its permission to Full Access. <u>If a report cannot be found or the permission cannot be changed, </u>**<u>please contact Ops Integration Team</u>**.
</Warning>

### Step 5: Review and Save Changes

a. Once you have assigned all the necessary permissions, scroll to the top of the window.

b. **Click** the **Review Changes** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Intg-83.png" alt="image-20251023-194348.png" />
</Frame>

c. Verify that the changes are correct, then **click Save** to apply the updates.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Intg-84.png" alt="image-20251023-194424.png" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
**IMPORTANT: **For each section of the reports selected you need to repeat again the step 5
</Warning>
