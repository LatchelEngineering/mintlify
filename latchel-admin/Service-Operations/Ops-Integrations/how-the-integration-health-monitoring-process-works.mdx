<Note>
  The purpose of this SOP is to detail the end-to-end automated process that detects, classifies, alerts, and resolves PM integration issues. This guide explains the automated script, Slack notifications, and email templates that operate on the data in the **Integration Issue PMs** tab.
</Note>

<Tip>
  A Script monitors the **PM Metrics tab, displays the issues detected in the[<u> Integration Issue PMs </u>](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1lqN_yI3vZUehb9BZylCvXK0KU-PFfpJCkDvw1_sgWug/edit?gid=2021689109#gid=2021689109)tab,** and sends email guides to the PM team and real-time alerts to the appropriate stakeholders team in Slack when a new issue is detected. **Currently, only PMs using AppFolio software are flagged when an issue is detected.**
</Tip>

## Step by step

### \*\* Step 1: Issue Detection\*\*

a. Whenever a problem is identified in the **PM Metrics** tab, a matching row is created in **Integration Issue PMs**.

b. Columns **A – F** are automatically filled with the data pulled from **PM Metrics**.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250512-225234.png](/images/Intg-23.png)
</Frame>

c. For each issue, an email guide is sent to the PM and their team—except for **“Last import date missing,”** which the CPT investigates first to determine how best to help the PM.

d. Once the email is sent, **columns G–I** are updated automatically.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250512-230209.png](/images/Intg-24.png)
</Frame>

e. Depending on the issue, Slack workflows are triggered either immediately or after a few days for stakeholders’ review. **Columns J–L** are updated automatically.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250512-230408.png](/images/Intg-25.png)
</Frame>

### Step 2: The issues currently flagged are:

| **Issue Type** | **Email Sent To** | **Slack Notification** | **Slack channel** | **Workflow Owner** | **Next Step if Unresolved** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Integration not running - 4 days | PM **Primary Member** (step by step guide included) | CPT is alerted to monitor | [<u>#cpt-updates</u>](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C04TZ3Y8ZLK) | CPT | Escalates to “7 days” state |
| Integration not running - 7 days | PM **Primary \+ Additional Members** (step by step guide included) | CPT continues monitoring | [<u>#cpt-updates</u>](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C04TZ3Y8ZLK) | CPT | Escalates to “\> 7 days” state |
| Integration not running - \+7 days | PM **Primary \+ Additional Members** (step by step guide included) | PS team is alerted to intervene | [<u>#ps-escalation</u>](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C08475CCBGR) | PS Team | PS provides hands-on assistance |
| Missing import date | — (no email) | CPT investigates root cause | [<u>#cpt-updates</u>](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C04TZ3Y8ZLK) | CPT | CPT contacts PM once cause is found |
| Outdated AppFolio version | PM **Primary \+ Additional Members** (step by step guide included) | If unresolved after **5 days**, PS team is alerted | [<u>#ps-escalation</u>](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C08475CCBGR) | PS Team | PS supports PM until version is updated |

<u>Depending on the issue type, a step-by-step email template and a Slack workflow will be triggered. </u>Below are the templates that will be sent to the PM and the relevant stakeholders.
