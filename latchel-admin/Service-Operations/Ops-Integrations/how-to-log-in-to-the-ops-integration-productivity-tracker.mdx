title: "How to: Log in to the Ops Integration Productivity Tracker"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to provide team members with a clear, step-by-step guide on how to properly use the [**Ops Integration Productivity Tracker**](https://productivity-tracker-bj1s.onrender.com/). This step-by-step is designed to standardize the time-logging process, ensuring that all activities are accurately recorded with the correct Task and Category, thereby maintaining the integrity of our team's productivity data.
</Note>

## Step by step

### **Step 1: Login**

a. Navigate to the Productivity Tracker website: [https://productivity-tracker-bj1s.onrender.com/tracker.html](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fproductivity-tracker-bj1s.onrender.com%2Ftracker.html)

b. Enter your assigned **Username** and **Password** into the respective fields.

c. Click the **Login** button to access your dashboard.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Intg-5.png" alt="image-20251104-233951.png" />
</Frame>

### **Step 2: Identify the Correct Task and Category**

a. Before you clock in, you must identify the correct Task and Category for the activity you are about to begin.

1. Open the [**Agent Productivity Tracker - Ops Integration Team**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1dnt8JCPxOkx4D1KQZVGksPdclyWXtv6QQlRueWkSw4M/edit?gid=1143660504#gid=1143660504) Spreadsheet.
2. Navigate to the tab with your name followed by "Tasks" (e.g., **"Diana Tasks"**, **"Martha Tasks"**).
3. This sheet is divided into two sections:
  - **Regular Tasks:** For daily, recurring activities like breaks, daily checks, etc.
  - **Projects:** For specific, assigned projects.
4. Locate the activity you are about to start and find its corresponding **Task** and **Category**. You will use these exact names in the tracker.

### **Step 3: Select Task and Category to Clock In**

a. Return to the **Productivity Tracker dashboard**.

b. Using the dropdown menus, select the exact **Task** and **Category** you identified in the previous step.

c. Click the green **"Clock In"** button.

d. A confirmation timestamp will appear below the selection boxes, showing "Clocked in at: [date/time]".

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Intg-6.png" alt="image-20251104-234249.png" />
</Frame>

e. If the task or category has not been added to your lists, **<u>please report it to Trisha.</u>**

### **Step 4: Monitor Your Session**

a. Once you have clocked in, you can monitor your activity in the following sections:

- **Current Session:** This box shows a real-time breakdown of your currently active task, including the start time.
- **Today Overview:** This box provides a summary of all tasks you have worked on throughout the day and the total time spent on each.

<Warning>
**Always** make sure your clock-in and clock-out times are recorded in your **Agent** tab in the [**Agent Productivity Tracker - Ops Integration Team**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1dnt8JCPxOkx4D1KQZVGksPdclyWXtv6QQlRueWkSw4M/edit?gid=1143660504#gid=1143660504). **<u>Don’t rely only on the web app, since it can fail. If that happens, report it to Trisha. </u>**
</Warning>

<Tip>
If you encounter any issues or need to submit a time correction, please follow this job aid: [How to: Submit a Time Correction Request](https://help.latchel.com/how-to-submit-a-time-correction-request)
</Tip>

### **Step 5: Clock Out**

a. When you have completed the task or are switching to a new one, you must clock out.

1. Click the red **"Clock Out"** button at the top right of the dashboard.
2. This action stops the timer for the current task. The logged data (Task, Agent, Clock in, Clock out, Overall time) is automatically captured and saved as a new row in your personal tab (e.g., **"Diana"**) in the [**Agent Productivity Tracker - Ops Integration Team**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1dnt8JCPxOkx4D1KQZVGksPdclyWXtv6QQlRueWkSw4M/edit?gid=1143660504#gid=1143660504) Spreadsheet.

<Warning>
### ** Important Notes:**

- **Session Persistence:** If you close your browser tab or shut down your computer without clicking "Clock Out," the timer will **continue to run**. You must explicitly click "Clock Out" to end a time entry.
- **Switching Tasks:** To switch from one task to another, you must first **Clock Out** of the current task and then **Clock In** to the new one.
- **Display Mode:** You can switch between light and dark themes using the **"Dark Mode"** toggle at the top of the dashboard.
</Warning>

<Warning>
If the web application is left idle for an extended period, it may enter sleep mode. Relaunching the application can take a few minutes while the system resumes. When the login page appears, you will be able to sign in again. **<u>If this occurs, please wait for the login page to load.</u>**
</Warning>
