<Note>
  The purpose of this article is to provide a guide on how an Appfolio Property Manager can resend the account activation email that will give Latchel access to their software, when the email was not received or the link expired.
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Ask the PM to login in their PMS

<Tip>
  Make sure the PM is logged in with an Admin- or President-level account.
</Tip>

a. Instruct the Property Manager to click their name at the top right of any page, then select **General Settings.**

<Frame>
  ![000.png](/images/Intg-85.png)
</Frame>

b. Next, under \*\*Users, **they need to** **click on** Manage Users. \*\*

<Frame>
  ![111.png](/images/Intg-86.png)
</Frame>

c. Lastly, to a user’s email, click the **Actions icon,** then select the option to <u>re-invite/resend the login link.</u>

<Frame>
  ![2.png](/images/Intg-87.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
  Please always confirm that the email user they are using is <u>success\+PM’s company name@latchel.com</u>
</Warning>
