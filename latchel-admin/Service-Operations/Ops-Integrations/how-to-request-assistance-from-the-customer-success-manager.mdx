<Note>
  The purpose of this job aid is to provide guidance on how to request assistance from the CSM when the CPT needs to confirm information about an active Property Manager in the [Integration Health Metrics Dashboard.](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1lqN_yI3vZUehb9BZylCvXK0KU-PFfpJCkDvw1_sgWug/edit?gid=186001220#gid=186001220)
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Go to the corresponding Slack channel

a. Go to the [**#integration-notification-feedback**](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C08TP7NL76W) channel in Slack and select the “**Request for CS Help”** workflow.

<Frame>
  ![inte2.jpg](/images/Intg-26.jpg)
</Frame>

### Step 2: Fill in the workflow with the required information.

<Note>
  \*\*a) Company Name: \*\*PM Company

  \*\*b) CSM: \*\*Assigned CSM (found in column H of the PM Metrics tab)

  \*\*c) Software Type: \*\*Select the correct PMS—AppFolio, Buildium, Rent Manager, RentVine, Propertyware, Other, etc.

  \*\*d) Assistance Required: \*\*Describe the issue for which you need help.

  \*\*e) Due Date: \*\*If you need a resolution by a specific date, please select it.

  \*\*f) Requested By: \*\*Select your Slack username.
</Note>

### Step 3: Once you have filled in the workflow with all the required information, click **Submit**.

<Frame>
  ![Request.jpg](/images/Intg-27.jpg)
</Frame>

a. The workflow will be posted in the channel. You can follow up on any updates in the thread of that post.
