<Note>
  The purpose of this job aid is to provide a step-by-step on how to escalate to the CSM in the appropriate Slack channel for an **AppFolio** customer who has reached **10 days below target** in the [**Latchel Intake Monitoring Dashboard**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1tUzHMzy3eJUZg_5orOYPnj4CNPtwgiMW89YPbt4Ccd4/edit?gid=111199548#gid=111199548) under the _Work Order Submission Monitoring_ tab. Then interpret the Customer Success Manager’s (CSM) response and take the correct actions to ensure proper follow-up (through the automated notifications of the “[How the Latchel Intake Monitoring Process Works](https://help.latchel.com/how-the-latchel-intake-monitoring-process-works)” SOP) or exempt the escalated PM from outreach.
</Note>

## Step by step

### **Step 1: Identify the Initial Alert in the Work Order Submission Monitoring tab**

a. In the _Work Order Submission Monitoring_ tab, check for any AppFolio customers who have reached 10 days below target.

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-222410.png](/images/Intg-112.png)
</Frame>

### Step 2: Go to the Customer success Channel to escalate it to the corresponding CSM.

a. Open the [**#customer-success**](#customer-success) channel and go to **Workflows → PM Risk – Below Target Alert**. _(Do not click **Start workflow**.)_ b. Once open, click the **three dots** on the right (**More actions**) → **Edit workflow**.

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-223546.png](/images/Intg-113.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-223622.png](/images/Intg-114.png)
</Frame>

c. Customize the alert notification by clicking **Edit** in the **Message** section.

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-223853.png](/images/Intg-115.png)
</Frame>

d. Tag the CSM name and add the PM company name, then click **Save**.

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-224053.png](/images/Intg-116.png)
</Frame>

e. After saving the message, click **Publish changes**.

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-224802.png](/images/Intg-117.png)
</Frame>

f. A confirmation message will appear. Close the notification after it displays.

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-224948.png](/images/Intg-118.png)
</Frame>

g. Go back to the [#customer-success](#customer-success) channel, open **Workflows**, and click _Start workflow_ for **PM Risk – Below Target Alert**.

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-225900.png](/images/Intg-119.png)
</Frame>

h. The notification will be sent successfully, tagging the CSM.

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-230010.png](/images/Intg-120.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  After each notification is sent, repeat **Step 2 (d–f)** to restore the message to the default text:

  Hi \[CSM Name\], **\[PM Company Name\]** has been **below the 90% target** for Latchel work order submissions **for the past 10 days**. Could you please confirm whether we should continue outreach if they reach 15 days below target? Thank you!
</Tip>

### **Step 3: Monitor and Interpret the CSM's Response**

Your next action depends on the CSM's response (or lack thereof).

a. **If the CSM clicks "Yes":** A confirmation will be posted in the same notification. **No immediate action is needed from you.** The PM will continue to be monitored and will be automatically contacted if they reach the 15-day mark following the [How: the Latchel Intake monitoring process works](https://help.latchel.com/how-the-latchel-intake-monitoring-process-works) job aid.

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-230222.png](/images/Intg-121.png)
</Frame>

b. **If the CSM requests an exemption:** The CSM will reply in the thread asking for the PM to be exempt.

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-230456.png](/images/Intg-122.png)
</Frame>

If this is the case, go to the [Latchel Intake Monitoring Dashboard](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1tUzHMzy3eJUZg_5orOYPnj4CNPtwgiMW89YPbt4Ccd4/edit?gid=555935922#gid=555935922) and select the hidden \_“PM Exclusions” \_tab. Add the requested PM. Once the PM is on that list, the process stops for this customer.

<Frame>
  ![image-20251001-230732.png](/images/Intg-123.png)
</Frame>

c. **If there is no response and the PM reaches 14 days:** One day before the 15-day mark, you must send a manual reminder. Go to the original alert's thread. Post the following template:

\_Hello \[CSM Name\]! \_

_\[PM Company Name\] is **14 days below target**. We’d like to confirm whether we should proceed tomorrow with sending the notification to the PM. Please let us know—thanks._

### **Step 4: Confirm Final Outcome at the 15-Day Mark**

The process concludes in one of two ways:

a. **If the PM was exempted:** The PM will be added to the \_“PM Exclusions” \_tab and won’t be contacted or flagged in the _PM Risk Alert Hub_. b. **If outreach was confirmed (or there was no response):** The system will proceed with the notification to the PM, and the client will be flagged in the _PM Risk Alert Hub_ for future tracking.

<Note>
  For more information on how the Automated Alert Notification process works, see the following job aid: [How: the Latchel Intake monitoring process works](https://help.latchel.com/how-the-latchel-intake-monitoring-process-works)
</Note>
