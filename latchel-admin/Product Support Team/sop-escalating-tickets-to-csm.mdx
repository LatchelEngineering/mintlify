# SOP: Escalating Tickets to the CSM

Overview
Before escalating an Ops Escalation to the CSM, itʼs essential to understand the Ops
Escalation Summary fully. This involves reaching out to the customer to:
Reviewing the full transcript between Ops and the CSM.
Gather additional details.
Ask clarifying questions.
Please only proceed with escalation if the ticket confirms that it aligns with the established
criteria in this resource.
After reviewing your ASM Ticket, and determine the issue should be escalated to the CSM
according to Product Support Team vs Customer Success Managers, these will be your next
steps to follow:

Issue Summary:
Avoid copying and pasting the PMʼs message verbatim.
Provide a detailed summary of the issue, including:
Context of the conversation.
Troubleshooting steps already performed.
Actions taken thus far.
Any related Slack links or Assembly notes.
Next Steps:
Clearly explain the reason for escalation.
Provide suggestions/resolution and a call to action (e.g., Does the PM appear frustrated?
Did they ask for a refund? Should the CSM reset expectations?).
