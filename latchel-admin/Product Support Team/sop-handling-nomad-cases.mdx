# SOP: Handling Nomad Cases

Latchel is proud to partner with RentRedi and Nomad Property Management to offer
dedicated support and services specifically tailored for their property owners. These are
the only two partnerships that exist today (Oct 2024) for direct owner support.
Review How to: Handle DIY Owner Contacts for approved actions and insight into actions
Latchel Ops Agents can take for RentRedi Owners.
Inquiries from Nomad Owners are managed differently than those from RentRedi Owners.
PS should only communicate directly with Nomad Owners if instructed by Nomad Support.
