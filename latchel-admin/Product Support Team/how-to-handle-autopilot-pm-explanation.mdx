# How to Handle: Autopilot PM Explanation

Latchel Ops agents communicate consistently with Property Managers (PM) to coordinate
work orders, provide updates, and outline the next steps. Our primary communication tool is
the Support Webchat.
When a Property Manager is on our Autopilot product and requests a Latchel Ops Agent to
perform an action or update a work order, the Ops agent should do the following:
1. If the Property Manager (PM) reports an emergency or urgent issue:
a. Take immediate action.
b. Do NOT provide a help guide.
2. If the PM says they are blocked or have a limitation:
Assist them directly—do not question their limitation or send a help guide.
Take the necessary action on their behalf and document the action and reason.
3. If the PM is simply unsure how to proceed (no blocker, not an emergency):
First, provide a relevant help guide and offer to walk them through the steps.
If there is no help guide available, take action for them and submit a request to create a
new help article.

If the Property Manager encounters any issues with self-service tools or grows frustrated on
their service expectations, the Ops agents should escalate the situation to Product Support,
which will create a ASM Ticket for resolution.
ASM Ticket Step-by-step Guide:
1. Review the ticket details and the escalation summary from Ops.
2. Navigate to the Latchel profile of the PM User in the escalation.
3. Review the chat transcript/call recording to fully understand the:
a. Context
b. Tone of the conversation (e.g., frustration, confusion, satisfaction, etc.)
c. What action the PM was asking Ops to take, including the Help Guide Ops shared with them.
If there are concerns about how our Ops Agent handled the conversation or if they failed to
share the most relevant Help Guide, please submit the improvement opportunity through
the Agent Feedback workflow to #svc-ops-feedback.
4. Send the Autopilot Explanation template to the PM user email.
5. Once the template has been inserted into the Ticket draft, fill out CONTEXT area with the
relevant information based on the webchat conversation.
6. Send the email.
If the PM pushes back on this response and becomes irate/frustrated, PS should escalate
ASM to the CSM to handle from there.
