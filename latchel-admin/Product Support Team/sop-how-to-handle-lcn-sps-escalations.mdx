# SOP: How to Handle LCN SPs Escalations

Latchel Contractor Network (LCN) is a network of service providers Property Managers can
decide to opt into as a backup to their preferred vendors:
Emergency Workflow: You may opt-in to have Latchel source new service providers if
nobody from your preferred network is available to accept the job. Our team may source a
new provider on the spot or source someone that we have previously worked with in your
area.
Non-Emergency Workflow: Get access to Latchel's service provider network when your
preferred providers are not available to complete your non-emergency jobs. If you select to
opt-in to have Latchel source new service providers for an emergency job, youʼll instantly gain
access to a directory of service providers Latchel has previously worked with in your area.
