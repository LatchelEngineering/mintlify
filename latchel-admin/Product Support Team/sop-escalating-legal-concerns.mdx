Escalation Scenarios against a Property Manager:
Resident: Instances where the resident reports they are going to sue their PM, report them to
the Better Business Bureau (BBB), threatening legal action, etc.
Service Provider: Instances where a service provider threatens legal action against a PM,
putting a lien on a PM property, report them to the Better Business Bureau (BBB), etc.
Step-by-Step Guide:
Access the resident/service provider profile in Latchel.
1.
Search for the profile in Latchel by using their email address.
Ensure that the “Disabled” option is checked when performing the search to include
deactivated accounts.
Once on the profile, identify the associated Property Management (PM) Company.
2.
For Residents:
Once on the Resident Profile, you will automatically be on the Details tab.
Click on the Property listed below the Details tab.
On the Property page, scroll down to the Details tab.
Locate the Property Manager section to find the PM Company associated with the
owner.
For Service Providers:
Once on the SP Profile, you will automatically be on the Details tab.
Click on the “Preferred PMs tab”.
Find the PM Company in question and click on the name to go to the PM Company page.
Find a PM Contact Email.
3.
Identify the Primary User for the PM Company from the Property Manager information.
Review the User Groups section for the Default User Group.
Select the most appropriate email for reaching out to the PM, typically from the Primary
User or Default User Group
Contact the PM Regarding the Legal Escalation.
4.
Use the Escalate to PM - Legal Concern in Salesforce to craft your email to the PM.
Send the email to the selected PM contact to address the escalation.
