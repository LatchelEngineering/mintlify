## **Bulk Update vs Small Update:**

**Bulk Update:**A Bulk Update is any update task that requires more than 15 minutes for a Property Manager (PM) and/or Product Support (PS) to fully complete.

**Small Update:** A Small Update is any task that can be completed by the PM or PS in 15 minutes or less.

## **Bulk Update Step-by-Step Guide:**

<Note>
We have a process for recurring updates for PMs: Excalibur Homes, GC Realty, DS Huber, Cal Property Management and Up&Up. 

Follow the process outlined [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/3222437894/Steps+to+Submit+a+Data+Support+Ticket#Schedule-of-Data-Support-Request-for-Customers-with-Automated-Reports](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/3222437894/Steps+to+Submit+a+Data+Support+Ticket#Schedule-of-Data-Support-Request-for-Customers-with-Automated-Reports) for these PM Companies.
</Note>

1. **Receive and Vet the Request:** Upon receiving an account update request from a PM, thoroughly review the request.

  1. Ask clarifying questions to understand the specifics.
  2. Confirm whether the update pertains to the entire account, a particular portfolio, or a specific property.
  3. Determine if the update concerns emergencies, non-emergencies, or both.

2. **Communicate with PM:**Once all necessary details are gathered, send the [Data Task Bulk Update SF Template](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/EmailTemplate/00XUS00000BFSbx2AH/view?queryScope=userFolders) to the PM user.
3. **Submit to Data Support**: Forward the collected information to Data Support. Follow the submission process outlined here: [<u>Data Support Ticket Submission Steps</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/PHONE_NUMBER_001/Steps+to+Submit+a+Data+Support+Ticket#How-to-Submit-a-Request).
4. **Update Case Thread:**Copy the Slack URL from your submission and paste it into the case thread.

  1. Resource: [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/3198353472/SOP+Latchel+Department+Escalation+Process+for+Product+Support+Cases#6.-Special-Escalated-Cases](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/3198353472/SOP+Latchel+Department+Escalation+Process+for+Product+Support+Cases#6.-Special-Escalated-Cases)

<Warning>
****More Details Needed:*** In the event that the Data Team is missing information and needs more details from the PM, they will reach out to PS via the Slack Thread from the submission.

1. PS is to email the PM via the Case and request the needed additional details.
2. If no response, PS is to follow the [Follow Up Procedure](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/3198812172/SOP+Managing+Unresponsive+Property+Managers+in+Case+Management#2.-Follow-Up-Procedure) 2 times.
3. In the event the PM never responds, escalate the case to PSM using the PSM Case Escalation workflow in #ps-case-review.
4. PSM will give the PM a call for one final attempt at gathering the details.
5. If still no response/answer, PSM will move the case to Closed Unresponsive and notify the Data Team.
</Warning>

6. **Move Case to Closed:**The template with the CSAT Survey will send **24 hours** after the case is moved into the Closed state.

## **Small Update Step-by-Step Guide:**

1. **Receive and Vet the Request**: Upon receiving an account update request from a PM, thoroughly review the request.

  - Ask clarifying questions to gather detailed information.
  - Confirm whether the update is for the full account, a specific portfolio, or a property.
  - Determine whether it involves emergencies, non-emergencies, or both.h.

2. **Provide Help Guides**: After gathering all details, send the relevant Help Guides and instructions for the PM to complete the updates themselves. Refer to: [<u>Help Guides</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/DIBNvQ).
3. **Handle Pushback**: If the PM resists performing the updates, Product Support should proceed to complete the updates.
4. **Inform the PM**: Once updates are completed, notify the PM.
