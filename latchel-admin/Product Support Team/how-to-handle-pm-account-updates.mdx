# How to Handle: PM Account Updates

In order to best service and update a PMs Account Settings, it is vital for PS to review the
accounts setup and understand Configuration Inheritance (Property, Portfolio, & Company
Level).

Bulk Update vs Small Update:
Bulk Update: A Bulk Update is any update task that requires more than 15 minutes for a
Property Manager (PM) and/or Product Support (PS) to fully complete.
Small Update: A Small Update is any task that can be completed by the PM or PS in 15 minutes
or less.
