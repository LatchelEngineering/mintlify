# Job Aid: Interaction Tags Explained

Tags related to CPT Churn Analysis are highlighted in blue.
New Tags are highlighted in yellow.
Tag Description
Integration Set Up Refers to the tasks involved in either initiating or
modifying the integration framework within a client's
Latchel account. This involves configuring account
slugs for synchronization purposes or establishing
integrations for clients that are already on board.
Ops Escalation Used for the original Ops Escalation ticket that is sent to
PS.
Poor Experience (LVN SP) Complaints of poor experience with LVN SP providers
causing PM frustration and/or disabling LVN all together.
WOs Not Syncing Correctly Refers to synchronization errors or inconsistencies
(Integration) specifically affecting Work Orders.
Tenants Not Syncing Correctly Refers to synchronization errors or inconsistencies
(Integration) specifically affecting the tenant's information.
Properties Not Syncing Correctly Refers to synchronization errors or inconsistencies
(Integration) specifically affecting property data.
Owners Not Syncing Correctly Concerns synchronization errors or inconsistencies
(Integration) specifically affecting the owner's information.
Outdated Sync Refers to instances where the customer synchronization
process has not been executed for an extended period,
resulting in outdated data.
Delayed Sync When customers report that the synchronization
process is taking longer than expected to complete.
Outdated Plugin Version (Appfolio) Relates to issues resulting from the use of an outdated
plugin version on the customer's end.
Bug - Integration Issue Concerns about synchronization issues arising from an
unidentified or undetermined root cause.
Feature Request - Integrations Applies to cases where the customer proposes or
requests enhancements to the data synchronization
process.
Pre-Dispatch Instructions Conflict Relates to pre-dispatch instructions that are internally
inconsistent or conflict with customer-provided
directives.
Unclear Pre-Dispatch Instructions Pertains to issues involving ambiguous or unclear pre-
dispatch instructions that require clarification from the
customer.
Outdated Pre-Dispatch Instructions Concerns issues arising from outdated information in
the pre-dispatch instructions, leading to complications
in managing Emergency WOs.
Outdated Access Instructions(Emg Applies to complications in processing Emergency WOs
WO) due to insufficiently detailed or outdated access
instructions to the property.
Unnecessary Emg Comms with PM Refers to customer complaints regarding perceived
excessive communication from Latchel during an
Emergency Work Order.
Emg WO Coordination Disagreement Pertains to customer reports involving disagreements or
dissatisfaction with the handling of an Emergency Work
Order.
Intake Settings Concerns the guidance and the sharing of best
practices related to adjusting work order intake settings.
This includes categorizing situations as Normal, Urgent,
Needs Pre Approval, or Emergency, and determining the
responsible party for each severity level.
Login Issues Problems that users face when attempting to log into
the Latchel system, including password reset issues,
login credentials, and access denials.
SP Prioritization Queries related to the establishment and adjustment of
priority levels for Service Providers (SPs) according to
specific predefined criteria.
Add SP Procedures and assistance required for Property
Managers (PMs) to add a new preferred Service
Provider (SP) to their Latchel account.
Blacklist/Remove SP Engagements wherein a Property Manager (PM)
requests to permanently exclude a Service Provider (SP)
from being used due to a negative experience.
Escalation Contacts Requests for or queries about the contact information
utilized for escalating critical or unresolved issues within
the Latchel system.
Data Team Request The process whereby an updated report on properties
or residents is sent by a PM for auditing and subsequent
updates by the Data team in bulk quantities.
Owner Settings Inquiries or issues regarding settings specific to
property owners, such as updating owner preferences,
adding or assigning an owner to a property, and
configuring owner notification settings.
Users/User groups Matters concerning the permissions, creation, or
management of user groups, including settings for
automated notifications tied to these groups.
Pre-Dispatch Instruction/Critical Note Request Issues involving the examination, request, or
rectification of instructions or critical notes that are
predefined before service dispatch
Enable/Disable Properties and/or Residents Cases related to the activation or deactivation of
individual properties or residents within the system,
covering requests affecting fewer than 10 records.
Budget Limits Queries about the parameters and restrictions on
spending limits for specific jobs.
Applied Action Set The application of an existing, predefined action set to a
Latchel account for operational execution. Requests go
to #pd_cn_actionset.
Warranty Notes Requests for updates or detailed explanations
concerning warranty fields related to specific properties
for property managers.
Feature Request (Non-Integration) Technical requests that propose significant changes or
improvements to system functionality, with the potential
to greatly enhance a PM's daily operations. Reports or
requests relating to minor technical improvements or
impacts on system performance.
Custom Scripting Requests for the development and implementation of
unique or non-standard communication settings or
functions
Custom Workflow/Action Set Request Needs for unique or specific operational workflows that
deviate from established procedures or workflows. New
requests that are submitted to the #data-reporting-help-
requests.
Custom Diagnostic/Troubleshooting Requests for distinctive or specialized troubleshooting
processes that differ from standard procedures.
Cancellation Request Requests submitted by Property Managers for the
cancellation of their Latchel subscription, usually
requiring escalation to the Customer Success Manager
(CSM)
Budget Approval Issues arising from work orders where there was a
breach of budget limits or where the established
approval process was not adhered to
IVR/Twilio/Wait Time Concerns or inquiries focused on call greeting setups or
analyzing the duration of wait times during customer
calls
Poor Experience (Customer Service) Feedback pertaining to suboptimal interactions or
extended wait times experienced during webchat, SMS,
or calls with property managers, service providers, or
residents, affecting work order outcomes.
Poor Experience (Troubleshooting) Feedback related to negative experiences incurred
during troubleshooting calls with residents
Poor Experience (Call Frequency) Complaints related to an excessive number of calls
made by Latchel to a party, causing dissatisfaction.
Poor Experience (Escalation Contacts) Incidents where a Property Manager did not receive
anticipated escalation calls, or received unwarranted
escalation calls, linked to expectations vs. actual calls
WO General Question General inquiries for clarification regarding the process
or outcome of a work order, not necessarily indicating
an issue.
Resident Responsible Job (SP Sourcing) Cases where a resident reports dissatisfaction with
service provider availability or Latchel's inability to
source a service provider for a resident-billed job.
Resident Responsible Job (Billing Responsibility) Issues where residents are accountable for bill
payments, often involving clarification of these
responsibilities.
Account Configuration Discrepancies between expected and actual work order
outcomes attributed to the way an account is
configured.
Unresponsive SP Situations where tasks took longer than desired due to a
Service Provider failing to respond appropriately.
Unresponsive Resident Instances where tasks were delayed because residents
did not respond in a timely manner.
Delayed PM Approval/Action Cases where job or process completion was deferred
due to delays from the Property Manager in approving
or acting upon a work order
Delayed Owner Approval Instances where task completion was delayed because
an Owner took longer than acceptable to approve a
work order.
Poor Experience (SP Sourcing) Complaints related to delays and/or unavailability to find
and assign an SP to a WO.
Subscription Invoice Inquiries or issues concerning monthly subscription
invoices, including corrections related to billing system
or unit count changes
Job Invoice Concerns or disputes related to invoices associated with
work orders, including Property Manager inquiries or
disagreements.
Subscription Upgrade Inquiries or procedures related to increasing
subscription tier level for additional or enhanced
services
Subscription Downgrade Requests regarding reducing subscription tier level for
cost-saving or service preference reasons
Bug - High Impact (Non-Integration) Reports on urgent and serious problems with system
functionality that critically affect operations, requiring
immediate resolution. These go to #tech-high-sev-
escalations.
Bug - Low Impact (Non-Integration) Reports on minor or non-urgent technical bugs that
have limited effect on system functionality but are still
addressed for user satisfaction. These go to #bug-
report.
Business Shift/Org Changes Matters related to adjustments needed due to changes
in a customerʼs business model, organizational structure,
or strategic direction.
Product Fit/Expectations Assessments of how well the Latchel product meets
customer needs and requirements, or aligns with their
expectations
PMS Software Change Issues or requests that emerge from the application of
software updates, modifications, or migrations
DD Conclusion Outcomes and insights from thorough DD investigations
followed by feedback intended to prevent similar issues
Not Actionable Identifications of cases that do not require actions due
to being resolved, irrelevant, or invalid.
Report Export Requests for extracting data for the purpose of business
analysis or generating actionable insights via the
Analytics Dashboard or requested with #data-reporting-
help-request.
SP Request or Inquiry Any questions or requests provided directly by Service
Providers, including contact form submissions at the
HCP level
Sales Lead Information regarding potential sales opportunities or
referrals that could lead to new business development.
CSM Call Request Interaction where a customer has explicitly asked for a
conversation or meeting, typically to discuss matters
live over the phone
Accessing Benefits Issues faced by residents trying to utilize the benefits
entitled to them, often requiring support for access
facilitation
Benefits Rollout Cases concerning questions or clarifications regarding
the deployment or availability of benefit programs
Latchel Proprietary Benefits Customer inquiries about specific Latchel benefits such
as Accidental Damages Reimbursement, Lockout
Reimbursement, Overnight Stay Reimbursement, Missed
Appointment Fee Reimbursement, Free Drain Packs,
Move-In Promise, and Security Deposit Review
Moving Concierge Cases that relate to the facilitation of move-in services
offered to residents, in cooperation with Utility Valet.
Marketplace Benefits Inquiries about or opportunities to enhance Latchel
Marketplace Benefits, including services like Obie
(liability insurance), FilterTime (air filters), Cover Pest
(pest control), Sure (renterʼs insurance), Piñata (rent
rewards), and RentCheck (inspections).
