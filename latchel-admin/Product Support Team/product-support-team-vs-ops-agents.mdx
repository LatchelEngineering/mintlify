# Product Support Team vs Ops Agents

Whatʼs the difference between Product Support and Ops Agents?
Product Support (PS) manages issues involving product knowledge, platform behavior, and
helping property managers (PMs) update workflows to meet their goals. PS addresses work
order escalations, Deep Dive submissions, invoice disputes, feature requests, and service
issues, focusing on functional product problems and certain business outcomes. PS also
handles initial responses, troubleshooting, and gathering additional details on customer support
issues before escalating to ensure the Customer Success Manager (CSM) receives
comprehensive information.
Ops Agents handle straightforward requests and account updates. Any request beyond a basic
Tier 1 issue will be escalated to PS for more specialized assistance.

two apart:
