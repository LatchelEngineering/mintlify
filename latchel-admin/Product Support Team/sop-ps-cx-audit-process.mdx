# SOP: PS CX Audit Process

Purpose
Purpose of this document
The purpose of this document is to provide a high-level overview of the process and steps
to complete the PS CX Audit.
Who should read this document?
PS Associates and CS Team

Step-by-Step Guide
Step 1 - Auditors complete PS CX Audit Form.
On a weekly basis, the auditors should complete the PS CX Audit Form for their agreed upon
number of cases.
Auditors should select a random sampling of cases from the week to audit. Auditing
Compliance will be monitored in this report to ensure the agreed upon number of cases
audits are being completed on a weekly basis.
Auditors should follow these guidelines when filling out the form per case.
Step 2 - Scores will be shared with PS Associates.
All completed audit entries will dump into this report. PS Associates will be given the
1.
opportunity to review their audits and scores from the previous week.
Step 3 - PS Associate has opportunity to Dispute an Audit.
Following the review of their scores, PS associates will be given the opportunity to dispute an
1.
audit score and request a reassessment via this PS CX Audit Reassessment Dispute form.
Audit Reassessment requests will dump into this report. Reassessment requests will be
reviewed by the PSM.
If the reassessment is Valid and Applicable, feedback will be provided to the auditor and
a new score will be determined for the PS Associate.
If the reassessment is Not Valid and Not Applicable, feedback will be provided to the PS
Associate.
