<Warning>
#### Overview

Before escalating an Ops Escalation to the CSM, it’s essential to understand the Ops Escalation Summary fully. This involves reaching out to the customer to:

- Reviewing the full transcript between Ops and the CSM.
- Gather additional details.
- Ask clarifying questions.

Please only proceed with escalation if the ticket confirms that it aligns with the established criteria in [this resource](Product-Support-Team-vs-Customer-Success-Managers_2614755413.html).
</Warning>

After reviewing your ASM Ticket, and determine the issue should be escalated to the CSM according to [Product Support Team vs Customer Success Managers](Product-Support-Team-vs-Customer-Success-Managers_2614755413.html), these will be your next steps to follow:

### **Step 1: Find the Assigned CSM for the PM**

1. Navigate to Hubspot.
2. Search for the PM Company and find the Company Owner → CSM.
![](attachments/2649325654/3645276161.png?width=612)

---

### **Step 2: Communicate with the Property Manager (PM)**

1. Use the **Escalate to CSM** email template as a **starting** point. Adjust it as needed based on the situation.

| ###### *Hi [PM’s Name],*    ###### *Thank you for reaching out to us regarding [issue/inquiry]. To make sure you’re fully supported, I’ve looped in your Customer Success Manager, [CSM’s Name]. They’ll connect with you directly to provide next steps and discuss this further.*    ###### *In the meantime, please don’t hesitate to share any additional details with us if needed.* |
| --- |

**Reminders:**

- Ensure the PM's Company is assigned to the interaction in the Customer Profile section.
![](attachments/2649325654/3645243398.png?width=352)

---

### **Step 3: Submit the Escalation via Slack Workflow**

1. Navigate to Slack. Use the #csm-escalations channel and initiate the PS Escalate to CSM workflow.
![](attachments/2649325654/3645177862.png?width=325)
1. Fill Out the Workflow Prompts: - **Escalation Category:** - PM Disagrees with Denied Invoice Dispute - PM Requesting Training - PM Requesting Call/Meeting with CSM - Cancellation Request - PM Growing Frustrated - Email Thread Growing Complex → Call Recommended - Harmonia Upgrade - Other - **Priority:** - URGENT → Needs to be dealt with ASAP, delay may result in Churn. - MEDIUM → Still a higher priority but can be dealt with the next day, the PM will likely not churn out. - NORMAL → Not urgent, training request, account upgrade. - **Issue Summary:** - Avoid copying and pasting the PM’s message verbatim. - Provide a detailed summary of the issue, including: - Context of the conversation. - Troubleshooting steps already performed. - Actions taken thus far. - Any related Slack links or Assembly notes. - **Next Steps:** - Clearly explain the reason for escalation. - Provide suggestions/resolution and a call to action (e.g., Does the PM appear frustrated? Did they ask for a refund? Should the CSM reset expectations?).
2. **Submit the Workflow:** - Once all prompts are completed, click **Submit**.

---

### **Step 4: Update Assembly Interaction**

1. **Copy Workflow URL:** - After submitting the Slack workflow, copy the Slack URL and paste it into the Assembly Interaction as an Internal Note.
2. **Update ASM Attributes:** - **Status:** Set to “Needs Response.” - **Owner:** Assign the case to the CSM. - **Team Owner:** Reassign from Product Support to Customer Success. - **Label**: [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/HADnlg](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/HADnlg)
![](attachments/2649325654/3645276197.png?width=381)
1. **DONE**![(blue star)](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/s/1414720876/6452/a1087354351947de7fb47b319b1da8f664a99135/_/images/icons/emoticons/72/2705.png)
