# SOP: Account Termination Steps

Step-by-Step Guide:
Once account is confirmed that they are terminating and we have the termination date:
Step 1.PS or CS receives cancellation request from the PM.
If request goes to PS, PS should escalate the ticket to the CSM.
### Step 2. CSM meets with PM to discuss cancellation and next steps.
ONCE CANCELLATION IS CONFIRMED:
Step 3.CSMcreates a calendar invite under PS Team Calendar for a week prior to the Date of
Cancellation of the PM and invites PS team (abegail@latchel.com, cjefferson@latchel.com,
cza@latchel.com). Invite must include:
PM Latchel URL
### Step 4. Upon this date, PS will send a Courtesy Reminder email to the PM confirming their
cancelation and disabled date. (Cancelation Courtesy Reminder Template here.)
Step 5.CSMcreates a calendar invite under PS Team Calendar for the Date of Cancellation at
COB of the PM and invites PS team (abegail@latchel.com, cjefferson@latchel.com,
cza@latchel.com). Invite must include:
PM Hubspot URL
PM Latchel URL
Primary Termination Reason
Assembly Desire to Churn Ticket URL
Step 6:On the Date of Cancelation, PS turns OFF all communications to ALL users in Latchel
account. SKIP THIS STEP IF TERMINATED FOR NON-PAYMENT.
Turn OFF invoice notifications via the Maint. Communication Settings.

### Step 8. Release all Twilio phone numbers associated with the customer.
Find phone numbers associated with the PM by going to the PM Account page in Latchel.
In Maint. Communication Settings, you will want use the Latchel-Assigned Phone
Number and the Latchel Communication Phone Number.
After logging into Twilio, navigate to Phone Numbers > Manage > Active Numbers.
1.

2.

3.
