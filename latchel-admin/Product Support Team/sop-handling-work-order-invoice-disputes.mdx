Conclusion Review and Client Communication

Scenario 1: Invalid Dispute (No Refund)
Action: Escalate to the CSM for them to communicate the DD conclusion directly with the PM.
Note: Do not send any templates to the PM. All communication should come from the CSM.
Steps to escalate:
Use the CSM Escalation Workflow to escalate to the CSM.
Assign the case to @maddie as the Case Owner.
Update the case state to Escalated.
Scenario 2: Valid Dispute (Refund Eligible)
Action: Share the DD conclusion with the PM and confirm that the refund process is
underway with the Finance team.
Submit details of the refund to the #accounts-payable Slack channel via the DD Invoice
Dispute Workflow.
In the Slack thread, tag @Will Gordon for visibility and @Samantha Terreros to execute the
refund and communicate with the service provider.
Once finalized, Finance will confirm in the thread, and PS should update the PM on:
Refund method
Processing timeline (5-10 business days)
Refund amount ($)
Handling Client Pushback on Findings
Action: If the PM disputes the findings or compensation, escalate the case to the Product
Support Manager (PSM).
Steps:
Escalate to PSM using the PSM Case Escalation Workflow in #ps-case-review .
Assign the case to @maddie as Case Owner.
Update the case state to Escalated.
