pre-dispatch instruction and/or critical note for approval from the Product Support and
Ops team.

What happened prior to this step?
Review SOP: Pre-Dispatch Instructions and Critical Notes
Process Overview
All requested pre-dispatch instructions and/or critical notes that fall outside of the scenarios
listed in SOP: Pre-Dispatch Instructions and Critical Notes will require approval from Product
Support and Operations.
Step 1 - Navigate to the #pd_criticalnotes Slack Channel.
From there, you will go to the Workflows tab and select: PD and CN Approval.

2.
Step 2- Fill out the Workflow:
Company Name - This is the name of the property management company as it appears in the
Latchel console.
PM Company ID - When accessing the PM company page in Latchel, the PM Company ID
are the last digits located at the end of the URL.
Active Subscription - From the PM company page, go to the “Subscription” tab and find the
green active subscription. (*You may also need to reference the “Product Package” tab to
fully understand the service level based on the name of the subscription.) Here is a video for
reference.
Pre-Dispatch Instruction or Critical Note - Simply indicate which option you are requesting for
approval.
Level of Inheritance - Here you will list if the requested addition is for the company account
as whole, for a specific portfolio or a specific property.
If Portfolio or Property Level, please share details - Here you will share which portfolio or
property the requested addition is for.
Please submit the drafted PD/CN Request - In this field, you will share the requested pre-
dispatch instruction or critical note as it would appear in Latchel for approval.
Step 3 - Submit approval after filling out the workflow.
Step 4 - Product Support and Service Operations will review the information. Approval and details will be shared
with the submitter within 4 hours of submission.
