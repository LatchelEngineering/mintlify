# Product Support Team vs Customer Success Managers

Whatʼs the Difference Between Product Support and Customer Success?
Product Support (PS) is the frontline partner for Property Managers. We handle nearly all
product-related issues — from platform functionality and workflow alignment to work order
escalations, deep dives, invoice disputes, feature requests, and service concerns. PS manages
initial responses, troubleshooting, and clarifying details to resolve issues directly whenever
possible. When escalation is needed, we ensure the Customer Success Manager (CSM)
receives full context and background.
Customer Success (CSM) steps in when conversations move beyond day-to-day support into
business-impact or relationship-level needs. CSMs manage cancellation requests,
disagreements with denied invoice disputes, training needs, and meeting requests. They
address moments of frustration, guide long-term business objectives, and strengthen the
partnership with Latchel.
Below outlines ownership by function between PS and CS. PS resolves the vast majority of
product, workflow, and operational issues, while CSMs act as the escalation point when PMs
hit frustration, request cancellations, need training/meetings, or require strategic alignment.

integrations, billing, disputes, and configurations.

cancellations, dissatisfaction), request meetings/trainings, or need business-level alignment.
