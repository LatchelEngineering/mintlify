# SOP: How to Handle LVN Escalations

Latchel Vendor Network describes a group of service provider companies that Latchel has
a special arrangement with. In return for being offered real work orders, LVN vendors
agree to:
use Latchel technology to interact with all work orders
get paid exclusively via Latchelʼs direct deposit payout technology
guarantee all technicians are vetted, licensed, bonded and insured
offer free recalls to Latchel customers
You can find the full list of Latchelʼs partner LVN companies here.

How to: Handle LVN Complaints
When a complaint is received from a PM regarding an LVN company, it is important to get all
the relevant details from them while communicating via the case.
After gathering all the information from the PM, review the WO in question as it pertains to the
PMʼs complaints.
If you are not able to identify the root cause of the problem or answer the customerʼs
question using the resources available, or if your investigation will take 10+ minutes,
submit the complaint for a Deep Dive.
The complaint will either be VALID (there is an issue) or INVALID (nothing went wrong and
expectations need to be reset with the customer).
For BOTH Valid and Invalid complaints, share the conclusion with the customer regarding the
LVN complaint and your findings/next steps.
