# SOP: Handling Resident Cases

Purpose: This procedure outlines how to handle cases related to residents who are
associated with a Property Manager. The majority of Resident Cases will be shared with
their property manager but there a few scenarios where PS will directly communicate with
the resident.
Scenarios Approved to Communicate directly with the Resident:
### Step 1: Access the Resident Profile
### Step 2: Identify the Residentʼs Property Management (PM) Company
### Step 3: Find a PM Contact Email
### Step 4: Contact the PM Regarding the Residentʼs Inquiry
Scenarios Approved to Communicate directly with the Resident:
Scenario 1: How to: Handle Resident Responsible WOs.
Scenario 2: If the Resident Profile is ACTIVE, PS can send their Public Dashboard URL if the
resident is looking for ways to access their dashboard.
All other scenarios should be sent to the Property Manager to communicate to the
Resident. Continue to the steps below if this is an unpproved scenario and an
escalation to the PM is required.
