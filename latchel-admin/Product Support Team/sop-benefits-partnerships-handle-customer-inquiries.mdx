How are residents reimbursed?
When a resident submits a Typeform request, they see the following
message on the final screen: “We will review your submission and
contact you with any questions. You can expect a follow-up within 5-7
business days. If your reimbursement request is approved, you will
receive an email from Ramp to provide your bank details.”
Once a reimbursement request is approved by Latchel, the resident will
receive an email from Ramp asking them to provide their bank details.
Our default reimbursement method is via ACH. However, a paper check
can also be issued upon resident request.

recommend is re-educating residents about their reimbursement
benefits at the time of a chargeback. This can help soften the impact
and ensure residents feel supported, even when chargebacks are
necessary.”

PM is asking if a Latchel work order is required for a resident to be eligible for an Overnight Stay Reimbursement.
It is only required if the Property Management Company is using our
maintenance subscription. If the PM is on a Benefits Only plan, a work
order does not need to be created in Latchel. A screenshot of the work
order in their Property Management System (PMS) is sufficient, as we
just need visual confirmation that an actual overnight emergency
occurred.
