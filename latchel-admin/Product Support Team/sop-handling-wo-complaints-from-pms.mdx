Step-by-step Guide:
### Step 1: Gather Details
Clarify: Promptly ask clarifying questions to obtain comprehensive details about the
complaint.
Request Documentation: Ask for any supporting files, such as recordings, screenshots, or
pictures, to aid in the investigation.
### Step 2: Review the Situation
Examine Details: Analyze the specifics of the work order issue, focusing on areas such as
delays, miscommunications, automations, and settings.
### Step 3: Conduct Investigation
Scenario 1: Investigation Exceeds 10 Minutes
Submit the complaint and associated details for a Deep Dive Request.
Scenario 2: Investigation Under 10 Minutes
Document all discovery details, findings, and resolution. Prepare to share these with the
property manager.
### Step 4: Communicate Resolution
Method of Sharing Findings: Whether conclusions arise from a deep dive or a personal
investigation, ensure the information and next steps are conveyed clearly to the property
manager.
