## **Purpose**

This SOP outlines the process for managing requests from clients wishing to cancel their Latchel subscription. The goal is to address client concerns effectively, retain the client if possible, and ensure compliance with contractual obligations.

## **Definitions**

- Churn Request: A client's formal or informal notification of their desire to terminate their Latchel contract. 
- Early Termination Fee (ETF): A fee charged for the cancellation of the Latchel service contract before its scheduled end date, as specified in the contract terms.

## **Procedure**

\*All steps through the initial communication should be completed ASAP, but within 24 hours of the client’s request at the latest.

### **Handling Churn Requests - Client Options**

1. Option 1: Update Product/Subscription and/or Contract (Preferred Option)
   - Propose a meeting or call to discuss potential adjustments to their maintenance configuration settings or contract terms that may better suit their needs. This might include:
     - Adjusting service levels
     - Offering temporary discounts or modified pricing
     - Extending the contract term with revised terms favorable to both parties
   - All contract negotiations or concessions should be discussed with the Director of Customer Success for approval before confirming with the client. 
   - Please see the[<u> Desire to Churn Talk Tracks</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2979528707 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2979528707") which address common objections you may come across during the save call. 
2. Option 2: Document and Schedule Cancellation
   - If the client insists on canceling without further negotiations, document their decision and inform them that the cancellation will be processed at the end of their current contract term. Provide detailed information about any actions they must complete prior to the termination and any services they will continue to receive until the contract ends.
   - Update the current Opportunity to **Deal Substage: Termination Scheduled**.
3. Option 3: Immediate Cancellation with ETF
   - If applicable, offer the option to cancel the contract immediately, subject to an early termination fee. Clearly explain the fee structure as outlined in their contract and the steps required to proceed with this option.
   - The CSM should prepare a proposal based on the initial assessment and present it to the client. This proposal should aim to address the client's concerns while maintaining the value of the contract.

### **Follow-Up and Resolution**

1. The CSM should schedule a follow-up meeting to discuss the proposed solutions with the client. During this meeting, negotiate in good faith to reach an amicable resolution.
2. If the client accepts the proposal, document the new agreement terms and have both parties sign the updated contract or amendment if required. 
3. If no resolution is found and the client chooses to proceed with cancellation, document the decision and the chosen method of cancellation (end of term or immediate with ETF), and initiate the required processes as found here:[<u> SOP: Account Termination Steps</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/2802647041 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/2802647041")
   - Be sure to update the Primary and Additional(if applicable) churn reason on the [<u>Churn Tracking Report</u>](https://app.hubspot.com/contacts/23701847/objects/0-3/views/45332833/list "https://app.hubspot.com/contacts/23701847/objects/0-3/views/45332833/list") using this resource:[<u> Churn Categories Defined</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/EYCJsQ "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/EYCJsQ").
