## **Background**

Prior to February 2024, the Customer Profitability Team (CPT) regularly shared a lot of data with Customer Success (CS) but the amount of information was overwhelming for CS to digest. There were no documented expectations on what CS should do with this data and therefore, we missed a lot of opportunities to help customers stay on track on the Road to Profitability (RTP). Many customers churned when they received a large bill at the end of their first 12 months with Latchel.

Beginning in February 2024, Customer Success has a central file to reference to obtain the information they need. We will also begin building a [<u>CSM Playbook</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2903212051/Assisted+Ledgering+Monthly+Scorecard+Meeting#Playbook "#Playbook") for each “flavor” of failure to ledger, as well as major risks, providing CSMs with a toolkit to guide meaningful conversations with customers.

## **Objective**

**_PMs understand where they are on the Road to Profitability (RTP) each month._** There are no surprises at month 12. PM doing great? Latchel celebrates with them! PM struggling? Their CSM understands where they are failing so they can help.

## **Key Dates & Process**

1. 5th of each month (or next business day): CPT shares the [<u>Monthly Scorecard</u>](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1e9psvlXljLhCK_j_Dzhz1LmHRCYp7S23ns9kzJh19x0/edit#gid=0 "https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1e9psvlXljLhCK_j_Dzhz1LmHRCYp7S23ns9kzJh19x0/edit#gid=0") with CS in the [<u>#ledgering-mkt-opt-benefits</u>](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C03D4N7B0LF "https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C03D4N7B0LF") Slack channel
2. 8th of each month (or next business day): Deadline for CSMs to review the [<u>Monthly Scorecard</u>](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1e9psvlXljLhCK_j_Dzhz1LmHRCYp7S23ns9kzJh19x0/edit#gid=0 "https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1e9psvlXljLhCK_j_Dzhz1LmHRCYp7S23ns9kzJh19x0/edit#gid=0") for their at risk customers

<Note>
  **Definition of at risk customer**

  1. Currently nonprofitable and 30%\+ missed opportunities last month
  2. Will not be profitable after their first 12 months and we do not have access to their PMS to track missed opportunities
  3. Major Risk identified by CPT
</Note>

3. 2nd Monday of each month: CPT/CS Strategy Meeting

- **Meeting purpose **
  - CSMs lead the meeting. For each at risk customer, they share their [Activation & Ledgering Action Plans](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1s1he8qctizbK8q9Wc95lvOM1020-nNRCRrlWuWPIzE0/edit)
  - If a CSM is stuck on what action to take next, they leverage their colleagues to brainstorm possible strategies.
  - CPT serves as a resource for CSMs. Their role is to answer clarifying questions or requests for more granular information.
  - If there is time remaining, CSMs can also discuss optional action plans for customers that don’t meet the “at risk” criteria above
  - Build out and improve the [<u>CSM Playbook</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2903212051/Assisted+Ledgering+Monthly+Scorecard+Meeting#Playbook "#Playbook")\
    1. For each scenario discussed (i. and ii. above), the team decides whether the action taken should be a standard/best practice to be added to the [<u>CSM Playbook</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2903212051/Assisted+Ledgering+Monthly+Scorecard+Meeting#Playbook "#Playbook").\
    2. After the meeting, the Director of Customer Success updates the [<u>CSM Playbook Outline</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2903212051/Assisted+Ledgering+Monthly+Scorecard+Meeting#Playbook "#Playbook") based on the conversation (or delegates this to a CSM).

4. Within 24 hours of CPT/CS Strategy Meeting
   - PM-Facing Reports are generated by CPT ([<u>example here</u>](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C0479V0CREW/p1736180321814769 "https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C0479V0CREW/p1736180321814769"))
5. 2nd Friday of each month: Deadline for CSMs to share PM-Facing Reports with their customers \+ implement [Activation & Ledgering Action Plans](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1s1he8qctizbK8q9Wc95lvOM1020-nNRCRrlWuWPIzE0/edit)
   - Sample language for sharing report with PMs who haven’t given us access
     - _Hi \[customer name\]!_\
       _I’m happy to share our new monthly Road to Profitability (RTP) report with you, attached here! As you’ll see, there are handful of “?”s in the report as our team doesn’t yet have access to your Appfolio. We’d love to help you set that access up so we can provide tailored insights on your ledgering progress and support you in reaching your resident experience and revenue generation goals as quickly as possible! Could we set up a quick call so we can walk through that together_
   - Sample language for sharing report with non-profitable PM
     - _Hi \[customer name\]!_\
       _I’m happy to share our new monthly Road to Profitability (RTP) report with you, attached here! As you’ll see, you’re 44% of the way to profitability with just 12 units to go to hit break-even! It looks like you had a few missed opportunities last month on your month-to-month leases. Is there anything we can do to support your team in getting the benefits charge code added to those next month?_
   - Sample language for sharing report with profitable PM
     - _Hi \[customer name\]!_\
       _I’m happy to share our new monthly Road to Profitability (RTP) report with you, attached here! As you’ll see, you’re doing fantastic generating revenue with Latchel benefits! It looks like your team added the benefits charge code to all eligible leases last month – keep up the great work!_
