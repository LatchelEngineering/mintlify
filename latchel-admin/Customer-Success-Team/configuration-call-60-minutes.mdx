## **Configuration Call Goals:**

- Confirm client’s understanding of our maintenance process, especially how we interact with residents, vendors and pms for emergencies vs non-emergencies.
- Review all areas of maintenance configuration, with additional focus on:
  - Service Provider Prioritization - complete example 
  - Severity & Responsibility Settings - Emergency vs Pre-Approval
- Enable integration (if applicable) and confirm unit count.

### **Work Order Intake Flow (10 Minutes) **

- Walk clients through an interactive example of the work order diagnostic flow.
- Outline process for handling non-emergencies, starting with the Ops Troubleshooting and then detail the process for dispatch and common examples of when we might call the emergency escalation contact. 

### **Maintenance Automation - Emergency vs Non-Emergency Workflows (10 Minutes)**

- Maintenance Automation - Emergency Work Orders (5 Minutes)
  - Intake - Latchel's proprietary intake delivers higher quality, actionable WOs while de-escalating and resolving up to 30% of WOs.
  - Troubleshooting - A Latchel troubleshooter attempts to call the resident to de-escalate, if unsuccessful, we reach out to your preferred service providers.
    - This step is unique to emergency work orders. Be sure to clarify this step only occurs for issues with the Severity set to Emergency. 
  - Scheduling - If your service provider accepts, the appointment is set! If they are unresponsive or unavailable, Latchel will create a task for you or, in limited circumstances, reach out to your Emergency Escalation contacts for next steps. 
    - This step is unique to emergency work orders. Be sure to give examples of when we call the PM - Property Damaging or Extreme Habitability Issues (Fire, Flood or Blood).
  - Dispatch - Once an appointment is set, Latchel automates communication and reminders to all parties leading up to the work date.
  - Follow-Up - Your resident confirms the issue was resolved. Positive resident reviews are encouraged to be posted on the customer's review site of choice.
  - Invoicing - Service providers can upload invoices directly to the work order, which will then be emailed directly to the contact of your choosing and also attached to the work order in Latchel.
- Maintenance Automation - Normal and Urgent Work Orders (5 Minutes)
  - Intake - Latchel's proprietary intake delivers higher quality, actionable WOs while de-escalating and resolving up to 30% of WOs.
  - Scheduling - Latchel collects resident availability or permission to enter and shares with SPs to automate the scheduling process for most WOs
    - Be sure to emphasize we never call vendors when dispatching non-emergencies, only email and text if they have a textable phone.
    - Additionally, it is important to clarify here that we don’t call PMs when we can’t source a SP for a non-emergency, they will receive an email to their Default Maintenance user group.
  - Dispatch - Once an appointment is set, Latchel automates communication and reminders to all parties leading up to the work date
  - Follow-Up - Your resident confirms the issue was resolved. Positive resident reviews are encouraged to be posted on the customer's review site of choice.
  - Invoicing - Service providers can upload invoices directly to the work order, which will then be emailed directly to the contact of your choosing and also attached to the work order in Latchel.

### **Maintenance Configuration (15 Minutes)**

- Review the 6 main areas of focus for maintenance configurations and system defaults. Follow-up email contains User Pilot links for each section with additional instructions and reference material.
  - Maintenance Communication - Ensure your team is always in the loop and never misses an important update.
  - Service Provider Prioritization - Configure dispatching rules to get service appointments booked at lightning speed. 
    - Let the client know you will cover this in more depth after reviewing all settings at a high level. 
  - Users & User Groups - Add your team to Latchel for increased visibility and improved communication.
  - Work Order Budget Limits - Enforce budgets and get automatic notifications for approvals.
  - Portal Settings - Customize your resident portal experience & drive positive online reviews.
  - Severity/Responsibility - Ensure Latchel is taking the right action for newly created work orders. Discuss Pre-Approval Setting for all non-emergency work orders starting out. 
    - Be sure to have the client share their screen here and walk through the settings together. Make it clear that starting out, all items should either be set to Emergency or Needs Pre-Approval and why. 

### **Service Provider Review (15 Minutes)**

- Launch Manager requests PM continue to share their screen so we can run through vendor prioritization for one trade. Plumbing is a great trade for this example as most PMs have multiple plumbers.This is also a category that is often dispatched for emergencies. Together, we walk through how to:
  - Remove a Service Provider from the priority list 
  - Edit priorities 
  - Edit service provider skills 
  - Edit response buffer times
