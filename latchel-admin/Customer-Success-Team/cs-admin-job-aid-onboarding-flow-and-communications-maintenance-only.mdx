## **Sales Hand Off**

1. The sales team closes on the product sale <Icon icon="smile-beam" color="#eaa90b" />
2. Sales Executive (AE) explains the Onboarding process and collects the required information from the client.
3. Once all parties sign the contract, the Salesforce opportunity will move to the “Closed Won” stage with the substage “Waiting Setup Fee”. At this point, a Customer Success Manager (CSM) will be automatically assigned to the opportunity.
4. The deal will automatically post in the #sales channel in Slack. The post will contain a link to the Sales notes, and customer profile, and tag the assigned CSM.
5. Once the CSM sees that post it triggers them to duplicate the “New Customer Onboarding Checklist (Maintenance Only) - Template in Slack lists.
   - Then they customize it with the customer’s company name.
6. The CSM should complete the following steps once they see that post in the #sales channel:
   - Create a new customer folder Labeled \[Company Name\] in The” [<u>Property Manager Files folder</u>](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1K-OxCEHlyfI6GNhukN9aNPsZho1yzz7T?usp=drive_link "https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1K-OxCEHlyfI6GNhukN9aNPsZho1yzz7T?usp=drive_link").

## **Onboarding / Kickoff - Launch**

### **The CSM conducts the “Kickoff” Call**

1. <u>Call Goals:</u>
   - CSM Goal 1: Connect their onboarding process to alleviating pain points
   - CSM Goal 2: Get commit dates from the customer on customer-only action items for kick-off items (e.g. share property list)
   - CSM Goal 3: Determine portfolio organization (if applicable)
   - CSM Goal 4: Schedule Configuration Call (60 min)
2. <u>Preparation:</u>
   - Open the customer's profile in Hubspot.
   - Open the customer’s Onboarding Slide Deck
   - Review the “Property Grouping” form for their responses.
   - Check whether the integration is live or we have received their property list.
   - Familiarize yourself with the client's current maintenance processes and pain points.
3. <u>Connecting Pain Points to Actions:</u>
   - Use slide deck during the call to discuss the client's pain points.
   - Highlight how the onboarding action items will address these pain points to motivate the client and maintain momentum.
4. <u>Integration Steps:</u>
   - If the customer has property management software and has not completed the integration with Latchel, complete these steps live on the call.
     - Integration Setup: Documents are stored [<u>here</u>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1hTV50za191xlvGEtJ2UTnmmzq_TFYeX4NzeXnkCABks/edit?usp=sharing "https://docs.google.com/document/d/1hTV50za191xlvGEtJ2UTnmmzq_TFYeX4NzeXnkCABks/edit?usp=sharing")
5. <u>Onboarding Tracker Review:</u>
   - Open the customer's Maintenance Onboarding Tracker and share your screen.
   - Review the “pre-kickoff” and “post-kickoff” tabs and associated action items with the customer.
6. <u>Property Groupings:</u>
   - Check if the customer has completed the property groupings form.
   - If not, review the form with the customer on the call.
   - Confirm the recommended way to group properties into portfolios.
   - If the customer doesn’t group their properties, skip this step.
7. <u>Roadmap Discussion:</u>
   - Discuss the roadmap to launch maintenance services.
   - Determine the customer’s target launch date.
8. <u>Commit Dates:</u>
   - Secure commitment dates for each action item from the customer.
   - Record these dates in Column E of their maintenance onboarding tracker.
9. <u>Next Call Scheduling:</u>
   - Schedule the next call, the “Configuration” call, before ending the current call.
10. Immediately following the meeting the CSM sends the  [<u>“Post Kickoff” Email</u>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1aNQaENlgVZi4TQ-iR7m0WNfuIOlCjDqFNJUuGLngTcU/edit#bookmark=id.nspddh43ula1 "https://docs.google.com/document/d/1aNQaENlgVZi4TQ-iR7m0WNfuIOlCjDqFNJUuGLngTcU/edit#bookmark=id.nspddh43ula1").
11. If they have property groupings once the CSM has the property lists for each portfolio, create a task for the Data Support Team to create their portfolios onto their account before the next call.
    1. Refer to this SOP for step-by-step instructions on how to create a Data Support team ticket in Slack: [<u>Steps to Submit a Data Support Ticket</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/3222437894 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/3222437894")
    2. Refer to this SOP [<u>(Data Support) How to Handle Assembly Tickets</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/3220111374 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/3220111374") for direction on their SLAs and how to manage and track your tickets once submitted.
       1.   Video: [<u>How to Submit and Manage Data Support Tickets</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/BoASw "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/BoASw")
12. The CSM sends an Onboarding Tracker “Post Kickoff” Action Items reminder (3 days after their last meeting. This may vary if the customer has an accelerated onboarding) and check-in email to the customer.
13. CSM sends the [<u>Configuration Call Reminder Email </u>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1aNQaENlgVZi4TQ-iR7m0WNfuIOlCjDqFNJUuGLngTcU/edit#bookmark=id.h7pgq33ewhh "https://docs.google.com/document/d/1aNQaENlgVZi4TQ-iR7m0WNfuIOlCjDqFNJUuGLngTcU/edit#bookmark=id.h7pgq33ewhh")that goes out 24 hours before the meeting.

### **The CSM conducts the “Configuration” Call (Working Session)**

1. <u>Call Goals</u>:
   - \*\* \*\*CSM Goal 1: Highlight how each goal for this meeting alleviates their pain points.
   - CSM Goal 2: Review data or export data live on the call
     - Property list (If no integration)
     - Property Group lists
     - Service Provider list
     - Owner list
   - CSM Goal 3: Align customer expectations for emergency vs non-emergency work orders with how Latchel operates.
   - CSM Goal 4: Get date estimates for the Customer’s remaining action items
   - CSM Goal 5: Schedule final onboarding Review & Launch Success call (60 minutes)
2. <u>Preparation:</u>
   - Open the customer’s Onboarding Slide Deck
   - Review the “Emergency Definitions” form responses. Make configuration adjustments to the default setting based on their definitions.
   - Confirm if we have received their service provider data. If so, create a task for the Data Support Team to get the service providers uploaded onto their account before the call. To review prioritization steps.
   - Review their progress on their onboarding tracker for the completion of action items under the “Action Items Post Kickoff” tab.
3. <u>Property lists:</u>
   - If the customer does not have a property manager system review their properties that are in Latchel. If they have not sent the list work together on this call to get that list emailed over.
   - If the customer has property groups that need to be organized into portfolios review the portfolios on the call to ensure we have them organized correctly. If we have not received those lists yet work together on the call to export those from their PMS or have an excel document sent over via email.
   - <u>Service Provider list:</u>
     - If we have their service providers in Latchel review those on the call and review skills and prioritization at the company level. Discuss if there are service providers at the portfolio or property level that need to be configured. If we do not have the service provider list then work together to export or create that list live on the call.
4. <u>Owner list:</u>
   - If we have their owners in Latchel review those on the call at the portfolio or property level. Discuss if there are specific owner approvals or notifications that need to be configured. If we do no have the owner list then work together to export or create that list live on the call.
5. <u>Commit Dates:</u>
   - Secure commitment dates for each action item from the client.
   - Record these dates in Column E of their onboarding tracker.
6. <u>Next Call Scheduling:</u>
   - Schedule the next call, the “Launch Success” call, before ending the current call.
7. After the call has concluded the CSM will take any data collected on the call and create account update tasks for the Data Support Team to complete.

   2. Refer to this SOP [<u>(Data Support) How to Handle Assembly Tickets</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/3220111374 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/3220111374") for direction on their SLAs and how to manage and track your tickets once submitted.
      1.   Video: [<u>How to Submit and Manage Data Support Tickets</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/BoASw "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/BoASw")
8. CSM sends [<u>"Post-Configuration Call" Email</u>](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1aNQaENlgVZi4TQ-iR7m0WNfuIOlCjDqFNJUuGLngTcU/edit#bookmark=id.m3ylo2c3g7sx "https://docs.google.com/document/d/1aNQaENlgVZi4TQ-iR7m0WNfuIOlCjDqFNJUuGLngTcU/edit#bookmark=id.m3ylo2c3g7sx")

### **The CSM conducts the “Launch Success” Call**

1. <u>Call Goals</u>:
   - CSM Goal 1: Final review of maintenance configurations (6 key areas). Ensure all maintenance settings are appropriately configured to align with the client's operational needs and expectations.
   - CSM Goal 2: Rerouting Maintenance to Latchel: Ensure that all maintenance requests are accurately rerouted to Latchel, facilitating seamless integration and management of tasks.
   - CSM Goal 3: Resident & Service Provider Communication: Discuss and solidify communication strategies and protocols with both residents and service providers to maintain clear and effective interaction.
   - CSM Goal 4: Training Roadmap: Schedule their first Training and review the training roadmap.
   - <u>Preparation:</u>
     - Open the client's Onboarding Notes document.
     - Open the client’s Onboarding Slide Deck
     - Check the status of all data tasks with the Data Support Team.
     - Have instructions on hand on how to reroute their maintenance via phone and property management System.
     - Have their maintenance communication one pager updated.
2. CSM schedules an account audit for another Latchel team member to review the account before intaking a live work order.
