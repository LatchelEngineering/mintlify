| “Emergency Sourcing” _(Emergency Workflow)_ | You may opt-in to have Latchel source new service providers if nobody from your preferred network is available to accept the job. Our team may source a new provider on the spot or source someone that we have previously worked with in your area. |
| :-- | :-- |
| **“Latchel’s List”**_(Non-Emergency Workflow)_ | Get access to Latchel's service provider network when your preferred providers are not available to complete your non-emergency jobs. If you select to opt-in to have Latchel source new service providers for an emergency job, you’ll instantly gain access to a directory of service providers Latchel has previously worked with in your area. **If none of your preferred service providers are available for a non-emergency job**, we’ll surface the work order for your team right away. From there, you’ll have direct access to Latchel’s list of service providers to move things along more quickly. **Contact them before you officially hire them** - If you decide to select a service provider from Latchel’s list, make sure to set out a firm description of the scope of work to be done at the home. Having access to Latchel’s list is a great tool to help you build up your preferred network! |
