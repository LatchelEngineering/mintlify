This article is to teach Customer Success Managers how to update the meeting cadence pipeline on the Opportunity record inside of Salesforce. The cadence pipeline is used to track how often you are meeting with that client.

## **Instructions**

Video demo: [https://www.loom.com/share/2d9b6d3413cc436bb92eba1da5d032cf](https://www.loom.com/share/2d9b6d3413cc436bb92eba1da5d032cf)

1. Access the **Opportunity** record for the Management company in Salesforce
2. At the top of the page, click on the tile that best describes how often you are meeting with that customer
3. Click **Mark as Current Stage** to save

<Note>
  **Recently Launched (No Cadence)** = Customers that have been using Latchel for **\<30 days** and do not have a meeting cadence on the books

  **No Cadence** = Customers that have been using Latchel for **30\+ days** and do not have a meeting cadence on the books

  **Cancellation Scheduled** = Customers that have reached out to cancel, but their subscription is still active. This could be customers that have an offboarding call scheduled or requested a termination date in the future

  **Fully Cancelled** = Customers that have been offboarded and their Latchel account has been terminated
</Note>
