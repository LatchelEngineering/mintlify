## **Background**

Every member of the CS team has an assigned number in [<u>Salesloft</u>](https://app.salesloft.com/app/dashboard "https://app.salesloft.com/app/dashboard"). In this SOP, you will learn how to log calls and texts with customers so they are recorded in Salesforce and reflected in the [<u>CSM Activity Dashboard</u>](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Dashboard/01Z5e000000eWwdEAE/view "https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Dashboard/01Z5e000000eWwdEAE/view").

<Note>
  Use this SOP for customer interactions that took place via phone. If you had a meeting via video conference, please refer to [SOP: Booking & Logging Meetings](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/edit-v2/2747334659?draftShareId=5acfcfbb-33c0-4b32-875c-04f557f04850&inEditorTemplatesPanel=auto_closed)
</Note>

## **Set Up**

1. Make sure you have the [<u>Salesloft Google Chrome extension installed</u>](https://help.salesloft.com/s/article/Download-the-Salesloft-Chrome-Extension "https://help.salesloft.com/s/article/Download-the-Salesloft-Chrome-Extension").
2. [<u>Record a custom voicemail greeting</u>](https://help.salesloft.com/s/article/Voicemail-Recordings#Setup_Your_Inbound_Voicemail "https://help.salesloft.com/s/article/Voicemail-Recordings#Setup_Your_Inbound_Voicemail").

## **Outbound Calls**

### **Dialing**

You have 3 options for calling customers:

1. Dial customers directly from Salesloft.
   - Search for the name of the person you want to call in the search bar at the top of your screen.
     <Frame>
       ![CSM 21](/images/CSM-21.png)
     </Frame>
   - Click the green “Call” button in the “Quick Actions” box on the left side of the screen.
     <Frame>
       ![CSM 22](/images/CSM-22.png)
     </Frame>
2. Dial customers from anywhere on the internet using Salesloft. Video overview [<u>here</u>](https://www.loom.com/share/b503f8a627ed48b8b460f10eba75977e "https://www.loom.com/share/b503f8a627ed48b8b460f10eba75977e").
3. Dial customers directly from Salesforce.
   - Make sure “Click to Call” is enabled in your Salesloft settings.
     <Frame>
       ![CSM 23](/images/CSM-23.png)
     </Frame>
   - Use the S Call Button in Salesforce to dial a Contact.
     <Frame>
       ![CSM 24](/images/CSM-24.png)
     </Frame>

 

### **Logging Calls**

No matter how you dial, **log your call afterwards** or the call will appear to not have happened! Failing to log the call will result in customers looking untouched, even if you’ve called them. Video overview [<u>here</u>](https://www.loom.com/share/1e009991866a42328acd6be8346c3bef "https://www.loom.com/share/1e009991866a42328acd6be8346c3bef").

1. Dial the customer using one of the 3 options above.
2. Log your call notes using the call notes box that pops up, then complete the dropdowns.
   - For the Disposition, select _Connected - CS Convo_ if you spoke with the customer. Otherwise, select _Hung Up_, _Left Voicemail_, _No Answer_, _Not in Service_, or _Wrong Number_, depending on the situation.
   - Sentiment: This will soon be updated. In the meantime, select _N/A_.
   - CRM Fields: This will soon be updated. In the meantime, select _Follow Up_.
3. Click the green “Log” button when you are done. 
   <Frame>
     ![CSM 25](/images/CSM-25.png)
   </Frame>
4. Your call notes will appear in the Comments field of the Customer Information section of the call in Salesforce. 
   <Frame>
     ![CSM 26](/images/CSM-26.png)
   </Frame>

<Warning>
  You must log calls as outlined above in order for the call duration to appear in the [<u>CSM Activity Dashboard</u>](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Dashboard/01Z5e000000eWwdEAE/view "https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Dashboard/01Z5e000000eWwdEAE/view"). Logging calls directly in Salesforce will update your number of calls in the dashboard but not their duration.
</Warning>

## **Inbound Calls**

Customers should always use your Salesloft number. You can answer this  number directly from your computer or set up call forwarding if you prefer to answer calls on your cell phone. See a video overview of how to set up call forwarding [<u>here</u>](https://www.loom.com/share/9341227621384ccb906c354aa1659a83 "https://www.loom.com/share/9341227621384ccb906c354aa1659a83").

If you answer the call through your browser:

1. Answer the incoming call.
2. The customer’s Salesforce page will open along with the Salesloft call notes box. Log your notes in the Salesloft call notes box, the same way you would for outbound calls.

If you set up call forwarding to your cell phone:

1. Answer the incoming call on your cell phone.
2. After the call, you will need to log it in Salesloft. Go to your Calls page and click the \+ symbol under the Log column for that call. 
   <Frame>
     ![CSM 27](/images/CSM-27.png)
   </Frame>
3. Log your notes in the Salesloft call notes box that pops up, the same way you would for outbound calls.

### **Send inbound calls to voicemail**

If you are busy on another call or in a meeting when you receive an incoming call, you can silence it and send it to voicemail by clicking the X (see screenshot below). You'll be able to later review who called you and if they left a voicemail in your Salesloft call log. You'll also get an email if they left you a voicemail.

</Frame>

## **Text Messages**

If customers (or you) decide to communicate via text, your Salesloft phone number can send and receive text messages. All texting should be done via Salesloft Messenger. Video overview [<u>here</u>](https://www.loom.com/share/f62cfbaa1289408ca026efdaacca4d01 "https://www.loom.com/share/f62cfbaa1289408ca026efdaacca4d01").

### **Outbound texts**

There are two ways to send a text message. Please note that you must have a mobile phone number saved for any customer you wish to text.

1. Click the Messenger icon in the Quick Actions menu in the profile of the customer you’d like to text. 
   <Frame>
     ![CSM 29](/images/CSM-29.png)
   </Frame>
2. Click the Messenger icon in the top navigation bar. _Note: This icon may not appear until you have messaging history._ 
   <Frame>
     ![CSM 30](/images/CSM-30.png)
   </Frame>

### **Inbound texts**

When a customer sends you a text message, you will receive a pop up Chrome notification in Salesloft.

<Frame>
  ![CSM 31](/images/CSM-31.png)
</Frame>

You will also see a red number next to the Messenger icon in the top navigation bar. 

<Frame>
  ![CSM 32](/images/CSM-32.png)
</Frame>

Click on either notification to open the Messenger window and respond to the customer.

You can learn more about texting in Salesloft in [<u>this help article</u>](https://help.salesloft.com/s/article/Messenger-from-Salesloft "https://help.salesloft.com/s/article/Messenger-from-Salesloft").

<Danger>
  **Known issue:** Texts sent and received in Salesloft are not syncing to Salesforce.
</Danger>
