### **Purpose**

This guide aims to provide clear and concise answers to the most commonly asked questions about subscription invoicing and payment processes. By equipping both customers and internal teams with readily accessible information, it aims to reduce response time, improve customer satisfaction, and streamline internal workflows.

### **Target Audience**

This document is designed primarily for Product Support to address property manager inquiries and serves as a reference for the Customer Success team. It ensures consistent and accurate responses to billing, payment, and account-related concerns.

### **Frequently Asked Questions**

  <Accordion title="Monthly Subscription Invoicing and Payment Terms">
    All customers are invoiced on the 10th of each month based on the total active units listed in Latchel at midnight UTC on the last day of the prior month. _(i.e. the February invoice reflects the unit count as of the end of January)._

    Property Managers must maintain accurate unit counts. A reminder email on the 21st of each month allows time for verification and corrections with the Data Team’s assistance. For details on requesting adjustments, see the [<u>Intake Process for Active Unit Adjustment</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/3194486792 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/3194486792").

    Payment terms vary by contract, with the most common being Net 5, or 5 days after the invoice was sent.<br />Customers on automatic payment will have their payments processed on the 14th or 15th of each month.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can customer change their subscription payment method on file?">
    To update a customer’s payment method on file for their monthly subscription invoice, please post in the #accounts-receivable Slack channel and submit a Subscription Invoicing Request workflow, selecting the category "Change Request." For more details on the process, refer to the[<u> SOP: Handling Subscription Billing Inquiries</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/3179446293 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/3179446293")
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do we report wrong unit counts for invoices that have already been posted/paid?">
    To handle a billing dispute, post in the #accounts-receivable Slack channel and submit a Subscription Invoicing Request workflow, selecting the "Billing Dispute" category. Be sure to include the unit count the customer is expecting and either the invoice number in question or at least the invoice date for reference.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do customers download invoice PDFs?">
    Currently, we do not have a billing portal for subscription invoices. However, invoices are sent as PDF attachments to the designated billing point(s) of contact on the 10th of each month. These PDF files can be downloaded directly from the email. If needed, customers can also contact us at [billing@latchel.com](mailto:billing@latchel.com) to request PDFs of specific invoices at any time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I permanently terminate a customer's services?">
    Account Termination Requests are managed by the Customer Success Manager (CSM). If you're a CSM, please refer to the SOP: Account Termination Steps on handling account terminations for detailed guidance. For PS, escalate the request to ps-csm-escalations via the "Escalate to CSM" workflow, ensuring that the correct CSM is tagged for prompt handling.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I temporarily inactivate a customer's services?">
    Not at this time
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I close a customer's account on a future date?">
    Closing a customer's account can only be done by the Customer Success Manager (CSM). If you are the CSM, please set a calendar reminder to terminate the account on the specified date and follow the procedures outlined in the SOP: Account Termination Steps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I update a customer’s billing contact?">
    To update the customer's billing contact, post in the #accounts-receivable Slack channel and submit a Subscription Invoicing Request workflow, selecting the "Change Request" category. Make sure to include the new preferred email address.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
