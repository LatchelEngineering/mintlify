### **Change Management Resources**

- **How do I prevent residents from entering a maintenance request through Propertyware?**
  - Advise the client to complete the following steps to disable the ability for tenants to submit maintenance requests through their Propertyware portal:
    - Step 1: From the main menu across the top of the screen in Propertyware, select **Portals** 
      <Frame>
        ![CSM 42](/images/CSM-42.png)
      </Frame>
    - Step 2: Under **Allow Tenants to Submit Service Requests**, select No and click **SAVE** 
      <Frame>
        ![CSM 43](/images/CSM-43.png)
      </Frame>
  - _Please note: this is only a viable option if Latchel is handling maintenance for ALL units in Propertyware, as this is a global change._
