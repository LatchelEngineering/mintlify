title: "Onboarding Review (60 Minutes)"
---

### **\*Prior to Meeting (15 Minutes)**

- Onboarding Audit 
  - Launch Manager will run through an internal audit of all onboarding checklist items and outstanding Jira tasks and surface any outstanding items.

## **Onboarding Review Goals:**

- Ensure the client has completed all required maintenance configurations so they can begin intaking work orders **PRIOR** to the Launch Call based on the clients go froward maintenance plan.
  - Fully document the maintenance go forward plan in the onboarding notes (Intake Only, Intake \+ 24/7 Emergency Dispatch, etc). 
- Confirm client has completed all CMAs for Assisted Ledgering Rollout (AL contract only)
  - Add GL/Charge Code and document details in SF
  - Add lease addendum to their leasing software
  - Automate monthly GL report if possible 
- Client understands how to customize their resident benefits and has everything they need to confidently roll out to their residents by the Launch Call. 

### **Maintenance Configuration Review (30 Minutes)**

- Final review of 6 key areas of maintenance configuration and system defaults now that the client has had the chance to review and update these settings. 

### **Resident Benefit Rollout (10 Minutes)**

- GL Code
  - Current RBP
    - Confirm if the client has a current RBP. If the answer is yes, determine if they will be adding a separate GL charge for Latchel (recommended) or if they plan on combining the charges. 
    - If they plan on combining the charges, document the GL/Charge Code name and how much they will increase the current charge amount to account for Latchel’s revenue. The increased amount will be documented in SF in the **Resident Charge Set by PM** field and the additional details in the **Resident Charge Additional Information** field so finance understands how to interpret the monthly concession report. 
  - No Existing RBP / New GL Code Required
    - Confirm the client has created the GL code and document the GL code name in the **Resident Charge Additional Information** field and charge amount in the **Resident Charge Set by PM field** in the opportunity in SF. If the client has not created the GL code, allow the client to screen share and walk them through the creation during the call. 
  - Monthly Concession Report
    - If possible, walk the client through customizing a report that shows total collections against the resident amenity GL code.
    - For any software that supports scheduling reports (AppFolio, Rent Manager) walk the client through scheduling the report to [<u>account@latchel.com</u>](mailto:account@latchel.com) for the 20th of each month. 
  - Lease Addendum
    - Confirm leasing software is the same as PMS or document what system they are using if third party (DocuSign etc)
    - Confirm if they will be adding a stand alone addendum or updating lease language. 
    - Review previously shared addendum with client for any questions or feedback. 
- Resident Benefit Resources
  - Review Resident Benefit Resources with the client to ensure they have everything they need to successfully communicate the changes to resident maintenance and drive excitement around the resident benefits. 

### **Customize your Resident Benefits (10 Minutes)**

- The goal for this section is to walk through all of the ways the PM can personalize the resident benefits hub and how they can surface these changes on their website such as:
  - Reorder benefits by pinning to the top
  - Disable any competing benefits (client has a current insurance program, etc)
  - Create a custom benefit
  - Embed the benefits on their website 

### **Schedule Launch Call with Success Manager (5 Minutes)**

- Outline the agenda for the launch call and high level overview of what the client can expect from training and their first business review. 
- Schedule Launch Call with Success Manager. 

### **Introduce Residents to Latchel! (5 Minutes)**

- Ensure Client has everything in place to begin rolling out HA and Maintenance services to all residents.
