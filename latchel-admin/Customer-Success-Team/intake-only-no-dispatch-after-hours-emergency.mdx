---
title: "Intake Only<>No Dispatch (After-Hours Emergency)"
---

### **Brief Description**

- Intake
  - Latchel will intake 100% of resident initiated maintenance requests.
- Emergency Work Orders:
  - After Hours:
    - An email will be sent to the Default Notification User Group with the work order details.
    - Emergency Escalation Contacts(s) will receive a phone call for any emergencies with PD/EH issues.
  - Normal Business Hours:
    - All emergency work orders will bypass troubleshooting and be immediately forwarded to the PM.
    - An email will be sent to the Default Notification User Group with the work order details.
  - For PMs with an active integration, the work order will sync to their PMS for them to work through to completion. 
- Non-Emergency Work Orders (Normal/Urgent/Needs Pre-Approval)
  - All non-emergency work orders will be forwarded to the PM.
    - An email will be sent to the Default Notification User Group with the work order details.
  - For PMs with an active integration, the work order will sync to their PMS for them to work through to completion. 

### **Configurations Required**

- Product Subscription
  - Maintenance - After-Hours Emergency
  - Saas - Disabled 
- Maint. Communication Settings
  - Users and User Groups established
  - Emergency Escalation Contact(s) established
  - Add the following Pre-Dispatch Instructions
    - Please DO NOT dispatch a vendor for any Emergency Work Orders
    - For PD/EH issues, please call the Emergency Escalations Contact List
    - For Non PD/EH issues, please Forward the WO to the PM by clicking "Contact accepted work order"
- Work Order Budget Limits
  - Not required 
- Severity & Responsibility Settings 
  - Ensure Emergency severity settings are clearly defined.
- Service Providers
  - No service providers required for this setup. If service providers are added, they **CANNOT** be prioritized for dispatch. 
- Integration
  - Not required but HIGHLY recommended as the PM will continue to work out of their current maintenance solution. 
- Portfolios
  - Required if they have any unique Maint. Communication Settings or Severity & Responsibility Settings for specific properties/units.
