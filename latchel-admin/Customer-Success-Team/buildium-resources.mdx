### **Change Management Resources**

- **How do redirect tenant maintenance submissions from Buildium to Latchel?**
  - Advise the client to complete the following steps to redirect Buildium maintenance requests to the Latchel maintenance URL.
    - Step 1: Select **Communication** and then click **Resident Center settings**.
      <Frame>
        ![CSM 44](/images/CSM-44.png)
      </Frame>
    - Step 2: Next, select **Requests** and under **Maintenance request redirect**, click **Edit**
      <Frame>
        ![CSM 45](/images/CSM-45.png)
      </Frame>
    - Step 3: Enter your Latchel Residents Work Order Submission URL into the REDIRECT URL field. This can be found in [<u>Portal Settings</u>](https://help.latchel.com/s/article/Configuring-Portal-Settings "https://help.latchel.com/s/article/Configuring-Portal-Settings").
      <Frame>
        ![CSM 46](/images/CSM-46.png)
      </Frame>
  - _Please note: this is only a viable option if Latchel is handling maintenance for ALL units in Buildium, as this is a global change._
