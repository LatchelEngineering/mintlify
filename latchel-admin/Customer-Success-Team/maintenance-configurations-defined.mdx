## **Glossary of Terms**

**After Hours Emergency Service**

- Our After-Hours Emergency service includes 24/7 answering of calls.
- Business hours for emergency dispatch are 5pm-9am (property time) Monday through Friday and all day Saturday and Sunday.
- We also include the following 6 major holidays:
  - New Years
  - Memorial Day
  - Independence Day
  - Labor Day
  - Thanksgiving Day
  - Christmas Day
