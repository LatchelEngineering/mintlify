title: "Intake + 24/7 Emergency + SaaS (Needs Pre-Approval)"
---

### **Brief Description**

- Intake
  - Latchel will intake 100% of resident initiated maintenance requests.
- Emergency Work Orders:
  - The Latchel operations team will attempt to dispatch a preferred service provider for all emergencies. If Latchel is unable to reach a service provider:
    - An email will be sent to the Default Notification User Group with the work order details.
    - Emergency Escalation Contacts(s) will receive a phone call for any emergencies with PD/EH issues.
  - For PMs with an active integration, the work order will sync to their PMS.
- Non-Emergency Work Orders (Needs Pre-Approval)
  - All non-emergency work orders will be surfaced to the PM for Pre-Approval.
    - An email will be sent to the Default Notification User Group with the work order details and request for approval. 
    - If the PM chooses to approve the work order, the work order will then flow through our non-emergency work order automation. 
    - If the PM chooses to Deny the work order, the work order will be canceled. 
  - For PMs with an active integration, the work order will sync to their PMS.

### **Configurations Required**

- Product Subscription
  - Maintenance - 24/7 Emergency
  - Saas - Enabled
- Maint. Communication Settings
  - Users and User Groups established
  - Emergency Escalation Contact(s) established
- Work Order Budget Limits
  - Emergency & Normal soft and hard limits
- Severity & Responsibility Settings 
  - Ensure emergency severity settings are configured correctly and all non-emergencies are set to “Needs Pre-Approval”.
- Service Providers
  - This setup requires prioritization of preferred service providers. 
- Integration
  - Not required for this setup as all work orders will be worked to completion in Latchel. 
- Portfolios
  - Required if they have any unique Maint. Communication Settings, Budget Limits, Service Provider Preferences or Severity & Responsibility Settings for specific properties/units.
