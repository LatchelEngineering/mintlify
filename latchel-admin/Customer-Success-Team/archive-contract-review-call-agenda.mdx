## Purpose

**Contract Review Call Objective:**

The contract review meeting is a meeting between the Account Executive, Launch Manager, and assigned Customer Success Manager. The meeting is used to review and discuss what is required of the client under the contract to ensure adequacy and onboarding checklist effectiveness.
