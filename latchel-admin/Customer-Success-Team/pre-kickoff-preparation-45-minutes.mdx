---

### **Review Details in Salesforce**

- Verify the following information on the Details \> Onboarding Tab
  - Service Start Date
  - Contracted Units 
  - Software
- Verify the following information on the Details \> Contract Tab
  - Primary Decision Maker
  - Primary Latchel User
  - Primary Maintenance User (POC)
  - Any notes in the Contract Modifications section
    - Any modifications to the standard contract/pricing should be surfaced here. 
- Notes and attachments (Contract PDF)
  - Confirm Service Start Date 
  - Review Free Period (if applicable)
  - Review Discount Period/Guarantee Period
  - Confirm subscription and Price Per Unit
- Review notes on the Notes Tab
  - Notes in this tab may vary. Typically we are looking for disco and demo notes. 
  - If no notes exist, reach out to AE to provide notes.

### **Review Client’s Website**

- Locate the client’s website
  - In most cases, the client’s website will be linked on the Account Page. You can surface this from the opportunity by hovering over the account name. 
  - If no website exists, attempt to surface the website URL through a Google search. Alternatively, you can reach out to the AE.
- Search for an “About Us” section if available. Use this page to identify:
  - Company history
  - Team members names and roles
  - General area they service 
  - Types if properties they manage (SFR, MFR, Mixed Portfolio)
- Current Tenants Tab
  - Instructions on how to get in touch with their team for maintenance. 
    - Phone number. 
    - Link to enter a maintenance request. 
    - Link to an online portal
  - Look for any existing Resident Benefits
