### [**<u>Kickoff Call (45 Minutes)</u>**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2728853525/Kickoff+Call+45+Minutes "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2728853525/Kickoff+Call+45+Minutes")

- Call Goals:
  - Identify team members, their roles today, and how they will work with Latchel
  - Confirm understanding of contract (Assisted Ledgering Only)
  - Identify current maintenance process and any potential red flags
  - Identify In-House tech, 3rd party or combination
  - Set target date for launch call/completion of onboarding

### [**<u>Configuration Call (60 Minutes)</u>**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2728493096/Configuration+Call+60+Minutes "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2728493096/Configuration+Call+60+Minutes")

- Call Goals:
  - Confirm client’s understanding of our maintenance process, especially how we interact with residents, vendors and pms for emergencies vs non-emergencies.
  - Review all areas of maintenance configuration, with additional focus on:
    - Service Provider Prioritization - complete example 
    - Severity & Responsibility Settings - Emergency vs Pre-Approval
  - Enable integration (if applicable) and confirm unit count.

### [**<u>Onboarding Review (60 Minutes)</u>**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2728558599/Onboarding+Review+60+Minutes "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2728558599/Onboarding+Review+60+Minutes")

- Call Goals
  - Ensure the client has configured the 6 key maintenance areas to ensure they can roll out Latchel maintenance by the Launch Call and fully document the maintenance go forward plan in the onboarding notes. 
  - Confirm client has completed all CMAs for Assisted Ledgering Rollout (AL contract only)
    - Add GL/Charge Code and document details in SF
    - Add lease addendum to their leasing software
    - Automate monthly GL report if possible 
  - Client understands how to customize their resident benefits and has everything they need to confidently roll out to their residents by the Launch Call.
