---
title: "Desire to Churn Talk Tracks"
---

For each [<u>Churn Category</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2978578449 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2978578449"), CSMs can provide thoughtful and strategic responses aimed at addressing concerns, providing solutions, and retaining the client whenever possible. Here are tailored responses for each scenario:

### **Product Fit/Expectations**

CSM Response: "We understand the importance of having a platform that meets all your property management needs. Let’s discuss the specific features and customizations you're looking for. We might have upcoming updates that align with your requirements or can explore custom solutions to ensure our platform supports your operations effectively."

### **Integration Capabilities**

CSM Response: "Integration capabilities are crucial for a seamless workflow. While we currently support \[integrations capabilities\], we’re open to exploring additional integrations that would benefit your operations. Could we schedule a call to discuss your key integration needs?"

### **Cost/Value Perception**

CSM Response: "We aim to ensure that our service provides significant value relative to its cost. Let's review your use of the platform and identify areas where you can maximize value, including features you might not be utilizing fully. Additionally, we’re open to discussing your current plan and adjusting it to better fit your needs and budget."

### **Customer Support**

CSM Response: "Your experience with our customer support is a top priority for us. We're committed to improving response times and the quality of support. Would you be open to sharing more about your experiences so we can address them directly and enhance our training and resources?"

### **Resident Benefits Adoption**

CSM Response: "It’s important that the benefits we offer align with your residents' needs. Could you share more about what your residents are looking for? This feedback is invaluable as we continually seek to enhance and tailor our resident benefits to meet diverse needs."

### **SP Issues \<\> Preferred**

CSM Response: "We understand the challenges with service provider experiences. Let's explore the specific issues you’ve encountered. We can offer solutions such as training for your in-house maintenance team or adjustments in how we dispatch service providers to better meet your expectations."

### **SP Issues \<\> LCN/LVN**

CSM Response: "Hearing your experiences with our vendor network is concerning and we take this feedback seriously. Let’s identify the gaps in vendor availability and expectations. We're committed to improving our network to ensure it meets the high standards your operations require."

### **User Experience Issues**

CSM Response: "A seamless user experience is critical. We apologize for any navigation difficulties or performance issues you've encountered. We’d greatly appreciate the opportunity to understand these issues in more detail and inform you about the specific steps we’re taking to address them."

### **Competitive Steal**

CSM Response: "We understand the competitive landscape offers various options. It would be valuable to understand what specific features or services you find attractive elsewhere. This insight helps us improve and potentially offer you a competitive package that meets your needs."

### **Changing Business Needs**

CSM Response: "As your business evolves, we want to ensure our platform continues to serve your changing needs effectively. Could we discuss these changes in detail? This will help us explore how we can adapt or develop solutions tailored to your new business model."

### **Regulatory Compliance**

CSM Response: "Compliance with industry regulations is paramount. We’re keen to understand the specific regulations impacting your operations and discuss how we can enhance our platform’s compliance features to meet these requirements."

### **Onboarding and Training**

CSM Response: "We aim for a smooth and informative onboarding process. Let’s discuss the obstacles you faced during onboarding and training. We’re here to provide additional support, resources, or training sessions to ensure your team can utilize our platform to its fullest potential."

### **Contractual Issues**

CSM Response: "Addressing any misalignments in contract expectations is important for our partnership. Let’s discuss your specific concerns regarding the contract or billing. Our goal is to find a flexible solution that aligns with both your needs and regulatory requirements."

 

For each of these scenarios, the key is to listen, understand the client's specific challenges, and offer tailored solutions that address their concerns. This approach not only aims to solve immediate issues but also strengthens the relationship by demonstrating a commitment to the client's success.
