### **Brief Description**

- Intake
  - Latchel will intake 100% of resident-initiated maintenance requests.
- Emergency Work Orders:
  - After Hours:
    - The Latchel operations team will attempt to dispatch a preferred service provider for all after-hours emergencies. If Latchel is unable to reach a service provider:
      - An email will be sent to the Default Notification User Group with the work order details.
      - Emergency Escalation Contacts(s) will receive a phone call for any emergencies with PD/EH issues.
  - Normal Business Hours:
    - All emergency work orders will bypass troubleshooting/dispatch and be forwarded to the PM.
    - An email will be sent to the Default Notification User Group with the work order details.
  - For PMs with an active integration, the work order will sync to their PMS.
- Non-Emergency Work Orders (Normal/Urgent/Needs Pre-Approval)
  - All non-emergency work orders will be forwarded to the PM.
    - An email will be sent to the Default Notification User Group with the work order details.
  - For PMs with an active integration, the work order will sync to their PMS for them to work through to completion. 

### **You should only take action on non-emergency after-hours work orders when:**

1. The PM explicitly asks you to do something (see PM Communication section below).
2. You receive an inbound communication from a resident or service provider, create a task containing all the details, and assign the task to the PM.
3. A system-generated task on the WO is assigned to Latchel Service Ops within normal business hours. If this happens, please handle the task, then submit a #bug-report for the incident.<br /><br /><br />**Configurations Required**

- Product Subscription
  - Maintenance - After-Hours Emergency
  - Saas - Disabled 
- Maint. Communication Settings
  - Users and User Groups established
  - Emergency Escalation Contact(s) established
- Work Order Budget Limits
  - Emergency Soft and Hard Limits only
- Severity & Responsibility Settings 
  - Ensure Emergency severity settings are clearly defined.
- Service Providers
  - This setup requires prioritization of preferred service providers. 
- Integration
  - Not required but HIGHLY recommended as the PM will continue to work out of their current maintenance solution for emergencies during business hours and all non-emergencies. 
- Portfolios
  - Required if they have any unique Maint. Communication Settings, Emergency Budget Limits, Service Provider Preferences or Severity & Responsibility Settings for specific properties/units.
