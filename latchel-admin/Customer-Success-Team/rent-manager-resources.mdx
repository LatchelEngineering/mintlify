### **Change Management Resources**

- **Monthly Reports**
  - You can use the[<u> Report Batches</u>](https://rmxhelp.rentmanager.com/Topics/Express/Reports/Report-Batches/Report-Batches.htm?Highlight=report%20batch#Introduction "https://rmxhelp.rentmanager.com/Topics/Express/Reports/Report-Batches/Report-Batches.htm?Highlight=report%20batch#Introduction") feature in Rent Manager to schedule your monthly reports to Latchel. Please send the following reports to Latchel each month:
    - Report Batch 1: Charge Detail Report
    - Start Date: Current Month Start
    - End Date: Today
    - Charges to Include: Charge Account Name
    - Frequency: Monthly on the 28th
    - Email to: [<u>accounting@latchel.com</u>](mailto:accounting@latchel.com)
  - Report Batch 2: Rent Roll Analysis and Rent Roll & Recurring Charges reports
    - As of Date: Today
    - Frequency: Monthly on the 28th
    - Email to: [<u>ledgering@latchel.com</u>](mailto:ledgering@latchel.com)
- **How do I prevent residents from entering a maintenance request through Rent Manger?**
  - Advise the client to complete the following steps to disable the ability for tenants to submit maintenance requests through their Rent Manager portal:
    - Step 1: Go to your **Admin** page in your Rent Manager account. 
    - Step 2: Open **System Web Preferences** and select **Tenant Web Access** from the list on the left. 
    - Step 3: Select **Service** from the options presented. 
    - Step 4: Uncheck the box labeled **Allow Tenant to Create Service Issues** to disable this feature.
  - _Please note: this is only a viable option if Latchel is handling maintenance for ALL units in Rent Manager, as this is a global change in Rent Manager._
