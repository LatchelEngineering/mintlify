### **Customer Success Top Channels**

1. #customer-success - primary channel for company wide CS announcements or requests
2. #customer-success-team - main ad hoc communication channel for CS, Product Support(PS), Product and Growth. 
3. #cs-team-chat - core CS team. Main communication channel for internal team
4. #cs-pipeline-escalations - used to escalate CS pipeline issues in Salesforce 
5. #customer-updates - updates from recent business reviews and NPS surveys
6. #salesforce-case-support - primary communication channel for CS\<\>PS
   - [<u>Product Support Team vs Customer Success Managers</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/2614755413 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/2614755413")
7. #service-changes - provides visibility when clients express their desire to churn
   - [<u>Off-boarding - Desire to Churn</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2802548740/Offboarding "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2802548740/Offboarding")
8. #task-share-jira-request - for any account update that will take the client longer than 15-20 mins, use this workflow for the Data team to make the bulk updates needed. 
   - [<u>Job Aid: Task Share Jira Improved Process</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/2772140035 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/2772140035")
9. #pd\_cn\_actionset - channel will be used for Pre-Dispatch Instructions and Critical Note approvals as well as discussion on different scenarios and requests.
   - [<u>SOP: Pre-Dispatch Instructions & Critical Notes</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/2818473985 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/2818473985")
10. #ps-dd-requests - use this channel to submit deep dive requests for our specialists to investigate. 
    - [<u>How to: Request a Deep Dive Investigation</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/2543026863 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/2543026863")
11. #ask-answer - great resource for any questions (I use this channel frequently)
12. #help-guide-requests - request new help guides or updates to current help guides 
13. #ledgering-mkt-opt-benefits - main communication channel for all CPT related topics
    - [<u>Customer Profitability</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL")
14. #partnerships-perks - channel to ask questions or share inquiries around current Partnerships for our RBP. 

### **Product Channels**

1. #bug-report - low urgency issue/bug in the Latchel system impacting customers or Latchel's ability to serve customers.
2. #tech-high-sev-escalations - high urgency or business-critical issue/bug in the Latchel system impacting multiple customers or Latchel's ability to serve multiple customers.
3. #product-release - where interesting product features and bug fixes are reported (manually)
4. #product - this channel is for general news, feedback and questions about Latchel's product.
5. #customer-product-requests - primary channel for communicating client feature requests
6. #data-reporting-help-requests - primary channel for requesting custom reports for clients 

### **Finance/Billing**

1. #accounts\_payable - Discussions and questions relating to residential/vendor work orders, invoices and payments.
2. #accounts-receivable - Discussions and questions relating to property managers, billing and payments, and their subscription.
3. #finance - general finance questions; Accidental Damage Typeform responses, Referral Program checks and Resident gift cards.

### **Growth Channels**

1. #growth-team - general communication for the growth team
2. #sales - reporting new sales deals as they close and additional updates  
3. #growth-assets-requests - Use this channel to submit design request tickets via the Asset Request workflow

### **Company Channels**

1. #core-team - main channel for any company wide announcements or updates (days off, town hall topics, company wide changes, etc).
2. #latchel-people-usa - HR announcements that only apply to employees in the United States.
3. #general - non work related company wide information (ie march madness, holiday party, etc).
4. #core-values - place to recognize people for demonstrating Latchel’s core values<br />Be sure to take advantage of the core value slackmoji’s. This is the easiest way to incorporate our core values into daily communication.
   1. :customer-obsession:
   2. :continuous-improvement:
   3. :develop-extraordinary-teams:
   4. :earn-trust:
   5. :think-big:
   6. :deliver\_and\_measure\_results:

We have a ton of social channels for specific interests (#pets, #cooking, #dad\_jokes, #random, many more – if you can’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to create one and post it in #general).
