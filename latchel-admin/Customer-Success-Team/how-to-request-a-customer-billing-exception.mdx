## **Step-by-Step Guide**

<Note>
  **What happened prior to this step?**

  Prior to requesting a discount or concession extension, strategize on ways we can bring product value to the customer first. Without the customer seeing the value Latchel delivers, a discount will only service as temporary bandaid.
</Note>

### **Step 1 - Open the _cs-escalations_ channel in slack and click on the workflow titled _billing exceptions_.**

<Frame>
  ![CSM 62](/images/CSM-62.JPG)
</Frame>

### **Step 3 - As the next step, your direct manager will be notified of the request.**

Hang tight! You’ll receive a response soon from your manager shortly.

<Frame>
  ![CSM 63](/images/CSM-63.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Suggested Scripting:**

##### **Positive response to a discount request**

<Tip>
  I have good news to share. After talking with my manager, we’re able to secure a \[10%\] discount for you \[if you commit to ledgering all new leases and renewals today\].

  This will help ensure that you are profitable with Latchel, and not paying out of pocket by \[date\]. 

  Let me know what you think and whether you have any questions. Let’s jump in a call if it’s more convenient.

  Thanks,

  \[Your name\]
</Tip>

##### **Negative response to a discount request**

<Tip>
  I’m getting back to you regarding the discount you mentioned in your last \[call/emal\]. I discussed this with my team and, unfortunately, we’re unable to provide a discount at this time. \[The pricing plan we discussed in our call is already our best value package and we can’t go any lower than that without doing our product a disservice.\]

  I’m sorry I couldn’t be of more help. Let me know if there’s anything else I can assist you with.

  Thanks,

  \[Your name\]
</Tip>

##### **Non-committal response to a discount request**

<Tip>
  Thanks for your question – I understand that pricing is important and I’d be happy to have a conversation about it.

  \[However, I’d first like to learn more about the challenges you’re facing and how Latche’s services can help. We have various configuration options that may match your needs and I’d like to help you find the best one for \[company name\].\]

  Let’s jump in a quick call sometime this week. Click on {this link} to book an appointment with me.

  Thanks,

  \[Your name\]
</Tip>
