<br />**<u>In this Library, you will find:</u>**

1. **Customer Offboarding** - Includes SOPs required for Offboard customers from the Latchel Console and configure accounts. We have not started to build these SOPs yet
2. **Customer Onboarding** - Includes  SOPs required for Onboarding new customers into the Latchel Console and configure new accounts. The onboarding folder is split into two:
   1. **Customer Data Collection** - Includes SOPs to request and collect information from customers. There is also a sub-folder with links to Customer Success Shared Drive where spreadsheet templates are stored as well as the files we receive back from Property Managers
   2. **Account Creation and Configuration** - Includes SOPs on how to enter the information we received from Property Managers into their accounts in the Latchel Console. It also includes how to configure accounts to customize to requests discussed during onboarding call
   3. **Customer Communication Templates** - Includes templates of messages we send to customers during the onboarding process. This page provides a link to the folder that has these templates. However, it is best to use this SOP as your guide to find the right template [(CS/Admin) Job-Aid: Onboarding Flow & Communications](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/851247114)
   4. **Customer Onboarding Process & Checklist** - It is an SOP that gives an overview of all information we need to collect from Property Managers to onboard them.  It provides links to data collection and data entry SOPs, so it is a good reference to use as a landing page to know all of the information  required to onboard a new customers
3. **Customer Account Management SOPs** - Includes SOPs on how to maintain customer accounts updated, how to spot problems before they impact the property manager, and how to monitor their experience. We have not started to build these SOPs yet
4. **Archived SOPs** - Includes resources that are outdated or no longer relevant to the Customer Success Team. We maintain those in the library in case we need to understand prior processes or decide to restore some of them
5. **Team Set-up & Routine Tasks** - Includes SOPs on how to set-up permissions and supporting softwares, as well on how to do some basic tasks within the team day-to-day. We have not started to build these SOPs yet
