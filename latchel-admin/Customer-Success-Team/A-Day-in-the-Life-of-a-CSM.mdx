## **Daily Checklist**

1. Review Calendar Events 
2. Prepare for Upcoming Meetings, later in the week 
3. Handle Escalated Support Cases (_Morning_)
4. New Customer Launch Calls 
5. New Customer Training Session Calls
6. Ad-Hoc Account Calls and Meetings
7. Draft & Send Recap Emails
8. Document & Action After-Call Action Items
9. Handle Escalated Support Cases (_Afternoon_)

## **Weekly Checklist**

1. Create & Manage Risk Cases
2. Monitor Product Releases (#product-release slack)
3. Create & Manage Data Tasks
4. Inter Meeting: CS Team Talk - take 10 mins to review agenda and add missing topics
5. Inter Meeting: Manager 1:1s
6. Inter Meeting: Bi-Weekly CS Fireside Chats
7. Inter Meeting: Town Hall 
8. Monitor Progress Towards Quarterly Goals
