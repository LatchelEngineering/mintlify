---

## **Kickoff Call Goals:**

- Identify team members, their roles today, and how they will work with Latchel
- Confirm understanding of contract (Assisted Ledgering Only)
- Identify current maintenance process and any potential red flags
  - Identify In-House tech, 3rd party or combination
- Identify client’s goals for Maintenance and Resident Benefits.
- Set target date for launch call/completion of onboarding

### **Introduction (10 Minutes)**

- Identify team members' names, roles with the company, and their level of involvement with Latchel.
- Ensure Key Stakeholders attend onboarding calls and which additional team members should join the launch and training calls with the CSM. 

### **Path to Profitability - Assisted Ledgering Only (5 Minutes)**

- Review billing slide in deck emphasizing the following points:
  - \$9.99 per door, monthly.
  - Auto-payments are generally processed between the 1st-6th day of each month for all doors.
  - We will use the GL report you send us each month to concession your invoice down to ensure you are never paying more than you have collected from your residents.
    - Provide simple example: If you enroll 5 units at \$30 per unit, your first invoice will be concessioned down to \$150
  - Latchel services are designed to generate a profit for Property Managers wishing to increase their profitability, via the industry-leading Resident Amenities. Our clients typically bill residents between \$25-\$50 monthly.
    - Review breakeven with client: If you charge \$30 per month you will break even when ⅓ of your portfolio is enrolled. 

### **Goal Setting (20 Minutes)**

- Describe your current maintenance process:
  - Intake
    - How are residents reaching out for maintenance today? Emergencies and After Hours?
      - If multiple ways, try to narrow down what percentage of requests come through each channel. 
  - Work Order review and dispatch
    - Are any steps taken to troubleshoot with the resident?
    - Who at the company is responsible for dispatching?
    - Are we dispatching to 3rd party service providers, in-house, or a combination of the two?
    - How are service providers being notified of new work orders?
  - Work Order follow up 
    - How do your service providers let you know the work is completed?
    - Do you have any internal timeframes you use to follow up on work orders?
- What are you hoping to improve on with Latchel?
- What Resident Benefits/Perks are you most excited about?

### **Onboarding Roadmap (5 Minutes)**

- Review the agenda for each onboarding call and time commitment for each meeting. 
- Lay out anticipated Launch date based on client bandwidth, shooting for within 10 business days of the kickoff call.
