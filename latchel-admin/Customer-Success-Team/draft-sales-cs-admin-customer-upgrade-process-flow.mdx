## **Objective**

<Note>
  **Purpose**

  The purpose of this document is to provide an overview of the upgrade process flow and provide links to relevant SOPs and Templates used to communicate with customers. All communications templates are located in the [<u>Customer Success shared drive</u>](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1n9ZSyi53tgTt0OK1covgFQEodFn-xh-F "https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1n9ZSyi53tgTt0OK1covgFQEodFn-xh-F").
</Note>

<Note>
  **What is happening prior to this process?**

  Make sure the customer is happy with the service and experience we have delivered to them. Never upgrade an angry, upset, or disappointed customer. 

  **How can we get this data?**

  - The Customer Success team should have the latest information on the customer. Consult with the team before reaching out to the customer. 
  - <u>Please note: </u>Customer Success now has the policy to log customer complaints in Hubspot as a "note" on the Deal. Moving forward, you will be able to check Hubspot for recent complaints.
</Note>

An upgrade is defined as moving from a lower level product to a higher level product (e.g. after hours to Premium).
