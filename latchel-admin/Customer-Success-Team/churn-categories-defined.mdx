title: "Churn Categories Defined"
---

The following is a list of churn categories that will help us better track the different themes we see when clients express their desire to churn. They will also map to the Primary and Secondary(if applicable) churn reasons here: [CS 2024: Churn & Renewals](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ZJLmUtXnnA3UivM__uuTGbNrPLnLzhCEpYTzfBdHFGU/edit?usp=sharing)

- **Product Fit/Expectations:** The client finds the platform’s features insufficient for their unique property management needs or find usability issues that hinder their operations. Also lack of customization. 
- **Integration Capabilities:** Difficulty integrating Latchel with other property management systems or third-party services; lack of integration. 
- **Cost/Value Perception:** The client believes the cost of the service doesn’t match the value they’re receiving.
- **Customer Support:** Slow response times, unhelpful support, or lack of personalized assistance (operations, product support, customer success).
- **Resident Benefits Adoption:** The client feels these benefits do not align with their residents' needs or fail to enhance satisfaction. 
- **SP Issues \<\>Preferred:** Poor service provider experience due to dispatching errors, lack of adoption, in-house maintenance teams etc. 
- **SP Issues\<\>LCN/LVN:** Poor experience with Latchel vendor networks, unrealistic expectations, lack of vendor availability.
- **User Experience Issues:** Difficult navigation, frequent bugs, slow performance, 
- **Competitive Steal:** The availability of more attractive offers from competitors with better features, pricing, or customer service.
- **Changing Business Needs:** The client's business model or needs may evolve in a way that the platform no longer serves their requirements adequately.
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Inability of the platform to comply with new or existing regulations affecting the property management industry.
- **Onboarding and Training:** A difficult onboarding process or lack of adequate training materials can hinder the effective use of the platform.
- **Contractual Issues:** Misaligned contract expectations, regulatory concerns (benefits), unique billing requests.
