**Why?** We need a simple and quick way of assessing how well a customer is doing on ledgering, with standardized criteria and minimum subjectivity.

**What is it used for?** Prioritization and focus. We must shift our focus to the largest value customers, that are not ledgering or on track to being fully paid by month 12.

**How do I find customer profitability scores?** Profitability scores are logged on a daily basis [<u>here</u>](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1fh_c34tUsMygpoYeveXRncstJftEyJhh5QgMzzRvRKM/edit#gid=1314492309 "https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1fh_c34tUsMygpoYeveXRncstJftEyJhh5QgMzzRvRKM/edit#gid=1314492309").

### **How are profitability scores calculated?**

**Monthly Calculation Explained:**

_Year 1 Revenue Projection_

| **Monthly Score** | **Defintion** |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Red Light** | MRR = \$0 |
| **Yellow Light** | MRR at year 1 mark is less than 100% |
| **Green Light** | MRR at year 1 mark is greater than 100% |

**Weekly Calculation Explained:**

| **Weekly Score** | **Definition** |
| :-- | :-- |
| **GREY LIGHT** | No renewals occurred |
| **RED LIGHT** | Renewals occurred, but 0% ledgered |
| **YELLOW LIGHT** | Renewals occurred, between 1% and 99% ledgered |
| **GREEN LIGHT** | Renewals occurred, 100% of leases ledgered |

**Now, let’s dive deeper into the weekly reD Light & Yellow Light scores, and the next steps:**

_For the purpose of this SOP, Grey Light and Green Light scores require no action._

<Frame>

**Yellow Light**

1. **Next Steps:**
   - a. CSM is empowered to leverage finance to push a [<u>notification </u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2584674397/Customer+Profitability+Score+The+Spotlight+Approach#Finance-Notification "#Finance-Notification")to the customer.
   - b. CSM is actively trying to get the customer from a **yellow** score to a **green** score. Here are some sample questions to unblock this customer:
     1. _Do you have any questions about how the ledgering process works?_
     2. _Do you or your staff have any questions about adding the Gl code/charge when a resident signs a new lease?_
     3. _Are you getting any pushback from any owners/residents?_
     4. _Do you need any additional outward-facing materials to help you overcome these objections_
     5. _When do you foresee the next batch of leases coming into Latchel?_

**Red Light**

1. **Next Steps:**
   - **<u>1-2 Consecutive Weeks</u> with a red Light** **:**
     1. CSM is actively trying to get the customer out of this red score
        - _It looks like you had a couple of leases signed on_ {date} _but there was nothing about home assistant. I would have expected them to have had this on their lease. Is there something you needed from us to get these residents ledgered? I’m here to help!_
     2. CSM can leverage finance to push a [<u>notification </u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2584674397/Customer+Profitability+Score+The+Spotlight+Approach#Finance-Notification "#Finance-Notification")to the customer. Refer to [<u>these steps </u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2584674397/Customer+Profitability+Score+The+Spotlight+Approach#Non-Compliant-Request "#Non-Compliant-Request")on how to request a non-compliant notice from Finance.
   - **<u>1 Month</u> with a RED Light** **:**
     1. The Assisted Ledgering Reporting team (@Lauyumi Michelle Rojas Brindis (Unlicensed) ) identifies customers in the red for 1 month and submits a non-compliance notice to notify the CSM and Finance. [<u>Learn how here.</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2584674397/Customer+Profitability+Score+The+Spotlight+Approach#Non-Compliant-Request "#Non-Compliant-Request")
     2. Finance (@Lynette Olanosa (Unlicensed) ) prepares a [<u>notice </u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2584674397/Customer+Profitability+Score+The+Spotlight+Approach#Finance-Notification "#Finance-Notification")to the customer to inform them that they are no longer eligible to receive a concession on all future invoices.
        - The CSM has **72 hours** to request an extension period of the concession timeframe which will be approved or denied by the profitability committee. [<u>Learn how to submit the extension request here.</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2584674397/Customer+Profitability+Score+The+Spotlight+Approach#Extension-Period "#Extension-Period")
          1. If the extension is **approved**, Finance will acknowledge the request and inform the CSM the request has been approved. 
          2.  If the extension is **denied or never requested**, a Finance notice goes out to the customer. 
             - The CSM is CC’d on the email and responds to quickly get this out of this situation
