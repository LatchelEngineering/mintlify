\*\*Onboarding Timeframes and Service Start Dates. \*\*

- Onboarding consists of 3 calls and takes roughly 3 weeks to complete, starting from the date of the Kickoff Call. Once onboarding concludes, the CSM will also lead the client through 2-3 weeks of training and office hours (as needed).
- Multiple internal departments rely on an accurate Service Start date to ensure we can accurately plan labor needs, forecast financials, and bill clients’ monthly subscriptions. 
- Clients also rely on this date as oftentimes, they have another maintenance solution in place and will lose service on that date, or have already begun planning the transition with their team. 
- **The Kickoff Call must be scheduled AT A MINIMUM 3 weeks in advance of the Service Start Date.** 

**Onboarding Call Preparation/Expectations**

The CS Team relies heavily on information collected from the Sales team to prepare for the Kickoff Call. The following information is required for a successful client kickoff:

- Salesforce Opportunity
  - Details\>Onboarding Tab
    - Service Start Date
    - Contracted Units
    - Current Software
  - Details\>Contract Tab
    - Primary Decision Maker
    - Primary Latchel User
    - Primary Maintenance Contact
    - Contract Modifications
      - Any and all modifications to the standard contract/pricing MUST be surfaced here.
  - Notes
    - Disco Call Notes
    - Demo Call Notes
    - Any additional notes that will help CS prepare for the kickoff call
      - Client’s current market
      - Service Provider Setup (In-house, 3rd party, both)
      - Goals for Maintenance 
        - Any current pain points
        - Current maintenance solution
      - Goals for Benefits
        - Do they have an existing RBP?
        - Lease renewal schedule (do 90% of leases renew in 1-2 months of the year?)
        - How much does the client plan on charging?
      - How well does the PM understand how Latchel will interact with the PM, Vendor, owners, and Residents?
      - Did the AE make any specific promises around Settings, Configurations, or Integrations?
      - How well does the PM understand the path to profitability with Latchel?

  - Notes & Attachments
