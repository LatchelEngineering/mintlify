title: "(In-Progress/DRAFT) How To Handle LCN Vendors that Charge Residents On The Spot"

---

**Context:** When a Latchel vendor completes a job the amount owed to the vendor is processed through the Latchel portal after the vendor submits an invoice. There are times when the Latchel vendor is unfamiliar with our invoicing process and takes payment from the resident directly. If this happens, we will take the necessary steps to turn around the resident's experience.

<Info>
  For Property Manager-responsible jobs, Latchel will send an automated text to the resident that states they should **not** pay the vendor that visits their property directly. This payment will be handled through Latchel. _(tech in progress)_
</Info>

1. If the resident pays the vendor, the PM should be made aware.
   - **Operations (R)**: If you receive an inquiry from a _resident_, inform them that you will be escalating this matter to **Customer Success**.
     - How to: Escalate to Product Support
   - **Operations (PM)**: If you receive an inquiry from a _Property Manager_, inform them that you will be escalating this matter to **Customer Success**.
     - How to: Escalate to Product Support
   - **Customer Success**: If you receive an escalation from a _resident or Property Manager_, confirm the following information in the system before moving forward:
     - The work order is deemed "Property Manager Responsible" _(this must be true)_
     - The resident must be an active resident on an active property in Latchel _(this must be true)_
       1. If this is **confirmed**, move forward with collecting the following information:
          - An itemized receipt _(this is required)_
          - Contact information for the qualified professional who performed the work _(this is required)_
       2. If you are in communication with the resident up until this point, reach out to the Property Manager to notify them of this open query. Provide them with the
          - Summary of the issue
          - Information we are requesting from the resident
          - Next steps _(expectation setting)_
            - If our finance team approves this refund, (1) the resident will be reimbursed, and (2) Latchel will add a line item to the Property Manager's next invoice for the charge.
            - If our finance team denies the refund, (1) the resident will be informed by Latchel, and (2) Latchel will not process any refunds.
     - Once this information is **confirmed** and you've notified the Property Manager, move forward with step #2.
2. Request a Refund
   - [How to: Submit a Refund Request](/latchel-admin/Customer-Success-Team/how-to-submit-a-refund-request)
3. (Finance) We add the cost to the monthly invoice for the PM
