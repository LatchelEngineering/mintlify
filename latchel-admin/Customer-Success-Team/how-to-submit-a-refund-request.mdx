## **Refunds**

1. Go to #Accounts-payable Slack Channel
2. Click Shortcut \> WO Refunds Form

<Frame>
  ![CSM 59](/images/CSM-59.png)
</Frame>

3. Fill out the Refund Form

<Note>
  **Note**: If you are aware the customer is contacting us to follow-up on a request that has already been submitted, include in the “Reason for refund request” that this is a follow-up request
</Note>

<Frame>
  ![CSM 60](/images/CSM-60.png)
</Frame>

4. Click **submit**.
