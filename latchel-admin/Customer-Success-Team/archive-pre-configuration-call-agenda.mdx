## Purpose

**Pre-Configuration Objective:**

Collect enough information to pre-plan the format of the client's account and give them confidence and excitement at Latchel's ability to achieve their success criteria. CSM runs this meeting with the Account Executive.

---

## Agenda

### High-Level Intro from Account Executive

- AE kicks off the call & greets the customer
- High-level intro of who the Customer Success Manager is and how this individual will manage their account
- AE recaps the client's high-level goal that was previously discussed
- AE identifies 1 or 2 success metrics for that client
- AE solidifies the value and key benefits of Latchel with the client

---

### Greeting from Customer Success Manager

- AE hands the call off to the Customer Success Manager
- Customer Success Manager greets the client

---

### Pre-Implementation Review

Customer Success Manager reviews the pre-configuration form with the client to understand their requirements, tying each question into the clients success criteria.

Pre-Configuration Risk Feedback & Best Practices Guideline

---

### Database Access

Customer Success Manager sets Latchel up as a user inside of the PMs database with RESTRICTED access. Our user will need access to service provider, resident, and property reporting. Document credentials.

- Pushback? Offer to screen share while you export data
- Pushback? Ask PM to export data on the call.
- Pushback? Discuss the data sets required for implementation.

---

### Questions for Latchel

Pause and take this moment to ask the client if they have any questions about the topics discussed.

---

### Set Contract Start Date & Get Agreement From Client

While the client is on the phone, explain time commitment to get setup at set attainable go-live date.

Get agreement from the client that they will be able to put in the time to launch on time. (Otherwise we will start charging them based on the minimum contract fee.)

---

### Customer Success Handoff to AE

- Customer Success Manager will pass the client back to the AE
- AE follows up with the next steps to get the contract delivered & signed
- AE books the kickoff meeting
