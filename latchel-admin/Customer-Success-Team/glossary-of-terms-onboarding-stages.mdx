title: "Glossary of Terms: Onboarding Stages"
groups: ["latchel"]
---

| **Salesforce Opportunity Stage** | **Stage Definition** |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Stage: Need Sales Help** **Substage: Client Disengaged** | Client is no longer engaged. Implementation Consultant has made  2-3 attempts via email and phone to re-engage the client. The Account Executive owns the opportunity until the client re-engages or the opportunity is updated to Close Lost. |
| **Stage: Need Sales Help** **Substage: Product Fit/Expectations** | Client has product requests that are incompatible with the core functionality of Latchel or has unrealistic expectations of the level of service Latchel provides. The Implementation Consultant will work with the Account Executive to reset client expectations, and if successful, reschedule the kickoff call. If unsuccessful, the Account Executive will move the opportunity to Closed Lost and record the churn. |
| **Stage: Need Sales Help** **Substage: Contract Questions/Concerns** | Client does not agree to the terms of their contract or has contract questions or concerns they would like addressed before moving forward with Launch. The Account Executive owns the opportunity and will work with the client to either reset expectation for the current contract, provide an updated contract, or move the opportunity to Closed Lost and record the churn. |
| **Stage: Kickoff Scheduled** | Opportunity is moved into this stage when the implementation fee has been paid and the Kickoff Call has been scheduled. |
| **Stage: Configuration Scheduled** | Opportunity moves into this stage once the Kickoff Call has been completed and the Account Configuration Call has been scheduled. |
| **Stage: Onboarding Review Scheduled** **Substage: Configuring Account** | Opportunity moves into this stage once the Configuration Call has been completed AND there are outstanding Jira tasks. |
| **Stage: Onboarding Review Scheduled** **Substage: Onboard Audit** | Opportunity moves into this stage once the Configuration Call has been completed AND there are NO outstanding Jira tasks. |
| **Stage: Recently Launched (No Cadence)** **Substage: Training** | Client has fully completed Onboarding, is Live, and has entered the training phase of Launch. The opportunity will be moved to **Live\<\>Training** once the Onboarding Review call has been completed. |
| **Stage: Recently Launched (No Cadence)** | Client has completed Onboarding and Training and has been transitioned to the CSM. |
