title: "How to Log a Call in Salesforce"
---

_The 'Log an Event' button creates a completed activity record for Customer Success to document communications. This data can be transformed into prescriptive actions that result in more meetings, opportunities, and revenue!_

## Instructions

**Video Demo:** [Recently Viewed | Accounts | Salesforce - 12 August 2022](https://www.loom.com/share/35dc10c756d64eecb8bdca4003f753db)

**Step-by-Step:**

1. Access the **Customer** record for the Management company in Salesforce
2. On the right-hand side of the Customer record, click **New Event**
3. Select the **Call or Meeting Type**. Refer to the Call Types and definitions [<u>here</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2508914721/How+to+Log+a+Call+in+Salesforce#call "#call").
4. Select the appropriate **disposition**. Refer to call dispositions and definitions [<u>here</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/2508914721/How+to+Log+a+Call+in+Salesforce#dispo "#dispo").
5. Add the **Attendees**.
6. Summarize the call in the **Description** field and paste a link to the Gong recording.
7. Click **Save.**

## **<u>Call or Meeting Types & Definitions</u>**

|  | **Call or Meeting Type** | **Definition** |
| :-: | :-- | :-- |
| 1 | Launch | Launch Manager → CSM handoff call |
| 2 | Training Session | Week 1-3 Training Session |
| 3 | Business Review | Executive Business Review |
| 4 | Quick Chat | Recurring account meetings Ad-hoc calls |
| 5 | Cancellation | Client deep dive call to (1) find an appropriate resolution, and (2) get the client all the information needed to successfully move forward. |
| 6 | Explodemo | Upgrade demo |
| 7 | Contract Signing | A call to discuss the contract and next steps to finalize the upgrade |

## **<u>Call Dispositions & Definitions</u>**

|  | **Disposition** | **Definition** |
| :-: | :-- | :-- |
| 1 | Busy | If you try to make a telephone call and get a busy signal. |
| 2 | Left Voicemail | You were not successful in talking to the person so you left a message |
| 3 | Wrong Number | You dialed an incorrect telephone number |
| 4 | Connected Call | When you called **out of the blue** and were able to connect with the client |
| 5 | No Answer/No Show | The client did not answer or join a **scheduled** meeting |
| 6 | Meeting Completed | A scheduled meeting has concluded |
| 7 | Meeting Rescheduled | The client asked to reschedule on the call |
| 8 | Canceled | The client canceled the scheduled meeting in advance or over the phone, upon joining the call |
