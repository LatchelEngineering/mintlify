## How Do Integrations Benefit Our Customers?

An integration between a customer's primary property management software and Latchel saves time and improves quality of service.

**Without an integration**, a property manager must manually copy/paste work order details, pictures, and files between systems, and update resident/property information in two places.

**With an integration**, work orders, pictures, and files automatically sync from Latchel back to the primary software, and any changes to resident or property data in the platform automatically reflect in Latchel.

## How Often Does Information Sync?

**Platform to Latchel**

- On first activation, a full synchronization of all properties and residents is triggered automatically (the "initial sync")
- Scheduled syncs occur every hour, at the 5-minute mark (e.g. 08:05 AM)
- Scheduled syncs can take up to 45 minutes to complete
- Only data updated in the previous 72 hours is considered

**Latchel to Platform**

- Work orders are generally synced on creation
- Files are synced when the work order is completed
