---
title: "Failed Work Order Syncs"
---

During a Chrome plugin sync, one of the operations performed is syncing newly created work orders from Latchel to the Appfolio platform. This article covers why work orders fail to sync and how to fix them.

## What Are the Ways a Work Order Can Fail to Sync?

### Unrecognized Resident

**Description** When Latchel tries to create the WO in Appfolio, the resident information is not found in the Appfolio system. Usually caused by the resident or call center associate providing incorrect information during work order creation.

**How to Fix** Research the resident report for the customer. Find a resident who matches the details of the resident in the work order. Find that resident in Latchel and re-assign the work order to that resident.

### Property Errors

**Error Types**

- No units associated with `ref_property_id`
- Unable to find property with `ref_unit_id`
- Unable to find property with `ref_property_id`

**Description** Latchel is unable to find a property with a matching `ref_property_id`.

**How to Fix**

1. Research the Latchel database for properties/units with a similar address and compare to the Appfolio report
2. Problems can be caused by duplicate properties in either platform
3. Properties/units can be merged or a missing ref ID can be added
4. It may be necessary to connect with the customer to validate the correct data

<Warning>
  We should not have two properties/units with a matching ref ID for the same PM company.
</Warning>
