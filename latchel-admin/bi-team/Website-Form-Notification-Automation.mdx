<Note>
The purpose of this tool is primarily to achieve the following objectives:

1. **Notify and Highlight New MQLs:**
   - Instantly alert the team about new Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) and any other forms submitted on the website.
   - Ensure visibility of new form submissions to the relevant team members.
2. **Efficient Lead Distribution:**
   - Facilitate the seamless and proper distribution of leads to Sales Development Representatives (SDRs).
   - Assign leads to the appropriate SDR based on predefined criteria, ensuring a streamlined process.
3. **Redirect Misplaced Form Content:**
   - Identify and redirect incorrectly submitted form content to alternative communication channels.
   - Minimize errors by rerouting misplaced information, maintaining the integrity of the data.
4. **Duplicate Prevention in Salesforce:**
   - Implement measures to prevent the creation of duplicate records in Salesforce.
   - Enhance data accuracy by avoiding unnecessary duplication, resulting in a cleaner and more reliable database.
</Note>

## High-Level Process Flowchart:

<Check>
This flowchart illustrates the comprehensive process of the Website Form Notification Automation, a recurring task executed every 30 minutes. At specific intervals, such as 11:10, 11:40, 12:10, 12:40, etc the main automation script is triggered. This high-level overview captures the sequential steps involved in notifying the team of new Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs), distributing leads to Sales Development Representatives (SDRs), redirecting misplaced form content, and preventing duplicate entries in Salesforce.

All the submissions that belong to current active customers (Property Managers) are converted into Cases, the ones that are matched with active vendors and tenants are notified in [#sf-duplicate-check](https://app.slack.com/client/T2M052QDC/C062Z2ZQJ23), and finally all the remaining ones (successful submissions) are notified in [#mql-notifications](https://app.slack.com/client/T2M052QDC/C061AU2RS11).

Why do we notify in [#sf-duplicate-check](https://app.slack.com/client/T2M052QDC/C062Z2ZQJ23)? Because, by the time the automation runs, the Account and Contact are already created in Salesforce, what we do is delete those records in Salesforce automatically, and this channel is the only record left that a vendor or tenant filled out a form. One of these submissions may be legit, so it's important they are also checked.
</Check>

## SDR Round Robin Process Explanation

<Check>
The SDR Round Robin Process operates based on the Rating value, leveraging a mechanism to ensure a fair and balanced distribution of leads among Sales Development Representatives (SDRs). In this process, we have established four distinct groups, each corresponding to a specific tier based on the lead's Rating and an additional fifth group designated for submissions where the contact is not located in Salesforce (Hubspot >Salesforce sync issue).

For a detailed overview of lead distribution results, please refer to the following spreadsheet: [2023-10-11 Website Forms Last IDs - Google Sheets](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/198969sg8Xses_X-GN8B1n2PJR0_PC2SaxZD5DI2-aQc/edit#gid=1035354374)
