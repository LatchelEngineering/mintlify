## Description

During the Discovery meeting call, the SDR qualifies the prospect and attempts to schedule a Demo meeting with the AE. This Flow shows a list of advice ¹ to the SDRs to help them to achieve the objectives and also, shows a template that helps the Rep assess whether the prospect is a good fit for our services. The SDR has to manually enter essential information into the template. ²

This Flow has a Subflow that referenced a “Master Flow” named Qualification Playbook and this is the one that has the template mentioned in the previous paragraph. QuasacascascaacsascascaslifiQcation Playbook
