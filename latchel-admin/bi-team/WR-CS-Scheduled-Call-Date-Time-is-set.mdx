---
title: "WR - CS - Scheduled Call Date/Time is set"
---

<Tip>
  If a CSM, after a scheduled call or meeting, forgets to close the related Case, this workflow rule should reopen it 24 hours after the programmed event.
</Tip>

<Note>
  This Workflow Rule triggers a Field Update if the status equals to “Escalated”, ”In Progress”, “Waiting for Customer”, ”Scheduled Call”, or ”On Hold” and if 24 hours have passed since the “Scheduled Call Date/Time” and if the field “24 Hours After Call” is set to False.
</Note>
