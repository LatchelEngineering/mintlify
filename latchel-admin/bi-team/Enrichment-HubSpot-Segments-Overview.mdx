## General Filter Criteria

- Contact General Criteria
  - NB Result is any of Valid
- Company General Criteria
  - Active Customer is none of Yes or is empty

## Current IAP and IPP Conditions

<Note>
  Description: This section lists the current criteria defining our Ideal Account Profile and Ideal Prospect Personas.
</Note>

- IAP Criteria:
  - Property Structure Type is any of Multi Family or Single Family
  - State/Region Code is not equal to any of Non-Continental, Non-US, or Overseas Territory
  - PMS Software is any of RentVine, Buildium, PropertyWare, Yardi, Appfolio, or Rent Manager
  - Number of employees is greater than or equal to 4
  - Number of employees is less than 20
  - Estimated Unit Count is greater than or equal to 50
  - Company Type is none of Not a PM Company or is empty
- IPP Criteria:
  - Job Title (Category) is any of Broker/Owner, Property Manager, or Director of Maintenance

## Current Enrichment Conditions

<Note>
  Description: This section lists the current enrichment criteria for our contacts and companies.
</Note>

- Contact Enrichment Criteria:

- Company Enrichment Criteria:
  - Property Structure Type is known
  - State/Region Code is known
  - PMS Software is known
  - Website URL is known
  - Phone number is known
  - Estimated Unit Count is known
