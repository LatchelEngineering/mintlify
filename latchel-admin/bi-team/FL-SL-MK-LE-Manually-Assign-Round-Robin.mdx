<Note>
  **Summary:**

  For non-demo requests inbound lead distribution through a round robin. This flow re assigns certain Contacts to another SDR. It should be used for cases where the MQLs have not been touched in more than 7 days since their assignment.

  The report that you can check to see the list of those MQLs with no activity in the last 7 days is the following: [Salesforce Report](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Report/00O5e000008FdogEAC/view)
</Note>

## Trigger

To trigger this Flow, you should go to a Contact Page under the Pardot tab and click on the “Round Robin to SDR” button. ( SF Admin User required)
