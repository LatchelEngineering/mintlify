<Note>
**Purpose of this document**: The purpose of this article is to explain the criterias used for the trigger that will send the template to the customer each time a new Email-To-Case is generated.
</Note>

<Note>
**Important comment**: Every rule that has been added in this formula has a correspondant rule in the “Case Assignment Rule”. The purpose of this is to not only redirect spam Cases to “7. All Spam Cases” but also to not send an Auto-Response to those senders.
</Note>

## FORMULA CRITERIA

```
(ISPICKVAL(Origin, "Email"))
&&
NOT(
OR(CONTAINS(SuppliedEmail, "@latchel.com"),
CONTAINS(SuppliedEmail, "@quadcity.mailer"),
CONTAINS(SuppliedEmail, "@mail.salesforce.com"),
CONTAINS(SuppliedEmail, "@salesforce.com"),
CONTAINS(SuppliedEmail, "@rpmin009.mailer"),
CONTAINS(SuppliedEmail, "@invested.mailer")
)
)
&&
NOT(
OR(SuppliedEmail = "elena.stewart@elenastewart.com",
SuppliedEmail = "mail@managebuilding.com",
SuppliedEmail = "donotreply@managebuilding.com",
SuppliedEmail = "noreply@propertyware.com",
SuppliedEmail = "donotreply@appfolio.com",
SuppliedEmail = "quickbooks@notification.intuit.com",
SuppliedEmail = "events@loom.com",
SuppliedEmail = "no-responder@mail1.alegra.com",
SuppliedEmail = "donotreply@rentmanager.com",
SuppliedEmail = "report@nmccentral.com"
)
)
&&
NOT(
OR(CONTAINS(Subject, "Typeform: New response for Home Assistant"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "PMI Elevation - Building/Unit"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "Your video has been viewed"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "TROUBLE E-MAIL SUBSCRIBER"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "RESTORE E-MAIL SUBSCRIBER"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "RedSail Property Mangement"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "Tenant Portal Access Request"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "Weekly AppFolio Community Digest"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "Move out date requested by"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "Bell Properties"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "Bank Account Information Collected from"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "Your Userpilot Weekly Digest"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "New Nomad Owner Details for Latchel"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "New Nomad Property Activation"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "New Latchel Preferred Vendor Request!"),
CONTAINS(Subject, "Scheduler Reports")
)
)
&&
NOT(
AND(
CONTAINS(Subject, "New work order for"),
CONTAINS(SuppliedEmail, "@v2properties.ma")
)
)
```

This formula listed all the different email senders that, based on the experience, do not require sending an auto-response email. Most of them are ‘Do not reply’ email addresses from customers or email addresses which doesn’t correspond to Customers (events@loom.com). Another part of the formula is listing several email subjects that can easily identify that they do not need an auto-response. (Subject, "Your video has been viewed).

The Auto-Response Rule will work for all the scenarios that do not fall into the mentioned rule.

The email template used in the Rule is the following and is going to be sent from that email: Success Team (success+auto-response@latchel.com) and the subject will be Latchel: Case {{{Case.CaseNumber}}}

**Template of the Response:**

Hi,

Thanks for contacting Latchel Customer Success for your inquiry.

We strive to provide excellent service and will respond to your request as soon as possible.

In the meantime, feel free to check out our help center to help answer any questions and resolve issues:

[Latchel Help Center](https://latchel.com/latchel-help-center/)

Sincerely,

Latchel Customer Success
