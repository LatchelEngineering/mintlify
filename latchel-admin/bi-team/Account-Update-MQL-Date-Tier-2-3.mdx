## Description

This flow is about to be able to label inbound leads more effectively, in specific Tier2 and Tier3. Tier1 comes into the normal Flow because they requested us something directly.

The reason for this flow is that the normal inbound flow will insert the MQLDate automatically but there is a queue setup for Tier2 and 3 and this is the way we can add the MQLDate here for easy reporting.

Tier1: Basically, they want a Demo 

Tier2: Some level of interest indicated but not necessarily directed. 

Tier3: Contacts that are not responding to our Email Campaigns but they showed a level of engagement by clicking on links that made us think that they are needing some kind of need around this. It is better than a cold lead. QuasacascascaacsascascaslifiQcation Playbook
