title: "How cancel a subscription in HubSpot"
---

## A. Cancel the subscription from the Deal record

1. Go to the deal record associated with the subscription.
2. On the right side, under the Quotes section, you will see the Subscriptions section. Open the subscription.

   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/DA-45.png" alt="Subscriptions section on the Deal record" />
   </Frame>
3. Click on Actions on the panel that appeared on the right side

   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/DA-46.png" alt="Actions panel" />
   </Frame>
4. Click on Cancel Subscription

   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/DA-47.png" alt="Cancel Subscription option" />
   </Frame>
5. Confirm the cancellation, the customer will be notified.

   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/DA-48.png" alt="Confirm cancellation" />
   </Frame>
6. This is the notification email the customer will receive.

   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/DA-49.png" alt="Cancellation notification email" />
   </Frame>

## B. Cancel the subscription from the 'Subscriptions' list

1. Go to the ['Subscriptions' list](https://app.hubspot.com/contacts/23701847/objects/0-69/views/all/list) and find the subscription you want to cancel
2. Click on the title
3. Click on Actions on the panel that appeared on the right side

   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/DA-50.png" alt="Actions panel" />
   </Frame>
4. Click on Cancel Subscription

   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/DA-51.png" alt="Cancel Subscription option" />
   </Frame>
5. Confirm the cancellation, the customer will be notified.

   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/DA-52.png" alt="Confirm cancellation" />
   </Frame>
6. This is the notification email the customer will receive.

   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/DA-53.png" alt="Cancellation notification email" />
   </Frame>
