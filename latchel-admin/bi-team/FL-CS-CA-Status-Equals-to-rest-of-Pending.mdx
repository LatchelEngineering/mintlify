<Tip>
  The goal is to not have a Case in any of the statuses “Escalated”, or “Scheduled Call” for more than 5 days.
</Tip>

<Note>
  This Workflow Rule triggers a Field Update when the Case field “Status” is equal to “Escalated”, or “Scheduled Call” for more than 5 days.
</Note>
