## What is the Product Release?

PMs, Admins, and Service Providers now have the ability to see a Service Provider's Latchel work orders in a calendar view on the company and user levels.

<Frame>
  ![Service Provider calendar view](/images/Eng-124.png)
</Frame>

## What was the Previous State?

Previously, there was no calendar view of a Service Provider's work orders. The only way to see them was in a list view (see screenshot below).

<Frame>
  ![Previous list view of Service Provider work orders](/images/Eng-125.png)
</Frame>

## Who Is Impacted?

All Latchel Users. See section below for details on what each user type sees.

## When Is It Relevant?

The Service Provider calendar is relevant for the following groups:

- **PMs/Admins/Latchel Ops** - These users can see Service Provider calendars on the company level through the Service Provider company page and the user level through the Service Provider User page.
- **Primary Service Provider** - The Primary Service Provider at a Service Provider Company will see the entire company calendar by default and has the option to filter by user. The calendar is housed in their public screen.
- **Non-Primary Service Provider** - Non-Primary Service Providers at a Service Provider Company will only be able to see their own calendar.

#### SP Company Calendar View for PM/Admin/Latchel Ops

1. Go to a SP Company profile page and look for a tab that says "Calendar" (see orange arrow in screenshot below).

<Frame>
  ![SP Company Calendar tab](/images/Eng-126.png)
</Frame>

2. As the default, the calendar tab contains all the Latchel work orders assigned to all SP Users at that SP company for the current calendar week. Important details include:
   - There is the option to go to past or future weeks or to filter for one or more SP Users.
   - Each individual user on this default view will have a different color.
   - Each appointment square has the SP User assigned, the WO title, and the street addresses/city/zip code.
   - The calendar view will be in the local time of the person viewing it. So if the PM who is looking at it is in ET, the calendar will be in ET regardless of what timezone the SP company is in.

<Frame>
  ![SP Company default calendar view](/images/Eng-127.png)
</Frame>

3. On the SP Company level calendar, you have the option to filter by specific user.
   - Click the "Filters" button at the top right of the calendar (see orange arrow in the screenshot below).
     <Frame>
       ![Filters button](/images/Eng-128.png)
     </Frame>
   - This will then show a field. When you click in it, it'll give you a dropdown with pre-set list of users to choose from or you can type in the user name.
     <Frame>
       ![User dropdown filter](/images/Eng-129.png)
     </Frame>
   - You have the option of choosing one or more user names to filter for.
     <Frame>
       ![Multiple users selected in filter](/images/Eng-130.png)
     </Frame>
4. If you'd like to see more info about the work order, click on the appointment square. A pop-up will appear with the following info:
   - SP User Assigned
   - WO Title
   - WO Scheduled Date/Time
   - WO Number
   - WO State
   - WO Address
   - Clicking on the WO info will take you to the WO page.

<Frame>
  ![Eng 131](/images/Eng-131.png)
</Frame>

### **Exception for PMs**

While Admins and Latchel Ops will be able to see all work orders on any SP calendar, PMs have some restrictions. For WOs that are not associated with their PM company, PMs will be able to see that the SP is unavailable for a block of time. However, they will not see any information other than “unavailable” and the block will be a grayed out color (see screenshot below). Examples of where this applies are:

- Their Preferred SP is a Preferred SP for other PM companies.
- They use an LCN SP who services many different PM companies.

<Frame>
  ![Eng 135](/images/Eng-135.png)
</Frame>

### **Printing the Calendar View**

For all PM/Admin/Latchel Ops views, there is an option to see a print view and/or print the calendar on a week by week basis.

- Press the “Print View” button at the top of the calendar view.

<Frame>
  ![Eng 136](/images/Eng-136.png)
</Frame>

- This will show you a larger print view version of the SP calendar for the week you have chosen and whichever SP users you filtered for. In the screenshot below, we didn’t filter for any specific users so it shows all the Latchel WOs for the SP company.

<Frame>
  ![Eng 137](/images/Eng-137.png)
</Frame>

- If you’d like to print this, press the “Print” button at the top of this print view. See orange arrow in the screenshot below.

<Frame>
  ![Eng 138](/images/Eng-138.png)
</Frame>

- This will then trigger you to print the calendar view for the week.

</Frame>
