## **What is the Latchel Vendor Network (LVN)?**

The Latchel Vendor Network describes a group of service provider (i.e. vendor) companies that Latchel has a special arrangement with. In return for being offered real work orders, LVN vendors agree to:

- use Latchel technology to interact with all work orders
- get paid exclusively via Latchel’s direct deposit payout technology
- guarantee all technicians are vetted, licensed, bonded and insured
- offer free recalls to Latchel customers

## **How can I tell if a vendor is part of the LVN?**

When viewed on a work order page, an LVN service provider will have a “Latchel Vendor Network” tag next to their name.

## **What’s the difference between LVN and LCN?**

LVN providers exclusively use Latchel’s direct deposit payout technology, and all technicians in the LVN are guaranteed to be vetted, licensed, bonded, and insured. They also offer free recall services for any issues, ensuring higher service quality and accountability. Additionally, LVN providers operate through a central dispatcher who coordinates all work orders, ensuring the best technician is assigned for each job. In contrast, LCN do not guarantee vetted technicians or free recall services. The LCN operational model is more varied and flexible, without a central dispatcher managing the process. Our Latchel associates find LCN service providers through search tools such as google, yelp, etc.

Below is a quick table to describe some of the differences between the Latchel Vendor Network and the Latchel Contractor Network

| **Category** | **LVN Service Provider** | **LCN Service Provider** |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Uses Latchel technology to interact with work orders? | Yes | Yes |
| Getting Paid | Exclusively using Latchel’s direct deposit payout technology | Most often via issued card but also can be paid through Latchel’s direct deposit payout tech. |
| Vetting | Guarantee all technicians are vetted, licensed, bonded and insured | No guaranteed vetting |
| Recalls | offer free recalls to Latchel customers | No guaranteed recalls |
| Used for emergencies? | not yet! | Yes, if customer settings allow it |
| Operational model | A central dispatcher receives all Latchel work orders and determines the best technician. Central dispatcher acts as the “bridge” between techs in the field and Latchel. | Varied |
