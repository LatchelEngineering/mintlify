## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1 - Identify an invoice that requires manual payment

1. Access the invoices page in the LVN application: [https://lvn.latchel.com/admin/invoices](https://lvn.latchel.com/admin/invoices)
2. Filter for invoices with the "N/A" status
3. Sort filtered invoices by the WO number (lowest to highest)
4. Click on the first invoice that appears
5. Download the generated invoice file
6. Save the file to a location you can find it later

### Step 2- Charge the customer in stripe

1. Find the customer in stripe by viewing the "payments" tab of their profile in LVN
2. Click "Create payment"
3. Enter the exact amount of the "total" from the invoice in Step 1
4. Enter the following note in the description "Services rendered for vendor request: (work order number)"
5. Click "Create payment"
6. Click the Ellipsis next to the payment
7. Select "View payment details"
8. Under "Receipt history" click "View receipt"
9. Copy the "permalink" at the bottom of pop out and paste it in a place to you can access later

### Step 3 - Payout the vendor

1. Find the vendor's connected account in Stripe. For example, here is Puls's account: [https://dashboard.stripe.com/connect/accounts/acct\_1NcbPFIsYS2lmbLL/activity](https://dashboard.stripe.com/connect/accounts/acct_1NcbPFIsYS2lmbLL/activity)
2. In the top right corner, click the ellipses and select "View dashboard as (vendor name)"
3. From the menu on the left, select "Payments"
4. Select "Create payment" → "Manual Payment"
5. In the "Amount" field, enter 90% of the amount from Step 2
6. In the "Description" field, enter "For invoice# (invoice number from step 1)
7. Enter the credit card info provided by @Taylor Wilson (Unlicensed)
8. Click "Submit payment"
9. Click the Ellipsis next to the payment
10. Select "View payment details"
11. Under "Receipt history" click "View receipt"
12. Copy the "permalink" at the bottom of pop out and paste it in a place to you can access later

### Step 4 - Mark the invoice as paid

1. Find the invoice from Step 1
2. Mark the invoice as paid in [lvn.latchel.com](https://lvn.latchel.com/)

### Step 5 - Communicate to the customer the payment is complete

1. Find the customer's phone number from the work order related to the invoice from step 1
2. Open up google voice
3. Create a message to the customer with the following message. Each
   - Confirmation: payment complete for (WO#) has been completed
   - You can find a receipt for any charges rendered here: (stripe link)
   - In a few moments, we'll send you the invoice submitted by your contractor
   - Need help? You can submit a help request via your (partner\_name) dashboard at any time.
   - Powered by Latchel
4. Send the customer the pdf attachment from Step 1

### Step 6 - Communicate to the vendor that the payment is complete

1. Find the invoice from Step 1
2. Find the vendor's email address by finding their account via the invoice from step 1
3. Create an email to the vendor from the [lcdsupport@latchel.com](mailto:lcdsupport@latchel.com) freshdesk account
4. Use the following template for content Subject: Payment Processed: Invoice (invoice number) Hi (job.vendor.name), Invoice (invoice number) was successfully processed. Payment should be deposited in your bank account within approximately one business day. You can find a link to the transaction here: (stripe link from step 4) And a link to the Latchel invoice here: (link to Latchel invoice from step 1) Powered by Latchel \*This is an auto-generated email. For inquiries, please request help from your Latchel dashboard or email us at [lcdsupport@latchel.com](mailto:lcdsupport@latchel.com)

### Step 7 - Mark the job completed in Latchel

1. Find the work order related to the invoice from step 1
2. Mark the job complete in [lvn.latchel.com](https://lvn.latchel.com/)
