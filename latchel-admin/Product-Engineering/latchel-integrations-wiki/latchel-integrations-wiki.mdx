### **How Do Integrations Benefit Our Customers?**

Put simply, an integration between a customer's primary property management software and Latchel **saves the customer time and improves quality** of service the customer can provide to their residents. Lets look at a comparison.

#### **Life without an integration**

In this scenario, the property manager (lets call him Bob) has two systems. He uses Buildium as his primary software to manage leases, residents communication, owner and property information. He uses Latchel to track and manage maintenance. Here are some of the challenges Bob is going to have to manage:

- Bob’s company has a policy of keeping any historical information about a property in Buildium. That means Bob has to manually copy/paste WO details, pictures and files from Latchel to Buildium. Ew!
- If anything about Bob’s residents change, lease dates, contact information etc. Bob has to makes these updates in TWO places! If he doesn’t, operational issues can occur.
- Similarly, if any property or owner information changes, Bob also has to make sure he executes these changes in two places. Gross!

#### **Life with an integration**

In this universe, Bob uses Propertyware which already has a supported integration with Latchel. Bob still has two systems, but they work seamlessly together to keeps Bob’s operations running efficiently and with less tedious work required of Bob. Lets look at the same scenarios as above:

- Work orders, pictures and files are automatically sync’d from Latchel back to Bob’s primary software. Easy!
- If Bob makes any changes to resident data in Propertyware, the changes are automatically reflected in Latchel an hour later.
- Same as above for property information!

### **How Often Does Information Sync Between The Systems?**

Sync timing depends on the exact integration platform but is aligned in general to the following.

#### **Platform to Latchel**

- When a customer first activates their integration, a full synchronization of all of their properties and residents is triggered automatically.
- After that, scheduled syncs occur every hour, on the 5 minute mark (e.g. 08:05 AM).
  - Scheduled syncs can take 45 minutes to complete
  - Only data updated in the previous 72 hours is considered

#### **Latchel to Platform**

- Work orders are generally sync’d on creation
- Files are sync’d when the WO is completed
