Latchel runs a remote-first and flexible work environment. Productivity is measured against the team hitting goals and individuals completing assigned work. However, communication is hugely important to the effectiveness of a team. As such, here are some communication expectations to follow:

### **Calendar**

Please keep your Google Calendar up to date with the following

- OOTO days should be indicated on the Product/Engineering OOTO calendar
- If you have appointments or small periods of a day where you will not be available for meetings please mark those as “busy” on your personal calendar

### **Ceremonies and Meetings**

- Engineers added to meetings are expected to attend meetings unless otherwise communicated.
- If you are unable to attend a meeting, please communicate this to relevant parties (typically the engineering manager or product manager)
- Additionally please reply “no” to the specific calendar event

### **Slack and synchronous communications**

Our work is a mix of asynchronous and synchronous collaboration. As such we expect that you will be available during business hours for questions or spontaneous pairing/testing sessions.

If you are going to be unable to answer slack messages for a portion of the day, please indicate as such on your slack profile with a message, such as “busy.”

The priority here is communication so we all are on the same page.
