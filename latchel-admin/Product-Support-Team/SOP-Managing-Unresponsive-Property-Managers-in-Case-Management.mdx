<Note>
  **Objective:** This SOP outlines the steps to handle unresponsive Property Managers (PMs) and manage case statuses effectively to ensure consistent communication and accountability in the case management process.
</Note>

## **1. Case Status Flow**

When a response to the PM is sent, the case status will automatically change to **"Open"**

**1.1 Automatic Re-Opening**

- If the PM is **unresponsive for 24 hours**, the system will automatically change the case status back to **"Needs Response."**
- When the case re-opens, the **PS team** is responsible for following up with the PM.

## **2. Follow-Up Procedure**

Upon the case automatically re-opening due to PM unresponsiveness, follow these steps:

#### **2.1 Sending the Unresponsive PM Follow-Up**

- The PS team should send the **Unresponsive PM Follow-Up** email using the pre-approved **Assembly Template.** **Action Required:**
  - Use the **Unresponsive PM Follow-Up Assembly Template**[<u> </u>](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/EmailTemplate/00XUS000002Tryp2AC/view?queryScope=userFolders "https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/EmailTemplate/00XUS000002Tryp2AC/view?queryScope=userFolders")(navigate to the template directory within Assembly).

<Warning>
  Follow Up is NOT necessary for Cases regarding the below, PS can move the case to Closed Unresponsive when case re-opens.

  - [<u>Feature Requests</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/B4BKvQ "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/B4BKvQ")
  - [<u>Bulk Account Updates</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/BICmvg "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/BICmvg")
  - Bug Reports - Low Impact
</Warning>

- This follow-up will return the case status to **"Open"**

## **3. Handling Responses from the PM**

If the PM responds to the follow-up, answer the PMs question/share next steps based on the conversation. Close the loop with the PM accordingly. (Resource: [SOP: Closing Cases in Product Support](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/EIBuvw)).

## **4. Managing Continued PM Unresponsiveness**

If the PM remains unresponsive and the case automatically re-opens again after another **24-hour period**, follow these steps:

#### **4.1 Closing the Case Due to PM Unresponsiveness**

- Change the case status to **"Closed."**

#### **4.2 CSAT Survey Automation**

- Upon closing the case as "Closed Unresponsive," the system will automatically trigger the[<u> **PS CSAT Survey template**</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/EoCnvg "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/EoCnvg") to be sent to the PM.
