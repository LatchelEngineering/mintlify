## Channel Purpose

The `#tech-high-sev-escalations` channel is **only to be used for issues that block the critical function of the platform where there is NO workaround available**, including:

- **Production-blocking issues**
- **Business-critical errors or bugs**
- Incidents impacting **multiple customers** or Latchel’s **core operational workflows**

<Warning>
Non-critical issues, single-account errors, or UI bugs **do not** belong here and should follow the standard #bug-report process.
</Warning>

<Tip>
To ensure a successful `#tech-high-sev-escalations`  submission, reference [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/AQDrog](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/AQDrog).
</Tip>

### Required Pre-Escalation Troubleshooting

Before posting, PS **must confirm and document**:

- **Reproduce the issue independently** to verify it’s not user error.
- **Check Twilio / API / Integration logs** for errors or abnormal states.
- **Impersonate the affected user** and replicate the issue step-by-step.
- **Test on control accounts** to rule out isolated account failures.
- **Identify scope** (# of PMs, residents, or workflows affected).
- **Document repro steps and key links** to provide Engineering with immediate context.
- **Capture screenshots and/or screen recordings** of every major step to support your escalation.

### Escalation Workflow — How to Fill It Out

Documenting your troubleshooting steps gives Engineering immediate context, eliminates guesswork, and speeds up resolution.

<img src="attachments/3658219549/3658350633.png?width=433" alt="" width="433" />
1. **Loom Recording**

  - Record a short (under 2 min) repro walkthrough.
  - Show the error behavior clearly.
  - Highlight timestamps and key steps.

2. **Short Title**

  - Use the format: (System/Feature) - (Symptom)

    - Example: Notification Dispatch — No Vendor SMS Sending

3. **What is the Problem?**

  - Clearly describe **what broke** and **how it impacts customers in production**. Include:

    - **Timestamps** — When the issue started and when it was reported.
    - **System behavior** — What actually happened vs. what should have happened.
    - **User path** — Outline the exact clicks or steps that led to the issue.
    - **Sequence of events** — “X → Y → Z” format showing the straight path to failure.
    - **Impact summary** — Who is affected, what’s blocked, and how critical it is.

  - **Avoid assumptions.**

4. **Example links or additional details.**

  - WO, property, account URLs
  - Activity Logs, screenshots, integration CSVs
  - Twilio error codes

5. **How many users are impacted?**

  - Check with the rest of PS team and Ops to confirm any additional reports.

6. **How would you categorize this request?**

  - When selecting **“How would you categorize this request?”**, choose the option that best aligns with the **root system or workflow being impacted**, not just the symptom.

| Category | Use When… | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Integrations** | Failures or outages in external partner systems impacting workflows. | RentRedi sync failing, Propertyware API down, webhook delivery errors. |
| **WO Creation (During or Immediately After)** | Issues during or immediately after work order creation. | WOs not generating, stuck in pending state, failing to hit vendor dispatch logic. |
| **Researching Work Order** | Errors during data retrieval, WO lookups, or search functions. | WO not loading or retrieving, search queries failing, internal error when opening WO. |
| **Assigning or Scheduling a Service Provider** | Failures in dispatch or scheduling functions for vendors. | Vendor not receiving notifications, dispatch not triggering, scheduling logic error. |
| **Communication** | Messaging, notification, or calling workflows failing. | SMS not sending, resident calls not connecting, outbound Twilio errors. |
| **Invoicing and Payments** | Failures in estimate, billing, or payment workflows. | Trip charge or invoice not processing, payment flow errors, duplicate or missing invoices. |
| **On Call Rotation** | Issues tied to escalation routing or on-call workflows. | On-call vendor assignment failing, escalation chain broken, after-hours routing not working. |
| **AI Chat** | Failures tied to automated resident or PM chat functionality. | AI assistant not responding, incorrect routing in Lucia chat, system timeouts. |
| **Other** | For confirmed issues that do not fit any of the above but still block production. | Critical but rare or cross-functional issues that still meet High-Sev criteria. |

7. **Who approved this escalation?**

  - You will always select [maddie](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/people/60c8f1a85c64b10071d7d0b9?ref=confluence) .
