<Warning>
  #### Overview

  Before escalating an Ops Escalation to the CSM, it’s essential to understand the Ops Escalation Summary fully. This involves reaching out to the customer to:

  - Reviewing the full transcript between Ops and the CSM.
  - Gather additional details.
  - Ask clarifying questions.

  Please only proceed with escalation if the ticket confirms that it aligns with the established criteria in [this resource](Product-Support-Team-vs-Customer-Success-Managers_2614755413.html).
</Warning>

After reviewing your ASM Ticket, and determine the issue should be escalated to the CSM according to [Product Support Team vs Customer Success Managers](Product-Support-Team-vs-Customer-Success-Managers_2614755413.html), these will be your next steps to follow:

### **Step 1: Find the Assigned CSM for the PM**

1. Navigate to Hubspot.
2. Search for the PM Company and find the Company Owner → CSM.

<Frame>
  ![3645276161](/images/3645276161.png)
</Frame>

---

### **Step 2: Communicate with the Property Manager (PM)**

1. Use the **Escalate to CSM** email template as a **starting** point. Adjust it as needed based on the situation.

| ###### _Hi \[PM’s Name\],_    ###### _Thank you for reaching out to us regarding \[issue/inquiry\]. To make sure you’re fully supported, I’ve looped in your Customer Success Manager, \[CSM’s Name\]. They’ll connect with you directly to provide next steps and discuss this further._    ###### _In the meantime, please don’t hesitate to share any additional details with us if needed._ |
| --- |

**Reminders:**

- Ensure the PM's Company is assigned to the interaction in the Customer Profile section.

<Frame>
  ![3645243398](/images/3645243398.png)
</Frame>

---

### **Step 3: Submit the Escalation via Slack Workflow**

1. Navigate to Slack. Use the #csm-escalations channel and initiate the PS Escalate to CSM workflow.

<Frame>
  ![3286630405](/images/3286630405.png)
</Frame>

1. Fill Out the Workflow Prompts:
   - **Escalation Category:**
     - PM Disagrees with Denied Invoice Dispute
     - PM Requesting Training
     - PM Requesting Call/Meeting with CSM
     - Cancellation Request
     - PM Growing Frustrated
     - Email Thread Growing Complex → Call Recommended
     - Harmonia Upgrade
     - Other
   - **Priority:**
     - URGENT → Needs to be dealt with ASAP, delay may result in Churn.
     - MEDIUM → Still a higher priority but can be dealt with the next day, the PM will likely not churn out.
     - NORMAL → Not urgent, training request, account upgrade.
   - **Issue Summary:**
     - Avoid copying and pasting the PM’s message verbatim.
     - Provide a detailed summary of the issue, including:
       - Context of the conversation.
       - Troubleshooting steps already performed.
       - Actions taken thus far.
       - Any related Slack links or Assembly notes.
   - **Next Steps:**
     - Clearly explain the reason for escalation.
     - Provide suggestions/resolution and a call to action (e.g., Does the PM appear frustrated? Did they ask for a refund? Should the CSM reset expectations?).
2. **Submit the Workflow:** - Once all prompts are completed, click **Submit**.

---

### **Step 4: Update Assembly Interaction**

1. **Copy Workflow URL:** 
   - After submitting the Slack workflow, copy the Slack URL and paste it into the Assembly Interaction as an Internal Note.
2. **Update ASM Attributes:**
   - **Status:** Set to “Needs Response.”
   - **Owner:** Assign the case to the CSM.
   - **Team Owner:** Reassign from Product Support to Customer Success.
   - **Label**: [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/HADnlg](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/HADnlg)
