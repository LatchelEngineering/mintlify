---
title: "SOP:  Handling Non-RentRedi or Non-Nomad Owner Cases"
---

<Note>
  **Purpose**: This procedure outlines how to handle cases related to owners who are associated with Property Manager companies **outside** of RentRedi or Nomad.

  **Important Note:** If the owner is a RentRedi or Nomad Owner, refer to the respective SOP below:

  - [SOP: Handling RentRedi Cases](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/GoCivg)
  - [SOP: Handling Nomad Cases](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/EACpvg)
</Note>

### **Step 1: Access the Owner's Profile**

1. Search for the owner’s profile in Latchel by using their **email address**.
2. Ensure that the **“Disabled” option** is checked when performing the search to include deactivated accounts.

### **Step 2: Identify the Owner's Property Management (PM) Company**

1. Navigate to the **Properties** or **Portfolio** tab within the owner's profile.
2. Click on any **Property/Portfolio** listed under the owner’s account.
3. On the Property/Portfolio page, scroll down to the **Details** tab.
4. Locate the **Property Manager** section to find the **PM Company** associated with the owner.

### **Step 3: Find a PM Contact Email**

1. Identify the **Primary User** for the PM Company from the Property Manager information.
2. Review the **User Groups** section for the **Default User Group**.
3. Select the most appropriate email for reaching out to the PM, typically from the Primary User or Default User Group

### **Step 4: Contact the PM Regarding the Owner’s Inquiry**

1. Use the **Owner Inquiry for PM Template** in Assembly to craft your email to the PM.
2. Send the email to the selected PM contact to address the owner’s inquiry.
