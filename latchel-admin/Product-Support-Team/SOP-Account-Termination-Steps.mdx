## Step-by-Step Guide:

Once account is confirmed that they are terminating and we have the termination date:

\*\*Step 1.\*\*PS or CS receives cancellation request from the PM.

- If request goes to PS, [PS should escalate the ticket to the CSM](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/VoDpnQ).

**Step 2.** CSM meets with PM to discuss cancellation and next steps.

_ONCE CANCELLATION IS CONFIRMED:_

\*\*Step 3.\*\*CSMcreates a calendar invite under PS Team Calendar for a **week prior to the** Date of Cancellation of the PM and invites PS team ([abegail@latchel.com](mailto:abegail@latchel.com), [cjefferson@latchel.com](mailto:cjefferson@latchel.com), [cza@latchel.com](mailto:cza@latchel.com)). Invite must include:

- PM Latchel URL

**Step 4.** Upon this date, PS will send a Courtesy Reminder email to the PM confirming their cancelation and disabled date. ([Cancelation Courtesy Reminder Template here.](https://www.dashboard.askassembly.app/preferences/templates))

\*\*Step 5.\*\*CSMcreates a calendar invite under PS Team Calendar for the **Date of Cancellation** at COB of the PM and invites PS team ([abegail@latchel.com](mailto:abegail@latchel.com), [cjefferson@latchel.com](mailto:cjefferson@latchel.com), [cza@latchel.com](mailto:cza@latchel.com)). Invite must include:

- PM Hubspot URL
- PM Latchel URL
- Primary Termination Reason
- Assembly Desire to Churn Ticket URL

\*\*Step 6:\*\*On the Date of Cancelation, PS turns OFF all communications to ALL users in Latchel account. **SKIP THIS STEP IF TERMINATED FOR NON-PAYMENT.**

1. Turn OFF invoice notifications via the Maint. Communication Settings.
2. Delete Emergency Escalation Contacts and Failed Email Contacts.
3. Go to each Users profile and toggle OFF all the Contact Settings: <img src="attachments/2802647041/3340992521.png?width=495" />

\*\*Step 7.\*\*PSterminates account in Latchel.

1. Disable all properties via the Properties tab using the Bulk Update tool.
2. Cancel any open active WOs.
3. Click the 3 dots in the top right corner of the account home page, then select **Terminate Service**.
4. Link the Assembly Desire to Churn case in the termination reason field and save. <img src="attachments/2802647041/3645243500.png?width=630" />

**Step 8.** Release all Twilio phone numbers associated with the customer.

Find phone numbers associated with the PM by going to the PM Account page in Latchel. In Maint. Communication Settings, you will want use the **Latchel-Assigned Phone Number** and the **Latchel Communication Phone Number**.

1. After logging into Twilio, navigate to Phone Numbers \> Manage \> Active Numbers.
2. Copy/Paste the PM phone number in the search bar and select **Filter**. <img src="attachments/2802647041/3028516876.png?width=712" alt="image-20240524-141908.png" />
3. Select the phone number from the filtered results, and then navigate to the **Properties** tab. From the **Properties** tab, select the option in the bottom right corner, **Release Phone Number**. <img src="attachments/2802647041/3028516886.png?width=712" alt="image-20240524-142159.png" />

\*\*Step 9.\*\*Complete the Account Termination Workflow in [#service-changes](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/CKA2W1KJA) using the information shared in the Calendar Invite. Once submitted, tag the CSM in the thread for confirmation.

**Step 10.** PS emails the PM to confirm cancellation completed and that they are good to go! ([Cancelation Confirmation Template here.](https://www.dashboard.askassembly.app/preferences/templates))

**Step 10**. CSM updates the Opportunity in Hubspot:

- Account it terminated in any pipeline stage BEFORE Live: - **Stage: Churned  \> Substage: Churned During Onboarding**
  - **Stage: Churned  \> Substage: Churned During Onboarding**
- Account is terminated in stage Live: - **Stage: Churned \> Substage: Churned Post-Launch**
  - **Stage: Churned \> Substage: Churned Post-Launch**
