## **What is required before closing a case?**

- **Contact Information**
- **Customer Details**
- **[Interaction Tags](2531721244.html):** Assigning the correct interaction tags is critical for accurate reporting.

## **How To: Close a Case**

To close a case, update the case status in the Case Details box to either:

- **Closed**: Use this when all actions have been completed, and the customer has been responsive. The issue, request, or question has been fully resolved. Even when the customer is unresponsive, ensure the **Unresponsive Follow-Up Templates** in Assembly have been sent, and no response was received before closing the case.
- **Closed- No Workflow**: Select this for Assembly tickets like the Ops Escalations we received from the Ops Team.

## **What happens after closing a case?**

After the case status is updated, an automated template is sent to the customer **24 hours later**, including a link to the **CSAT survey**.

**Important Note**: The automation will only trigger if the case is closed by one of the following users: @Maddie, @Abi San Juan, @Cres Jefferson, @Cza Guinto, or @Jennifer Folch. If a different user closes the case, the automation will not work.

If additional users need to be added or removed from this automation, please contact @Diego Arizmendi in the `#data-reporting-help-requests` channel.
