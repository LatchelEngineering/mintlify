<Info>
  This procedure provides guidance on managing cases related to service providers associated with a Property Manager (PM) and/or enrolled in our Latchel Contractor Network (LCN).
</Info>

### Responsibilities:

- **Ops Agents** are primarily responsible for communication with service providers, using Help Guides and specialized scripts for common scenarios. See this resource: [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OPS/pages/2563276894/Help\+Center\+Reference\+Guide#Special-Scripts.1](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OPS/pages/2563276894/Help+Center+Reference+Guide#Special-Scripts.1)
- **Escalation to Product Support** occurs if the SP becomes extremely irate or frustrated.

# Step-by-Step Guides:

## <u>Payment Issues</u>

For All Scenarios,

1. \*\*Navigate to the Work Order (WO):\*\*Locate the WO in question.
2. **Identify SP Type:** Determine if the SP is Preferred, LCN, or LVN for that WO.
3. Based on what kind of SP it is, follow the below:

### Scenario 1: Payment is Delayed.

| **Preferred SPs** | **LCN \| LVN SPs** |
| --- | --- |
| 1. Access the Property Manager’s (PM) Company Profile and open the Payments tab. 2. Scroll to the Payment Settings box. 3. Check the status of "Preferred Service Provider Payments."   1. \*\*If ON:\*\*Forward the escalation to team@latchel with "ATTN TO: SAM with FINANCE" and request payment processing.   2. **If OFF:** Notify the PM via email that their preferred SP seeks payment for the WO | 1. Forward the escalation to team@latchel with "ATTN TO: SAM with FINANCE" and request payment processing. |

### Scenario 2: No Payment Due to Over-Budget Invoice (Not Approved)

| **Preferred SPs** | **LCN \| LVN SPs** |
| --- | --- |
| 1. Access the PM Company Profile in Latchel, open the Payments tab, and verify payment status.   1. **If ON** → Forward the escalation to team@latchel with “ATTN TO: SAM with FINANCE” for payment processing **up to the approved budget.**     - Any balance disputes with the SP will be managed by finance.   2. **If OFF**→ Notify the PM via email that the SP is seeking payment.     1. As this is their preferred SP, any invoice negotiation for going over budget without approval should be done between the PM and SP. |  |

### Scenario 3: Setting Up Direct Deposit Payment

- All SPs can set up direct deposit. PS can email SPs directly with a link to the [<u>Help Guide</u>](https://help.latchel.com/s/article/How-can-a-Service-Provider-Add-Their-Bank-Account-Information).

## <u>Feature Request</u>

1. When a feature request is received from an SP, email the **SP Feature Request Template in Assembly**.
2. Once all details are gathered, submit the request via the Feature Request workflow in the #feature-requests Slack channel.
3. Communicate submission confirmation to the SP, then close the case.

## <u>Update Assigned Skills</u>

<Note>
  A service providers assigned skills correlates to the type of WOs they offered. It directly reflect the trades/work the service provider **can** complete.
</Note>

| **Preferred** | **LCN \| LVN** |
| --- | --- |
| 1. Review the request - Is the request to **add** or **remove** a skill? 2. Navigate to the SP Profile. 3. Under the Details tab, review the Skill box for current skills assigned. 4. Determine, based on the SP request, if the skill needs be **removed** or **added.** 5. Notify the PM of this update needed and share the following help guide to assist: [How to Edit Service Provider Skills](https://help.latchel.com/s/article/How-to-Edit-Service-Provider-Skills) 6. If PM is unable to update, PS may perform the update on their behalf. | Ops Agents can take care of this directly. See: [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/AQB1sQ](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/AQB1sQ) If the case is received <u>by the Ops Team</u>: 1. Reply by to Ops at [team@latchel.com](mailto:team@latchel.com) and request for them to make this update in Latchel. 2. PS can refer to [this SOP](Product-Support-Team-vs-Ops-Agents_2977234945.html). If the case is received <u>directly by the SP</u>: 1. Forward the email to Ops at [team@latchel.com](mailto:team@latchel.com) and CC the SP. 2. Request for Ops to make this update in Latchel. |

## <u>Training Request</u>

| **Preferred SPs** | **LCN \| LVN SPs** |
| --- | --- |
| 1. Provide relevant resources such as [<u>Top Service Provider Articles</u>](https://help.latchel.com/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wpGAA/top-service-provider-articles). If the shared Help Guide(s) does **not** help with SP request, PS should: 1. Try impersonating the SP in Latchel to assist with question. 2. Create a loom video and/or detailed email (with screenshots) detailing the steps to assist the SP. 3. Submit for a New Help Guide in #help-guide-requests to assist SPs going forward. 4. Close the loop with the SP upon confirmation of understanding/completion. |  |

## <u>Complete Opt-Out of Latchel:</u>

| **Preferred SPs** | **LCN \| LVN SPs** |
| --- | --- |
| 1. Notify the PM that their SP requests to opt-out and await further instructions on how to proceed.   1. If approved, disable the SP account after obtaining SP feedback.     - Share feedback in the #product Slack channel and close the loop with the SP.   2. If not approved, request the PM to address this with their preferred SP. | 1. Gather feedback by sending the **SP Request to Opt Out Template in Assembly.** 2. Follow the [Follow-Up Procedure](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SR/pages/3198812172/SOP+Managing+Unresponsive+Property+Managers+in+Case+Management#2.-Follow-Up-Procedure). 3. If/When Feedback is gathered, PS should share that feedback in the #product Slack Channel. 4. Close the loop with the SP. 5. Disable the SP account. |

Close the loop with the SP. |

## <u>Request to be Preferred for a Specific PM Company</u>

| **Preferred SPs** | **LCN \| LVN** |
| --- | --- |
| 1. Confirm the current preferred status of the SP:   1. If the SP is already a preferred SP for the PM company in question, notify the SP that they are already a Preferred SP for that PM company.   2. If the SP is preferred, but not for the PM company in question, refer to the steps in the LCN \| LVN column on the righthand side of this table. | 1. Notify the PM of the SP request to be added to the PM’s preferred service provider network.   1. If **approved**:     1. add the SP to the PM network by following these steps:     2. Navigate the SP Profile in Latchel.     3. Go to the Preferred PMs tab.     4. From there, click the “\+ Add PM” button on the right-hand side of the screen.     5. Search the PM Company name and select them.     6. Once selected, click “Submit”.     7. Notify the PM and the SP that this is completed and close the loop.   2. If **denied**:     1. Thank the PM for letting us know confirming that no changes will be made to their account.     2. Close the loop with the SP, letting them know this PM has denied the request at this time. |

## <u>Request to Join LCN</u>

1. Sometimes we get requests from SP Companies reaching out requesting to join our Latchel Network and opt in to accept WOs.
2. After searching for the SP email address in Latchel, if there is no record of the email or SP in Latchel, PS can send the [**SP wants to join LCN** Assembly email template](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/EmailTemplate/00XUS00000CI7VZ2A1/view?queryScope=userFolders).
