# Purpose

<Info>
  **Purpose of this document**

  The purpose of this document is to share the steps necessary to take when submitting a pre-dispatch instruction and/or critical note for approval from the Product Support and Ops team.
</Info>

<Info>
  **Who should read this document?**

  CSMs, Service Ops and Product Support.
</Info>

# Content

## Step-by-Step Guide

<Info>
  **What happened prior to this step?**

  Review [SOP: Pre-Dispatch Instructions and Critical Notes](https://latchel.atlassian.net/l/cp/PAr0e11h)
</Info>

### Process Overview

All requested pre-dispatch instructions and/or critical notes that fall outside of the scenarios listed in [SOP: Pre-Dispatch Instructions and Critical Notes](https://latchel.atlassian.net/l/cp/PAr0e11h) will require approval from Product Support and Operations.

### Step 1 - Navigate to the #pd\_criticalnotes Slack Channel.

1. From there, you will go to the Workflows tab and select: PD and CN Approval.

### Step 2- Fill out the Workflow:

<img
  src="attachments/2880634881/2880634890.png?width=574"
  width="574"
/>

- Company Name - This is the name of the property management company as it appears in the Latchel console.
- PM Company ID - When accessing the PM company page in Latchel, the PM Company ID are the last digits located at the end of the URL. <img src="attachments/2880634881/2880634893.png?width=560" width="560" />
- Active Subscription - From the PM company page, go to the “Subscription” tab and find the green active subscription. (\*\*You may also need to reference the “Product Package” tab to fully understand the service level based on the name of the subscription.)\*[_Here is a video for reference._](https://www.loom.com/share/ba2843907f2441c596cbe55f70f7e2ac?sid=6a8073ca-6695-4202-93d9-17fc715dbf40)
- Pre-Dispatch Instruction or Critical Note - Simply indicate which option you are requesting for approval.
- Level of Inheritance - Here you will list if the requested addition is for the company account as whole, for a specific portfolio or a specific property.
- If Portfolio or Property Level, please share details - Here you will share which portfolio or property the requested addition is for.
- Please submit the drafted PD/CN Request - In this field, you will share the requested pre-dispatch instruction or critical note as it would appear in Latchel for approval.

### Step 3 - Submit approval after filling out the workflow.

### Step 4 - Product Support and Service Operations will review the information. Approval and details will be shared with the submitter within 4 hours of submission.
