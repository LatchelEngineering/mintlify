## **Step-by-Step Process for Handling Invoice Disputes**

1. **Initial Review of[Latchel Guarantee and Refund Request Policy](https://help.latchel.com/s/article/Latchel-Guarantee-and-Refund-Request-Policy)**

- Always review the policy to determine if the request is valid before initiating an investigation.

2. **Responding to a WO Invoice Dispute (via Salesforce Case)**

- **Action**: Reply to the Property Manager (PM) using the[**Invoice Dispute Form Template**.](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/EmailTemplate/00XUS00000BofOD2AZ/view?queryScope=userFolders)
- **Link**: Include the[**Invoice Dispute Typeform**](https://latchel.typeform.com/to/iBtjvKHP) link in your reply.
- Once submitted, a notification will be sent to the `#invoice-dispute-form` Slack channel for review.

3. **Special Note for Nomad Owners**

- Follow the standard process by sending [**Nomad Support**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/AgC6t)the [**Nomad Escalation Form Template**.](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/EmailTemplate/00XUS00000BdCW12AN/view?queryScope=userFolders)
- If Nomad Support requests additional details, continue with the steps outlined below.

## **Eligibility Review Based on Invoice Submission Date**

Review the Invoice Submission Date to determine refund eligibility:

| **Was the dispute reported within 72 Hours of the invoice being submitted?** | **Was the dispute reported within 45 Days of the invoice being submitted?** | **Was the dispute reported more than 45 days after the invoice date?** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Full Refund Eligible | Partial Refund Eligible with the refund amount up to 50% or a maximum of \$300 per invoice. | Not Eligible for a Refund. |

<Warning>
  If the dispute reported is 45\+ days after the invoice was submitted, send [the Ineligible Invoice Dispute SF Template](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/EmailTemplate/00XUS00000CW5452AD/view) to the client.
</Warning>

## **Next Steps for Eligible Invoice Disputes**

1. **Submit Deep Dive (DD) Request**
   - **Action**: Fill out the [**Invoice Dispute DD Request workflow**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/r4KTlw) in the `#ps-dd-requests` Slack channel.
   - Inform the client using the [**Submit for DD SF Template**](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/EmailTemplate/00XUS00000Bccif2AB/view?queryScope=userFolders), which explains that the process may take up to seven business days.
2. **Keep the Client Updated**
   - If the case remains active for 3-4 business days, send a status update using the [**Department Escalation - Touch Base SF Template**.](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/EmailTemplate/00XUS00000CI7yb2AD/view?queryScope=userFolders)
3. **Investigation and Conclusion**
   - Review the refund eligibility thoroughly in alignment with the policy before writing the final conclusion.
   - Based on findings, the dispute will be marked as:
     - **VALID**: Refund is approved.
     - **INVALID**: Refund is denied.
   - Share the conclusion by navigating to the **Case Link URL** and posting the **Jira Link URL**, \*\*WO Link URL,\*\*and **Conclusion**in the case thread as an internal comment.[(Example Here).](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/r/Case/500US00000EpaB3YAJ/view)
   <Frame>
     ![3211952144 1](/images/3211952144-1.png)
   </Frame>
   - Tag the Case Owner and set the Case to **Escalated**.
4. **Conclusion Review and Client Communication**
   - In `#ps-team-chat`, submit a draft of the conclusion for review using the **DD Conclusion Workshop**. This helps ensure clear and professional communication with the PM.

### **Scenario 1: Invalid Dispute (No Refund)**

- **Action**: [Escalate to the CSM](2649325654.html) for them to communicate the DD conclusion directly with the PM.
- **Note**: Do not send any templates to the PM. All communication should come from the CSM.
- **Steps to escalate**:
  - Use the **CSM Escalation Workflow** to escalate to the CSM.
  - Assign the case to @maddie as the Case Owner.
  - Update the case state to **Escalated**.

### **Scenario 2: Valid Dispute (Refund Eligible)**

- **Action**: Share the DD conclusion with the PM and confirm that the refund process is underway with the Finance team.
- Submit details of the refund to the `#accounts-payable` Slack channel via the **DD Invoice Dispute Workflow**.
- In the Slack thread, tag **@Will Gordon** for visibility and **@Samantha Terreros** to execute the refund and communicate with the service provider.
- Once finalized, Finance will confirm in the thread, and PS should update the PM on:
  - Refund method
  - Processing timeline (5-10 business days)
  - Refund amount (\$)

## **Handling Client Pushback on Findings**

- **Action**: If the PM disputes the findings or compensation, escalate the case to the Product Support Manager (PSM).
- **Steps**:
  - Escalate to PSM using the **PSM Case Escalation Workflow** in `#ps-case-review`.
  - Assign the case to **@maddie**as Case Owner.
  - Update the case state to **Escalated**.
