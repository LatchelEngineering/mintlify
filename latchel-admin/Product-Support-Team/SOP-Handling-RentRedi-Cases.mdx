## Owner Communication Overview:

### Owner Work Order Submission Channels

Owners may submit work orders through the following channels:

- **RentRedi Portal** – Direct submission through the RentRedi owner portal
- **Phone or SMS** – Call or text **206-717-8374**

For step-by-step guidance on assisting owners who call to submit work orders, refer to: **Handling Owner WO Submission Requests**
[Handling Owner WO Submission Requests](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OPS/pages/3600941059/Handling+Owner+WO+Submission+Requests)

<Note>
Note: Owners have limited functionality within their software portals. Agents are expected to provide hands-on support as needed.
</Note>

## RentRedi Owner Access & System Limitations

- Owners **do not have Latchel logins**.
- Owners **cannot access Latchel webchat support or Latchel Help Guides**.

### Sharing Help Guides with RentRedi Owners

- Service Ops agents **may send RentRedi Help Guides** to RentRedi owners.
- RentRedi Help Guides should be shared **as if they were Latchel Help Guides**.
- If the owner pushes back, agents may proceed with any approved actions listed in the referenced table.[How to: Handle DIY Owner Contacts](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OPS/pages/3032088578/How+to+Handle+DIY+Owner+Contacts)

### Work Order Status Updates & Owner Communication

- Automated emails are **not sent** to owners when a work order moves to **"Pending"** status.
- If owner input is required due to a status change:

  1. Contact the owner directly.
  2. If unresponsive, follow up via SMS or email.
  3. Clearly explain why the owner’s input is required and what action is needed.

### Property Damaging (PD) & Extreme Habitability (EH) Situations

For PD/EH work orders:

- Make **three (3) call attempts** in a single follow-up when contacting residents and/or service providers.
- **Non-emergency tasks**: Defer to the next business day.
- **Emergency tasks**: Defer to the following day.

All updates and outcomes from resident or service provider communications **must be communicated to the owner**.

### Handling Owner Requests

#### Straightforward Requests

Examples include:

- Updating owner approval
- Canceling a work order
- Marking a work order as completed

**Action:** Update the work order accordingly and move it forward without delay.

### Requests to Follow Up with Resident or Service Provider

**Action:** Make the **first call attempt** to the resident or service provider and document the outcome in the work order.

### Requests to Add Preferred Service Providers

Refer owners to the following guide: [https://help.rentredi.com/en/articles/4757352-add-preferred-vendors-to-latchel](https://help.rentredi.com/en/articles/4757352-add-preferred-vendors-to-latchel)

All RentRedi | Latchel Help Guides are available through the [RentRedi Help Center.](https://help.rentredi.com/en/collections/922087-landlord-help-maintenance)

### Documentation Requirements

- All owner communications, follow-ups, and outcomes must be documented in the work order.
- Ensure notes are clear, time-stamped, and actionable for future reference.

## Cancellation Requests

1. When a RentRedi customer requests to cancel their Latchel services, send this email template:

  - **RentRedi - Cancel Service in Assembly**.

## Monthly Subscription Payments

<Note>
Latchel bills RentRedi in total, and RentRedi subsequently charges each individual customer according to their property unit count.
</Note>

<Warning>
The RentRedi support team handles all customer concerns regarding monthly payments.
</Warning>

1. To loop in the RentRedi support team, click **Reply All** on the customer’s email and CC [team@rentredi.com](mailto:team@rentredi.com).
2. Respond with the **RR Escalation to RR Team Assembly Template**.

### RentRedi Questions

If you do not know the answer to a particular inquiry and/or there isn’t a relevant help guide, ask for help in the joint #latchel-rentredi Slack Channel.

<Note>
The RentRedi team does **not** have access to Latchel. When reaching out to their team, include the owner name and contact information.
</Note>
