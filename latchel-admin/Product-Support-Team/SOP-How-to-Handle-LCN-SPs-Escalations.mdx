Latchel Contractor Network (LCN) is a network of service providers Property Managers can decide to opt into as a backup to their preferred vendors:

- **Emergency Workflow:** You may opt-in to have Latchel source new service providers if nobody from your preferred network is available to accept the job. Our team may source a new provider on the spot or source someone that we have previously worked with in your area.
- \*\*Non-Emergency Workflow: \*\*Get access to Latchel's service provider network when your preferred providers are not available to complete your non-emergency jobs. If you select to opt-in to have Latchel source new service providers for an emergency job, you’ll instantly gain access to a directory of service providers Latchel has previously worked with in your area.

### **How to: Blacklist an LCN Company**

If a PM requests to blacklist an LCN Company so that they are NEVER assigned to their WOs, you will:

1. Navigate to the LCN Company in Latchel.
2. Under the Preferred PMs tab, you will click the “\+ Add PM” button.
3. From that pop-up, search the PM Company name and add them as a Preferred PM.
4. Once added, then navigate to the PM Company in Latchel.
5. From the PM Company page, click on the “Service Providers” tab.
6. Once on this page, go to the “Service Provider Management” tab. In the Search bar, search for the LCN Company.
7. When the LCN Company populates, click the three dots on the right side, and the choose “Blacklist Service Provider” option from the drop-down.
8. Once completed, confirm that this is complete by going to the “Blacklisted Service Providers Management” tab.
9. When on this page, confirm that you see the LCN Company and then you are good to go!

### **How to: Disable LCN**

Refer to: [<u>How to Opt Out of the Latchel Contractor Network</u>](https://help.latchel.com/s/article/How-to-Opt-Out-of-Latchel-Contractor-Network "https://help.latchel.com/s/article/How-to-Opt-Out-of-Latchel-Contractor-Network")

1. **Opt the PM out of LCN** (if not already done).
2. Attempt to **delete all LCN payment methods**.

**If Payment Methods Cannot Be Deleted**

If deletion fails, it means the payment method is **actively in use**.

Check portfolios and/or properties where LCN vendor payments are enabled.

Queries to find where LCN is payment method is being used:

```text
SELECT * from payment_method
WHERE relation_id = 37645 -- replace with PM company Id
AND relation_type = 'Latchel\Company'
```

```text
SELECT * from portfolio -- this can be replaced by property as well
where pm_company_id = 37645
AND use_lcn_vendor_payments in (
) -- list payment method ID's you want to find
```

- Remove Usage - Temporarily **activate LCN (if needed)** to allow edits.
- Remove the LCN payment method from:
  - Portfolios
  - Properties
- **Deactivate LCN again fully.**

### **How to: Handle SPs requesting to be removed from WOs**

Single Individual WO:

- Already Assigned to the WO?
  - The SP can go to the WO in their dashboard and click the blue button in the top right corner of the WO that says “Provide Work Order Update”.
  - From there, select “Remove Me From This Work Order”. For more details, see [<u>this Help Guide</u>](https://help.latchel.com/s/article/A-Service-Provider-s-Guide-in-Updating-a-Work-Order "https://help.latchel.com/s/article/A-Service-Provider-s-Guide-in-Updating-a-Work-Order").
- Not Assigned?
  - The SP can reject the WO when it is offered to them. For more details, see [<u>this Help Guide</u>](https://help.latchel.com/s/article/How-to-Reject-a-Work-Order-Service-Provider "https://help.latchel.com/s/article/How-to-Reject-a-Work-Order-Service-Provider").

All Latchel WOs:

1. Navigate the LCN Company page.
2. Click the three dots in the top right corner. Select Disable Account.
