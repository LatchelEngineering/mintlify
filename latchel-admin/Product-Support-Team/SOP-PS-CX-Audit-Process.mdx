  **Purpose of this document**

  The purpose of this document is to provide a high-level overview of the process and steps to complete the PS CX Audit.

  **Who should read this document?**

  PS Associates and CS Team
</Note>

## **Step-by-Step Guide**

### **Step 1 - Auditors complete [<u>PS CX Audit Form</u>](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd_G_7r_l4KI7rAoMDZ6Ib4YSPlnOV9LYh17nPo8MS3kscuvA/viewform "https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd_G_7r_l4KI7rAoMDZ6Ib4YSPlnOV9LYh17nPo8MS3kscuvA/viewform").**

- On a weekly basis, the auditors should complete the PS CX Audit Form for their agreed upon number of cases.
  - Auditors should select a random sampling of cases from the week to audit. Auditing Compliance will be monitored in [<u>this report</u>](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1jGgQoUBhIH3ehQHM7BNdtjp9ZmFVLrPhqsZaHvd5Lds/edit?resourcekey#gid=668825421 "https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1jGgQoUBhIH3ehQHM7BNdtjp9ZmFVLrPhqsZaHvd5Lds/edit?resourcekey#gid=668825421") to ensure the agreed upon number of cases audits are being completed on a weekly basis.
  - Auditors should follow [<u>these guidelines</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/l/cp/2Pnjamp7 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/l/cp/2Pnjamp7") when filling out the form per case.

### **Step 2 - Scores will be shared with PS Associates.**

1. All completed audit entries will dump into [<u>this report</u>](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1jGgQoUBhIH3ehQHM7BNdtjp9ZmFVLrPhqsZaHvd5Lds/edit?usp=sharing "https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1jGgQoUBhIH3ehQHM7BNdtjp9ZmFVLrPhqsZaHvd5Lds/edit?usp=sharing"). PS Associates will be given the opportunity to review their audits and scores from the previous week.

### **Step 3 - PS Associate has opportunity to Dispute an Audit.**

1. Following the review of their scores, PS associates will be given the opportunity to dispute an audit score and request a reassessment via this [<u>PS CX Audit Reassessment Dispute form</u>](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeT6dIOGvyP6JfgJRWcAjsKiMjYp7lRr_4D4MQ5eR8iJz3OrQ/viewform "https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeT6dIOGvyP6JfgJRWcAjsKiMjYp7lRr_4D4MQ5eR8iJz3OrQ/viewform").
   - Audit Reassessment requests will dump into [<u>this report</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/pages/resumedraft.action?draftId=2887811074&draftShareId=02d36e46-5e8d-4fe1-86e7-2a1387684857 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/pages/resumedraft.action?draftId=2887811074&draftShareId=02d36e46-5e8d-4fe1-86e7-2a1387684857"). Reassessment requests will be reviewed by the PSM.
     - If the reassessment is Valid and Applicable, feedback will be provided to the auditor and a new score will be determined for the PS Associate.
     - If the reassessment is Not Valid and Not Applicable, feedback will be provided to the PS Associate.
