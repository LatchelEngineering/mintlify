### Owned and Maintained by PS

1. Slack Channels:
  - #ps-team-chat - Private team slack channel
    - <u>Morning Standup</u> - Scheduled message sent in the AM to share queues and workload volume.
    - <u>Pulse Check</u> - Scheduled message sent in the afternoon to check in on queues and see where support may be needed.
    - <u>ASM Template Request</u> - Workflow to submit a request for a template to be added to salesforce to be used for cases.
    - <u>DD Conclusion Workshop</u> - Workflow to fill out original DD Conclusion and then share a new updated, PM-facing conclusion. Used to help PS improve communication skills and how we present these conclusions in a digestible manner.
    - <u>PS SOP Request</u> - When PS cannot find an answer or next steps, they should use this workflow to submit for a new SOP or add an update to an existing SOP.
    - <u>FYI Heads Up PMs</u> - Scheduled message sent every Friday for PS to fill out any PM Tickets that should be shared with the CSM as a heads up! (This should be different from tickets already escalated to the CSM).
  - #ps-asm-escalate - Used for PS to submit cases they need assistance with, do not know the answer, are not sure the next steps to take, etc.
    - <u>ASM Ticket Escalate</u> - Workflow for when any ticket that PS has questions on or gets stuck on. Escalation point to the PSM.
  - #ps-dd-sddi - Used for PS and CSM to submit WOs to be deep dived by PS.
    - <u>DD Request</u> - Workflow for all deep dives needed; complaints regarding delays, missed appointments, confusion on automation, invoice disputes, etc.
    - <u>SDDI Request</u> - Workflow for dives for negative WO Reviews, CSATs, Google Reviews, etc.
  - #pd-cn-actionset - Used for PS and CSM to submit pre-dispatch instructions, critical notes and action sets for review, approval and execution.
    - <u>PD and CN Request</u> - Workflow for any pre-dispatch instructions or critical note that a PM, Owner or SP wants added to their account. Submit for review and approval.
    - <u>Action Set Request</u> - Workflow for any action set that a PM wants added to their account.
  - #csm-escalation - Used for ASM Tickets that PS needs to escalate to the CSM to handle.
    - <u>PS Escalate to CSM</u> - Workflow for cases that have been deemed scenarios that require escalation to the CSM to handle.
      - For reference: [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/VQDamw](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/VQDamw)
  - #ps-cs-sync - Used a cross functional PS &lt;&gt; CS comms channel; not to be in place of the formal official #csm-escalation channel. This is just used to confirm details with the CSM, ask leadership advice for how to handle a situation, give a quick "heads up" to the CSM, etc.
