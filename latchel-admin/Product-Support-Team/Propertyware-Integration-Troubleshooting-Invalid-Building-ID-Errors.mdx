**Error Description**: This indicates that the Propertyware ID Latchel has for the property is no longer valid in Propertyware. This may have occurred because the PM deleted and recreated the building or moved the property/building to a different portfolio within Propertyware. Reconciliation steps are needed to realign the data.

## Reconciliation Steps

1. Ask the PM for the reference IDs for the correct properties/units in Propertyware. The IDs can be found in the PW URL.

  1. **Suggested template:***Thank you for bringing this to our attention. After reviewing the error you reported, our Engineering Team found that the Propertyware ID associated with the property in question no longer exists. This can happen when a property or building is deleted and recreated, or when it’s moved to a different portfolio. These changes require reconciliation to realign the data.* *To resolve this quickly and accurately, could you please provide the following from your Propertyware account?* ***--A screenshot of the full property page with the URL clearly visible.*** ***--If the property is assigned to a portfolio, a screenshot of the portfolio screen with the URL clearly visible, including the portfolio name and any relevant details.*** *Once we have these screenshots, we’ll promptly verify and update the data on our end to ensure everything is aligned.*

    1. (Internal Tip:light_bulb_on: <img src="attachments/3291283475/3291381780.png?width=712" alt="image (155).png" width="712" />

2. After obtaining the screenshots from the PM, report the bug in the appropriate channel and ensure the screenshots are included. Reference [Job Aid: Slack Channels & Escalation Workflows](2873131039.html)

  1. **Please note that Engineering cannot action the request without the screenshots.**
