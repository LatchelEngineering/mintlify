<Info>
### **Purpose**

When escalating issues or seeking answers from other departments, it’s essential to use the **correct Slack channels and workflows**. Doing so ensures visibility, proper tracking, and faster resolution by the appropriate team.
</Info>

<Info>
### Who Should Use This

- Product Support (PS)
- Customer Success Managers (CSMs)
- Any team member who needs to escalate a case or ask cross-functional questions.
</Info>

| **Channel** | **Purpose / Details** | **Workflow** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `#accounts-receivable` | Discussions related to monthly subscription invoices, PM billing POCs, payment methods, disputes, failed payments, internal errors, and unit adjustments. Used to notify Accounting of a PM’s current Active Unit. | **Subscription Invoicing** — Billing disputes, change requests, general questions, past due invoices, failed payments, internal error catch, active unit adjustment alerts. |
| `#work-order-payments` | Payment escalations (e.g., urgent SP payouts, payment issues). Anything tied to the **vendor payment process**. | **Payment Inquiry** — General payment questions. **Invoice Dispute** — Escalate to Finance with context. |
| `#bug-report` | Reporting low-urgency system issues or app misbehavior impacting customers or internal processes. | **Bug Report** — Submit bug details for triage. |
| `#tech-high-sev-escalations` | Escalating **business-critical** technical issues impacting multiple customers or Latchel operations. Resource: [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/HQAM2g](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/HQAM2g) | **High Severity Tech Escalation** — Alert on-call engineers for immediate response. |
| `#product` | General product feedback, questions, and news. Use to confirm product behavior or share PM feedback. | *No workflow.* |
| `#feature-requests` | Submit and discuss feature requests or PM pain points that could inform product enhancements. | **Feature Request** — For specific asks. **Problem / Pain Point** — When PMs express issues or friction points. |
| `#pd_cn_actionset` | For Pre-Dispatch Instructions (PD), Critical Note approvals, and action set requests. | **PD & CN Approval** — Non-standard PM requests. **Action Set** — Add PM-specific instructions or triggers. |
| `#ps-team-chat` | Internal PS comms for SOP gaps, bug coordination, template requests, and DD conclusion reviews. One PS agent manages each bug to keep comms streamlined. | **PS SOP Request** — Missing SOPs. **DD Conclusion Workshop** — Share DD conclusions. **ASM Template Request** — Template use cases. **Bug Reported** — Share bug report link. |
| `#ps-asm-review` | Peer review channel for tough cases. Use when you’ve investigated but want team input before responding. | **ASM Ticket Escalation** — Case review workflow. |
| `#csm-escalations` | Escalations from PS to CSM for ownership and comms. Resource: [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/VQDamw](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/VQDamw) | **PS Escalate to CSM** — When CSM should take over. |
| `#ps-dd-sddi` | Intake channel for complex or mini investigations. | **DD Request** — For full investigations. **SDDI** — For low-score feedback (WO reviews, CSAT, Google). |
| `#help-guide-requests` | Request to create, update, or escalate Help Guides. | **New Guide Request** / **Update Existing** / **Help Guide Escalation** |
| `#ps-cs-sync` | CS ↔ PS comms and FYIs. Used for coordination — *not* a replacement for CSM escalations. | *No workflow.* |
| `#data-reporting-help` | For requesting custom reporting or engineering guidance on automations. | *No workflow.* |
| `#growth-team` | Sales team channel. | **Escalate to Sales** — Share prospect info. |
| `#partnership-perks` | Questions or inquiries around current RBP partnerships. | **Question for PP** — Submit partnership questions. |
| `#service-changes` | Tracks account terminations/offboarding and notifies Finance. Resource: [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/AQANpw](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/AQANpw) | **Account Terminated** — Notify Finance. |
| `#svc-ops-feedback` | Submit feedback for Latchel Ops Agents — praise, concerns, or improvement opportunities. | **Agent Feedback** — Feedback workflow. |
| `#svc-ops-requests` | Submit SOP policy requests or update needs. | **SOP Update Request** — New or updated SOPs. |
| `#ask-answer` | Wide channel for cross-departmental questions or guidance when no other channel fits. | *No workflow.* |
