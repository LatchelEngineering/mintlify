</Info>

<Note>
  Review [<u>How to: Handle DIY Owner Contacts</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/AgC6t "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/AgC6t") for approved actions and insight into actions Latchel Ops Agents can take for RentRedi Owners.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Inquiries from Nomad Owners are managed differently than those from RentRedi Owners. PS should only communicate directly with Nomad Owners if instructed by Nomad Support.
</Warning>

## **<u>Step-by-Step Guide:</u>**

1. For ALL NOMAD OWNER Cases, PS should escalate via email to [<u>support@nomadlease.com</u>](mailto:support@nomadlease.com) using <u>NOMAD SUPPORT ESCALATION FORM</u> in Assembly.
2. Once this is sent to Nomad Support, the case will automatically go into the “Escalated” status. Close the original email and change the escalated email status to “Open”.
   1. If Nomad Support responds and asks for additional support, continue the conversation by supporting the customer.
   2. If they are unresponsive, follow to Step 3.
3. The escalated case will automatically reopen 24 hours after being in "Open" status. If there is no response from Nomad, the associate can move the case to "Closed" without needing to follow up.

 

**USE THIS ESCALATION TEMPLATE FOR NOMAD SUPPORT:**

> **Owner Portfolio URL:**
>
> -  Copy & paste the Nomad Owner profile link from Latchel.
>
> **Name of the person that reached out (include contact info):**
>
> -  Include the owner’s name, email and phone number.
>
> **Escalation Summary:**
>
> - Give full context and all details. It is very important **not** to just copy & paste the Ops Escalation (as these are internal facing) → Paint the picture for Nomad Support to understand the escalation and any relevant details.
>
> **Is this related to a specific work order? (If so, please paste the link to the work order):**
>
> -  Copy & paste the WO link from Latchel if the escalation is in regards to a specific work order.
>
> **Action Needed from NOMAD:**
>
> - Share next steps for Nomad Support to take regarding the escalation. We can suggest Nomad sets up a call with the Owner to review settings/process/questions. We can also suggest settings updates and anything else relevant to the escalation.
> - If the escalation is simply for awareness, PS can put in this field “No action required - Simply for your records.”
