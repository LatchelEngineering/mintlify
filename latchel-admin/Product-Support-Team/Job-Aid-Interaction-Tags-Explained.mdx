---
title: " Job Aid: Interaction Tags Explained"
---

Tags related to CPT Churn Analysis are highlighted in **blue**.\
New Tags are highlighted in **yellow**.

| <h3>**Tag**</h3> | <h3>**Description**</h3> |
| :-- | :-- |
| <h4>**Integration Set Up**</h4> | Refers to the tasks involved in either initiating or modifying the integration framework within a client's Latchel account. This involves configuring account slugs for synchronization purposes or establishing integrations for clients that are already on board. |
| **Ops Escalation** | Used for the original Ops Escalation ticket that is sent to PS. |
| **Poor Experience (LVN SP)** | Complaints of poor experience with LVN SP providers causing PM frustration and/or disabling LVN all together. |
| **WOs Not Syncing Correctly (Integration)** | Refers to synchronization errors or inconsistencies specifically affecting Work Orders. |
| **Tenants Not Syncing Correctly (Integration)** | Refers to synchronization errors or inconsistencies specifically affecting the tenant's information. |
| **Properties Not Syncing Correctly (Integration)** | Refers to synchronization errors or inconsistencies specifically affecting property data. |
| **Owners Not Syncing Correctly (Integration)** | Concerns synchronization errors or inconsistencies specifically affecting the owner's information. |
| **Outdated Sync** | Refers to instances where the customer synchronization process has not been executed for an extended period, resulting in outdated data. |
| **Delayed Sync** | When customers report that the synchronization process is taking longer than expected to complete. |
| **Outdated Plugin Version (Appfolio)** | Relates to issues resulting from the use of an outdated plugin version on the customer's end. |
| **Bug - Integration Issue** | Concerns about synchronization issues arising from an unidentified or undetermined root cause. |
| **Feature Request - Integrations** | Applies to cases where the customer proposes or requests enhancements to the data synchronization process. |
| **Pre-Dispatch Instructions Conflict** | Relates to pre-dispatch instructions that are internally inconsistent or conflict with customer-provided directives. |
| **Unclear Pre-Dispatch Instructions** | Pertains to issues involving ambiguous or unclear pre-dispatch instructions that require clarification from the customer. |
| **Outdated Pre-Dispatch Instructions** | Concerns issues arising from outdated information in the pre-dispatch instructions, leading to complications in managing Emergency WOs. |
| **Outdated Access Instructions** **(Emg WO)** | Applies to complications in processing Emergency WOs due to insufficiently detailed or outdated access instructions to the property. |
| **Unnecessary Emg Comms with PM** | Refers to customer complaints regarding perceived excessive communication from Latchel during an Emergency Work Order. |
| **Emg WO Coordination Disagreement** | Pertains to customer reports involving disagreements or dissatisfaction with the handling of an Emergency Work Order. |
| <h4>**Intake Settings**</h4> | Concerns the guidance and the sharing of best practices related to adjusting work order intake settings. This includes categorizing situations as Normal, Urgent, Needs Pre Approval, or Emergency, and determining the responsible party for each severity level. |
| <h4>**Login Issues**</h4> | Problems that users face when attempting to log into the Latchel system, including password reset issues, login credentials, and access denials. |
| <h4>**SP Prioritization**</h4> | Queries related to the establishment and adjustment of priority levels for Service Providers (SPs) according to specific predefined criteria. |
| <h4>**Add SP**</h4> | Procedures and assistance required for Property Managers (PMs) to add a new preferred Service Provider (SP) to their Latchel account. |
| <h4>**Blacklist/Remove SP**</h4> | Engagements wherein a Property Manager (PM) requests to permanently exclude a Service Provider (SP) from being used due to a negative experience. |
| <h4>**Escalation Contacts**</h4> | Requests for or queries about the contact information utilized for escalating critical or unresolved issues within the Latchel system. |
| <h4>**Data Team Request**</h4> | The process whereby an updated report on properties or residents is sent by a PM for auditing and subsequent updates by the Data team in bulk quantities. |
| <h4>**Owner Settings**</h4> | Inquiries or issues regarding settings specific to property owners, such as updating owner preferences, adding or assigning an owner to a property, and configuring owner notification settings. |
| <h4>**Users/User groups**</h4> | Matters concerning the permissions, creation, or management of user groups, including settings for automated notifications tied to these groups. |
| <h4>**Pre-Dispatch Instruction/Critical Note Request**</h4> | Issues involving the examination, request, or rectification of instructions or critical notes that are predefined before service dispatch |
| <h4>**Enable/Disable Properties and/or Residents**</h4> | Cases related to the activation or deactivation of individual properties or residents within the system, covering requests affecting fewer than 10 records. |
| <h4>**Budget Limits**</h4> | Queries about the parameters and restrictions on spending limits for specific jobs. |
| <h4>**Applied Action Set**</h4> | The application of an existing, predefined action set to a Latchel account for operational execution. Requests go to #pd\_cn\_actionset. |
| <h4>**Warranty Notes**</h4> | Requests for updates or detailed explanations concerning warranty fields related to specific properties for property managers. |
| <h4>**Feature Request (Non-Integration)**</h4> | Technical requests that propose significant changes or improvements to system functionality, with the potential to greatly enhance a PM's daily operations. Reports or requests relating to minor technical improvements or impacts on system performance. |
| <h4>**Custom Scripting**</h4> | Requests for the development and implementation of unique or non-standard communication settings or functions |
| <h4>**Custom Workflow/Action Set Request**</h4> | Needs for unique or specific operational workflows that deviate from established procedures or workflows. New requests that are submitted to the #data-reporting-help-requests. |
| <h4>**Custom Diagnostic/Troubleshooting**</h4> | Requests for distinctive or specialized troubleshooting processes that differ from standard procedures. |
| <h4>**Cancellation Request**</h4> | Requests submitted by Property Managers for the cancellation of their Latchel subscription, usually requiring escalation to the Customer Success Manager (CSM) |
| <h4>**Budget Approval**</h4> | Issues arising from work orders where there was a breach of budget limits or where the established approval process was not adhered to |
| <h4>**IVR/Twilio/Wait Time**</h4> | Concerns or inquiries focused on call greeting setups or analyzing the duration of wait times during customer calls |
| <h4>**Poor Experience (Customer Service)**</h4> | Feedback pertaining to suboptimal interactions or extended wait times experienced during webchat, SMS, or calls with property managers, service providers, or residents, affecting work order outcomes. |
| <h4>**Poor Experience (Troubleshooting)**</h4> | Feedback related to negative experiences incurred during troubleshooting calls with residents |
| <h4>**Poor Experience (Call Frequency)**</h4> | Complaints related to an excessive number of calls made by Latchel to a party, causing dissatisfaction. |
| <h4>**Poor Experience (Escalation Contacts)**</h4> | Incidents where a Property Manager did not receive anticipated escalation calls, or received unwarranted escalation calls, linked to expectations vs. actual calls |
| <h4>**WO General Question**</h4> | General inquiries for clarification regarding the process or outcome of a work order, not necessarily indicating an issue. |
| <h4>**Resident Responsible Job (SP Sourcing)**</h4> | Cases where a resident reports dissatisfaction with service provider availability or Latchel's inability to source a service provider for a resident-billed job. |
| <h4>**Resident Responsible Job (Billing Responsibility)**</h4> | Issues where residents are accountable for bill payments, often involving clarification of these responsibilities. |
| <h4>**Account Configuration**</h4> | Discrepancies between expected and actual work order outcomes attributed to the way an account is configured. |
| <h4>**Unresponsive SP**</h4> | Situations where tasks took longer than desired due to a Service Provider failing to respond appropriately. |
| <h4>**Unresponsive Resident**</h4> | Instances where tasks were delayed because residents did not respond in a timely manner. |
| <h4>**Delayed PM Approval/Action**</h4> | Cases where job or process completion was deferred due to delays from the Property Manager in approving or acting upon a work order |
| <h4>**Delayed Owner Approval**</h4> | Instances where task completion was delayed because an Owner took longer than acceptable to approve a work order. |
| <h4>**Poor Experience (SP Sourcing)**</h4> | Complaints related to delays and/or unavailability to find and assign an SP to a WO. |
| <h4>**Subscription Invoice**</h4> | Inquiries or issues concerning monthly subscription invoices, including corrections related to billing system or unit count changes |
| <h4>**Job Invoice**</h4> | Concerns or disputes related to invoices associated with work orders, including Property Manager inquiries or disagreements. |
| <h4>**Subscription Upgrade**</h4> | Inquiries or procedures related to increasing subscription tier level for additional or enhanced services |
| <h4>**Subscription Downgrade**</h4> | Requests regarding reducing subscription tier level for cost-saving or service preference reasons |
| <h4>**Bug - High Impact (Non-Integration)**</h4> | Reports on urgent and serious problems with system functionality that critically affect operations, requiring immediate resolution. These go to #tech-high-sev-escalations. |
| <h4>**Bug - Low Impact (Non-Integration)**</h4> | Reports on minor or non-urgent technical bugs that have limited effect on system functionality but are still addressed for user satisfaction. These go to #bug-report. |
| <h4>**Business Shift/Org Changes**</h4> | Matters related to adjustments needed due to changes in a customer’s business model, organizational structure, or strategic direction. |
| <h4>**Product Fit/Expectations**</h4> | Assessments of how well the Latchel product meets customer needs and requirements, or aligns with their expectations |
| <h4>**PMS Software Change**</h4> | Issues or requests that emerge from the application of software updates, modifications, or migrations |
| <h4>**DD Conclusion**</h4> | Outcomes and insights from thorough DD investigations followed by feedback intended to prevent similar issues |
| <h4>**Not Actionable**</h4> | Identifications of cases that do not require actions due to being resolved, irrelevant, or invalid. |
| <h4>**Report Export**</h4> | Requests for extracting data for the purpose of business analysis or generating actionable insights via the Analytics Dashboard or requested with #data-reporting-help-request. |
| <h4>**SP Request or Inquiry**</h4> | Any questions or requests provided directly by Service Providers, including contact form submissions at the HCP level |
| <h4>**Sales Lead**</h4> | Information regarding potential sales opportunities or referrals that could lead to new business development. |
| <h4>**CSM Call Request**</h4> | Interaction where a customer has explicitly asked for a conversation or meeting, typically to discuss matters live over the phone |
| <h4>**Accessing Benefits**</h4> | Issues faced by residents trying to utilize the benefits entitled to them, often requiring support for access facilitation |
| <h4>**Benefits Rollout**</h4> | Cases concerning questions or clarifications regarding the deployment or availability of benefit programs |
| <h4>**Latchel Proprietary Benefits**</h4> | Customer inquiries about specific Latchel benefits such as Accidental Damages Reimbursement, Lockout Reimbursement, Overnight Stay Reimbursement, Missed Appointment Fee Reimbursement, Free Drain Packs, Move-In Promise, and Security Deposit Review |
| <h4>**Moving Concierge**</h4> | Cases that relate to the facilitation of move-in services offered to residents, in cooperation with Utility Valet. |
| <h4>**Marketplace Benefits**</h4> | Inquiries about or opportunities to enhance Latchel Marketplace Benefits, including services like Obie (liability insurance), FilterTime (air filters), Cover Pest (pest control), Sure (renter’s insurance), Piñata (rent rewards), and RentCheck (inspections). |

<Warning>
  Can’t find what you’re looking for? Reach out to your direct manager for help.
