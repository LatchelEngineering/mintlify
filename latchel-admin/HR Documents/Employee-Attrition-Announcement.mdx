# Employee Attrition Announcement

## Purpose

<Info>
  **Purpose of this document**

  The purpose of this document is to identify Latchel standards for announcing and notifying other associates about regrettable and non-regrettable employee attrition.
</Info>

<Info>
  **Who should read this document?**

  All hiring managers, HR related roles, and Leadership
</Info>

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 - Meeting with the employee**

1. It is the current direct supervisors responsibility to notify an employee when their termination date is. This should be done in a private meeting via video chat.
2. If the agent is leaving the company by their own choosing, an exit interview should be scheduled on their final intended departure date. This should be performed by the HR Administrator and an impartial member of leadership. The direct supervisor of the individual should not be involved in the exit interview so that the exiting person feels completely free to give full feedback.

<Info>
  It is important that the hiring manager is direct and clear in the employees termination and effective date of said termination.
</Info>

**Step 2- Offboarding**

1. Whenever sufficient notice is possible, offboarding should be performed immediately following an employees termination meeting or on the last day of their intended departure. This is to be performed by an HR Administrator or the Department Supervisor.

<Info>
  Each department has their own tools that the agent may have access to, but a general rule of thumb is to check the onboarding plan and work in reverse removing or reallocating access of the former employee. A general checklist can be accessed here.
</Info>

**Step 3 - Announcement**

- **Who:** The former employees direct manager should notify affected departments. There is not need to communicate with unimpacted departments.
- **What:** A respectful blurb should be provided via Slack. The former employees direct manager should be sure to include any relevant information.

```
[EMPLOYEE NAME] is departing the company effective [DATE].

[EMPLOYEE NAME] will be handling all of their current workload until further notice.

As of [DATE] please direct all further questions to [DIRECT MANAGER].

We appreciate the work [NAME] did for the company and wish them the very best in their future endeavors.
```

\*If a more collaborative announcement is preferred for regrettable attrition, this can be arranged at the discretion of the former employees direct supervisor. The Slack announcement should still be posted on the employees last working day.

- **When:** Announcements should be made on the employees final day. If it is non-regrettable attrition this should only be done after Steps 1 and 2 have been completed.
- **Where:** The announcement should be made in a unified channel for each impacted department. (Ex. Operations most unified channel is #operations.)

Please keep respect for the former employee through all communications.
