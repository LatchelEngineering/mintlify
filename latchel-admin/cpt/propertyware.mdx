## **Logging into Propertyware**

<Warning>
  Keep in mind each PM has their own personal link to Propertyware so make sure you are always using the correct one. Once you select the link it will ask you for the email and password which you will find in the tracker.
</Warning>

### **Step 1 - Locate the correct PM and their specific login credentials**

a. Navigate to the **"[CPT Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bQ45AVpZG5-ONwnSLFlJP8M5GCl7J__VczN1rfeCFQI/edit?gid=0#gid=0)"** and open the **"Overall Details"** tab.

b. Find the PM you need to track and confirm their PMS is listed as "Propertyware."

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-212.png)
</Frame>

c. Locate the following three columns for that PM:

- `PMS URL` (This is the PM's unique Propertyware link)
- `Username`
- `Password`

d. Keep this information ready for the next step.

### **Step 2 - Execute the login and complete security verification**

a. Click the `PMS URL` from the tracker to open the correct login page.

b. Enter the `Username` and `Password`.

c. Check the **"Remember me"** box to simplify future logins.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-213.png)
</Frame>

d. Click **"Sign me in."**

e. Complete the Captcha verification as prompted.

f. Click **"Sign me in"** again to finalize the login and access the software.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-214.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-215.png)
</Frame>
