<Note>
  The purpose of this document is to guide the Customer Profitability Associate through the process of reporting to the Finance department the billing inputs for Assisted Ledgering, Marketplace Benefits, Optional Benefits, and Fixed Monthly Payment Schedule Property Managers.
</Note>

## **Step by step**

<Check>
  CPT needs to have billing inputs ready before the <u>8th working day</u> of the month, as Finance department sends invoices to Property Manager on that day.
</Check>

### Step 1: Open the Billing inputs Master Doc

a. Go to the [Billing Inputs Master Doc](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1HG_bRF-JM8DqSz1lusDrfJVGIpohvJwBP4fNAipclvM/edit?gid=748580391#gid=748580391) and locate the tab for the month that just started. _Example: If you are going to report November’s period from 11/01–11/30, you need to locate December tab._

b. Locate the Property manager you need to fill in. (All the cells highlighted in light purple will be the ones the CP associate needs to fill in).

<Frame>
  ![11.png](/images/CPT-366.png)
</Frame>

c. When you have entered a billing input, and it is confirmed, turn the cell into a light green highlight, to let Finance know those are ready.

<Frame>
  ![22.png](/images/CPT-367.png)
</Frame>

d. Once all billing inputs provided by the CPT are ready and confirmed, make a post in the [#ledgering-mkt-opt-benefits](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C03D4N7B0LF) channel to inform Donita and Finance department the inputs are ready.
