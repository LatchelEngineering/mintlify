---
title: "Identifying Property Types in Buildium"
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this document is to be able to identify the exceptions for the weekly reporting directly in Buildium, to be able to report this correctly in the tracker.
</Note>

<Accordion title="Month to Month Units">
  1. From the list of residents shown, manually search in Buildium for the resident you want to check. Click Lease Ledger from the search result.

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-179.png)
  </Frame>

  2. Go to the Summary page.

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-180.png)
  </Frame>

  3. Under the **Renewal history** and the **Type** section, you will see **At-will (Month to Month).**

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-181.png)
  </Frame>

  4. You can also verify this when you open the resident’s profile, and under the address and resident’s name you see the legend “At-will (month-to-month)”

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-182.png)
  </Frame>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Section 8">
  1. From the list of residents shown, manually search in Buildium for the resident you want to check. Click Lease Ledger from the search result.

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-183.png)
  </Frame>

  2. Go to the Summary page.

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-184.png)
  </Frame>

  3. Under the **Recent files**, we need to check the additional files and/or the lease.

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-185.png)
  </Frame>

  4. Usually when it’s a section 8 one of the files will be the section 8 application. It will look like this.

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-186.png)
  </Frame>

  <Warning>
    **Housing Voucher Program, HAP, and LHC** are other ways for identifying Section 8. Some PMs will also include a Section 8 recurring charge to have a record of the tenant being part of an Assistance Program.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="HOA">
  1. From the list of residents shown, manually search in Buildium for the resident you want to check. Click Lease Ledger from the search result.

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-187.jpg)
  </Frame>

  2. Go to the Summary page.

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-188.jpg)
  </Frame>

  3. Under the **Recent files**, we need to check the additional files and/or the lease.

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-189.jpg)
  </Frame>

  4. Usually, when it’s an HOA property, the lease would specify it under **Other information section**, and It will look like this.

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/CPT-190.jpg)
  </Frame>
</Accordion>
