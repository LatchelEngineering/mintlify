title: "How to: Follow up on an Air Filter Issue Report"
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this article is to describe the steps that need to be followed when tenants report they received a damaged filter, the wrong size, or an incomplete shipment.
</Note>

## Step by Step

### Step 1: Open the CPT updates channel in slack

a. When a tenant makes an air filter issue report, an alert message will be triggered to the [#cpt-updates](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/2961113113/How+to+Follow+up+on+an+Air+Filter+Issue+Report#cpt-updates) channel in Slack, providing all the issue’s details and tenant’s information.

<Frame>
  ![1.png](/images/CPT-441.png)
</Frame>

b. The CPT will reply **“Checking”** to this message, and proceed to revise the issue to determine the next steps that need to be followed to solve it.

### Step 2: Analyze the type of issue received

a. There can be 3 different issues reported by the tenants, for which we will follow the next steps:

<Accordion title="Wrong Size / Incomplete Shipment">
  1. If the issue being reported refers to receiving a wrong filter size, or incomplete shipment, the CPT will  first do some research to **<u>investigate the root cause of the error</u>**<u>.</u> The reason can either be because the Property Manager or the resident provided the wrong information, or that Latchel made a mistake. To confirm what the reason was, check the original filters' list that was provided by the property manager, which can be found in the [Property Manager Files folder](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1K-OxCEHlyfI6GNhukN9aNPsZho1yzz7T). Look for the Property Manager’s folder that you need to check, and in there you will find the Filters folder where the original list is included. If the information provided by the Property Manager or resident is correct, check the property’s manager information in the AU tab to see if the mistake was done by Latchel.  **Once the cause is confirmed, leave the note in the Air Filter Issue Report Response in the "Responsibility" column.**
  2. After this, collect the information to make the changes in the [Benefits CPT Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qd4oXlu6dBYwvPCVnl8fMwoulezF2MTZQoGOhqPqdo0/edit#gid=675879001).
  3. When the necessary changes have been done, we will contact Josie to have the replacement sent to the tenant as soon as possible. The CPT will use the following email templates:

  <Check>
    **Wrong Size:** Hi Josie, good day! Please be advised that we received a report from a tenant that (1, 2, 3…) filter(s) they received was/were of the wrong size. The tenant is (tenant’s name), at (tenant’s address with city, state and zip code). The filter size(s) they received was/were (size), which need to be replaced with (size).

    We would appreciate if you can send the missing filter(s) as soon as possible, and please let us know if you have any question or we can help you with anything else. Thank you!
  </Check>

  <Check>
    **Incomplete Shipment:** Hi Josie, good day! Please be advised that we received a report from a tenant that they received an incomplete shipment. The tenant is (tenant’s name), at (tenant’s address with city, state and zip code). The filter size(s) missing is/are (size).

    We would appreciate if you can send the missing filter(s) as soon as possible, and please let us know if you have any question or we can help you with anything else. Thank you!
  </Check>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Damaged Filter">
  1. If the issue being reported refers to receiving a damaged filter, the CPT will collect the information to report this back to Josie, to have the replacement sent to the tenant as soon as possible. The CPT will use the following email template:

  <Check>
    **Damaged Filter(s):** Hi Josie, good day! Please be advised that we received a report from a tenant that (1, 2, 3…) filter(s) were damaged upon arrival. The tenant is (tenant’s name), at (tenant’s address with city, state and zip code). The filter size(s) to be replaced is/are (size).

    We would appreciate if you can send the replacement as soon as possible, and please let us know if you have any question or we can help you with anything else. Thank you!
  </Check>
</Accordion>

If important information is missing in the Typeform report, reach out to the TL for further instructions.

### Step 3: Reach out to the resident

a. When we receive confirmation from Josie that the replacement / missing filter(s) will be sent, the CPT will contact the tenant to confirm that we are working on rectifying the issue, and provide an approximate shipping date along with the tracking number.

For the different scenarios, the CPT will use the following templates:

<Check>
  **Damaged Filter:** Hello \[tenant’s name\],

  We trust you're enjoying a fantastic day! We'd like to verify that we received your report concerning the damaged filter(s). Rest assured, your replacement has been dispatched and you should be receiving it within the next 3 to 7 business days. Your **tracking number is (number)**, should you wish to monitor your shipment's progress. Ensuring service excellence is our utmost priority!

  Best regards, The Latchel Team
</Check>

<Check>
  **Wrong Size:** Hello \[tenant’s name\],

  We trust you're enjoying a fantastic day! We'd like to verify that we received your report concerning the wrong filter(s) size. Rest assured, we have updated this information in our database, to prevent this from occurring in the future. Your replacement (filter size) has been dispatched and you should be receiving it within the next 3 to 7 business days. Your **tracking number is (number)**, delivered by (delivery company’s name), should you wish to monitor your shipment's progress. Ensuring service excellence is our utmost priority!

  Best regards, The Latchel Team
</Check>

<Check>
  **Incomplete Shipment:** Hello \[tenant’s name\],

  We trust you're enjoying a fantastic day! We'd like to verify that we received your report concerning the missing filter(s). Rest assured, the package with the missing filter(s) (filter size) has been dispatched and you should be receiving it within the next 3 to 7 business days. Your **tracking number is (number)**, should you wish to monitor your shipment's progress. Ensuring service excellence is our utmost priority!

  Best regards, The Latchel Team
</Check>

<Warning>
  If a resident reaches out to request Filters but belongs to a PM Company that doesn’t have this benefit, the CPT will send an email to the resident using the following template:

  _Hello \[Resident name\]!_

  _We received your submission for the missing filters for \[PM Company Name\], but unfortunately, we don't provide this service to this property management company. Could you please let us know how were you provided with the Typeform link? We recommend you contact the property manager directly to submit Filters information, thank you. Please let us know if we can help you with anything else. Have a great day!_

  _The Latchel Team_
</Warning>

When reaching out to tenants, the messages will be sent from [benefits@latchel.com](mailto:benefits@latchel.com).

### Step 4: Make note in the Air Filter Issue Report

a. When the issue has been resolved and the tenants are informed, the CPT will leave a brief description of the steps taken to solve the issue. This will be noted in the Notes column, in the [Air Filter Issue Report Responses.](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/13N2F9mlQQo1h3--bzM3dfiDVv_n4hgoMPctG3B6Ivts/edit#gid=491589522)The same note will be left in the original Typeform thread in Slack, adding the word “Completed” to state this issue has been resolved and how the CPT proceed. The CPT will also react to the message with a checkmark  , for a quick overview of the unresolved issues.
