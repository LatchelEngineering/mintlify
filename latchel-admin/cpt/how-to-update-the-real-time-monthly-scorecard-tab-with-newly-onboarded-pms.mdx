---
title: "How to Update the Real-Time Monthly Scorecard Tab with Newly Onboarded PMs"
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to add Newly Onboarded PMs to the Real-Time Monthly Scorecard Tab.
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Navigate to the CPT Tracker and open the Real-Time Monthly Scorecard tab.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250916-205414.png](/images/CPT-415.png)
</Frame>

### Step 2: In the CPT Tracker, go to either the _Optional Benefits_ tab or the _Overall Details_ tab for (Assisted Ledgering), depending on the PM’s contract, to copy the new PM’s company name.

a. Copy the PM Company name and Paste it in the 'Real Time Monthly Scorecard' tab.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250916-210813.png](/images/CPT-416.png)
</Frame>

### Step 3: Update the columns as needed and reformulate the formulas accordingly.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250916-214125.png](/images/CPT-417.png)
</Frame>

a. **Column B:** _Type of Contract_

- Select the contract type for the new PM’s subscription (e.g., Optional Benefits, Assisted Ledgering, etc.).

b.Column C: _CSM_

- You can check who the CSM is in the [#sales](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C2Y2ZN9LL) or [#cpt-updates](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C04TZ3Y8ZLK) channel.

<Warning>
  **Verify the PM is enrolled in the applicable benefits (e.g., Assisted Ledgering, Optional Benefits) before adding.**
</Warning>

<Frame>
  ![da2c3235-9248-4705-942c-7acf086cce51.png](/images/CPT-418.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![image-20250916-214955.png](/images/CPT-419.png)
</Frame>

c. Column D: _Months on Service_

In the formula `=DATEDIF('Optional Benefits Overall Details'!P17, TODAY(), "M")+1`, and with corresponding Columns, replace `P17` with the corresponding row number of the new PM from the Optional Benefits Overall Details Tab.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250916-220928.png](/images/CPT-420.png)
</Frame>

<Check>
  Example: For the new PM, _Insite Property Solutions_ is in (**row 18**) in the Optional Benefits Overall Details tab, update the formula as follows:

  =DATEDIF('Optional Benefits Overall Details'!P**18**, TODAY(), "M")\+1
</Check>

f. **Column H:** _Is the PM receiving concessions?_

- In this column, it is specified whether the PM is receiving concessions or not. **Example:** <u>If the PM is profitable, it will be marked **“No”,** and if the PM is still in the RTP (</u>**Road to Profitability Percentage**),<u> it will be marked **“Yes”**.</u>

g. **Column I:** _Expected % Profitability after 12 months on Latchel._

- Update the formula to reflect the PM’s correct row number in the '**_Real Time Monthly Scorecard_**' tab.

j. **Columns O:** _Total Opportunities this Month_

From **Column O** onward, ensure you follow the applicable SOP before adding the formulas; otherwise, an error will occur. Please refer to the following job aids: [How to Create an Assisted Ledgering Tab (Appfolio Customers)](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3584524391/How+to+Create+an+Assisted+Ledgering+Tab+Appfolio+Customers) [How to Create an Optional Benefits Tab (Appfolio Customers)](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3585278033/How+to+Create+an+Optional+Benefits+Tab+Appfolio+Customers)

<Check>
  For example, if the new PM company name is _Insite Property Solutions_, update the formula as shown below (note that the row numbers `T2:T2548` and `K2:K2548` stay the same):

  =ARRAYFORMULA(SUM((MONTH('_Optional Benefits - Insite Property Solutions_'!T**2**:T2548) = MONTH(TODAY())) \* (YEAR('_Optional Benefits - Insite Property Solutions_'!T**2**:T2548) = YEAR(TODAY())) \* ('_Optional Benefits - Insite Property Solutions_'!K**2**:K2548 = "Yes")))
</Check>

<Warning>
  When copying formulas, note that the cell ranges may shift automatically. Always verify and update the ranges as needed. This applies to **Columns O to S.** For example:

  **Update the PM company name in the formulas as needed. The row number should remain unchanged.**
</Warning>
