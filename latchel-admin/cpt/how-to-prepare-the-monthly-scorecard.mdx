title: "How to Prepare the Monthly Scorecard"
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to prepare the Monthly Scorecard for stakeholder analysis
</Note>

<Warning>
  [**Active units**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3574497370/How+to+Update+Active+Units) should be updated first in the CPT tracker\*\* before updating the Monthly Scorecard.
</Warning>

## Step by step

<Warning>
  <u>All amounts entered in the Monthly Scorecard should reflect the numbers **as of the last day of the previous month**</u>. **For example**: _if it’s September 1st and you’re preparing the Monthly Scorecard for August, all numbers entered should be as of 08/31._
</Warning>

### Step 1: Open the [Monthly scorecard doc](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1e9psvlXljLhCK_j_Dzhz1LmHRCYp7S23ns9kzJh19x0/edit?gid=1397479597#gid=1397479597) to customize the new report.

a. Go to the Monthly scorecard doc and duplicate the last month’s tab.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250807-184647.png](/images/CPT-791.png)
</Frame>

b. Once you have duplicated it, make sure to rename the new tab with the month being reported. **Example:** _If today is September 1st and you need to report August's information, the tab name should be 'August'._

<Frame>
  ![Picture 23.jpg](/images/CPT-792.jpg)
</Frame>

c. Delete the information in columns D, E, F, and I (only for non-profitable Property Managers), as well as columns J, K, L, M, and O through U.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250807-185310.png](/images/CPT-793.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![image-20250807-185335.png](/images/CPT-794.png)
</Frame>

### Step 2: Update the new report's information.

<Check>
  If a Property Manager began their service during the reporting month, they should be included in the report. <u>Property Managers who canceled during the reporting month should be removed.</u>
</Check>

**Column Q – Total Opportunities This Month** This column includes all residents who are _Ledgerable_ (Assisted Ledging) or have _Opted In_ (Optional Benefits), regardless of whether they have a GL code.

**Column W – Total Missed Opportunities of the Month** This column aggregates counts from _New Leases with No GL code, Renewals with no GL code, and New Month-to-Month Leases with no GL code_, and includes only residents who are _Ledgerable_ or have _Opted In_ and **do not** have a GL code.

<Accordion title="Columns D - M">
  - **For column D** **(Months on Service)**: Refer to the previous tab to see which month they were in, and update it accordingly in the new tab. **Example:** Southern Dreams Realty was in its 8th month in the July tab. In the August tab, it will be in its 9th month of service.
  - Repeat the step to fill the column for all the Property managers.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-190154.png](/images/CPT-795.png)
  </Frame>

  - **For column E (Active Units for the Month)**: Open the [CPT Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bQ45AVpZG5-ONwnSLFlJP8M5GCl7J__VczN1rfeCFQI/edit?gid=2144956335#gid=2144956335) and go to the **'Real Time Monthly Scorecard'** tab. <u>Refer to column E in that tab to fill in the active units in the Monthly scorecard</u>.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-190840.png](/images/CPT-796.png)
  </Frame>

  - Repeat the step to fill the column for all the Property managers.

  <Warning>
    For Optional Benefits, column E should match the number reported in the Billing Inputs document for that month reported. This equals the count of residents with a GL code. If a property manager doesn’t have GL codes, enter the number of active units instead.
  </Warning>

  - **For Column F** (Price per unit): Retrieve this information from the CPT Tracker, Real Time Monthly Scorecard tab, Column F.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20251002-230450.png](/images/CPT-797.png)
  </Frame>

  - **For column G (% Profitability Attained to Date):** Only fill out this column for Property Managers who are not yet profitable. For those already marked as profitable, you can leave the cells as they are. Go to the 'Real Time Monthly Scorecard' tab and <u>refer to column G to fill in the data in the Monthly Scorecard</u>.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-191355.png](/images/CPT-798.png)
  </Frame>

  - Repeat the step to fill the column for all the Property managers.
  - **For column I (Is the PM receiving concessions?):** Please leave it as is, unless the Property Manager became profitable—in that case, change it to 'No'.
  - **For column J (Expected % Profitability after 12 months on Latchel):** Only fill out this column for Property Managers who are not yet profitable. For those already marked as profitable, you can leave the cells as they are. Go to the 'Real Time Monthly Scorecard' tab and <u>refer to column J to fill in the data in the Monthly Scorecard</u>.

  If PM is already Profitable, just highlight in gray and leave bank.

  - Repeat the step to fill the column for all the Property managers.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-192044.png](/images/CPT-799.png)
  </Frame>

  - **For column K (Total Units Ledgered To Date):** Go to the 'Real Time Monthly Scorecard' tab and <u>refer to column K to fill in the data in the Monthly Scorecard</u>.
  - Repeat the step to fill the column for all the Property managers.

  <Check>
    For Property Managers with optional benefits, it is not necessary to fill out columns F-K.
  </Check>

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-192421.png](/images/CPT-800.png)
  </Frame>

  - For columm L (Resident Charge): Obtain the required information from the CPT Tracker, Real Time Monthly Scorecard tab, Column L

  <Frame>
    ![image-20251002-233949.png](/images/CPT-801.png)
  </Frame>

  - **For column M (Units needed to hit profitability/breakeven):** Go to the 'Real Time Monthly Scorecard' tab and <u>refer to column M to fill in the data in the Monthly Scorecard</u>.
  - Repeat the step to fill the column for all the Property managers.

  <Check>
    For units with a minus sign before the number, report them as 0 in the Monthly Scorecard
  </Check>

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-192637.png](/images/CPT-802.png)
  </Frame>

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-192808.png](/images/CPT-803.png)
  </Frame>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Columns N & O">
  - **For column N (Amount that Opted-in to Benefits):** Go to the CPT Tracker and select the Optional benefits tab for the corresponding PM.
  - **Apply a filter to the 'Lease From' column** (select the month to report — for example, if today is September 1st and you're reporting August data, select all dates from August) and **the 'Opt In' column (select 'Yes')** **at the same time.**

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-193818.png](/images/CPT-804.png)
  </Frame>

  - Remove the filters
  - Repeat the same step by **filtering the 'Lease Start Date' column** and the **'Opt In' column (select 'Yes') at the same time.**
  - Count all the residents who opted in, as this will be the number entered in column L.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-194322.png](/images/CPT-805.png)
  </Frame>

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-194456.png](/images/CPT-806.png)
  </Frame>

  - Repeat the step to fill the column for all the Property managers.
  - **For column O (Amount that Opted Out of Benefits):** Filter all rows with 'No' in the 'Opt In' column while also selecting the reporting month's date in the 'If so, when?' column to see how many residents opted out.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-194937.png](/images/CPT-807.png)
  </Frame>

  **If so, when?**

  - The residents displayed will be the ones reported in the column O of the Monthly Scorecard.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-195029.png](/images/CPT-808.png)
  </Frame>

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-195209.png](/images/CPT-809.png)
  </Frame>

  - Repeat the step to fill the column for all the Property managers.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Columns Q - W">
  - **For column Q (Total Opportunities This Month):** In the CPT Tracker, filter the ‘Lease From' column for the corresponding Property Manager you are filling out. Also, filter the column 'Date of Missed Opportunity’ separately to check if there are any opportunities for the month.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-195828.png](/images/CPT-810.png)
  </Frame>

  - Make sure to count only the residents who are marked as 'Yes' in the 'Ledgerable' column.

  <Warning>
    **<u>Do not remove the filters, as they will still be used for the next columns.</u>**
  </Warning>

  For Property Managers with optional benefits, you should repeat the step above and also filter the 'Lease Start Date' column separately, counting only those marked as 'Yes' in the 'Opt In' column.

  - Enter the total number of opportunities for this month in the corresponding column of the Monthly Scorecard.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-200349.png](/images/CPT-811.png)
  </Frame>

  For column R (New Leases with No GL Code): Scroll to the right side of the tab to identify which of the total opportunities are new leases marked in the 'Missed Opportunities' column, and confirm that they do not have a GL code in the 'GL Code' column.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-201007.png](/images/CPT-812.png)
  </Frame>

  For Property Managers with optional benefits, filter the 'Lease Start Date' column by selecting only those from the reporting month. Then, identify the new leases in the 'Type' column and confirm they do not have a GL code in the 'GL Codes' column.

  - Enter the New Leases with no GL code in the corresponding column of the Monthly Scorecard.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-201401.png](/images/CPT-813.png)
  </Frame>

  - **For column S (Renewals with no GL code):** Scroll to the right side of the tab to identify which of the total opportunities are Just renewed leases marked in the 'Missed Opportunities' column, and confirm that they do not have a GL code in the 'GL Code' column.

  For Property Managers with optional benefits, filter the 'Lease Start Date' column by selecting only those from the reporting month. Then, identify the Just renew leases in the 'Type' column and confirm they do not have a GL code in the 'GL Codes' column.

  - Enter the Renewals with no GL code in the corresponding column of the Monthly Scorecard

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-201906.png](/images/CPT-814.png)
  </Frame>

  - **For column T (New Month to Month Leases with no GL code):** Scroll to the right side of the tab to identify which of the total opportunities are Monthly leases marked in the 'Missed Opportunities' column, and confirm that they do not have a GL code in the 'GL Code' column.

  For Property Managers with optional benefits, filter the 'Lease Start Date' column by selecting only those from the reporting month. Then, identify the Monthly leases in the 'Type' column and confirm they do not have a GL code in the 'GL Codes' column.

  - Enter the New Month to month leases with no GL code in the corresponding column of the Monthly Scorecard

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-202127.png](/images/CPT-815.png)
  </Frame>

  - **For column U (Total Month-to-Month Leases with No GL Code): <u>Remove all filters applied in the tab</u>**, then filter the Column R 'Type' column by selecting only the 'Monthly' option, and also Filter **Column L** and select (Ledgerable) count only residents, no GL codes.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-202337.png](/images/CPT-816.png)
  </Frame>

  - Enter the Total Month-to-Month Leases with no GL code in the corresponding column of the Monthly Scorecard

  <Frame>
    ![image-20251003-171203.png](/images/CPT-817.png)
  </Frame>

  For Property Managers with **Optional Benefits**, count only the entries with “Monthly” in **Column Q** and “Yes” in **Column K.**

  - For column V (Total Missed Opportunities with Lease and Addendum for the Month): Review the missed opportunities listed in columns R, S, and T of the Monthly Scorecard, and check if any of them have both a lease and an addendum.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20251003-171420.png](/images/CPT-818.png)
  </Frame>

  For Property Managers with optional benefits, if they have a letter, email, or chat message advising about the optional benefits instead of an addendum, it should be counted as an addendum

  - Enter the Total Missed Opportunities with lease and addendum for the month in the corresponding column of the Monthly Scorecard

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-203449.png](/images/CPT-819.png)
  </Frame>

  - **For column W (Total Missed Opportunities of the Month):** Review the missed opportunities listed in columns R, S, and T of the Monthly Scorecard, and record the total of the three columns in column V.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20251003-171420.png](/images/CPT-820.png)
  </Frame>

  <Check>
    **If there are** missed opportunities reported in column V, <u>the missed opportunities report should be linked to the number.</u>
  </Check>

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-203953.png](/images/CPT-821.png)
  </Frame>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Columns X-Z">
  <Check>
    These columns should only be filled out if applicable.
  </Check>

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-204321.png](/images/CPT-822.png)
  </Frame>

  - **For column X (This Month’s Success Stories):** This should only be filled out if a Property Manager became profitable or is on track to profitability (applicable for Assisted Ledgering PMs)
  - **For column Y (This Month's Major Risks):** This should only be filled out if a Property Manager meets one of the following conditions: - Removed our access ⚠️ - Removed GL codes on all units ⚠️ - Hasn't set up GL codes ⚠️ - Started charging break-even rates⚠️ - No GL Report Received ⚠️
  - **For column Z (This Month's Major Risks):** This is a support column that should only be filled out if more than one option applies to that Property Manager in column W.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Columns X to Z">
  <Check>
    These columns should only be filled out if there is an update or note to highlight from the CSMs. Column AA should be completed by the stakeholders if they have an action plan for a Property Manager who is experiencing an issue.
  </Check>

  <Frame>
    ![image-20250807-205132.png](/images/CPT-823.png)
  </Frame>
</Accordion>
