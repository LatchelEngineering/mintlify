---
title: "Weekly Reviews"
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this SOP is to organize the processes that the CPT must complete on a weekly basis and to have the job aid guides readily available for easy reference.
</Note>

<Accordion title="Assisted Ledgering">
  <Check>
    **Assisted Ledgering** is performed once a week to keep the GL code totals up to date. To learn more about how to complete the weekly process, refer to the job aid guide: [How to do the Weekly Review for Assisted Ledgering (Appfolio)](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3562766351/How+to+do+the+Weekly+Review+for+Assisted+Ledgering+Appfolio)
  </Check>

  <Warning>
    For newly onboarded property managers, ensure you first perform a one-time property-by-property review. Refer to the job aid guide:  [How to Conduct a Property-by-Property Review for Assisted Ledgering (AppFolio)](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3584589826/How+to+Conduct+a+Property-by-Property+Review+for+Assisted+Ledgering+AppFolio)
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Filters">
  <Check>
    **Filters** is performed once a week to keep the GL code totals up to date and to identify which properties should be reported at the end of the month. To learn more about how to complete the weekly process, refer to the job aid guide: [How to do Weekly Filters Review](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3563978794/How+to+do+Weekly+Filters+Review)
  </Check>
</Accordion>
