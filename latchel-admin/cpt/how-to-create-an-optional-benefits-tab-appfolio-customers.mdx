## Overview:

This SOP outlines the process for duplicating and setting up a new Optional Benefits tab in the CPT Tracker for a newly onboarded property manager. The goal is to ensure users maintain the correct structure, formulas, and headers in the tab, making it ready for accurate rent roll data entry and reporting
