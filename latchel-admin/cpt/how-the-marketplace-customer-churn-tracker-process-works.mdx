title: "How: the Marketplace Customer Churn tracker process works"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to define a clear process for detecting Marketplace-Benefits churn signals, coordinating prompt Customer Success follow-ups, and sending partner-specific cancellation notices—ensuring data accuracy in the Churn Tracker, consistent team communication, and swift action to safeguard active partnerships.
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Monitor Slack and Populate the Tracker

a. The Script continuously watches the [#service-changes](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/CKA2W1KJA) Slack channel.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-778.png" alt="image-20250512-202133.png" />
</Frame>

b. Whenever a **“Desire-to-Churn”** notification appears, the script checks whether the **Property-Manager Company** is listed as an **active Marketplace Benefits** **customer**.

c. If it finds a match, the script writes the relevant data to the correct partner tab (Piñata, Filters, Coverpest, Obie, etc.) in the [**Marketplace Customer Churn Tracker**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1FycRv5tniwSGDvGfGED8u4_bujlczMMheiPWjIys56E/edit?gid=1536833690#gid=1536833690).

**Note: **The full list of the active marketplace benefits PMs can be found in the PM Reference tab.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-779.png" alt="image-20250512-202240.png" />
</Frame>

<Accordion title="In the PM Reference tab, the data source columns are:">
**Column A:** Customer Name – List of active property managers
**Column B:** Partner – Type of marketplace benefit they have
**Column C:** Partnership Notifications Email / Slack Channel – Destination for cancellation notifications
**Column D:** Slack User IDs to Tag – Piñata users who will be tagged in cancellation notifications
**Column E:** DM Name – Decision maker’s name, included in the cancellation template
**Column F:** DM Email – Decision maker’s email, included in the cancellation template

d. CPT will promptly follow up with the CSM in the relevant “Desire to Churn” alert thread for each property manager and update the **Churn Status** column in that partner’s tab.

  <img src="/images/CPT-781.png" alt="image-20250512-204501.png" />

To ensure timely, consistent communication, follow-up templates have been created for every possible status in the **Churn Status** column of the Marketplace Customer Churn Tracker. Whenever a follow-up is needed, the matching template will be posted in the “Desire to Churn” workflow thread for Marketplace Benefits property managers, keeping the process streamlined.

| Churn Status | Follow-Up Purpose & Template | Recommended Timing |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 🟧 **CSM follow-up meeting in process** | _Confirm the customer meeting and gather notes._ _**Template**:_ `Hi [CSM Name],` `We see that you're in the process of scheduling a call with this PM. Could you let us know once that's scheduled? We are closely following the outcome since they are on Marketplace Benefits.` `Thank you in advance!` | Send if no update within **5 business days** |
| 🟪 **Pending CSM updates** | _Remind the CSM that progress updates are needed._ _**Template: **_ `Hi [CSM Name],` `We see there's a save attempt in progress for this PM. Could you please help us by sharing any relevant updates regarding the customer's situation? Thank you in advance!` | Repeat **every 5 days** until status changes |
| 🟩 **Churn Prevented – Customer Staying Onboard** | _Celebrate success and capture what worked._ _**Template:**_ `Hi [CSM Name]! 🎉` `Great to see that [Customer Name] is staying onboard!` `Could you briefly share what actions or efforts helped prevent the churn? This helps us replicate successful strategies with other accounts. ` `Awesome work, and thank you!` | Send **once**, immediately after the save is confirmed |

If the client confirms their cancellation after all retention outreach, no additional follow-up is required from the CSM. However, the CPT must follow the next steps to change the status to **“CSM follow-up completed — client still canceled”** in the** **[**Marketplace Customer Churn Tracker**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1FycRv5tniwSGDvGfGED8u4_bujlczMMheiPWjIys56E/edit?gid=1536833690#gid=1536833690)**.**

#### When the Client Confirms Cancellation:

**a) Open **[**HubSpot**](https://app.hubspot.com/contacts/23701847/objects/0-3/views/45332833/list) → open the customer record and check the _“Churn Status”_ column to confirm the client has canceled.

**b) In the Tracker** → Select in the _**Churn Status column**_ the status **“CSM follow-up completed — Client still canceled.”**

**c) Automatic next step** → the script will send the appropriate cancellation notification

- Cancellation **emails** are sent to _<u>Filters, Coverpest, and Obie partners</u>_.
- For **Piñata**, a **“Marketplace Benefits Churn” bot** posts the workflow message and tags **Clara** and **Ben** when a cancellation is detected.
