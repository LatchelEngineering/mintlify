title: "How to Prepare the Monthly Piñata Report for Metropolitan Real Estate"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide to ensure the accurate and complete preparation of the Monthly Piñata Report. This includes verifying GL-coded residents from the Rent Roll and Piñata reports sent by the Property Manager, updating tenant details in the Benefits Tracker, validating the count of enrolled units, and submitting the finalized report to both the Property Manager and the billing team for proper processing and tracking.
</Note>

## Step-by-step guide

a. Access the Piñata Overview tab in the Benefits Tracker.

Metropolitan Real Estate PMS access is unavailable. Go to the Assisted Ledgering Email to check the Rent Roll and Piñata reports sent by the property management.

### Step 3: Cross-Check and Update Residents

a. Check residents one by one:

- Use the **resident’s last name** to search. Use CTRL + F to search for every resident in the new list in the previous month’s data.
- Compare it to the previous month’s data in the Benefits Tracker.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-746.png" alt="image-20250807-193309.png" />
</Frame>

b. If the resident exists in the previous report:

**Note:** Ensure the resident's **First and Last NAME** in the new report matches the one from the previous report.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-747.png" alt="image-20250807-193935.png" />
</Frame>

- Copy (CTRL+C) the name from **Column B** and paste it into **Column C**.
- This indicates they’re already enrolled.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-748.png" alt="image-20250807-194631.png" />
</Frame>

c. If the resident **is not found** in the Piñata tab:

- Then it is a **new** **resident** and needs to be added to the report.

- Shipping State > ‘Primary Contact Info’ section
- Shipping Zip > 'Primary Contact Info' section

- Lease End Date > 'Lease Information' section

## **Step 5: Unit Count & Validation**

a.** In Column J** (Merge column) of the Piñata report:

- Apply the formula to count units being charged.
- Copy formula from previous month.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-761.png" alt="image-20250807-215451.png" />
</Frame>

- Paste the formula into your current report tab, which you previously duplicated.
- Drag the formula down to all rows.

b. From the previous month's report, scroll to the bottom of the columns and copy the formula used to calculate the count of Piñata enrolled.

c. Ensure it matches the GL codes total (For the Month to be reported) in the PM's tab.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-762.png" alt="image-20250807-221603.png" />
</Frame>

d. Paste the formula into your current report. Then, update it to reflect the correct number of rows based on your current data.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-763.png" alt="image-20250807-222225.png" />
</Frame>
