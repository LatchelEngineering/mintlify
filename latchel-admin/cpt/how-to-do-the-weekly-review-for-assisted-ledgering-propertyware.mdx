<Note>
  The purpose of this document is to be a guide for generating a Rent Roll report in Propertyware for the weekly reporting process.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Currently, we are **NOT** downloading the file, as we are not doing the weekly process as we do with other PMS. For this PM, you will only count the GL codes manually and report these in the **Compliance tab** on the [**CPT Tracker**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bQ45AVpZG5-ONwnSLFlJP8M5GCl7J__VczN1rfeCFQI/edit?gid=0#gid=0)[.](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1fh_c34tUsMygpoYeveXRncstJftEyJhh5QgMzzRvRKM/edit#gid=0)
</Warning>

<Warning>
  In **Assisted Ledgering**, **once their contract reaches the 12-month period, it will end**; you should stop doing their review. <u>Make sure to check regularly the **service start date** for your PMs assigned to Assisted Ledgering</u>.
</Warning>

## Step by step

### **Step 1 - Locate and run the pre-configured Latchel compliance report**

a. Log in to the PM's Propertyware account.

b. Navigate to **Desktop \> Home** and select **Reports**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-216.jpg)
</Frame>

c. In the "Report gallery," use the search bar to find the **"Latchel"** report folder.

d. Click on the folder and select **View Report** to open the pre-configured report.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-217.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Step 2 - Filter the report to capture all data from service start to the present day**

a. In the top bar of the report view, click on **Filters**.

b. A pop-up window will appear. Apply the following settings **exactly**:

c. Report Time Frame:

`- Where`: **Charge Detail – Start date**

`- Falls Within`: **Custom date range**

`- From`: The PM's **service start date**.

`- To`: The **current date**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-218.jpg)
</Frame>

d. Note: You can find the "Service start date" in the **Overall Details** tab of the CPT Tracker (column X).

e. Click **Apply**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-219.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Step 3 - Extract the total count of active GL codes**

a. Once the filtered report loads, scroll to the **bottom right side** of the page.

b. Locate and note the **total number** of GL codes displayed. This is the only data point you will extract from this report.

c. DO NOT download or export the file.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-220.jpg)
</Frame>

### **Step 4 - Manually update the CPT Tracker with the new data**

a. Navigate to the CPT Tracker Google Sheet.

b. Update the "Overall Details" tab:

- Find the Property Manager's row and update the date in the "Last Checked" column to today's date.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-221.jpg)
</Frame>

c. Update the "Compliance Timeline" tab:

- Find the PM's section and manually update the total number of GL codes and the date.
- In the row for the current week, enter the new GL code count and the resulting compliance percentage.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-222.jpg)
</Frame>
