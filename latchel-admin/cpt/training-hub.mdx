## Overview

This section serves as a foundational guide for new members of the Customer Profitability Department. It outlines key concepts, processes, and best practices that will help team members understand how the department operates and contributes to the organization’s goals. By following these job aid guides, new members will gain the knowledge necessary to perform their responsibilities effectively and align with departmental standards from the start.
