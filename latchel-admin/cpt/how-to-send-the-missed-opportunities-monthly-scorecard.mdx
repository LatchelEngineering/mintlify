<Note>
  This article provides a step-by-step guide on how to send the Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard to stakeholders.
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Download the Missed Opportunities report

a. Once the report is ready, select it and navigate to File \> Download \> PDF.

<Frame>
  ![MR6.jpg](/images/CPT-833.jpg)
</Frame>

b. Customize it by selecting under **Export \> Selected cells \> Export**.

<Frame>
  ![MR7.jpg](/images/CPT-834.jpg)
</Frame>

### Step 2: Customize the email

a. Create a new email to send the report, and use the following information:

<Frame>
  ![image-20241218-164437.png](/images/CPT-835.png)
</Frame>

b. Use the PM's POC you regularly use to send the Piñata and Filters reports and CC the CSM c. Type the subject: PMs Company Name \+ Updated Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard d. Use the following script depending on the report:

<Check>
  **Profitable and on track to profitability PMs:**

  Hi \[Property Manager's Name\], 

  We hope you’re doing well! We wanted to let you know that we’ve sent your updated Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard and have attached it here for your records. This report is a valuable tool designed to help you identify properties that are ready for a GL code addition and to provide a clear overview of the profitability achieved as of the last day of the previous month. By using this resource, you can better track opportunities and take proactive steps to enhance profitability for your portfolio.

  To clarify some key points from the report: 

  1. The "Missed Opportunities" section highlights properties where a GL code hasn’t been added yet, even though all other steps in the process are complete. 
  2. Regarding the terms "Has Lease" and "Has Addendum": 
     - "Has Lease" indicates that the lease has been uploaded to the system. 
     - "Has Addendum" means that an addendum has been included, which confirms the ability to apply the charge. This distinction is important for understanding where each property stands in the process. 

  If you have any questions about the report or need assistance with next steps, please don’t hesitate to reach out. We’re here to support you. 

  Best regards, 

  The Latchel Team
</Check>

<Check>
  **Non-Profitable PMs:**

  Hi \[Property Manager's Name\], 

  We hope you’re doing well!\_ 

  We wanted to let you know that we’ve sent your updated Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard and have attached it here for your records. This report is a tool designed to help you identify properties where GL codes can be added to address missed opportunities. Currently, your portfolio is at risk of not reaching profitability. By reviewing and acting on the opportunities highlighted in this report, you can take the necessary steps to improve profitability moving forward. 

  To clarify some key points from the report: 

  1. The "Missed Opportunities" section highlights properties where a GL code hasn’t been added yet, even though all other steps in the process are complete. 
  2. Regarding the terms "Has Lease" and "Has Addendum": 
     - "Has Lease" indicates that the lease has been uploaded to the system. 
     - "Has Addendum" means that an addendum has been included, which confirms the ability to apply the charge. This distinction is important for understanding where each property stands in the process. 

  If you have any questions about the report or need assistance with the next steps, please don’t hesitate to reach out. We’re here to support you. 

  Best regards, 

  The Latchel Team
</Check>

e. Attach the report as a PDF file.

f. Send the report.

g. Once you send the report, make sure to save the file in the folder [Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard](https://drive.google.com/drive/u/2/folders/1KksNvhC_zJkJiKK5s44roKFMJksL9JH2) and search for the corresponding folder for the PM you are working on.
