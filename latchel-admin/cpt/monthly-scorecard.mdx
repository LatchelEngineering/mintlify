<Note>
  The purpose of this article is to provide resources to help understand how the Monthly Scorecard process works. It serves as a tool to support effective conversations with stakeholders about assisting Property Managers, analyzing their performance, and identifying pain points that require further support.
</Note>

The Scorecard will be shared with stakeholders on the 6th of each month, prior to the invoices being sent. The data included will reflect the previous month.

**Example:** _On September 6th, the CPT will share the Monthly Scorecard containing August’s data points._
