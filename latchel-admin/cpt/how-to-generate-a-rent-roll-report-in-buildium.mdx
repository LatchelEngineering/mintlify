<Note>
The purpose of this document is to be a guide for generating a Rent Roll report in Buildium for the Tracker process.
</Note>

## **Step-by-step guide**

### **Step 1 - Navigate to the Rent Roll report configuration page**

a. Log in to the Property Manager's Buildium account.

b. From the main dashboard, select **"Reports"** from the left-side menu.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-155.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

c. Click on the **"Rent Roll"** report. If it's not in your favorites, use the search bar to find it.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-156.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

### **Step 2 - Apply the correct filters to capture the complete dataset**

a. In the report configuration section, set the following filters precisely:

- **As of date:** Ensure this is set to **today's date**.
- **Balances to include:** Check both the **"Zero"** and **"Credit"** boxes.
- **Lease status:** Ensure **"Current"** is selected.
- **Current lease type:** Ensure both **"Fixed"** and **"At will"** are selected.

b. Once all filters are set, click the **"Run Report"** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-157.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-158.jpg" alt="" />
</Frame>

**Step 3 - /Customize the report columns and export the data/**

a. After the report preview loads, click the **"Show/hide columns"** button.

b. Ensure that **only** the following columns are selected:

`- Lease end`

`- Lease start`

`- Rent`

`- Rent Cycle`

`- Rent Start`

`- Tenants`

`- Unit`

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-159.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

c. Click **Save** to apply the column changes.

d. Finally, click the **"Export"** button and choose the **Excel (.xlsx)** option. The file will be downloaded to your computer, ready for the next step in the weekly review process.

</Frame>
