---
title: "Monthly Reviews"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to organize the processes that the CPT must complete on a Monthly basis and to have the job aid guides readily available for easy reference.
</Note>

<Accordion title="Filters">
<Check>
**The Filters Report** is sent monthly to the stakeholders. This report includes properties (that had a GL code during the previous month) with their filter sizes. Refer to the job aid guide to learn how to do the report: [How to do Monthly Filters Review](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3564142678/How+to+do+Monthly+Filters+Review)
</Check>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Piñata">
<Check>
**The Piñata Report **is sent monthly to the stakeholders. This report includes properties (that had a GL code during the previous month), with all the financially responsible residents. Refer to the job aid guide to learn how to complete the report: [How to do Monthly Report for Piñata](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3564273738/How+to+do+Monthly+Report+for+Pi+ata)
</Check>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Optional Benefits">
<Check>
**The Optional Benefits Review** is conducted once a month. It includes properties that had a GL code during the previous month. After completing the review, the properties are reported to the Data Team so they can activate or deactivate them in Latchel’s console. Refer to the following job aid guides to learn how to perform the review:

[How to do the Monthly Review for Optional Benefits](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3564306545/How+to+do+the+Monthly+Review+for+Optional+Benefits) 
[How to: Request activation/deactivation of properties in Latchel console for OB Customers](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3119808527/How+to+Request+activation+deactivation+of+properties+in+Latchel+console+for+Optional+Benefits+Only+and+Maintenance+Optional+Benefits+Customers)
</Check>

<Warning>
For newly onboarded property managers, ensure you first perform a one-time property-by-property review. Refer to the job aid guide: [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/2953609279/How+to+do+Property+by+property+review+for+new+PMs+on+Optional+Benefits?atlOrigin=eyJpIjoiNjRiNjc2YjQ1NGMwNDI4YWI1ODYwMmMwODk4Y2E5MzUiLCJwIjoiYyJ9](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/2953609279/How+to+do+Property+by+property+review+for+new+PMs+on+Optional+Benefits)
</Warning>
</Accordion>
