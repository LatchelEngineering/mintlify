---
title: "How to Organize Monthly GL Reports"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to organize GL reports received on a monthly basis, ensuring they are saved in the correct email folder so they can be properly uploaded to the appropriate Drive folder.
</Note>

## Step by step

<Warning>
**<u>Excalibur</u>**<u>, </u>**<u>McElwee</u>**<u>, and </u>**<u>Trademark</u>**<u> send the GL report daily</u>. Make sure to save only the report from the last day of each month; all other reports can be deleted.
</Warning>

### Step 1: Go to the Assisted Ledgering email and check the Inbox section.

a. **Look for the GL reports received** in the Inbox section. <u>These are usually received on the 21st and 1st of each month.</u>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-375.jpg" alt="picture 11.jpg" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Open the email received and apply the correct label.

a. Open the email and apply the corresponding label for the month. **Select 'Move to' and type the month in the search bar.**

<Check>
**Example:** _**If it’s July 21st** and you just received the GL report, it should be labeled in the **July folder**. If the GL report is received on **August 1st**, it should still be labeled in the **July folder**. <u>Always check the report dates to ensure you're saving it in the correct folder.</u>_
</Check>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-376.png" alt="image-20250806-204543.png" />
</Frame>

b. Once it’s moved, **a confirmation message will be displayed showing the folder where the email is now saved**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-377.png" alt="image-20250806-204756.png" />
</Frame>

### Step 3: Confirm that the report was correctly saved and uploaded to the Drive folder.

a.** Select the GL Reports folder** for the current year to display the monthly folders. **Then, select the appropriate month's folder** to **confirm that the email was saved correctly**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-378.jpg" alt="picture 12.jpg" />
</Frame>

b. Open the email to check if the report has been uploaded to the Drive folder.<u> A '</u>**<u>Processed'</u>**<u> label will appear automatically if it was uploaded.</u>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-379.png" alt="image-20250806-205632.png" />
</Frame>

c. Go to the [**Drive folder**](https://drive.google.com/drive/u/1/folders/17-agFh9n0pDWY8wZiPrJq-t3NfXxXzr8) and select the month's folder to double-check that the report was uploaded successfully.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-380.png" alt="image-20250806-205952.png" />
</Frame>

d.** Search for the report.** <u>The name displayed will match the sender listed in the </u>**<u>'From' section</u>**<u> of the email.</u>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-381.png" alt="image-20250806-210345.png" />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-382.png" alt="image-20250806-210507.png" />
</Frame>

<Check>
**On the 1st of every month**, make sure to create the new month's label in the email and the new month's folder in Drive.
</Check>
