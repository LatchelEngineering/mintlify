## Overview:

This SOP outlines the process for duplicating and setting up a new Filters tab in the Benefits CPT Tracker for a newly onboarded property manager. The goal is to ensure the users can ensure the tab maintains the correct structure, formulas, and headers, making it ready for accurate rent roll data entry and reporting.
