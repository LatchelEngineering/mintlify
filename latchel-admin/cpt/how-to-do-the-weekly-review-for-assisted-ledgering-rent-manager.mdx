---
title: "How to do the Weekly Review for Assisted Ledgering (Rent Manager)"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this document is to provide a guide into how to do a weekly review of a Property Manager in the Tracker when the PMS is Rent Manager.
</Note>

<Warning>
In **Assisted Ledgering**, **once their contract reaches the 12-month period, it will end**; you should stop doing their review. <u>Make sure to check regularly the </u>**<u>service start date</u>**<u> for your PMs assigned to Assisted Ledgering</u>.
</Warning>

## **Step-by-step guide**

### **Step 1 - Login into Rent Manager**

a. Log in to the Property Manager's Rent Manager account.

b. Generate and download the **Rent Roll** report as a CSV file, following the specific SOP for that task.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-270.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

### **Step 2 - Go to the CPT Tracker**

a. Navigate to the PM's specific tab within the CPT Tracker.

b. Select cell **T1**.

c. Click **File > Import > Upload** and select the CSV file you just downloaded.

d. In the Import location options, select **"Replace data at selected cell"** and click **Import data**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-271.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

### **Step 3 - Analyze the changes flagged automatically by the tracker**

a. Once the new data is imported, review columns **J through M**. The tracker is designed to automatically highlight any changes in this section.

b. Investigate each flagged change to understand what has occurred on the account.

c. Before investigating, review the **"Notes"** section for that PM to see if there are any special cases to ignore (e.g., future renewals that shouldn't be tracked yet).

### **Step 4 - Record the final compliance numbers for historical tracking**

a. Navigate to the **"Compliance Timeline"** tab.

b. Find the row for the PM you are reviewing.

c. In the columns for the current week, update the GL code count and compliance percentage to match the final numbers calculated in the PM's main tab.
