<Note>
  The purpose of this job aid is to provide guidance to the Operations Support Specialist on handling escalations received from operations agents regarding residents' inquiries about the Marketplace Benefits we manage, specifically Piñata and Filters.
</Note>

## Overview

Occasionally, residents contact Latchel Service Ops seeking assistance with their Marketplace Benefits because these benefits do not appear in their Resident Hub or they are unsure how to request them. Since not all Property Managers offer these benefits, it is crucial to guide agents effectively to provide proper assistance to residents.

## Step by Step

<Check>
  **For all residents**, the agent must first check the Resident Hub for the resident reaching out to **verify if the benefit is active**. <u>If the benefit is active, the agent should provide the resident with the link to request assistance directly through their hub</u>.
</Check>

### Step 1: Login the Latchel Console

a. Go to the resident’s profile in the Latchel console and copy-paste the link to open it in a new tab.

  ![image-20241212-210708.png](/images/CPT-558.png)

b. Once you open the resident’s hub, **check if the benefit is active**. **Example**: _Resident inquiring about Filters or Piñata. The hub will appear as in the pictures._

<Warning>
  Since the PM may be enrolled in Piñata or Filter Time directly through those companies (and not via Latchel), there could still be an active tile in the hub even though the PM isn’t signed up through Latchel. If that’s the case, **<u>please verify the link within the tile. If the benefit is provided through Latchel, it will display our Typeform link.</u>**<u> **However, if the tile was set up by the PM, it will lead to a different link.**</u> In such cases, kindly direct the resident to follow the steps indicated after clicking the benefits link **Example**: \_A phone number appears when you click on the benefits link. \_

  _Hello \[Resident's Name\],_

  _Thank you for reaching out! To request assistance with \[Type of benefit\], please visit your Resident Hub at \[Link\]. Navigate to the “My Benefits” section in the top right corner, and from the menu, select the \[Benefit\] option and follow the indicated steps._

  _If you have any additional questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out._

  _Have a great day!_
</Warning>

<Check>
  Depending on the type of request the resident is inquiring about, you will notice the Typeform will be different.

  - **Air Filters:** For Air Filters, in the tile, it will direct you to the [FAQ Air Filters for Residents article](https://help.latchel.com/s/topic/0TO5e000000h9wrGAA/FAQ-Air-Filters-for-Residents). If the resident needs to submit filter sizes, they must fill out the [Typeform](https://latchel.typeform.com/to/ve5O9pds) in question #3. If the resident needs to submit a request due to receiving damaged, wrong, missing, or no filters, he will need to fill in the [Typeform](https://latchel.typeform.com/to/lKZfspdn)in question #5.
  - Piñata: For Piñata, in the tile, it will direct you to the following [Typeform](https://latchel.typeform.com/to/uHMYTNkh?typeform-source=latchel.atlassian.net).
</Check>

c. Share the resident’s hub link with the resident and adapt the following scripts depending on the benefit they are inquiring about.

### **Filter Time:**

a. <u>Resident inquiring on how to request filters:</u>

> _Hello \[Resident's Name\],_
>
> _Thank you for reaching out! To learn more about requesting Air Filters, please visit your Resident Hub at \[Link\]. Navigate to the “My Benefits” section in the top right corner, and from the menu, select the "Air Filters" option. Be sure to review all the information in the article FAQ Air Filters for Residents and submit the required filter sizes using the Typeform under Question #3._
>
> _Please note that only eligible residents are entitled to receive air filters. If you have any additional questions, feel free to reach out._
>
> _Have a great day!_

b. <u>Resident reaching out due to receiving damaged, wrong, missing, or no filters:</u>

> _Hello \[Resident's Name\],_
>
> _Thank you for reaching out, and we sincerely apologize for the inconvenience. We are committed to helping you receive the correct air filters. To proceed, please visit your Resident Hub at \[Link\]. Navigate to the “My Benefits” section in the top right corner, and from the menu, select the "Air Filters" option. Kindly review all the information in the article FAQ Air Filters for Residents and submit the required filter sizes using the Typeform under Question #5._
>
> _If you have any additional questions, please don’t hesitate to contact us._
>
> _Have a great day!_

### Piñata:

a.<u> Resident reaching out requesting assistance for any concern related to Piñata:</u>

> _Hello \[Resident's Name\],_
>
> _Thank you for reaching out! To request assistance with Piñata, please visit your Resident Hub at \[Link\]. Navigate to the “My Benefits” section in the top right corner, and from the menu, select the "Piñata" option. Kindly complete the Typeform provided in the link so your concern can be directed to the appropriate team for resolution._
>
> _If you have any additional questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out._
>
> _Have a great day!_

d. **<u>If the benefit the resident is inquiring about is not active in their hub</u>**, please check the following steps:

<Accordion title="Filters">
  **a. <u>If the Air Filters tile is not active in the resident’s hub</u>**. Please let the residents know they need to confirm with their property management company whether this resident benefit is included in their Latchel subscription and if they are eligible to receive it, as not all are enrolled. Please use the following script:

  > _Hello \[Resident's Name\],_
  >
  > _Thank you for reaching out! Since Air Filters are inactive in your Resident Hub at \[Link\], we recommend contacting your Property Manager to confirm whether this benefit is included in your Latchel subscription. If you have any additional questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out._
  >
  > _Have a great day!_
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Piñata">
  **a. <u>If the Piñata tile is not active in the resident’s hub</u>**. Please let the residents know they need to confirm with their property management company whether this resident benefit is included in their Latchel subscription, as not all are enrolled. Please use the following script:

  > _Hello \[Resident's Name\],_
  >
  > _Thank you for reaching out! Since Piñata is inactive in your Resident Hub at \[Link\], we recommend contacting your Property Manager to confirm whether this benefit is included in your Latchel subscription. If you have any additional questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out._
  >
  > _Have a great day!_
</Accordion>

**For any Marketplace benefits not listed** that the resident inquires about, ensure to check the Resident Hub to verify if they are active. <u>If the benefit is not active, kindly direct the resident to their Property Manager for further assistance.</u>
