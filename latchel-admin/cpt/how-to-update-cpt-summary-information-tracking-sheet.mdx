## **Daily Maintenance (Sheet Owner / Analyst)**

1. Open the Pending Information Tracking Sheet each workday.
2. Scan **Column L **(If so, please state what's missing)
3. Correct data entry errors or flag rows for follow-up if additional context is required.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-562.png" alt="image-20251112-223236.png" />
</Frame>

- **Recording an Update (whenever a change occurs)** 1. Locate the PM row(s) affected. 2. Update the specific fields (e.g., change `PMS Access` from "No" to "Yes" and add the access date in Column M - CSM Communication Date)

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-563.png" alt="image-20251118-143223.png" />
</Frame>

1. If the change impacts calculations (e.g., profitability units), verify formulas are correct and confirm updated results.

- **Notifying the CSM (after update) in **[**#ledgering-mkt-opt-benefits**](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C03D4N7B0LF) 1. Use the notification template (Slack). Fill in the variables: PM name, change description, affected fields, date, and link to sheet. 2. Send the message to the CSM and cc the Team Lead if the change is high-impact.
