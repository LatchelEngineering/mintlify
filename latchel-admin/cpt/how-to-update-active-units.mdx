---
title: "How to Update Active Units"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to update active units for each property manager in Assisted Ledgering and Optional Benefits on the 1st of every month, to ensure the monthly scorecard reflects accurate numbers.
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Open the [CPT Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bQ45AVpZG5-ONwnSLFlJP8M5GCl7J__VczN1rfeCFQI/edit?gid=0#gid=0) in the Overall Details tab

<Check>
Active units should be updated **every 1st of the month**.
</Check>

a.** Locate the column X** in the row of the corresponding Property Manager you’ll be checking.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-383.png" alt="image-20250806-194441.png" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Open [Latchel Console](https://app.latchel.com/admin/jobs) to look for every Property Manager.

a. **Go to the left menu** at the top of the main page and **click on People > Property Managers**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-384.png" alt="image-20250806-195054.png" />
</Frame>

b. **Type the name of the property manager** in the search bar and **click on the result displayed**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-385.png" alt="image-20250806-200732.png" />
</Frame>

c. The property manager’s profile will open. **Click on the Properties section**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-386.png" alt="image-20250806-200921.png" />
</Frame>

d. **Look at the number of properties displayed in the search box**. That will be the number you’ll enter in column X of the CPT Tracker.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-387.png" alt="image-20250806-201130.png" />
</Frame>

### Step 3: Go back to the CPT Tracker.

a. **Enter the total number in column X** for the corresponding property manager.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-388.png" alt="image-20250806-201447.png" />
</Frame>

b. **Repeat the same process for all Property managers** in Assisted Ledgering and Optional Benefits.

The Active Units column in Optional Benefits is located in column T.

<Warning>
For **DS Huber** and **Urban Coast**, update the active units until you finish the OB Review, as the active units total will be the same as the one reported and sent to the Data team each month. **<u>This applies to any other PM who has GL codes</u>**.
</Warning>
