<Note>
The purpose of this document is to provide the information needed to be able to check leases and addendums in DoorLoop to be able to report this in the Tracker.
</Note>

## Checking Leases

### Step 1: Log into DoorLoop for the Property Manager you will be working on.

DoorLoop offers different ways to view information. Here you will find 2 different processes to check leases.

<Accordion title="Through the Tenants' profile">
a. Your first view of the PMS will be the main page, you will need to find the tenant you need to check. To find a tenant, click **People** on the main menu and then click **Tenants**. Use the Search bar to type in the name of the tenant you are trying to find.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-349.png" alt="0.png" />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-350.png" alt="00.png" />
</Frame>

b. Once you have located the tenant you are looking for (you should always confirm with the property address), click on the tenant’s name, and next you will be directed to their portal. Once you are on the tenant's profile, click the **Leases **tab at the top. On this page, you will see their current lease. If they have multiple leases, you will see them all on this page as well.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-351.png" alt="0000000.png" />
</Frame>

c. Click on the lease you want to view.

This method is great if you need to look up multiple leases that a tenant might have.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Through the Active Leases section">
a. From the main page, go to the **Leasing **section, and click on **Active Leases. **

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-352.png" alt="00000000.png" />
</Frame>

b. Find the lease you are searching for and click on it. You may use the **Search Leases** search bar to look for the property or the tenant.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-353.png" alt="000000000.png" />
</Frame>

</Accordion>
