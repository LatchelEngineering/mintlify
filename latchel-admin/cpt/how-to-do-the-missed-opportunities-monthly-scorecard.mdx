---
title: "How to: do the Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard"
---

<Note>
This article is designed to provide a step-by-step on how to create the Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard, which is designed to assist PMs in achieving profitability and identifying missed opportunities throughout the month.
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Open the Monthly Scorecard of the month you’re reviewing

a. Once the [Monthly Scorecard](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1e9psvlXljLhCK_j_Dzhz1LmHRCYp7S23ns9kzJh19x0/edit?gid=2089192824#gid=2089192824) for the corresponding month is ready, locate the tab for the month to be reported. **Example**: _It’s December 07th; you will need to create the reports that include the information as of November._

b. Look at the column T to identify the Total Missed Opportunities of the Month.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-827.jpg" alt="MR4.jpg" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Open the Missed Opportunities Scorecard

a. Click on the link to display the information on the missed opportunities.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-828.png" alt="image-20241217-183653.png" />
</Frame>

b. And locate the tab for the PM and month that you will be reporting. **Example**: _You will be reporting Rent-LBK for November._

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-829.jpg" alt="MR5.jpg" />
</Frame>

c. Before copying and pasting the missed opportunities for the month, <u>refer to the Monthly Scorecard and check column F to determine whether the PM was profitable during the reporting month</u>. This will help you identify which report template to use.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-830.png" alt="image-20241217-184532.png" />
</Frame>

<Accordion title="Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard for Profitable Customers.">

In this [**template**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1cUXyWArqsBbbyLXQx6W0NXBPj97QfZdQ2dKTbI1tXMo/edit?gid=0#gid=0), you can find the information that should be included in the report.

1. Create a copy of the selected template and update it using the PM info in the Monthly Scorecard in the tab for the month to be reported.
2. Type the corresponding month to be reported before you fill in the template. **For example: **_if it’s December 07th, you will fill in the report for November._ 
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-831.png" alt="image-20241218-160033.png" />
</Frame>

3. To learn how to fill in the sections in the report, see the job aid [Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3292725254/Missed+Opportunities+Monthly+Scorecard) under point #3 for Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard for Profitable Customers.

You can find the data needed in the [Monthly Scorecard](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1e9psvlXljLhCK_j_Dzhz1LmHRCYp7S23ns9kzJh19x0/edit?gid=2089192824#gid=2089192824) for the corresponding month. For the % profitability attained to Date and Expected % Profitability after 12 months on Latchel, please refer to the [Real Time Monthly Scorecard in the CPT Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bQ45AVpZG5-ONwnSLFlJP8M5GCl7J__VczN1rfeCFQI/edit?gid=2144956335#gid=2144956335), this information should be the same as of the last day of the previous month to be reported. **Example**: _For the November Scorecard that will be sent on December 07th, you should include all the information as of November 30th._
</Accordion>
