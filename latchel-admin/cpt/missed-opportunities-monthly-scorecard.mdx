title: "Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard"

<Note>
This article provides an overview of the resources and processes related to the Missed Opportunities Monthly Scorecard, which is sent to PMs and CSMs every month. The report is designed to assist PMs in achieving profitability and identifying missed opportunities throughout the month.
</Note>

## Overview** **

1. Between the 5th and 7th of each month (or the next business day), the CPT will prepare and send the monthly report for both profitable and non-profitable customers. <u>This report will include all missed opportunities from the previous month. </u>**Example:** _On December 7th, the CPT will send the report highlighting all properties classified as missed opportunities as of November 30th._
2. Depending on the customer type (profitable or non-profitable), a different report template will be used. To determine whether a PM is profitable or non-profitable, refer to the[Monthly Scorecard in the CPT Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bQ45AVpZG5-ONwnSLFlJP8M5GCl7J__VczN1rfeCFQI/edit?gid=2144956335#gid=2144956335), specifically column F. **Example**: _For Green customers, you should choose the Monthly missed opportunities scorecard for Profitable customers. Whereas, for Red and Yellow customers, the Monthly missed opportunities scorecard for Non-profitable customers should be selected._

3. The report must include the following information:

a. In this [**template**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1cUXyWArqsBbbyLXQx6W0NXBPj97QfZdQ2dKTbI1tXMo/edit?gid=0#gid=0), you can find the information that should be included in the report:

1. **<u>Type of Contract: </u>** In this section, you need to include all the types of benefits the PM has with Latchel. **Example**: _Assisted Ledgering + Piñata + Filters._
2. **<u>Months on service:</u>** The amount of months on service as of the last of the previous month. **Example**: _You need to send the report on December 07th, you will include the number of months as of November 30th._
3. **<u>Active units for the month</u>**<u>:</u> The amount of active units as of the last of the previous month. **Example**: _You need to send the report on December 07th, you will include the active units as of November 30th._
4. <u>T</u>**<u>otal Units Ledgered to Date:</u>**<u> </u> The amount of GL codes added as of the last of the previous month. **Example**: _You need to send the report on December 07th, you will include the total GL codes added as of November 30th. _
5. **<u>Opportunities Missed this Month:</u>** The number of opportunities missed as of the last of the previous month. **Example**: _You need to send the report on December 07th, you will include the total number of missed opportunities as of November 30th. _
6. **<u>Current RTP Status:</u>** In this section, you will type the status in which the PM is as of the last of the previous month. **Example**: _You need to send the report on December 07th, you will type the status (Already Profitable) as of November 30th. _
7. **<u>% Profitability Attained to Date:</u>** In this section, you will type the profitability number of the PM as of the previous month. **Example**: _You need to send the report on December 07th, you will type the profitability number as of November 30th. _
8. **<u>Expected % Profitability after 12 months on Latchel:</u>** In this section, you will type the profitability number of the PM that was reflected as of the previous month. **Example**: _You need to send the report on December 07th, you will type the profitability number reflected as of November 30th. _
9. **<u>Missed Opportunities: </u>** This section includes the missed opportunities information such as: - Resident’s name - Address - Missed Opportunity Date - Has lease? - Has addendum? - Type of missed opportunity: New lease, renewal, monthly.
</Accordion>

**On Track to Profitability: **This status will be selected if the column G "% Profitability Attained to Date" indicates that the PM is projected to reach 100% or more profitability by the end of this period.

**At Risk of Achieving Profitability: **This status will be selected if the column G "% Profitability Attained to Date**% Profitability Attained to Date**" indicates that the PM is not projected to achieve profitability by the end of this period.

1. **<u>% Profitability Attained to Date:</u>** In this section, you will type the profitability number of the PM as of the previous month. **Example**: _You need to send the report on December 07th, you will type the profitability number as of November 30th. _
2. **<u>Expected % Profitability after 12 months on Latchel:</u>** In this section, you will type the profitability number of the PM that was reflected as of the previous month. **Example**: _You need to send the report on December 07th, you will type the profitability number reflected as of November 30th. _
3. **<u>Missed Opportunities: </u>** This section includes the missed opportunities information such as: - Resident’s name - Address - Missed Opportunity Date - Has lease? - Has addendum? - Type of missed opportunity: New lease, renewal, monthly.
