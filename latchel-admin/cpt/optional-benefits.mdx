## Overview

Customers on optional benefits offer Latchel benefits to their residents at each new, renewing, or month-to-month lease signing. **Residents can choose to opt in or out**. If they opt in, it is for the entire lease term, and the resident must pay a monthly charge each month. Residents can opt out at any time.
