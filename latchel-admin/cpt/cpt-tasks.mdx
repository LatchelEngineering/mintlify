title: "CPT Tasks"
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this SOP is to provide an overview of all the general tasks, including reviews and reports, that the CPT has to do throughout the month
</Note>

<Accordion title="Every 1st of the month">
  - [Update active units](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-update-active-units)
  - [Organize GL Reports](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-organize-monthly-gl-reports)
  - [Send follow-up emails for missing GL reports](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-follow-up-on-concession-reports)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Between the 1st-5th of the month">
  - [Prepare Monthly Scorecard](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-prepare-the-monthly-scorecard)
  - [Prepare Missed Opportunities](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-do-the-missed-opportunities-monthly-scorecard)
  - [Send Missed Opportunities Reports](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-send-the-missed-opportunities-monthly-scorecard)
  - [Prepare Piñata Review and send reports](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3564273738/How+to+do+Monthly+Report+for+Pi+ata)
  - [Special process for J&D Management (Piñata, Filters & Coverpest reports)](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3574169606/How+to+Do+the+Weekly+Monthly+Review+and+Reporting+J+D+Management)
  - [Special process for Chosen Management to do Piñata review](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3572400132/How+to+do+the+Pi+ata+review+in+RentVine)
  - [Special process for Chosen Management to send the Piñata report](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3572727836/How+to+do+the+Monthly+Pi+ata+Report+in+RentVine)
  - [Special process for New age to send the Piñata report](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3576627211/How+to+Prepare+the+Monthly+Pi+ata+Report+for+New+Age+Realty+Group)
  - [Special process for New age on how to report to Finance the Monthly totals for Piñata](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3577184297/How+to+Report+the+Monthly+Pi+ata+Totals+for+New+Age+Realty+Group)
  - [Special process for Metropolitan Real Estate on how to do the Piñata review & report](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3576791195/How+to+Prepare+the+Monthly+Pi+ata+Report+for+Metropolitan+Real+Estate)
  - [Special process on how to report Monthly totals to Finance for Property Managers who send their GL Report.](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-update-gl-code-totals-for-property-managers-who-submit-their-monthly-gl-report)
  - [Special process on how to report Monthly totals to Finance for Property Managers who are exceptions](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-update-gl-code-totals-for-property-managers-with-exceptions)
  - [Special process on how to report Monthly totals to Finance for Property Managers who are in a fixed monthly payment schedule](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-provide-billing-inputs-for-customers-on-fixed-monthly-payment-schedule)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Between 1st and 7th of the month">
  - [Prepare Monthly Filters Review & Report](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-do-monthly-filters-review)
  - [Prepare Monthly Optional Benefits Review & Report for the data team for units with GL code](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-do-the-monthly-review-for-optional-benefits)
  - [Special process to prepare the Monthly Optional Benefits Report for the data team, for units with a GL code](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-do-the-monthly-review-in-appfolio-for-clients-whose-optional-benefits-contract-has-ended)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Every 18th of the month">
  - [Confirm that the last three invoices are paid in Rillet for property managers in Filters](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-confirm-in-rillet-if-property-managers-invoices-are-paid)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Every 20th of the month">
  - [Process Filters Shipment](https://help.latchel.com/latchel-admin/cpt/how-to-process-the-filter-shipment)
</Accordion>
