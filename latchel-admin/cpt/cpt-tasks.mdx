<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to provide an overview of all the general tasks, including reviews and reports, that the CPT has to do throughout the month
</Note>

<Accordion title="Every 1st of the month">
- [Update active units](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3574497370/How+to+Update+Active+Units)
- [Organize GL Reports](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3574890529/How+to+Organize+Monthly+GL+Reports)
- [Send follow-up emails for missing GL reports](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/2971041814/How+to+Follow+up+on+Concession+Reports)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Between the 1st-5th of the month">
- [Prepare Monthly Scorecard](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3576725609/How+to+Prepare+the+Monthly+Scorecard)
- [Prepare Missed Opportunities](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3292332051/How+to+do+the+Missed+Opportunities+Monthly+Scorecard)
- [Send Missed Opportunities Reports](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3293216785/How+to+send+the+Missed+Opportunities+Monthly+Scorecard)
- [Prepare Piñata Review and send reports](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3564273738/How+to+do+Monthly+Report+for+Pi+ata)
- [Special process for J&D Management (Piñata, Filters & Coverpest reports)](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3574169606/How+to+Do+the+Weekly+Monthly+Review+and+Reporting+J+D+Management)
- [Special process for Chosen Management to do Piñata review](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3572400132/How+to+do+the+Pi+ata+review+in+RentVine)
- [Special process for Chosen Management to send the Piñata report](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3572727836/How+to+do+the+Monthly+Pi+ata+Report+in+RentVine)
- [Special process for New age to send the Piñata report](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3576627211/How+to+Prepare+the+Monthly+Pi+ata+Report+for+New+Age+Realty+Group)
- [Special process for New age on how to report to Finance the Monthly totals for Piñata](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3577184297/How+to+Report+the+Monthly+Pi+ata+Totals+for+New+Age+Realty+Group)

<Accordion title="Between 1st and 7th of the month">
- [Prepare Monthly Filters Review & Report](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3564142678/How+to+do+Monthly+Filters+Review)
- [Prepare Monthly Optional Benefits Review & Report for the data team for units with GL code](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3564306545/How+to+do+the+Monthly+Review+for+Optional+Benefits)

<Accordion title="Every 18th of the month">
- [Confirm that the last three invoices are paid in Rillet for property managers in Filters](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/2999091351/How+to+Confirm+in+Rillet+if+Property+Managers+invoices+are+paid)

<Accordion title="Every 20th of the month">
- [Process Filters Shipment](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3564306454/How+to+Process+the+Filter+Shipment)
