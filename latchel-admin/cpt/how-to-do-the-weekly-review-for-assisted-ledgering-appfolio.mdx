---
title: "How to do the Weekly Review for Assisted Ledgering (Appfolio)"
---

<Note>
The objective of this SOP is to ensure accurate tracking of properties with GL codes through weekly reviews, identify any newly added or vacant properties, and maintain a record of GL codes that are paid, unpaid, or partially paid.
</Note>

<Warning>
In **Assisted Ledgering**, **once their contract reaches the 12-month period, it will end**; you should stop doing their review. <u>Make sure to check regularly the </u>**<u>service start date</u>**<u> for your PMs assigned to Assisted Ledgering</u>.
</Warning>

## Step by step

### **Step 1: How to Generate Rent Roll in the PMS**

a. Log in to the Property Management Software (PMS) – AppFolio.

b. Navigate to the top menu and click on **Reporting**.

c. Select **Reports** from the dropdown menu.

d. Click on **Rent Roll (Itemized)** to open the report.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-30.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

### **Step 2: Filter and Update the Rent Roll Report**

a. Search for the GL Code

b. You can find the GL code for the Property Manager (PM) in the [**<u>CPT Tracker</u>**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bQ45AVpZG5-ONwnSLFlJP8M5GCl7J__VczN1rfeCFQI/edit?gid=0#gid=0), under the

**Overview Details** tab.

c. Set the Filters

d.** Units:** Select **"Show Active Units"**

e.** As of Date:** Enter the date of your review

f. Click** "Update"** to generate the filtered report.

  <img src="/images/CPT-31.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

### **Step 3: Customize and Export the Rent Roll Report**

a. Click on Customize Columns and ensure the following fields are selected:

- _Unit _
- _Status_
- _Property Address_
- _Tenant_
- _Lease From_
- _Lease To_
- _Eligible For Rent Increase_
- _Move-in_
- _Move-out_

b. Once customized, go to Actions and click Export as CSV.

a. Open a new spreadsheet (Google Sheets)

b. Click the blank cell in the upper-left corner (between Column A and Row 1) to select the entire sheet.

c. Click the Filter icon (or go to Data > Create a Filter) to enable filters for all columns.

a. In your new spreadsheet, go to **File > Import**.

b. Click the **Upload** tab.

c. Click **Browse** and select the **CSV file** you exported from PMS.

d. When prompted, choose **"Replace data at selected cell"**.

e. Click **Import data** to load the data into your spreadsheet.

a. In the current Rent Roll sheet, copy **Columns A to J starting from Row 2 (A1 to J1)** down to the last row, ensuring the number of properties matches the count in the PMS report.

b. Open the CPT Tracker and go to the tab of the PM you are reviewing.
Paste the copied data into the sheet starting at** Column AI to AR, using CTRL + SHIFT + V** to paste without formatting.

### **Step 13: What are the Missed Opportunities **

a. New or renewed leases are sometimes entered without a GL code in their recurring charges. This happens even when there’s a Latchel addendum or a lease uploaded. Without the GL code, these charges can’t be tracked properly, leading to reporting and accounting issues. Ensure you mark these accordingly by selecting any of the following options if that’s the case:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-56.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

<Note>
_**Reminder:** Will renew in the next 15-60 days - If there is a current lease that is about to expire soon, and the resident is likely to renew_
</Note>

- _Just renewed, and no GL code was added; enter the lease start date._
- _Month to Month with no GL code added- If the resident is on a month-to-month lease _
- _New tenant and no GL code added - If the resident moved in after the PM’s onboarding date, and no GL code has been added in the PMS_

b. ** **Insert the lease start date in **Column X. **If no matching information is found, proceed to **Step 14**.
