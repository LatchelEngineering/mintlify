title: "How to: Send monthly updates to Marketplace Benefits Customers"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this article is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to send the emails with monthly updates, to customers that have Marketplace Benefits (Piñata, Air Filters, Cover Pest).
</Note>

## Step by step

If a customer is on more than one Marketplace Benefit, consolidate the email templates provided below so that the customer only receives one monthly update email.

<Accordion title="Piñata">
## Launch

### **Step 1: Share the launch rent roll with Piñata**

By the <u>5th</u> of the launch month (or next business day), for each customer on their first month of Piñata:

a. Locate the customer’s folder in [<u>Property Manager Files</u>](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1K-OxCEHlyfI6GNhukN9aNPsZho1yzz7T)<u>,</u> within the customer’s folder, locate the Piñata folder.

b. Within the customer’s Piñata folder, create a Google sheets workbook and name it [Customer Name] Latchel Piñata Rent Rolls. <u>Example:</u> All American Latchel Piñata Rent Rolls. Name the first sheet **Launch.**

c. Copy columns A-R from the customer’s Piñata tab in the [Benefits CPT Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qd4oXlu6dBYwvPCVnl8fMwoulezF2MTZQoGOhqPqdo0/edit?gid=675879001#gid=675879001) and paste it into the Launch tab.

d. Complete columns A-C using the information on the customer’s Piñata order form, located inside the customer’s Piñata folder. Example: [<u>Piñata Pro Order Form - All American.pdf</u>](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1dsqNyCQwGdH6QWfqPgyp0qdq1ep-244O/view?usp=sharing)

e. Provide Commenter access to Clara ([clara@pinata.ai](mailto:clara@pinata.ai)) and Ben ([benm@pinata.ai](mailto:benm@pinata.ai)) on the [PM Name] Latchel Piñata Rent Rolls spreadsheet, keeping the “Notify people” checkbox selected.

### **Step 2: Share the launch rent roll with the customer**

a. Provide Commenter access to the PM contact from the Piñata order form on the [PM Name] Latchel Piñata Rent Rolls spreadsheet. Deselect the “Notify people” checkbox before sharing.

b. Email the PM contact from the rent roll with Clara ([clara@pinata.ai](mailto:clara@pinata.ai)), Ben ([benm@pinata.ai](mailto:benm@pinata.ai)), and the CSM on copy. Insert the file using Google Drive ([PM Name] Latchel Piñata Rent Rolls spreadsheet).

<u>Email template:</u>
Subject: [PM Name] Latchel Piñata Launch

_Hi [PM Name],_
_We're excited to confirm that we’ve just sent your first rent roll to Piñata for this month’s launch! All of the residents in the attached file will receive an invitation from Piñata to join the app within the next week. In the unlikely event that a resident doesn't receive or can't find their invitation in their email inbox, they can always reach out to _[_support@pinata.ai_](mailto:support@pinata.ai)._ _
_Your upcoming invoice from Latchel will also reflect the number of Piñata units listed in the attached file. Please let us know if you have any questions!_
_Kind regards,_
_The Latchel Team_

a. By the <u>5th</u> of the month (or next business day), for each customer on their 2nd+ month of Piñata, locate the customer’s folder in  [<u>Property Manager Files</u>](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1K-OxCEHlyfI6GNhukN9aNPsZho1yzz7T), within the customer’s folder, locate the Piñata folder.

b. Within the customer’s Piñata folder, locate the Google sheets workbook named [Customer Name] Latchel Piñata Rent Rolls. Create a new tab and name it DDMonYY. 
Example: [<u>All American Latchel Pinata Rent Rolls</u>](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/13ejVZoWdCoMbxg8J0NN5xzh_fKyA4eNRMeal9pE6BpI/edit#gid=926432614)

<Accordion title="Air Filters">
## Launch

### **Step 1: Share the launch filter summary with the customer**

a. By the <u>6th working day</u> of the month, for each customer on their first month of filters, locate the customer’s folder in Property Manager Files. Within the customer’s folder, locate (or create) the Filters folder.

b. Within the customer’s Filters folder, create a Google sheets workbook and name it [Customer Name] Filters Monthly Report. Name the first sheet DDMonYY. 
**Example:**  All American Filters Monthly Report.

c. Add filter information from [<u>Benefits CPT Tracker</u>](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qd4oXlu6dBYwvPCVnl8fMwoulezF2MTZQoGOhqPqdo0/edit#gid=270241980) for all properties that had a GL code added during the previous month. If we’re missing some/all filter information, include those addresses but leave Filter Size 1 as TBD. 
**Example:** [<u>RPM Sunshine City Monthly Filters Report</u>](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1y1142OWzLcGcRDUb1FE_vtirx5sH-nGJQtqDlfT-088/edit#gid=0)

d. Verify that the Frequency column is correct based on the customer’s subscription

- Annual: 12
- Semi-annual: 6
- Quarterly A: 6 (2 sets)
- Quarterly B: 3

e. Verify that the Start Date is on the 20th of the 3rd month of service (ex: if the customer is launching on August 1, 2024, the Start Date is 10/20/2024)

f. Provide Commenter access to the PM contact from the customer’s Onboarding Checklist on the [Customer Name] Filters Monthly Report spreadsheet. Deselect the “Notify people” checkbox before sharing.

g. Email the PM contact from the customer’s Onboarding Checklist with the CSM on copy. Attach the [Customer Name] Filters Monthly Report spreadsheet.

<u>Subject: [PM Name] Latchel Filters Launch</u>

_Hi [PM Name]!_

_I’m excited to confirm that we’ve programmed your first shipment with Filter Time, scheduled to ship on [date], to all of the units that had a benefits charge code added last month. Your upcoming invoice from Latchel will reflect the number of filters you see in the attachment. Please let us know if you have any questions!_

_Best,_

_The Latchel Team_

**Example email when we don’t have filter info at launch:**

<u>Subject: [PM Name] Latchel Filters Launch</u>

_Hi (PM Name),_

_I hope this message finds you well! Attached here, you’ll find your May filters report. This report shows all units that had a benefits charge code added during the month of April. We'll just need to collect filter quantity and sizes from you prior to the first shipment on July 20. For now, we'll just assume all of these units have 1 filter that will be fulfilled by Latchel. If we find out they have more/less, we can make billing adjustments next month._

_Please let us know if you have any questions!_

_Best,_

_The Latchel Team_

## Monthly Update

### **Step 1: Share the monthly filter summary with the customer**

a.<u> By the 6th working day</u> of the month, for each customer on their 2nd+ month of filters, locate the customer’s folder in Property Manager Files. Within the customer’s folder, locate the Filters folder.

b. Within the customer’s Filters folder, locate the Google sheets workbook named [Customer Name] Filters Monthly Report. Create a new tab and name it DDMonYY. Example:  All American Filters Monthly Report

c. Add filter information from Benefits CPT Tracker for all properties that had a GL code added during the previous month. If we’re missing some/all filter information, include those addresses but leave Filter Size 1 as TBD. 
**Example:** [<u>RPM Sunshine City Monthly Filters Report</u>](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1y1142OWzLcGcRDUb1FE_vtirx5sH-nGJQtqDlfT-088/edit#gid=0)

d. Verify that the Frequency column is correct based on the customer’s subscription

- Annual: 12
- Semi-annual: 6
- Quarterly A: 6 (2 sets)
- Quarterly B: 3

e. Verify that the Start Date is on the 20th of the 3rd month we’re billing a property for filters (ex: if a GL code was added to a property on 8/15/2024, we report it and bill the PM for this property’s filters for the first time in August’s invoice. The Start Date should be set to 11/20/2024).

f. Email the PM contact from the customer’s Onboarding Checklist with the CSM on copy. Attach the [Customer Name] Filters Monthly Report spreadsheet.

<u>Subject: [PM Name] Latchel Filters Monthly Update</u>

_Hi [PM Name]!_

_I hope you’re doing well. Attached here, you’ll find our updated list of filters for each of your units enrolled in this benefit. Your upcoming invoice from Latchel will also reflect the number of filters you see in the attachment. As a reminder, a property’s first filter shipment is programmed for the 20th of the 3rd month we’ve charged you for its filters. Please let us know if you have any questions!_

_Best,_

_The Latchel Team_
