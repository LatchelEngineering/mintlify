---
title: "How to: Confirm in Rillet if Property Managers' invoices are paid"
---

<Note>
  The aim of this article is to be a guide on how to check Property Managers' invoices in the Rillet platform, and confirm if they have been paid in each month.
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Go to Rillet

a. First, sign in to [Rillet platform.](https://app.rillet.io/) (If your account had already been set up, select the option “Sign in with Google).

<Frame>
  ![01.png](/images/CPT-389.png)
</Frame>

b. Once in, go to the left-side menu, and click on the Accounts Receivable section.

<Frame>
  ![0.png](/images/CPT-390.png)
</Frame>

c. Next, click on **Customers,** which will direct you to the customers' list.

<Frame>
  ![00.png](/images/CPT-391.png)
</Frame>

d. Using **Ctrl \+ F** open the search bar, enter the Property Manager account’s name that you need to check, and once it is showing, <u>click on the account’s name</u> which will display the Property Manager’s profile.

<Frame>
  ![000.png](/images/CPT-392.png)
</Frame>

e. When you are in the Property Manager’s profile that you need to check, go to the **Invoices** section.

<Frame>
  ![0000.png](/images/CPT-393.png)
</Frame>

f. In the invoices' list look for the date of the month you need to confirm paid, which will be found in the <u>Invoice Date column.</u> If the invoice has been paid, you will see the label \*\*Paid \*\*at the very beginning of the row, in the <u>Status column.</u>

<Frame>
  ![00000.png](/images/CPT-394.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
  Keep in mind that the date showing in the **Invoice Date** column, is the date when the invoice was sent, but this will cover the previous month’s charges. Example: If the Invoice date says 4/10/2024, this is March’s invoice.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  The CPT must confirm if the invoices have been paid during the week after the invoices were sent by the Finance department (after the 8th business day of each month). For Air Filters' customers the invoice must be paid before the 20th of the month to be able to proceed with shipments.
</Warning>
