<Note>
  The purpose of this section is to provide a step by step on how to do our **monthly process** for reviewing and paying our partners, such as Piñata and Coverpest. The goal is to review each invoice sent by our partners, ensure the amounts align with what our team reported in the [**Billing inputs Master Doc**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1HG_bRF-JM8DqSz1lusDrfJVGIpohvJwBP4fNAipclvM/edit?gid=1596062573#gid=1596062573), and make sure our partners are paid accurately and on time
</Note>

## **Step by Step**

### **Step 1: Receive and Organize Invoices:**

a. Invoices will be delivered to the **Assisted Ledgering inbox** from each provider.

b. Note that **<u>we typically have a 30-day payment term</u>**<u>, so there is no need for immediate action upon receipt.</u>

### Step 2: **Verify Invoice Accuracy:**

a. Review each invoice against internal data to confirm its accuracy.

For detailed verification steps, refer to the specific SOP for each provider.

<Warning>
  If you identify an error (e.g., an overcharge), **do not approve the invoice**. Reply directly to the provider's email, clearly explain the discrepancy, and request a corrected invoice.
</Warning>

### **Step 3: Submit for Payment:**

a. Once an invoice is confirmed as correct, forward the original email to Accounts Payable (AP).

- **Recipient:** @Samantha Terreros (AP)
- **Body:**

> _Hi Sammy,_ 
>
> _This invoice is approved for payment._  
>
> _Thank you._

**Key Considerations:**

- **Time Management:** It is efficient to block one hour mid-month to review all outstanding invoices at once.
- **Payment Follow-Ups:** If a provider reports a missed payment for an invoice you've already approved, please loop in Sammy (AP). This is typically due to a payment card issue that she can resolve directly.
