<Note>
  This article will provide the step by step on how to automate reports in Propertyware during the onboarding process.
</Note>

## Step by Step

### **Step 1 - Prepare the report for automation by verifying its filters**

a. Log in to the PM's Propertyware account.

b. Navigate to the **Reports** module.

c. Use the search bar or the report list to find the specific report you need to schedule.

d. Click **View Report** to open it.

<Warning>
  **Note:** Before scheduling, you must verify the report's filters. To ensure the report automatically updates each month, you **must** use a dynamic date range option provided by Propertyware (e.g., "Last Month"). **Do not** use "Custom Date Range," as this will lock the report to a static period.
</Warning>

e. Once the filters are correct, click **Save** on the toolbar to lock in the settings.

f. Once you select the report, click the “view report” option.

### **Step 2 - Configure the automated delivery schedule and recipients**

a. With the correctly filtered report open, click **Email / Schedule** on the toolbar.

b. In the pop-up window, click **Send Later**, then select the **Scheduled** tab.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-235.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-236.png)
</Frame>

c. Fill out the scheduling form with the following details:

- **Start Sending:** Click the calendar and select the date the first report should be sent (e.g., the last day of the current month).
- **Frequency:** Use the dropdowns to select the appropriate frequency (e.g., "Monthly").
- **From:** Select the appropriate user's email address from the dropdown list.
- **Send To:**

d. Click the **"Specified Recipients"** option.

e. Enter the required email addresses, separated by a semi-colon (e.g., `accounting@latchel.com; ledgering@latchel.com`).

f. Message: Enter a clear subject and a brief message.

g. Review all settings one last time, then click **Send** to activate the schedule.
