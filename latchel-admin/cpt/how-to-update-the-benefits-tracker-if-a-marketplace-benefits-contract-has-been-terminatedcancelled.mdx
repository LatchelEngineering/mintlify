---
title: "How to Update the Benefits Tracker if a Marketplace Benefits Contract Has Been Terminated/Cancelled"
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on updating the [Benefits Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qd4oXlu6dBYwvPCVnl8fMwoulezF2MTZQoGOhqPqdo0/edit?pli=1&gid=675879001#gid=675879001) when a Property Manager (PM) terminates or ends their Benefits contract. This ensures records are kept accurate.
</Note>

### Step 1: Go to the Benefits Tracker and navigate to the appropriate tab based on the PM’s subscription type: Filter, Piñata, or Coverpest.

a. Move the PM to the ‘Account Terminated’ section – Terminated/Cancelled Contracts and highlight it in ‘gray’
