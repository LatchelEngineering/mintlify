---
title: "How to: Report to the Operations Support Specialist when they need to collect Air Filter Information from Residents."
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this document is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to report to the Operations Support Specialist the list of properties from which they need to collect Air Filter information from residents, and how to follow up when the residents have been unresponsive or they don’t know how to provide the Air Filters measurements.
</Note>

## Instructions

<Accordion title="First Contact">
  1. On the 15th of each month **(or next business day), the CPT posts in** #operations\_support channel a list of any properties with missing air filter information.
  2. Make a copy of [<u>April 2024 Legacy Oak filter measurements</u>](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1WH7NIDV_yR4ABP2ik3q78oHbZsiWvT6_1yczuPTRLfw/edit#gid=0)), and update it for the addresses we need to collect information for. Leave the same spreadsheet’s title, only change the month with the month on which we are doing the follow-ups, and name the tab ”First Contact”. Make sure sharing permissions are set to <u>anyone at Latchel can edit.</u>

  **Slack post template:**

  > _Hi @support, Happy (day of the week)!_
  >
  > The initial list of this month for the first reach-out from your team, is ready. Kindly send the filter information survey to active residents for the properties included in this spreadsheet \[link “First Contact” Tab\] by following <u>Steps 1 and 2</u> of this [**_SOP_**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OPS/pages/3091267606/Service+Operations+Collecting+Air+Filter+Information+from+Residents). We will update you by (expectation of the second updated list to be ready) for the second reach-out, and steps that need to be followed in the SOP. Let us know if you have questions, thank you!

  The Operations Support Specialist will proceed to send the necessary emails and SMS to all residents in the properties listed, where they will include the [**Filter Measurements Typeform**](https://latchel.typeform.com/to/ve5O9pds.) **for residents to complete.** The Operations Support Specialist will notify CPT in the same thread when that is done.

  1. We will be receiving residents' Typeform answers to the #cpt-updates channel in Slack. CPT will collect and register this information in the PM’s AU tab in the[Benefits Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qd4oXlu6dBYwvPCVnl8fMwoulezF2MTZQoGOhqPqdo0/edit?gid=675879001#gid=675879001), and add it in FilterTime Platform.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Second Contact">
  1. On the 20th of each month (or the next business day), the CPT provides an update in the original post thread in the #operations\_support channel on which properties still haven’t provided filter information.
  2. Create a new tab in the same spreadsheet previously shared with the Operations Support Specialist and name it “Second contact”, include the list of properties that still have not provided filter information.

  **Slack post template:**

  > _Hi @support, Happy (day of the week)!_
  >
  > _We’re still missing filter information for the addresses listed in this spreadsheet \[link “Second Contact” Tab\].  Kindly send the filter information survey to active residents for the properties included in this spreadsheet by following <u>Steps 3 and 4</u> of this **[SOP](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OPS/pages/3091267606/Service+Operations+Collecting+Air+Filter+Information+from+Residents)**._ \_We will update you by (expectation of the third updated list to be ready) for the third reach out, and steps that need to be followed in the SOP. Let us know if you have questions, thank you!

  The Operations Support Specialist resends the email and SMS templates to all active residents at the properties that still haven’t provided filter information.

  1. We will be receiving residents' Typeform answers to the #cpt-updates channel in Slack. CPT will collect and register this information in the PM’s AU tab in the [Benefits Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qd4oXlu6dBYwvPCVnl8fMwoulezF2MTZQoGOhqPqdo0/edit?gid=675879001#gid=675879001), and add it to FilterTime Platform.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Third Contact">
  1. On the 25th of each month (or the next business day), the CPT provides an update in the thread of the original post in the **#operations\_support channel** linking the spreadsheet of properties that still haven’t provided filter information.
  2. Create a new tab in the same spreadsheet previously shared with the Operations Support Specialist and name it “Third Contact”, include the list of properties that still have not provided filter information. 

  **Slack post template:** 

  > _Hi @support, Happy (day of the week)!_ _We’re still missing filter information for the addresses listed in this spreadsheet \[link “Third Contact” Tab\].  Kindly assign a Service Ops Associate to call active residents and take notes in the spreadsheet by following <u>Steps 5 and 6</u> of this **[SOP](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/OPS/pages/3091267606/Service+Operations+Collecting+Air+Filter+Information+from+Residents)**._ Let us know if you have questions, thank you for your help!

  After the Service Ops follows up with residents, they will update the assignee to their name for tracking purposes, in the spreadsheet shared by the CPT. Then they will post in **#cpt-updates** when they’ve called all residents and made notes in the spreadsheet.

  1. We will be receiving residents' Typeform answers to the #cpt-updates channel in Slack. CPT will collect and register this information in the PM’s AU tab in the[Benefits Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qd4oXlu6dBYwvPCVnl8fMwoulezF2MTZQoGOhqPqdo0/edit?gid=675879001#gid=675879001), and add it to FilterTime Platform.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Tenant Responses">
  <Check>
    If a resident submits the Typeform with an unusual filter size or if there is any incorrect information we need to double-check, it will be best to reach out to the resident via email. You can send it through the email [ledgering@latchel.com](mailto:ledgering@latchel.com) to the resident. **NOTE**: Make sure to CC the PM and the CSM.

    You can customize the following template:

    Hi (_Tenant’s name_),

    Thank you for providing your air filter information! We just wanted to double-check the dimensions with you since the size you submitted (_Filter’s size_) is uncommon. We believe you likely need size (_suggested size_). Could you kindly review [**Section 2**](https://help.latchel.com/s/article/FAQ-Air-Filters-for-Residents) of our help guide and reply to this email to confirm the size needed? Thank you in advance!

    Kind regards,

    The Latchel Team
  </Check>

  <Check>
    If for some reason a resident reaches out to us via email, we can reply by requesting the resident to fill out the form. You can use or adapt the following script:

    _Hi (resident name),_

    _Our team attempted to reach out regarding the filter size needed for the next shipment. Could you please review **[Section 2](https://help.latchel.com/s/article/FAQ-Air-Filters-for-Residents)** of our help guide and submit **[this form](https://latchel.typeform.com/to/ve5O9pds?typeform-source=help.latchel.com)** for the required size? Thank you in advance!_

    _Kind regards,_ _The Latchel Team_
  </Check>
</Accordion>

**On the 27th (or the next business day), the CPT notifies the CSM in #ledgering-mkt-opt-benefits** if we’re still missing responses. Create a new tab in the same spreadsheet previously shared with the Operations Support Specialist and name it “CSM Contact”, include the list of properties that still have not provided filter information.

**Slack post template:**

_Hi \[CSM Name\]! Could you please reach out to \[PM Name\] to let them know we’re still missing filter information for the addresses in this spreadsheet \[link spreadsheet “CSM Contact” Tab\]? Here is an email template you can use:_

> _“Hi \[PM Name\],_
>
> _After multiple emails, texts and calls to residents last week, we are still missing filter information for a few addresses._
>
> _This spreadsheet \[link spreadsheet\] includes the addresses for which we’re missing information. For billing purposes, we'll just assume each of these properties with missing info have 1 filter that will be fulfilled by Latchel. If we find out they have more/less/will be fulfilled by the AC company, we can make billing adjustments next month. Please let us know if you have any questions!”_ 
>
> _Thank you, (CSM name)_ 

<Check>
  CPT waits for updates from the CSM, and follows up next month (10th, or the next business day), if the CSM has not provided any update.
</Check>

For residents who have already been contacted in a previous list sent to the Operations Support Specialist, you must follow up directly with the CSM. Do not include them in the list again for the Operations Support Specialist to follow up. Remind the CSM that we need their help to determine the next steps with the PM.
