## A-D

| **Name** | **Definition** | **Abbreviation** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Actual Collected Revenue | This is the actual revenue collected by the customer based on the resident charge and the GL codes that were actually paid by the residents. This will be determined by the amount paid from each property which is affected by partial payments, prorated charges or unpaid GL codes. The difference between the expected collected revenue and the actual collected revenue will be concessioned by Latchel during the term of the Assisted Ledgering contract or until the customer hits profitability. | ACR |
| Assisted Ledgering | System in which Latchel is able to enforce their contract and help the client become profitable. | AL |
| Committed Monthly Recurring Revenue | The value of the recurring portion of subscription revenue. This is the portion of subscription revenue that is recognized each month. | CMRR |
| Compliance percentage | It shows how compliant has the PM company been on adding GL codes each month, this is based on the projection of how many GL codes they should be adding each month to reach profitability. | N/A |
| Concession report | Financial report that discloses a list with the residents that currently have the Latchel charge. This is usually provided directly by the PM company in a monthly basis. | N/A |
| Customer Success Manager | Manager assigned specifically to a PM to be the point of contact for Latchel. We can also refer to them as Stakeholders. | CSM |
| Customer Profitability | Customer profitability refers to a metric that involves assessing the revenue generated by a property management company based on the GL codes added over a 12-month period by the customer against the associated number of ledgerable properties. | CP |
| Customer Profitability Team | The customer profitability team is a department within Latchel that is specifically focused on analyzing and managing the profitability of each property management company. The primary responsibilities of a customer profitability team include analysis and reporting, which includes examining data related to property manager properties added each week and month and reporting to the appropriate stakeholders any issues related to the properties added by the property manager. | CPT |
| Deep Dive | Process in which the CP agent confirms in the customer’s PMS the status of the payment for each GL code for all the properties with a GL code added during a month. During this process the agent confirms if the GL codes are paid, partially paid (noting the amount), or unpaid. The agent will sum all the payments to determine the customer’s actual collected revenue. | N/A |

## E-K

| **Name** | **Definition** | **Abbreviation** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Expected collected revenue | This is the revenue Latchel expects the customer to collect during a month based on the resident charge and the properties with a GL code added (Assuming all GL codes are paid by the residents). | ECR |
| Filter subscription | As part of the filters benefit Latchel offers different types of subscriptions which determine the frequency of shipments, and the amount of filters included in each shipment. Currently Latchel offers 4 types of subscriptions (Annual, semiannual, Quarterly A, Quarterly B). FilterTime | N/A |
| FilterTime | FilterTime is a filter delivery platform that Latchel works with to handle shipments for customers that are part of the Air Filters marketplace benefit. | N/A |
| Fixed Monthly Payment Customer | Customers on a fixed monthly payment schedule, are customers who like the benefits of an Assisted Ledgering Contract, but are not willing to give access to their PMS, and therefore cannot be tracked by the CPT. These customers will be charged a fixed amount every month which will be determined and noted on the customer’s contract for each month. | N/A |
| Flavor | This is the category which an issue belongs to. This is included in the “Findings Tracker”, and it is used to specify the type of issue being reported. | N/A |
| General Ledger Code | General Ledger code that identifies there is a Latchel charge. Each PM has their own GL code. | GL Code |
| Homeowners Association | Organization conformed by owners or residents from a residential community, who oversee the properties management and enforce specific rules and regulations. | HOA |

## L-O

| **Name** | **Definition** | **Abbreviation** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Latchel Addendum | Wording or verbiage in a lease that informs the tenant they will be added a fee for Latchel services. It is not always included in the lease itself, as it could be added as a separate file, or sent as email or letter. This doesn't always need to include the word Latchel. Example key words: - Latchel - Benefit package - Home assistant - Service - Maintenance - Resident benefit package | N/A |
| Lease scraper | Tool employed to subtract specific information from the lease files. | N/A |
| Ledgerable | Term designated to identify a property that meets all the requirements to have the Latchel charge added. | N/A |
| Marketplace Benefits | Benefits offered by Latchel to customers as optional enhancements that can be added to their contract. Currently offering Air Filters, Piñata, Obie, and Cover Pest. | N/A |
| Month to Month | Lease with no definite expiration date, and renewed on a monthly basis. | N/A |
| New Lease | When the PM adds the Latchel service and a new resident rents a vacant property. This new resident will have the Latchel service and they will be explained about the Latchel recurrent charge (GL code). | N/A |
| Onboard End Date | The day the PM ends the Onboarding process with the Launch Manager (Zach). | OED |
| Opportunity Owner | The Sales agent that managed and integrated the PM through the sales process up until the onboarding process. | Opp Owner |
| Opt-in report | This is the report that we create by collecting the information of residents that are being charged an insurance fee, and that were added within the previous months. This report will be sent to the insurance company at the beginning of each month. | N/A |

## P-S

| **Name** | **Definition** | **Abbreviation** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Piñata | Piñata is a resident rewards program that is available as an optional addition to the Property Manager's subscription with Latchel. | N/A |
| Point of contact | Person at the Property Management company that Latchel can reach out to for inquiries, or to provide updates for any of their services contracted with Latchel. | POC |
| Property Manager | Our client. They manage the properties for the Owners. | PM |
| Property Manager Software | Software that allows** **landlords and real estate professionals to track leases, residents, and maintenance tasks as well as collect rent and manage finances to reduce costs and streamline operations. Each Property Manager has their own Property Manager Software (PMS). We use their Property Manager Software (PMS) to be able to get the reports we need to track and verify GL codes in the properties. We currently work with the following Property Manager Software: - Appfolio - Buildium - Rent Manager - Rent Direct - Yardi - Propertyware - Rent Vine | PMS |
| Provisioned invoice | Amount that will be charged to the PM company every month for the Latchel service. | N/A |
| Renewal Lease | When the PM adds the Latchel service while maintaining prior leases. These get updated when residents renew their lease and it will now include the latchel charge (GL code). | N/A |
| Rillet | Platform used for keeping track of the customers’ financial records, just as invoices status, payment history, points of contact, and revenue. | N/A |
| Road to profitability | Percentage that shows how close is the PM company to reach profitability. Ideally they should reach at least 100% by the end of their 1 year contract. | RTP |
| Rolled Over | A missed opportunity that was included in the monthly reporting of a previous month that “rolled over” to the current monthly reporting. Ex: Missed opportunity reported on December is still a missed opportunity in the report for January. | N/A |
| Section 8 | It refers to the residents that are part of an assistance program, which subsidizes partially or fully the resident’s rent charge. | N/A |
| Service start date | This is the date when the PM company ends with the onboarding process, and officially starts with the Latchel service. | N/A |
| Signed before OBD | When residents sign the lease before their PM company starts with the Latchel service. | N/A |
| Students housing | Student housing is a form of accommodation that university or college students can choose as an alternative to on-campus housing. For this type of contract all of the students part of the lease are identified as financially responsible. | N/A |
