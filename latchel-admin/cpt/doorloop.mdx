## **Logging into DoorLoop**

### **Step 1 - Locate the correct PM and their specific login credentials**

a. Navigate to the **"Overall Details"** tab in the CPT Tracker.

b. Find the row for the PM you will be tracking and confirm their PMS is "DoorLoop."

c. Locate the following columns for that PM:

`- PMS URL`

`- Username`

`- Password`

d. Keep this information ready for the next step.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-327.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>

</Frame>

### **Step 2 - Execute the login and complete the two-factor authentication (2FA) process**

a. Click the `PMS URL` from the tracker to open the DoorLoop login page.

b. Enter the `Username` and `Password`.

c.\*\* Note:\*\* The session often stays logged in after the first time.

d. If prompted for a verification code (or if it's your first time logging in), you must access the \*\*CPT email account at \*\*`ledgering@latchel.com` to retrieve the code.

e. Enter the verification code to complete the login.
