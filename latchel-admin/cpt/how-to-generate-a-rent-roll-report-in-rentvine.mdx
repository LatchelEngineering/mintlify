---
title: "How to: Generate a Rent Roll Report in RentVine"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this article is to be a guide for generating a Rent Roll report in RentVine for the weekly reporting process.
</Note>

## Step by step

### **Step 1 - Locate and access the Rent Roll report**

a. Log in to the PM's RentVine account.

b. On the left side of the screen, click on **Reports**.

c. Navigate to the **Lease Reports** section and find the **Rent Roll** report.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-319.png" alt="26.png" />
</Frame>

<Check>
To mark the Rent Roll report as “Favorite”, locate the report in the <u>Lease Reports Section</u>, click the ellipses on the right connected to the report and select **Favorite**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-320.png" alt="27.png" />
</Frame>

</Check>

### Step 2: Customize the Columns

a. Click on **"Columns"** to customize the report view.

<Warning>
**Important:** _Ensure the Date is set to the last date of the month._
</Warning>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-321.png" alt="image-20250805-165158.png" />
</Frame>

b. Select the following columns:

- Unit Address
- Unit Address 2
- Unit City
- Unit State
- Unit Postal Code
- Primary Tenant Name
- Lease Start Date
- Lease End Date
- Monthly Rent Amount

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-322.png" alt="image-20250805-165926.png" />
</Frame>

### **Step 3 - Export the configured report as a CSV file**

a. After the report preview loads, click the **Export Icon**.

b. In the "Export Report" window, select **CSV** from the "Export Type" dropdown menu.

c. Click **Export**. The file will be downloaded to your computer.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-323.png" alt="31.png" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
The Rent Roll report in RentVine does not provide the option to include GL Accounts in its data points. For this, you will need to generate the **Lease Recurring Charges **report to update the properties that were added/deleted the Latchel charge. 
See how to generate the Lease Recurring Charges report in this job aid: [How to: Generate the Lease Recurring Charges Report in RentVine](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3214016615/How+to+Generate+the+Lease+Recurring+Charges+Report+in+RentVine)
</Warning>
