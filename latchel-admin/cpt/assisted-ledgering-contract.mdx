# What is it?

Ledgering is the process of keeping a record of transactions. At Latchel, it’s a system used to enforce contracts and help customers stay profitable by reducing the risk of lost revenue. It provides a clear, consistent way to track transactions, especially for resident charges.

By ensuring GL codes are added for ledgerable properties, this process helps customers increase revenue, save time, and strengthen their partnership with Latchel.
