title: "How to: Provide Billing Inputs for Customers on Fixed Monthly Payment Schedule"
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this document is to be a guide on how to report the monthly billing inputs for Customers on a fixed payment schedule.
</Note>

Customers on a fixed monthly payment schedule are customers who like the benefits of an Assisted Ledgering Contract, but are not willing to give access to their PMS, and therefore cannot be tracked by the CPT. These customers will be charged a fixed amount every month.

## Step by step

### Step 1: Open the Billing inputs Master Doc

<Check>
  This process will be performed once a month. Within the first 8th business days of the month, the CPT will report the active units amount on the [Billing Inputs Master Doc](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1HG_bRF-JM8DqSz1lusDrfJVGIpohvJwBP4fNAipclvM/edit?gid=1936941891#gid=1936941891), for each of the customers on a fixed monthly payment schedule.
</Check>

a. Go to the [Billing Inputs Master Doc](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1HG_bRF-JM8DqSz1lusDrfJVGIpohvJwBP4fNAipclvM/edit?gid=1936941891#gid=1936941891), and look for the customer you will be reporting. (Customers on a fixed monthly payment schedule are marked in **column F**).

<Frame>
  ![5.png](/images/CPT-568.png)
</Frame>

b. Click on the customer’s name, and the link to their Hubspot Opportunities page will show; click on it, and you will be directed to that page.

<Frame>
  ![6.png](/images/CPT-569.png)
</Frame>

c. Once on their Hubspot page, go to the **Resources tab**, and in the **Notes & Attachments section**, look for their <u>addendum file.</u>

d. Click on the addendum file, and once opened, scroll down and look for a table titled “Schedule of Units Billed”. This will be the source for the CPT to report the active units amount of the month.

<Frame>
  ![8.png](/images/CPT-570.png)
</Frame>

### Step 2: Input the totals in the corresponding column

a. Get the amount for the specific month you are reporting, and record this in Column AD “Latchel Basic Usage”, on the [Billing Inputs Master Doc](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1HG_bRF-JM8DqSz1lusDrfJVGIpohvJwBP4fNAipclvM/edit?gid=1936941891#gid=1936941891). Once you fill them in and they are confirmed, turn them to a light green highlight.

<Check>
  **<u>Example:</u>** If you are working on this on the first business days of October 2025, then you will take the amount from the **Schedule of Units Billed table** that says <u>October 2025. </u>
</Check>
