title: "How to: Generate a General Ledger Report in Rent Manager"
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this document is to be a guide for generating a General Ledger report in Rent Manager.
</Note>

<Check>
  The General Ledger report allows you to examine transactions that impacted specific general ledger (GL) accounts over a given date range. This gives you the ability to see how GL accounts reached their balances.
</Check>

## Step by Step

### **Step 1 - Navigate to the General Ledger report interface**

a. Log in to the PM's Rent Manager account.

b. Navigate to **Reports \> General Ledger \> General Ledger**.

c. Make sure these 4 boxes are <u>not</u> checked.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-281.png)
</Frame>

### Step 2: Customize the report

a. Secondly, we will confirm that in the Properties to Include section, the option showing is **All selected**, and in the _Cash or Accrual_ section, the option selected is **Cash**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-282.png)
</Frame>

b. Usually by default these options are already selected as we need them, but we want to make sure they are as follows:

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-283.png)
</Frame>

c. Next we will select the date range of the information the report will display, which for Latchel purposes will be from the 1st of the month, to the last day of the current month.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-284.png)
</Frame>

d. Then we will make sure that only the Latchel’s GL Account is marked in the “Chart Accounts to include” section.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-285.png)
</Frame>

### Step 3: Proceed to download the report.

a. Download the report as a PDF file, and will click on “Generate Report” at the bottom right corner.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-286.png)
</Frame>

This report is usually sent by the Property Manager to our Finance department, however it is important for us to know how to generate it in the event the Property Manager doesn’t know how to do it, or if there is a month when they don’t send it to our Finance Department. This for us to be able to update the database with the most accurate information.
