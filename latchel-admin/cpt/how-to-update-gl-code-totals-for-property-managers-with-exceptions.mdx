---
title: "How to Update GL Code Totals for Property Managers with Exceptions"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to report GL code totals on a monthly basis, ensuring accurate data and avoiding incorrect reporting to Finance when applicable.
</Note>

<Accordion title="Legacy Oak">
<Check>
Since Legacy Oak has a Filters tab in the Benefits CPT Tracker, we only need to look at the monthly total reported for the corresponding month. This will be the same total to report in the Billing Inputs document.
</Check>

<Warning>
Make sure the last monthly review is completed before you proceed to check the total monthly GL codes.
</Warning>

### Step 1:  Open the **codes** tab in the [Benefits CPT Tracker](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qd4oXlu6dBYwvPCVnl8fMwoulezF2MTZQoGOhqPqdo0/edit?pli=1&gid=1908721725#gid=1908721725) and apply the filters to count the Monthly totals.

a. In the Legacy Oak Realty **GL Codes tab**, **go to column D **(Residents Benefit Package), **click the three lines in the corner,** **select the GL code**, and **click OK**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-685.jpg" alt="Picture 13.jpg" />
</Frame>

b. **Count the total GL codes displayed in column D**. You can confirm the total in the bottom-right corner.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-686.jpg" alt="picture 14.jpg" />
</Frame>

c. **Remove the filter**

d. **Click the three lines in column AA **and **type the month you are reporting** into the search bar. **Select only those notes **and **click OK**.

<Check>
**Example: **_It’s August 1st and you’re reporting for July. Make sure to look for any note indicating that a resident <u>had a GL code in July</u>._
</Check>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-687.jpg" alt="picture 15.jpg" />
</Frame>

e. **Select the entire Notes column** and **confirm the total displayed **in the bottom-right corner.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-688.jpg" alt="Picture 16.jpg" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Report the totals in the [Billing Inputs Doc](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1HG_bRF-JM8DqSz1lusDrfJVGIpohvJwBP4fNAipclvM/edit?gid=494873615#gid=494873615).

a. Go to the corresponding monthly tab in the Billing Inputs document to **report the totals in column AB “24/7 Emergency, Home Assistant**”. **Example:** _If it’s October 1st, you’ll be reporting the totals for September. Look for the October tab in the Billing Inputs Document._

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-689.png" alt="image-20250918-221038.png" />
</Frame>

</Accordion>
