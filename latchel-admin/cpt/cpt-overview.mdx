  # **What is our Mission?**

  We are a team that Empowers Property Managers to attain profitability by **providing transparent insights** into their progress, **highlighting missed opportunities and best practices**, and equipping them with **essential tools** to accomplish their profitability objectives.
</Callout>

## **Documentation overview**

In this space, you will find all the tools and processes related to the products we work with and track:

- Assisted Ledgering Program
- GetCovered insurance.

## **📕 User guide**

This space is divided into products and then into categories, starting with everything related to creating reports, identifying and use of tools, and inner processes to deliver the Assisted Ledgering Tracker. It will also include additional resources about interaction with other departments and follow-up processes to ensure the optimal implementation of the Assisted Ledgering Program.
