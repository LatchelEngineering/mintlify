## **Logging into RentVine**

### **Step 1 - Locate the correct PM and their specific login credentials**

a. Navigate to the appropriate tracker (**CPT Tracker** or **Benefits Tracker**).

b. Open the **"Overall Details"** tab.

c. Find the row for the PM you will be tracking and confirm their PMS is listed as "RentVine."

d. Locate the following three columns for that PM:

`- PMS URL` (This is the PM's unique RentVine link)

`- Username`

`- Password`

e. Keep this information ready for the next step.

### **Step 2 - Login to the RentVine platform**

a. Click the `PMS URL` from the tracker to open the correct login page.

b. Enter the `Username` and `Password` into the corresponding fields.

c. Click the **"Sign In"** button to access the software.
