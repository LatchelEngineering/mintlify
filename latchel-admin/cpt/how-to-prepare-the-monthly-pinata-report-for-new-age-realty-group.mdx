title: "How to Prepare the Monthly Piñata Report for New Age Realty Group"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to prepare the Monthly Piñata Report for this specific Property Manager. It also outlines the steps for updating the report and sending it to the relevant stakeholders.
</Note>

## Step by step

<Check>
For this Property Manager, a monthly review is not required. Instead, you can download the most recent Rent Roll Itemized Report dated the last day of the month to submit the totals to Finance. To obtain the report, follow the steps below to download the Monthly Piñata Report.
</Check>

## **Step 1: Download the Piñata Monthly report.**

a. In the **PMS**, go to **Reports > Report Builder (Tenant Directory)**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-701.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-702.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

b. Select the following columns:

- _Emails_
- _Phone Numbers_
- _First Name_
- _Last Name_
- _Property Street Address 1_
- _Unit_
- _Property City_
- _Property State_
- _Property Zip_
- _Lease From_
- _Lease To_
- _Rent_
- _Tenant Birthdate_
- _Past Due (from Rent Roll Itemized report)._

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-703.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

c.** Tenants: “Show Active Tenants”**

d. **Tenant Status:** Select the statuses **Current, Notice, and Evict**.

e. **Tenant Type:** Set the filter to **Financially Responsible**.

f.** Properties:** Leave the property filter as is (no changes required).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-704.png" alt="image-20250807-163334.png" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
In the Filter Columns From Other Reports section is** not necessary to select the GL code. **For this PM, you will report **all** properties, whether they have a GL code or not. That’s why you don’t need to select it.
</Warning>

g. Ensure Date = last day of the month. **Example: **_You are on August 1st, so the report to download should contain July’s information. Set the date to 07/31/2025._

h. Click **UPDATE**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-705.png" alt="image-20250807-163536.png" />
</Frame>

i. **Click on Actions> Export as CSV**.

### **Step 2: Process Piñata report Data**

a. Open a **new spreadsheet**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-706.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

b. **Import and upload** the CSV file > replace data in the selected cell > Import Data.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-707.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

c. Arrange **columns in the correct order**.

- _Email_
- _Phone number_
- _First name_
- _Last name_
- _Shipping address_
- _Shipping address 2_
- _City_
- _State_
- _Zip_
- _Lease from_
- _Lease to_
- _Rent amount_
- _**New column for the Rent Due Day of Month**_
- _Past Due Balance_
- _DOB_

d. Add a **column (L onwards)** for **Rent Due Day of the Month** (manual entry based on previous month).

e. **Reapply filters** (they reset after rearranging columns).

f. Sort **Column C (Unit)** **A to Z**.

g. Make sure to **delete all commercial units**. To do this, press **CTRL + F to search for commercial properties **and then delete the entire row.

h.** Select CTRL + H** to search for the Mobile, Home, and Phone options to remove in the Phone number column, and delete them.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-708.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-709.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-710.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

i. All amounts in the Past Due column displaying this icon (–) are $0.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-711.png" alt="image-20250731-173000.png" />
</Frame>

j. Replace any blank cells caused by missing information with 'NA'.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-712.png" alt="image-20250731-173446.png" />
</Frame>

k. Copy filtered data into the **Benefits CPT Tracker PM Piñata**.

l. Add the Rent Due Day Of Month for the column you created. **Example:** _If you’re doing the report on July 02nd, it will contain the information of June, so the date in the Rent due day of the month column should be 06/01/2025._

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-713.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

_m.** Open the Piñata Report Tab:** Go to the **Piñata Report** tab in the Benefits Tracker._

_n.** Copy Data from PMS Report:** From the updated PMS report, copy the required columns._

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-714.png" alt="image-20251001-213929.png" />
</Frame>

o. Before copying the information into the Piñata report, click the three-line menu in the (Column C) **First Name **column and select Sort **A to Z** to arrange the names alphabetically.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-715.png" alt="image-20251001-214734.png" />
</Frame>

### **Step 3: Open the Monthly Report**

a. Open the Monthly report from the last email sent.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-716.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

b. **Right-click on the tab, select 'Duplicate,' and rename the duplicated tab** with the corresponding date and month of your review, following the required format.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-717.png" alt="image-20250807-164842.png" />
</Frame>

c. From Benefits CPT Tracker (Piñata tab for the PM),** copy all the data into the Piñata Monthly Report.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-718.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

### **Step 4: Send the Report.**

a. Draft email using the previous template

- Recipients:  Check the “To” and “CC” refer to the photos below. If this is the first time you are sending the report, you should request the CSM for the POC information to send the report.
- Subject: **PM Company Name + Piñata Rent Roll Update**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-719.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

b. Click the “drive” Icon

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-720.png" alt="image-20250730-195929.png" />
</Frame>

c. Search for the Piñata report by typing the name of the Property Manager (PM) and the report title.
**Example: **New Age Realty Piñata Rent Roll Report.

<Warning>
Attach the report and first verify that it is the correct file by clicking on it before sending. If the file is incorrect, make sure to unselect it and attach the correct one.
</Warning>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-721.png" alt="image-20250730-193109.png" />
</Frame>

d. Click on **send**.
