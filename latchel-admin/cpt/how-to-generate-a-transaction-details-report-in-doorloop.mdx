---
title: "How to: Generate a Transaction Details Report in DoorLoop"
---

The purpose of this document is to be a guide for generating a Transaction Details Report in DoorLoop.

<Check>
  The Transaction Details Report allows you to examine transactions that impacted a specific general ledger (GL) account over a given date range. This gives you the ability to see how the GL account reached their balances.
</Check>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Login into DoorLoop

a. Login the DoorLoop account of the property manager you’ll be checking

b. Identify the Accounting **section on the left side menu. Once in that section, select the** Charts of Accounts option.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-338.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![9393.png](/images/CPT-339.png)
</Frame>

c. Look for the GL Account of which you need the report. Click on the 3 dots **at the end, and select** Run Report.

<Frame>
  ![000000000.png](/images/CPT-340.png)
</Frame>

### Step 2: Customize the report

a. The customizing chart will open up. You will first customize the date range, which for our purposes will be from the 1st of the previous, to the last day of the current month.

<Frame>
  ![0000000000.png](/images/CPT-341.png)
</Frame>

<Check>
  In the **Accounting Method** dropdown you will select Accrual when you want all the charges displayed despite if they are paid or not, and you will select Cash when you want only the paid charges to be displayed.
</Check>

b. Lastly, when you have customized the report as needed, you will be able to download it in Excel format. When clicking the button shown below, the file will be saved in .xlsx format to your default download location.

<Frame>
  ![939393.png](/images/CPT-342.png)
</Frame>
