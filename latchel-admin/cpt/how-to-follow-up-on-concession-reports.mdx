title: "How to: Follow up on Concession Reports"
---

<Note>
  The aim of this article is to outline the steps involved in following up with Property Managers when the monthly concession report has not been sent to the accounting department **from non-profitable PMs**, and the CPT does not have access to download it from their PMS.
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1: First Contact

a. On the first day of each month (or the next business day), the CPT will review the [accounting@latchel.com](mailto:accounting@latchel.com) inbox to locate the GL reports for every Assisted Ledgering Property Manager. These reports should pertain to the previous month, including transactions from the first to the last day of the month. **Example:** <u>April 1st the CPT will collect the reports from March, which should cover from March 1st to March 31st. </u>

b. The reports retrieved from the email will be uploaded in the [Concession Reports folder](https://drive.google.com/drive/u/2/folders/17-agFh9n0pDWY8wZiPrJq-t3NfXxXzr8). For the missing reports, the CPT will download them from the Property Managers' PMS (for those who have granted access).

c. If there are Property Managers that have not sent the report, and there is no access to their PMS for downloading them, the CPT will reach out to those Property Managers' points of contact to send a reminder regarding the missing report, and CC the CSM assigned to each account. (This reminder will be sent only to non-profitable property managers, unless there is a request from the CS team to include certain profitable Property Managers).

First contact template (Sent from [**acccounting@latchel.com):**](mailto:accounting@latchel.com) **(Subject: “(Month) GL report”)**

> _Hi \[customer name\],_
>
> _We hope this email finds you well! We’re reaching out because we haven't yet received your General Ledger report for last month and we want to ensure accurate billing on your upcoming invoice. Can you shoot that report over to us by the 5th?_
>
> _We are looking for a Rent Roll (Itemized) Report or a General Ledger filtered for the month of \[month\] and your \[name and # of GL code\] GL account to be sent to [accounting@latchel.com](mailto:accounting@latchel.com)._
>
> _Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns on getting that report over to us by the 5th._
>
> _Thank you! The Latchel Team_

### Step 2: Second Contact

a. The 5th of the month (or next business day), the CPT will review the [accounting@latchel.com](mailto:accounting@latchel.com) inbox, to confirm if the missing reports were forwarded by the Property Managers. The ones received will be labeled in the correct month’s folder for the script to upload them in the correct drive folder for Finance review.

<Check>
  To learn more, see the job aid: [How to Organize Monthly GL Reports](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3574890529/How+to+Organize+Monthly+GL+Reports)
</Check>

b. For the Property Managers that still have not sent the report, the CPT will send a second reminder replying “to all”, in the same first contact email thread.

**Second contact template:** 

> _Hi \[customer name\], This is a friendly reminder that we’re still looking for your GL report from last month. We bill on the 8th business day of the month based on the information we have on record, so if we do not receive your GL report by \[calendar date - day before the 8th business day of the month\] at the very latest, you will receive a full-priced invoice._
>
> _Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns._
>
> _Best, The Latchel Team_

### Step 3: Third Contact

a. The 8th of the month (or next business day), the CPT will do a final review of the [accounting@latchel.com](mailto:accounting@latchel.com) inbox.

b. For the Property Managers that still have not sent the report, the CPT will notify Donita and the CSM in the thread of post made in the [#ledgering-mkt-opt-benefits](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C03D4N7B0LF) channel.
