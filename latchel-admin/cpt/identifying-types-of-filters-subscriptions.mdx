---
title: "Identifying types of Filters subscriptions"
---

<Note>
  The aim of this article is to outline the subscription options available to Property Managers when integrating the Filters benefit into their contract. These options will dictate the frequency of shipments and the amount of filters included in each shipment.
</Note>

|  | ## Types of Subscriptions |
| --- | --- |
| **Annual:** The Property Manager accepts to have only 1 shipment per year with 1 single set of filters for each size needed. | **Semiannual:** The Property Manager accepts to have 2 shipments per year (every 6 months), with 1 single set of filters for each size needed. |
| **Quarterly A:** With this subscription the Property Manager accepts to have 2 shipments per year (every 6 months), considering that for the properties that need to change their filters every 3 months, <u>they will receive 2 sets of filters for each size needed on every shipment. </u> | **Quarterly B:** With this subscription the Property Manager accepts to have 4 shipments per year (every 3 months) with 1 single set of filters for each size needed. |

When working on the shipments for a Property Manager, make sure to confirm what type of subscription they signed for, as this will determine the frequency to ship, and the amount of filters included in each shipment. This information can be found in the tab [Filters Overview](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qd4oXlu6dBYwvPCVnl8fMwoulezF2MTZQoGOhqPqdo0/edit#gid=0) in the Benefits CPT Tracker.

<Warning>
  Keep in mind that the Sales team may occasionally sell a customized package to a Property Manager, that deviates from the 4 options above. This will also be indicated in the Filters Overview in the Benefits CPT Tracker, for which the CPT will reference to the Third Party Addendum attached to the Sales Force Opportunity, to include any remarks needed in the subscription’s details.
</Warning>
