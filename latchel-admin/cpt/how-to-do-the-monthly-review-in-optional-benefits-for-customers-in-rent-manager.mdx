---
title: "How to do the Monthly Review in Optional Benefits for Customers in Rent Manager"
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to perform the monthly review of Optional Benefits for clients whose PMS is Rent Manager.
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Open the Optional Benefits tab

a. Go to the Optional Benefits Overview tab to check the property manager’s login information.<u> You can find these details in columns J, K, and L.</u>

<Frame>
  ![image-20250808-164808.png](/images/CPT-769.png)
</Frame>

b. Additionally, open the PM’s tab — where you will do the review — in another window.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250808-165224.png](/images/CPT-770.png)
</Frame>

### Step 2: Go to the PMS to begin the review

a. Log in to the PMS and go to the main menu at the top. **Click the three lines to display the available options**, then **select Rental Info** _\>_ **General / Residents**.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250808-165837.png](/images/CPT-771.png)
</Frame>

b. Once in the Residents tab, scroll down to see the new residents added. You will notice that the new residents appear at the end of the list, based on their lease start date.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250808-170039.png](/images/CPT-772.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
  Only look at the residents whose status is “Current” in the Status column. Those marked as “Past” are no longer part of the list.
</Warning>

### Step 3: Update the Optional Benefits tab for the corresponding property manager.

a. Go back to the Property Manager’s tab and add the new residents manually. To do this, click the resident’s name in the PMS to view their information.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250808-170535.png](/images/CPT-773.png)
</Frame>

b. Copy-paste all the resident’s required information displayed from columns A-J and AI to AR.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250808-170743.png](/images/CPT-774.png)
</Frame>

c. Once done, update columns K, Q, and R–AG as normally done for Optional Benefits.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250808-171011.png](/images/CPT-775.png)
</Frame>

d. If the resident is an opt-in, this information will be shown in the UDFs section in the PMS.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250808-171204.png](/images/CPT-776.png)
</Frame>

**Once the review is complete**, you can proceed to prepare the report to send <u>all residents with a GL code</u> (as of the last of the previous month) to the Data team by following this job aid.  [How to: Request activation/deactivation of properties in Latchel console for OB Customers](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3119808527/How+to+Request+activation+deactivation+of+properties+in+Latchel+console+for+Optional+Benefits+Only+and+Maintenance+Optional+Benefits+Customers) . If no residents are reported, you can disregard following the job aid.
