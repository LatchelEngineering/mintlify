<Note>
  The purpose of this document is to be a guide for generating a Rent Roll report in Buildium for the Tracker process.
</Note>

## Step-by-step

### Step 1: Login into DoorLoop

a. Log into DoorLoop for the Property Manager you will be working on.

b.  Your first view of the PMS will be the main page. Once there, your first step for this will be to **select Reports** from the left side menu. Under the **Business Overview** section, click **Rent Roll**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-336.png)
</Frame>

By default, the rent roll will show a wide variety of information, the units on those properties, whether or not they are vacant or occupied, how much rent is on each unit, if there's a deposit being held, and if there's a balance due. To see more information, you can click the **3-dots** on the top title row and enable extra columns. You can also use the filter bar at the top to sort the report in different ways or narrow down the results which is where you will find **Latchel’s GL account** to be added to the report, and remove the columns that are not needed.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/CPT-337.png)
</Frame>

c. Regarding the date of the report, you will need to select the date when you are generating the report.
