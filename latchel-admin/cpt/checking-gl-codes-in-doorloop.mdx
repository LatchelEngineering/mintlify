<Note>
The purpose of this document is to provide a guide on how to navigate the DoorLoop tool, to be able to track the GL codes for each new added property into the CPT tracker.
</Note>

## Step-by-step

### Step 1: Login to DoorLoop

a. Login to the DoorLoop account of the Property Manager you’ll be checking

b. First you will need to find the tenant you need to check. To find a tenant, click **People** on the main menu and then click **Tenants**. Use the Search bar to type in the name of the tenant you are trying to find.

### Step 2: Go to the Transactions section

a. Once you have located the tenant you are looking for (you should always confirm with the property address), click on the tenant’s name, and next you will be directed to their portal. When the tenant's portal is displayed, go to the **Transactions **section.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-365.png" alt="000.png" />
</Frame>

b. Once in that section you will be able to see the list of transactions.

- First you will have the **Type **of transaction (Charge, credit, or payment).
- Then you will see the **Date **of the transaction. (With this you can confirm if Latchel’s GL code was paid on time).
- After that you will have the **Category, **where you can confirm which charges and payments are for Latchel’s GL code.
- Next, you will see the **Total **for the transaction.
- And lastly, you have the **Running Balance. **

<Warning>
You may look for the first time the Latchel’s charge shows on this list, if you need to confirm the date when the charge was added for the first time.
</Warning>
