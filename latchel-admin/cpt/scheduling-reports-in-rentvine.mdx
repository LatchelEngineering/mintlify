title: "Scheduling Reports in Rentvine"

<Note>
This article will provide the step by step on how to automate reports in Rentvine during the onboarding process.
</Note>

<Check>
In Rentvine to create scheduled reports, <u>you will first need to have at least one saved report.</u> Follow the steps below to create a report, save a report, and then schedule a report.
</Check>

## Step by step

### **Step 1 - Configure a new report with the required data and filters**

a. Log in to the PM's Rentvine account.

b. On the left side of the screen, click on **Reports**.

c. Under one of the sections (e.g., Accounting Reports, Lease Reports), select the base report you need to schedule.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-311.png" alt="11.png" />
</Frame>

d. Carefully configure the report:

1. **Filters:** Set the appropriate filters for the report.
2. **Columns:** Drag and drop the specific columns you want to appear in the final report.
3. **Grouping:** (If applicable) Configure how the data should be grouped (e.g., group properties by portfolio).

e. Click **Run Report** to generate a preview.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-312.png" alt="20203.png" />
</Frame>

### **Step 2 - Save the configured report as a reusable template**

a. Once the report preview looks correct, click the **Actions** button on the right side.

b. Select **Save** from the dropdown menu.

c. You will now see this report listed in your **"Saved Reports"** section, ready to be used for automation.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-313.png" alt="13.png" />
</Frame>

## Schedule a report

### **Step 1 - Create a new scheduled report job**

a. In the Reports section, click on the **Scheduled Reports** tab.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-314.png" alt="14.png" />
</Frame>

b. On the right, click the green **New Scheduled Report** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-315.png" alt="12.png" />
</Frame>

### **Step 2 - Configure the report delivery and scheduling options**

a. In the "Add Scheduled Report" pop-up, fill out the form with the following details:

- **Scheduled Report Name:** Give the job a clear, descriptive name.
- **To:** Enter the email addresses of the primary recipients. Press the **Tab** key after each email to add multiple.
- **BCC:** Enter any email addresses for blind carbon copy.
- **Subject:** Enter a clear subject line for the email (e.g., "Monthly GL Report - [PM Name]").
- **Message:** Enter a brief message for the body of the email.
- **Start Date:** Select the date the _first_ report should be sent.
- **Repeat Every / Schedule Frequency:** Set the recurrence (e.g., Repeat Every `1` and Schedule Frequency `Month`).

b. Click the green **Save** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-316.png" alt="16.png" />
</Frame>

c. Your scheduled report will now appear in the Scheduled Reports tab of the Reports section of your Rentvine account. If you ever want to delete this scheduled report or any other scheduled report, you can click the ellipses on the far right side for the report and click delete.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-317.png" alt="17.png" />
</Frame>

d. In here you can click edit report to edit any of the report details like the frequency or who this report gets sent out to.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-318.png" alt="18.png" />
</Frame>
