<Note>
  The purpose of this article is to guide how to respond to residents with Piñata benefits who reach out to request assistance with their Piñata account. Providing clear and concise information is important to ensure a smooth process. In this article, you will learn how to handle the different scenarios to assist residents effectively.
</Note>

## **Overview**

When a PM signs up for Piñata through Latchel, a tile is activated in the Resident Benefits Hub for all residents, including those not yet enrolled.\*\* To enhance the customer experience\*\* <u>and prevent residents from mistakenly signing up on their own</u>, the link has been replaced with a **[Typeform](https://latchel.typeform.com/to/uHMYTNkh)** for inquiries managed by the CP Team.

<Frame>
  ![image-20241018-171639.png](/images/CPT-472.png)
</Frame>

The resident will fill out the Typeform to request assistance from the CP Team, which you will receive through the [#cpt-updates](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C04TZ3Y8ZLK) channel.

The answers received will be also saved for internal use in the [Piñata Resident Inquiry Form Responses](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qNAAcxh4Ho64aWQJKRTsJpriJvpTu48ZPyuM6Te22wE/edit?gid=657199615#gid=657199615) file.

The resident will fill the Typeform with the following information: 

1. First and last name 
2. Email 
3. Property address 
4. Property management company name 
5. Phone number 
6. The issue they require assistance with.

**_Example_:**  I haven't received my invite to join the Piñata app.

<u>Main communication will be delivered to the residents **via email**</u>; however, they need to include their phone number in case the CP Team is unable to reach them if the email does not go through for some reason.

<Check>
  CP Team will review resident inquiries on a case-by-case basis and <u>will respond via email **within 2 business days**, based on the resident’s specific situation</u>.
</Check>

Please refer to the next section to see the common scenarios and how to reply to each resident.<u> For the email subject for all scenarios, you should type the name of the PM company followed by the benefit they are enrolled in</u>. **Example**: _New Age Realty - Piñata_

## **Common Scenarios**

<Accordion title="Resident says he hasn't received the invite to join Piñata">
  <Check>
    This scenario applies when the resident’s information has already been submitted to Piñata via the monthly report. If this is the case, please respond to the resident using the following script:
  </Check>

  _Hi \[resident name\],_

  _Thanks for reaching out! We submitted your information to Piñata for account activation in \[Month Year\] with the email address \[resident’s email address\]. You should have received an email invite to join the app at that time. If you can’t find the email in your inbox, you can also visit [https://www.pinata.ai/welcome-renters/Latchel/](https://www.pinata.ai/welcome-renters/Latchel/) to activate your account._

  _Rest assured – your rent rewards and positive credit reporting have been accumulating since you were enrolled. You can reach out to [support@pinata.ai](mailto:support@pinata.ai) with any additional questions or concerns._

  _Kind regards,_

  _The Latchel Team_
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Resident’s GL code was added in the current month">
  <Check>
    Since the GL code was just added, this means their information won’t be reported to Piñata until next month. For this scenario, please use the following script:
  </Check>

  _Hi \[resident name\],_

  \_Thanks for reaching out! It looks like your Property Manager recently added you to the Piñata program. You can expect to receive an invite to join the app via email at \[resident’s email address\] by the 15th of next month. \_

  _Rest assured – your rent rewards and positive credit reporting have been accumulating since you were enrolled. Thanks for your patience!_

  _Kind regards,_

  _The Latchel Team_
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Resident doesn’t yet have a GL code added, or the GL code was removed">
  <Check>
    Since every PM company is different, we don’t always have an answer on why a GL code was removed or not added yet. For this, it will be best to direct the resident to the PM to confirm that they can gain access to Piñata. For this scenario please use the following script:
  </Check>

  _Hi \[resident name\],_

  _Thanks for reaching out! While your Property Manager does indeed participate in Piñata through Latchel, it looks like you’re not currently enrolled. Please kindly reach out to your Property Manager for more information on how you can be added to the program._

  _Kind regards,_

  _The Latchel Team_

  <Warning>
    If the resident replies requesting the PM’s contact information, you can advise them to look for it in the lease. <u>The PM's POC should never be shared with the resident, as this information is for internal use only.</u>
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Resident doesn’t want to participate in credit reporting">
  <Check>
    Since the resident has been clear about their decision not to be part of the credit reporting, you will need to update the monthly report for the next month. For this scenario, please use the script below to respond to the resident and follow the next steps:
  </Check>

  1. Send the following script to the resident: _Hi \[resident name\],_ _Thanks for reaching out! We understand you’d like to opt out of Piñata credit reporting. This change will be communicated to Piñata and effective next month. You will continue to receive rent rewards._ _Kind regards,_ _The Latchel Team_
  2. Once you prepare the PM’s next monthly report, make sure to include checkboxes for each service and **opt the resident out of credit reporting**. See [All American Latchel Pinata Rent Rolls](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/13ejVZoWdCoMbxg8J0NN5xzh_fKyA4eNRMeal9pE6BpI/edit?gid=1759568414#gid=1759568414)for reference.

  <Frame>
    ![image-20241018-195407.png](/images/CPT-474.png)
  </Frame>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Submission from a resident whose PM is not enrolled in Piñata through Latchel">
  <Check>
    For this specific scenario, since the resident claims to belong to a PM company our team does not handle, you will need to request the assistance of the CSM Team.  For this scenario, please use the script below to respond to the resident and follow the next steps:
  </Check>

  1. Send the following email to the resident: _Hi \[resident name\],_ _Thanks for reaching out! Your Property Manager does not currently participate in Piñata through Latchel. Please kindly reach out to your Property Manager for more information on how you can be added to the program._ _Kind regards,_ _The Latchel Team_
  2. Submit a Request for **CS Help workflow** in the [#ledgering-mkt-opt-benefits](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C03D4N7B0LF) channel to alert the CS team, asking them to review the PM’s benefits hub tiles. We may have added the tile by mistake in onboarding, or the PM may be working directly with Piñata.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="No Access to the Property Manager's PMS">
  Hi \[Resident's Name\],

  Thanks for reaching out. We received your Piñata request, and while your Property Manager does participate in Piñata through Latchel, it looks like we don’t have your account details on file, so we’re unable to assist directly. Please contact your Property Manager, as they can review your information and help you with Piñata.

  Kind regards, The Latchel Team
</Accordion>
