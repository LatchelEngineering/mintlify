<Note>
The purpose of this document is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to navigate the HubSpot tool and identify the information relevant to the Customer Profitability Team.
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1 - Access to the HubSpot platform

a. Navigate to the HubSpot login webpage.

b. Enter your credentials to log in.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-370.png" alt="20.png" />
</Frame>

### **Step 2 - Locate and open the specific customer's record**

a. In the top navigation bar, go to **Sales > Deals**. _(Note: The original said CRM > Customers, but finding the Deal is often the more common CPT workflow. Adjust if "Customers" is correct for your team.)_

b. Use the search bar at the top right to find the customer's account by name.

c. Click on the customer's name to open their full profile.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-371.png" alt="50.png" />
</Frame>

### **Step 3 - Gather key data points from the customer's profile**

**a. Review the left-side panel** to identify:

- The CSM in charge of the account.
- The customer's Latchel ID.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-372.png" alt="24.png" />
</Frame>

**b. Review the middle section** to find:

- Active units on Latchel console.
- Price per Unit.
- Contract Start Date.
- Filter subscription details (if applicable), including start date and type.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-373.png" alt="26.png" />
</Frame>

**c. Review the right-side panel** to find:

- Point of Contact (POC) information.
- Any available files attached to the account (e.g., contracts).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-374.png" alt="28.png" />
</Frame>

<Check>
When a customer’s information is updated on HubSpot, CPT may refer to the [Data Points to CPT Updates spreadsheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1BcQYR2qSGO2fskm_WXC-6PALHGplFZoVwBSXksNeSGo/edit?gid=0#gid=0), where information is automatically updated on a daily basis (except for the Console Unit Count which is updated on a monthly basis).
</Check>
