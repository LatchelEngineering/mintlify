---
title: "How to do the Monthly Piñata Report in RentVine"
---

<Note>
  The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to prepare the monthly report for Piñata Property Managers whose PMS is Rentvine.
</Note>

## Step-by-step guide

<Warning>
  Perform the Monthly Rent Roll Review before preparing the Monthly Piñata Report.
</Warning>

### Step 1: Go to the PM Piñata tab in the Benefits Tracker

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-192825.png](/images/CPT-635.png)
</Frame>

### Step 2: From the **GL Code** tab, copy all resident names with GL code and paste them into the **PM Piñata** tab

a. Filter **Column J (Benefits GL Code)** in the **PM GL Codes** tab of the **Benefits Tracker** to display only rows with GL code amounts (e.g., \$2.80, etc.).

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-194135.png](/images/CPT-636.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-194256.png](/images/CPT-637.png)
</Frame>

b. From the **GL Code Tab**, copy all _Resident Names, Addresses, Lease Start Date, Lease End Date, Rent,_ and paste them to the **PM Piñata Tab**.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-201751.png](/images/CPT-638.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-202753.png](/images/CPT-639.png)
</Frame>

**Note:** _Separate the Resident’s First and **Last Name**_

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-194952.png](/images/CPT-640.png)
</Frame>

c. The resident’s **Email, Cell Phone Number, Outstanding Balance,** and **Date of Birth** can be retrieved from **PMS**.

**Note:** _Search the Resident’s Address in PMS so you’ll be able to see the resident’s profile_

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-203552.png](/images/CPT-641.png)
</Frame>

d. Copy the resident’s **address** from the **Piñata** tab in the **Benefits Tracker** search for it in **PMS \> Lease Recurring Charges Report**.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-204458.png](/images/CPT-642.png)
</Frame>

e. Search for the address in **PMS** under the **Lease Recurring Charges Report**, then click the highlighted link of the address.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-204926.png](/images/CPT-643.png)
</Frame>

f. Copy the resident’s **Email, Phone Number,** and **Date of Birth (DOB)** from **PMS**. If the DOB is not listed, enter **“NA”** in the **Piñata** tab of the **Benefits Tracker**. Paste all the details into the **Piñata** tab.

<Note>
  Note: Ensure that you are copying the correct resident information from PMS to the Piñata tab.
</Note>

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-210753.png](/images/CPT-644.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-210010.png](/images/CPT-645.png)
</Frame>

g. Outstanding Balance: For example, if you are reviewing the month of **July**, check the outstanding balance for the **end of July** by going to the **Ledger** tab in the resident’s profile in **PMS** (same tab as above).

**Note:** If the **Outstanding Balance** displays a negative amount (e.g., -\$1,977.80), enter **“0”** in the **Piñata** tab.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-211503.png](/images/CPT-646.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-212023.png](/images/CPT-647.png)
</Frame>

h. Add the Rent Due Day of Month for the column you created. **Example:** _If you’re doing the report on August  02nd, it will contain the information of July, so the date in the Rent due day of the month column should be **07/01/2025.**_

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-214452.png](/images/CPT-648.png)
</Frame>

### Step 3: **Open the Monthly Report**

a. Open the Monthly report from the last email sent.

b. Right-click on the tab, select **'Duplicate,'** and rename the duplicated tab with the corresponding date and month of your review, following the required format.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-215351.png](/images/CPT-649.png)
</Frame>

c. From Benefits CPT Tracker (Piñata tab for the PM), copy data into the Piñata Monthly Report.

- Copy from the Tenant email to the unit column and paste it in the report’s tab. Then repeat the process to copy from the Property city till the DOB columns, respecting the merge column in the report.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-220329.png](/images/CPT-650.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-224729.png](/images/CPT-651.png)
</Frame>

### **Step 4: Unit Count & Validation**

a. In Column J (Merge column) of the Piñata report

- Copy the formula from the **previous monthly report** and paste it into the tab of the **current report** you created.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-225417.png](/images/CPT-652.png)
</Frame>

b. Reformulate the Formula till the end of your report.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-225857.png](/images/CPT-653.png)
</Frame>

c. To calculate the number of Properties reported in the **Piñata Report**, update the row count. **Example:** _The first property is in column J row 3, and the last property is in column J row 6. Update the formula to reflect these, as it’s shown in the picture below._

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-230245.png](/images/CPT-654.png)
</Frame>

### Step 5: **Update the Billing Inputs Doc**

a. Go to Billing Input Spreadsheet

b. Update Column Q with the new counts of Piñata Enrollments

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-230936.png](/images/CPT-655.png)
</Frame>

### **Step 6: Send the Report**

If this is the first time you are sending the report, you should request the CSM for the POC information to send the report.

a. Add the same Recipients that appear in the:  “To” and “CC” sections (refer to the photos below).

- Subject: **PM Company Name \+ Piñata Rent Roll Update**

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-231517.png](/images/CPT-656.png)
</Frame>

b. Click the “drive” Icon

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-231630.png](/images/CPT-657.png)
</Frame>

c. Search for the Piñata report by typing the name of the Property Manager (PM) and the report title. **Example:** _Chosen Property Management_

<Warning>
  Attach the report and first verify that it is the correct file by clicking on it before sending. If the file is incorrect, make sure to unselect it and attach the correct one.
</Warning>

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-232106.png](/images/CPT-658.png)
</Frame>

d. Click on **send**.

<Frame>
  ![image-20250805-232533.png](/images/CPT-659.png)
</Frame>
