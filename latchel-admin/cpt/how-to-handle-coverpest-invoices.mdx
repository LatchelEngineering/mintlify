title: "How to Handle Coverpest Invoices"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to accurately verify and approve the monthly Coverpest invoice by calculating the monthly totals reported by CPT in the** **[**<u>Billing Inputs Master Doc</u>**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1HG_bRF-JM8DqSz1lusDrfJVGIpohvJwBP4fNAipclvM/edit?gid=1596062573#gid=1596062573)
</Note>

**Coverpest bills us per unit** added in the monthly report sent, and they charge **$14.00 per unit**. Minor discrepancies where they have under-billed us are acceptable and do not require follow-up. **<u>They typically send the invoices after the 5th of each month</u>**<u>. If, for some reason, they don’t send it, no follow-up is needed</u>.

## Step by step

<Check>
Currently, we only have J&D Management enrolled in Coverpest. Coverpest sends the invoices directly to your personal Latchel inbox.
</Check>

### Step 1: Look for the most recent invoice received from Coverpest

a. Open your email inbox and type in the search bar “_Coverpest + Latchel_”, it will display the most recent one received.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-849.png" alt="image-20250922-181947.png" />
</Frame>

b. Scroll down in the opened email and look for the attached invoice

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-850.png" alt="image-20250922-182030.png" />
</Frame>

c. In the email subject, you will find the period that the invoice corresponds to.

**For example:** _If it says July, it means the amount charged is for the month of June, and we have 2 weeks to process the payment._

d. Look for the number of units charged on the invoice next to the description section.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-851.png" alt="image-20250922-182233.png" />
</Frame>

e. Then multiply the number of units by the Coverpest charge to verify if it matches the total amount. **Example:** _94 units × $14 = $1316._

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-852.png" alt="image-20250922-182406.png" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: **Calculate Units from the Billing Inputs** **Master Doc:**

a. Open the [**<u>Billing Inputs Master Doc</u>**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1HG_bRF-JM8DqSz1lusDrfJVGIpohvJwBP4fNAipclvM/edit?gid=1596062573#gid=1596062573) and navigate to the tab for the corresponding billing month. **Example:** _If you are checking the invoice for July, you should look for the July tab._

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-853.png" alt="image-20250922-182708.png" />
</Frame>

b. Count all the displayed totals showing in the **# of Cover Pest Enrollments**.

### Step 3: **Determine the Final Expected Count and Submit for Payment:**

a. Compare your **Expected Units** count with the "Quantity" listed on the Coverpest invoice.

b. If the invoiced quantity matches or is slightly less than your calculated total, the invoice is considered correct.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-854.png" alt="image-20250922-182233.png" />
</Frame>

c. Once an invoice is confirmed as correct, forward the original email to Accounts Payable (AP).

- **Recipient:** @Samantha Terreros** **(AP)
- **Body:**  _Hi Sammy, _ _This invoice is approved for payment. _ _Thank you._

d. **Click on reply all **and add Sammy if she’s not added in the original email.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-855.png" alt="image-20250922-183359.png" />
</Frame>

e. Click **send**.
