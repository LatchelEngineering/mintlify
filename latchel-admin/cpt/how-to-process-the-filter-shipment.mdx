<Note>
The purpose of this document is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to ship filters.
</Note>

<Check>
**Filters are sent every 20th of the month**. The list is sent and processed by Josie, who will send them through Home Depot. Home Depot offers standard shipping, faster delivery in some areas, and scheduled delivery for larger items. Residents usually receive their filters within 3–5 business days after shipment.
</Check>

## Step-by-step guide

<Warning>
<u>Ensure that </u>**<u>filters are shipped only after confirming payment (Last 3 months’ invoices) in Rillet</u>.**
</Warning>

## **Step 1: Check Payment Status**

a. Go to the [_<u>#</u>**<u>LEDGERING-MKT-OPT-BENEFITS</u>**_](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C03D4N7B0LF)_ _channel.

- **Donita** will post the **payment or shipment status** for filters **for the current month** on the 18th of the month.

<Check>
If any invoice payment is **pending, do not ship until it appears as paid in Rillet.**
</Check>

b. Verify in Rillet:

- Go to [**Rillet**](https://app.rillet.io/login?restore=)
- Navigate to:  `Accounts Receivable > Customers`
- Press `CTRL+F` and search for the **Property Manager (PM)** name.
- Check the **last 3 consecutive months** of invoices

## **Step 2: Review in Benefits Tracker**

a. Open the **PM’s tab** in the Benefits Tracker.

b. **Filter Column D** for "GL codes".

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-461.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

c. While you have Column D filtered, check Column T (Shipment Date) and select the current shipment month. Repeat the same step for Column V (Next Shipment Date). **For example**: _If it’s July 20th, you will filter all the dates for the month of July; those are the addresses that need to be shipped._

d. Scroll further to check the **Notes** column:

- Review the **last shipment date**. If the last shipment was **less than 6 months ago **_(or the corresponding time according to their subscription)_, **do not ship**.

To learn more about the corresponding PM subscription, check the **Benefits CPT tracker** under the [**Filters Overview**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1qd4oXlu6dBYwvPCVnl8fMwoulezF2MTZQoGOhqPqdo0/edit?pli=1&gid=0#gid=0)** tab**. For more information about subscriptions and their frequency, see the following job aid: [https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/2953412664/Identifying+types+of+Filters+subscriptions?atlOrigin=eyJpIjoiMzI2MTE0ZTkzNGMxNDljNjg4MTJiMWNhMDc1NTRmYzIiLCJwIjoiYyJ9](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/2953412664/Identifying+types+of+Filters+subscriptions)

## **Step 3: Confirm Filter Size (AU Tab)**

a. Open a new tab of the Benefits CPT tracker to view the AU tab for the PM you'll ship.

b. Select the addresses from the PM’s tab and search for them in the AU tab to see if there are filter sizes to ship. Use **CTRL + F** to search for the addresses. If the filter size is found, proceed with option C; if not. Leave a note in the notes column stating: **Missing Filter size(s).**

- Change the name of the PM company in the new spreadsheet. Also, make sure to include the date in the report title.

<Check>
**Note:** _Shipments are sent on the 20th of every month; if it falls on a weekend, they will be sent the following Monday._
</Check>

b. Use the following template to send the list via email:

**Subject:** [PM Company Name] Shipments List

_Hello Josie,_

_Please find attached the list of filter sizes that need to be shipped for [PM Company Name]. Kindly let us know if we can assist you with anything further._

_Thank you_

c. Make sure you tag Josie and [people@latchel.com](mailto:people@latchel.com) in the email, and also ensure the file is attached and sent as a PDF.

- Go to the **PM GL Codes** tab and leave a note in the notes column for each of the addresses included on the list: **"Filter list sent to Josie. Shipment sent on [MM/DD/YYYY]"**
