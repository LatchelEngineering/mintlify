title: "How to Check the Assisted Ledgering Email"
---

<Note>
The purpose of this SOP is to provide a step-by-step guide on how to check the emails received daily in the AL inbox, ensuring they are saved in the correct folder or that unnecessary emails are deleted.
</Note>

## Step by step

### Step 1: Go to the Assisted Ledgering email and check the Inbox section

a. **Look for newly emails received** in the Inbox section.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-409.png" alt="image-20250821-231407.png" />
</Frame>

### Step 2: Classify the emails

<Accordion title="GL Reports">
<Check>
See the following job aid for reference [How to Organize Monthly GL Reports](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AL/pages/3574890529/How+to+Organize+Monthly+GL+Reports)
</Check>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Emails to Delete">
You should delete **ALL** emails with the following subject:

- Work order
- Move out date
- SPAM Emails for workshops
- Any Marketing emails
- A document has been signed and is ready for review

<Warning>
**<u>Excalibur</u>**<u>, </u>**<u>McElwee</u>**<u>, and </u>**<u>Trademark</u>**<u> send the GL report daily</u>. Make sure to save only the report from the last day of each month; all other reports can be deleted.
</Warning>

a. Select all emails to delete

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-410.png" alt="image-20250821-232929.png" />
</Frame>

b. Click on the trash icon

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-411.png" alt="image-20250821-233037.png" />
</Frame>

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Emails to Save in a Folder">
Emails received with the following subjects should be saved in the corresponding folders:

- **Integration Alerts > **_Integration Alerts Label_
- **Piñata Invoices >** _Piñata Label_
- **Filter Invoices >** _Filters Label_
- **Latchel: Support Case** **>** _Customer Success Label_
- **Other invoices > **_Other Finance Label_

<Warning>
**<u>Always pay attention to the email subject to determine if it requires a response</u>**—for example, emails received from stakeholders such as third-party products (Piñata, Filters, Coverpest), Property Managers requesting assistance, or residents.
</Warning>

a. Select the email to save

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-412.png" alt="image-20250821-234246.png" />
</Frame>

b. Click on Move to

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-413.png" alt="image-20250821-234344.png" />
</Frame>

c. Type the name of the corresponding folder where you will save the email and select it. **Example:**_ For Integration Alerts, you should save them in the Integration Alerts Label_

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/CPT-414.png" alt="image-20250821-234505.png" />
</Frame>

d. The email will now be moved and saved in the corresponding folder/label.

e. Repeat the same steps for all emails to be saved.
</Accordion>

<Warning>
**<u>Always pay attention to the email subject to determine if it requires a response</u>**—for example, emails received from stakeholders such as third-party products (Piñata, Filters, Coverpest), Property Managers requesting assistance, or residents.
</Warning>
