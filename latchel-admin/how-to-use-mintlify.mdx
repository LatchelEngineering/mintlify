Welcome to Mintlify! In this help article you will find steps on how to use Mintlify to create new articles, update your existing pages, as well as some cool features it has to make your article even more visually appealing. So let’s get started!

### How to Log In to the Private Space in the Help Center

If you are a Latchel employee and want to access your team’s SOPs follow the steps below to access Latchel Team Spaces.

1. Access the private space within the help center through this link: [https://help.latchel.com/login](https://help.latchel.com/login), then enter the password "**_latchel-admin_**".

<Note>
  This password is the same for all users. Please reach out to Malen Macasaquit if the password is not working.
</Note>

<Frame>
  ![Image 06 22 2026 01 22 PM](/images/image-06-22-2026_01_22_PM.png)
</Frame>

2. Once logged in, you will land on the home page of the help center. From the home page, click **Latchel Admin** to go to the Latchel Team Spaces page. This page will give you access to all available Latchel Team Spaces. There are 2 options to navigate to each team space:
   - **Option 1:** Via the tiles
   - **Option 2:** Via the dropdown menu on the left sidebar.
3. As you navigate through the pages, you will find navigational menus on the right side to jump to a particular section of an article. Keep in mind that these page sections depend on the heading styles of the subsection titles. They must be set as Heading style in order to appear in the menu. Otherwise, the team editor will need to update your subsection font styles.
