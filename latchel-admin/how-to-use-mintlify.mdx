Welcome to Mintlify! In this help article you will find steps on how to use Mintlify to create new articles, update your existing pages, as well as some cool features it has to make your article even more visually appealing. So let’s get started!

1. Access the private space within the help center through this link: [https://help.latchel.com/login](https://help.latchel.com/login), then enter the password "**_latchel-admin_**".

<Note>
  This password is the same for all users. Please reach out to Malen Macasaquit if the password is not working.
</Note>

### **How to Create a New Page**

1. Upon accessing Editor, you will find the list of your pages by expanding the Latchel-Admin folder.

<Frame>
  ![How To Use Mintlify Mintlify 06 22 2026 01 21 PM](/images/How-to-Use-Mintlify-Mintlify-06-22-2026_01_21_PM.png)
</Frame>

2. From the Latchel Admin folder, look for your dedicated team space folder. To add a new page or folder, click the **\+** sign besides your team folder’s name.
   - **Add a page** will create a new page just within your main folder (not grouped)
   - **Add a folder** will create a new sub-folder within your main team folder (grouped pages).

<Tip>
  If you have multiple sub-topics under one umbrella, it is highly recommended to group them into folders.
</Tip>

<Frame caption="In this example, we will choose Add a page to create a new article.">
  ![Image 06 22 2026 01 27 PM](/images/image-06-22-2026_01_27_PM.png)
</Frame>

3. **Add the title** of your article then hit **Enter**. A blank canvas with the title of your article should appear. From here, you can now compose your new page's content.

<Warning>
  This step is very important as this will create your article's slug URL. Make sure the URL has no spaces between words. Use a hyphen "**-**" instead of a space.
</Warning>

<Frame>
  ![Data Support Overview Mintlify 6 July 2026](/images/Data_Support_Overview_Mintlify_6_July_2026.gif)
</Frame>

4. Once you're new page is ready for publishing, click the **Publish** button at the top right corner of the page.

<Frame>
  ![Image 07 06 2026 11 44 AM](/images/image-07-06-2026_11_44_AM.png)
</Frame>

### **Slash Commands**

The following commands can be triggered by typing **"/"**. You can use this to design and compose your page articles.

For example, if you type `/note` then hit **Enter**, it will trigger to show the note callout or nore card as shown below:
