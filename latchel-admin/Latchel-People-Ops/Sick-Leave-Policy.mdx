## Purpose

Latchel recognizes that team members will need days off from work from time to time if a team member or a family member is sick. Team members may need time off for rest, recuperation, and to address their medical needs.

## Eligibility

All regular full-time team members are granted 56 hours of sick leave from the date of hire for a total of 7 days per year, and all regular part-time team members are granted 28 hours of sick leave from the date of hire for a total of 3.5 days per year.

For US employees using Justworks, hours will be frontloaded into JustWorks on January 2nd of each year or the first day of employment for new employees.

For international contractors, hours accrue biweekly at 2.1536 hours for every 80 hours completed. If fewer than 80 hours are worked, sick leave accrual will be adjusted accordingly. Hours do not carry over to the next year.

## Procedure

### Leave requests for US Employees

To schedule sick leave:

Employees must submit a completed leave request on their JustWorks dashboard by clicking the "time off" tab on the left toolbar; then click the "Request Time Off" button; view the steps [here](). All requests must be submitted as soon as possible.

Employees must ensure that they have enough accrued leave available to cover the time off requested. In the event they have exhausted sick leave, they can submit a request for vacation leave with supervisor approval.

If the employee is absent unexpectedly due to personal or a family member's illness, the employee should notify their supervisor or the People Operations Department by Slacking @people or emailing [people@latchel.com](mailto:people@latchel.com) as soon as reasonably possible. People Operations can review all possible leave benefits available to you.

### Leave requests for International Contractors

To schedule sick leave:

Employees must submit a completed leave request on their Deputy dashboard by clicking the "time off" tab on the left toolbar, then clicking the "Request Time Off" button. All requests must be submitted as soon as possible.

Contractors must ensure that they have enough accrued leave available to cover the time off requested. In the event they have exhausted sick leave, they can submit a request for vacation leave with supervisor approval.

If the employee is absent unexpectedly due to personal or a family member's illness, the employee should notify their supervisor or the People Operations Department by Slacking @people or emailing [people@latchel.com](mailto:people@latchel.com) as soon as reasonably possible. People Operations can review all possible leave benefits available to you.

Sick leave may be used in accordance with the following provisions:

- If sick leave is exhausted, paid time off leave will be used in its place.
- Sick leave may be used for a team member's personal illness, well-care, and medical and dental appointments. Sick leave also may be used for illness and well-care of a member of a team member's immediate family (including the team member's spouse, children, mother, and father).
- A team member with a sick leave absence in excess of three consecutive work days must present medical documentation for the absence.

Employees are not paid for unused sick leave upon termination of employment.
