# Job Abandonment Policy

Latchel expects all team members to report to work on every scheduled day. A team member who is unable to report to work at the designated time is required to notify the direct supervisor as soon as practicable but no later than the team member's scheduled start time in accordance with the sick leave policy. Team members who fail to report to work for three consecutive business days without notifying the company of the absence will be considered as having voluntarily resigned as a result of job abandonment.

If the team member is unable to contact the company for any absence, the team member should ask a representative (such as a family member or friend) to do so on the team member's behalf. If the team member or a representative is unable to contact Latchel due to extreme circumstances (such as a medical emergency or natural disaster that prohibits the team member or a representative from contacting the company within three days), the team member or their representative must contact Latchel as soon as practicable to explain the situation. In extreme circumstances, the employer will consider the explanation and its timing before determining if the voluntary resignation will be upheld.
