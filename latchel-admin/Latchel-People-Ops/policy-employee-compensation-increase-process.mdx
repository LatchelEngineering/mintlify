---
title: "Policy Employee Compensation Increase Process"
---

## Purpose:

The purpose of this policy is to establish a process for determining and implementing compensation increases for employees based on performance and justified need.

## Scope:

This policy applies to all employees of the organization.

## Process Steps:

1. The manager determines the need for an increase in employee compensation.
2. The manager evaluates the employee's performance and reviews the job description and core competencies on the [leveling matrix](#) to ensure the compensation increase is justified.
3. The manager documents the justification and compensation increase details by completing a [Compensation Requests Form](#).
4. The manager submits a compensation increase request to the People Operations Department for approval.
5. People Operations reviews and works with the compensation team then notifies the manager of approval or denial of the request.
6. If approved, People Ops updates the employee's compensation in TriNet and notifies the employee of the updated compensation.

## Procedure:

**1. Determine the need for a compensation increase**

a. The manager identifies the need for a compensation increase for an employee.

b. The manager considers the employee's job responsibilities, experience, market conditions, and other relevant factors to determine the need for the increase.

**2. Evaluate employee performance**

a. The manager evaluates the employee's performance to ensure that the compensation increase is justified.

b. The manager considers the employee's performance data, including feedback from colleagues and customers, as well as other relevant factors such as attendance, attitude, and behavior.

**3. Document justification and increase details**

a. The manager documents the justification and compensation increase details.

b. The documentation should include the reasons for the increase, the amount of the increase, and any other relevant details.

**4. Submit a request to People Operations**

a. The manager submits the compensation increase request form to HR.

b. The form should include the documentation of the justification and compensation increase details.

**5. Review and approve or deny the request**

a. People Operations reviews the compensation increase request and evaluates the justification and documentation.

b. People Operations approves or denies the request based on the established criteria and policies.

**6. Update employee compensation in TriNet**

a. If the request is approved, People Ops updates the employee's compensation in the TriNet.

b. People Ops ensures that the updated compensation is accurately reflected in the employee's records.

**7. Notify team members of updated compensation**

a. The employee is notified of the updated compensation.

b. The notification should include the amount of the increase, the effective date, and any other relevant details.

## Exceptions:

In exceptional circumstances, where immediate action is necessary, a compensation increase may be approved by People Operations without the complete documentation. In such cases, the manager must provide a written explanation and justification for the expedited process.

## Policy Compliance:

Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

## Policy Review:

This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure that it reflects the organization's current practices and complies with applicable laws and regulations.
