---

#### **Purpose/Objective**

Latchel offers health care benefits/medical, dental, and vision insurance benefit plans to eligible employees. The company reserves the right to prospectively add, change, delete, or modify the benefits offered to employees as it deems appropriate. This policy is only a general summary of the health care benefits offered. Employees should consult the applicable plan document for information regarding, coverage, and benefits details. It is the plan document that ultimately governs employee entitlement to benefits.

#### **Eligibility**

All regular, full-time employees 

#### **Procedures**

Employees will have the opportunity to enroll in the company’s health care benefits/medical, dental, and vision insurance benefits plans on their first day of employment if they meet the eligibility requirements listed above. 

The company’s open enrollment period generally begins on November 1st of each year. During open enrollment, eligible employees will have the opportunity to review the health care benefits/medical, dental, and vision insurance benefit plans and make any benefits election changes they wish to make for themselves, their spouses, domestic partners, and dependent children.

Additionally, upon the occurrence of a qualifying change in status, eligible employees may make benefits election changes. For more information regarding benefits election change opportunities, please contact the People Operations Department by emailing [<u>people@latchel.com</u>](mailto:people@latchel.com) or request information in the people\_requests channel in Slack.

As detailed in the applicable company leave policies, when employees are on a leave of absence, the company maintains health care benefits for employees as required under applicable law and offers continuation coverage as required under applicable law. However, if on unpaid leave for an extended period the employer contributions towards your benefits may pause until the eligible employee returns to work.
