## People Operations Purpose:

The purpose of People Operations is to keep Latchel healthy and enduring as a business by attracting and retaining the best, most productive talent for Latchel.

## Space Purpose:

The purpose of this space is to have all human resources documentation available in one space. As we build out the resources, they will all be added here. Documentation that can be found here:

- Employee Handbook
- Latchel Policies & Procedures
- Manager Training
- Annual Review Process Documentation
