---

## Purpose/Objective

Latchel offers health care benefits plans to eligible international team members. The company reserves the right to prospectively add, change, delete, or modify the benefits offered to team members as it deems appropriate. This policy is only a general summary of the health care benefits provided. Team members should consult the applicable plan document for information regarding, coverage, and benefits details. It is the plan document that ultimately governs the member's entitlement to benefits.

## Eligibility

All regular, full-time international contractors who have been with the company for at least 6 months.

## Procedures

Team members will have the opportunity to enroll in the company's healthcare benefit plans when they reach 6-month tenure with the company and meet the eligibility requirements listed above.

Latchel will contribute a percentage of the plan cost depending on the selected plan. The team member's portion will be deducted from their paychecks on a bi-weekly basis.

The company's open enrollment period will begin in July of each year. During open enrollment, eligible employees will have the opportunity to review the health care benefits plans and make any benefits election changes they wish to make for themselves, their spouses, domestic partners, and dependent children.

Additionally, upon the occurrence of a qualifying change in status, eligible team members may make benefits election changes. For more information regarding benefits election change opportunities, please contact the People Operations Department by emailing [people@latchel.com](mailto:people@latchel.com) or submitting a change request in the people_requests channel in Slack.

As detailed in the applicable company leave policies, when team members are on a leave of absence, the company maintains health care benefits. However, if on unpaid leave for an extended period the employer contributions towards your benefits may pause until the eligible team member returns to work.

## Alternative Stipend for Existing Insurance:

Latchel will provide a stipend of USD 50 on the first paycheck of each month to help cover your existing insurance costs if you choose to waive the benefits provided through SafetyWing Global Insurance. To qualify for this stipend, you will need to provide proof of your current health insurance coverage to the People Operations Department during the open enrollment period for the company's SafetyWing coverage.
