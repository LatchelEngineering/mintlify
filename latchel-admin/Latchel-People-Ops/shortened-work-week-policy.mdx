## **Document Highlights**

- Latchel’s shortened workweek schedule will start July 5, 2024.
- Salaried team members will work a 4-day work week every other week, with that Friday off. This schedule will not change from one pay period to the next.
- The full schedule can be viewed [<u>here</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/LPO/pages/3312484353 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/LPO/pages/3312484353").
- Only team members paid on a salary are eligible. If you are a part-time salary, you still qualify, but are required to work your full scheduled hours over the 9-day pay period.
- When a paid holiday falls on the scheduled Friday off, another day off will not be provided during that pay period.

## **Purpose**

Alternative work schedules, including the shortened workweek, are consistent with Latchel’s efforts toward work/life balance.  

A shortened workweek allows salaried team members to compress their work for the week or pay period, in exchange for a Friday off every other week or one Friday per pay period.

## **Eligibility**

Eligible team members must meet the following criteria:

- Team members must be paid a salary not hourly. 
  - If part-time, you must meet your regular scheduled hours each week.
- Team members must be meeting or exceeding equivalent performance and productivity expectations as if there was not a compressed workweek.

## **Set Schedule**

Latchel’s shortened workweek schedule is set (not varying from pay period to pay period). Latchel will work a 4-day work week every other week, with that Friday as a paid day off starting July 5, 2024. The 2026 schedule can be viewed [<u>here</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/LPO/pages/3312484353 "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/LPO/pages/3312484353").

## **Requirements & Standards**

**Compressed workweek schedules must meet the following requirements:**

- All team members must comply with the [<u>Time & Attendance policy</u>](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/CgB3q "https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/x/CgB3q").
- Operational requirements must be met.
- Service to the customer must be maintained or improved.
- Costs to Latchel will not be increased.

**Compressed workweek schedules will meet the following standards:**

- Compressed workweek schedules will not diminish the ability of Latchel to assign responsibility and accountability to individual team members for the provision of services and performance of their duties.
- When a paid holiday falls on the scheduled Friday off, another day off will not be provided during that pay period.
- The company reserves the right to change or terminate this policy at any time.
