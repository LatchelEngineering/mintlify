## 👋 Welcome to Latchel!

We are really excited to have you join us and help us push our mission and vision forward.

Our Leadership Principles guide us in every decision we make. We use them to help make hiring decisions, set our strategic direction, and even determine how we will interact with customers daily. These principles are the bedrock of our culture. Just like bedrock, they can shift and change. We develop this culture continuously and improve it as the company outgrows old ones and needs new ones.

We have flagged the most important Leadership principles and call them Core Values. They are still part of our overall leadership principles. The difference between them and the rest of the leadership principles is that we see them as critically important to be a focal point of our external branding.

We understand that every team member at Latchel will be strong in some principles and need help with others, but we hope every team member will first focus on strengthening the core values before moving on to the rest of the leadership.

## 📖 History

Latchel began when Will's family needed help running the family rental properties. His grandfather managed the properties full-time until his mid 90's! When his age caught up with him, and he could no longer take care of the family business, Will stepped in to help his father with the properties. He still worked full-time at Amazon, but he was quickly overwhelmed trying to deliver great resident service. Maintenance was the most overwhelming part. After looking for solutions online, Will called Ethan for help. Will and Ethan knew each other from UC Berkeley, where they lived together for four years during college. Ethan had been building lead generation products to connect homeowners and contractors, but these products didn't fit Will's needs. No solutions existed to help solve Will's problems!

Looking for an expert software engineer, Will and Ethan turned to Jullian. Both Ethan and Jullian had worked together before building software products at another startup. Together, they set out to build what they couldn't find on the market: a service to level up the resident's experience for every interaction - starting with a great maintenance experience.

Latchel began beta operations in February 2017, exclusively focused on making daytime maintenance manageable on a software platform for residents.. The service had high demand, but the founders knew they needed to do more to truly be accessible to residents and property managers. In March 2018, Latchel committed to end-to-end resident experience by handling the maintenance experience 24/7/365, even for emergencies. They quickly grew from 2,000 units to 33,500 units.

Latchel earned a place in the Y Combinator Winter 2019 batch. Through Y Combinator, Latchel explored even more ways to expand its reach to help property managers level up resident experience.

They realized that the best way to solve for the residents' experience was to package the maintenance experience tooling into a wider amenity platform that could deliver exponential value to residents. After just four months of development, Latchel launched the beta for Home Assistant, now their flagship product. Home Assistant became an amenity platform for residents that solved the property manager's maintenance headaches. They included benefits like damage reimbursements, renters insurance, cash-back programs, and a plethora of local deals and other benefits into the amenity marketplace. In just 2 years, Latchel grew from under \$1m in revenues to \$8.5m in contracted revenues. The foundation had been laid to change the industry!

## 🏅 Latchel's Team

Latchel was founded by a team that chose each other for their complementary strengths. In small teams, everyone does a little bit of everything (including taking out the garbage). However, to be most effective, everyone needs to specialize in what they do best.

As such, we structure our company into a few specific talent categories.

[Latchel Org Chart](https://canva.link/rl2g9gqtviff6vk)

**Service Operations**

Our Service Operations team at Latchel is dedicated to ensuring a seamless maintenance experience for our valued customers. They handle all resident maintenance requests and work closely with property managers and maintenance technicians to promptly address any needs. Our Service Operations team is always available to provide prompt and efficient emergency screening, troubleshooting, and dispatching services to ensure the comfort and satisfaction of our residents. Additionally, we also provide product support to assist our customers in utilizing our platform to its fullest potential. Through our interactions via phone, email, text, and our platform, our goal is to empower property managers to consistently deliver exceptional resident experiences.

**Growth**

Latchel is constantly evolving. Our mission is to empower property managers to create a new revenue stream and offer better benefits to residents through our software platform. Our Growth team is focused on educating potential customers and coaching current customers about our service and the value it brings to their business. This team helps drive product improvements through customer feedback and facilitates desired outcomes for our customers. Latchel is not only customer-centric but customer-obsessed, and the Growth team works in close alignment to ensure property managers don't have both happy residents and a healthy bottom line.

**Research & Development (R&D)**

Our R&D team is focused on product development, design, and conducting experiments that drive scalable technology improvements across the entire company. They increase knowledge, solve problems and develop new technologies that will lead to new products, services, or a better way of doing things. Latchel constantly pushes beyond the boundaries of what our technology is capable of, and our industry and technology teams continuously improve our capabilities to meet our ever-growing customer demand. They build the foundation that empowers our customers to deliver incredible service to residents.

**General & Administration (G&A)**

Our G&A team is dedicated to the general running of Latchel. This team provides administrative and logistical support to every area of the business. The G&A team keeps an effective communication channel open, so everyone is informed of any new changes and how the changes may affect Latchel. This department is focused on keeping all departments within Latchel operating at maximum efficiency so we can continue to enable our customers to create a new revenue stream, so they have both happy residents and a healthy bottom line.
