---
title: "Team Member/Employee/Contractor Term Sheet"
---

### **Team Member:**

Anyone who works at Latchel. This includes all employees and contractors.

### **Regular Employee:**

Latchel Employees are individuals who Latchel employs to perform specific job duties through employment. Employees may work full-time or part-time, and their job duties may vary depending on their position. Latchel’s employee payroll and benefits are managed on the JustWorks dashboard.

### **Regular International Contractor:**

Contractors at Latchel are team members who do not live in the United States. Hired through a contract to provide a specific service,  perform specific job duties,  or complete a specific project. Their payroll is processed through the Deel platform.

### **Temporary Employee or Contractor:**

Temporary Employees or Contractors at Latchel are individuals who are hired on a temporary basis to perform certain job duties or complete specific projects. They may be domestic or international, but what distinguishes them from regular employees or contractors is the non-ongoing nature of their relationship with Latchel. Their compensation is managed through the applicable platform, but they do not have access to Latchel's leave policies or health benefits. Their engagement with Latchel ends once the agreed-upon work or project duration is complete.
