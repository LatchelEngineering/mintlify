## <strong>Purpose</strong>

This document establishes a systematic process for handling subscription invoice adjustments. Its goal is to ensure a transparent and fair approach that benefits both Latchel and Property Managers. It provides a standardized framework for reviewing, approving, and applying invoice adjustments and credits.

## <strong>Target Audience</strong>

This document is intended for the Accounts Receivable team to categorize and process adjustment requests according to the standard computation and approval procedures. It also serves as a reference for the Product Support and Customer Success teams to understand how billing disputes from Property Managers are handled, enabling them to set accurate expectations and provide clear updates to customers.

## <strong>Overview </strong>

Latchel’s preferred method of resolving billing issues is through issuing a credit, rather than a refund.  Credits can be applied to either future invoices or the current month’s invoice, depending on when the request was received and the invoice status.

Refunds are only considered in exceptional circumstances, such as internal errors or when a customer has been incorrectly billed for services they should not have been charged for. Both credits and refunds require approval from the team members designated below, depending on the amount and the reason for the request.

## <strong>Credit Approval Policy and Workflow</strong>

### <strong>1. Credit Request Submission</strong>

The requester submits a Subscription Invoicing Request workflow in the #accounts-receivable channel with the Category Billing Dispute, providing the reason for the adjustment, the specific invoice to be adjusted, and any supporting details. This request can be submitted by the PS team, the CSMs, or AR if received through the billing@ email.

### <strong>2. AR Initial Review</strong>

The submitted request must be thoroughly reviewed to ensure a clear understanding of the reason, including the impacted invoice(s), the type of credit, the amount, and the time of submission.

For Active Unit Count Credit Requests, refer to the <a href="https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/finance1/pages/3361112102/Active+Unit+Count+Credit+Request+Policy">Active Unit Count Credit Request Policy</a> to confirm if the situation qualifies for credit approval. AR should proactively include any relevant threads, computation, or information that could affect the approval process before tagging or involving the designated approver.

### <strong>3. Approval Decision</strong>

#### For Requests Submitted by the PS Team and AR:

- Credits up to \$250: Tag the account’s CSM for approval.
- Credits over \$250: Tag the Head of Finance, Amrit Parajuli, for approval.

#### For Requests Submitted by CSMs:

- Credits up to \$250: Proceed with credit processing following the <a href="https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/edit-v2/3361112073?draftShareId=f18af687-dfb8-4934-b9f6-89b74106a04f#Timing-for-Credit-Process"><em>Timing for Credit Requests</em></a> guidelines.
- Credits over \$250:  Tag the Head of Finance, Amrit Parajuli, for approval.
  - If there is no response from the approver within two days, follow up on the thread for confirmation.

### <strong>4. Credit Processing </strong>

Once approved, AR processes the credit as soon as the approval is received

#### <strong>Timing for Credit Process</strong>

- <strong>Adjustments Before the 8th calendar day of the Month</strong>

1. Requests for unit count billing adjustments that are submitted and approved before the 8th calendar day of the month will be applied to the current month’s invoice.
2. These adjustments will be reflected immediately since the notice is received before invoices are sent
3. Add credits to draft invoices as needed. See the step-by-step guide on how to apply credit and create a credit memo on this SOP: <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ZOPAhACwQkp4KZChsRJ0zdMOpsWNPhk8fmk1I-_Nem4/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.xuvn8cjkdasf">Invoice creation process</a>

- <strong>Adjustments on or after the 8th calendar day of the Month</strong>

1. Requests for unit count billing adjustments submitted and approved on the 8th business day or later will be recorded in the <a href="https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1gp_zAl2PZEOAFzYKWwm3tink5xjf9NUtgaTeUr4k_rE/edit?gid=425319084#gid=425319084">AR Rolling Credits/Discounts Tracker</a>.
2. These adjustments will be applied to next month’s invoice, as the invoices for the previous month have already been finalized.

<Warning>
  <strong>Exception:</strong> If the adjustment is due to an internal error (and, if applicable, autopay hasn’t run yet), it would be more customer-focused to update the current month’s invoice rather than issuing a credit for the next month. Please see this <a href="https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C05N2RRD14P/p1739307684836739">thread</a> for reference.
</Warning>
