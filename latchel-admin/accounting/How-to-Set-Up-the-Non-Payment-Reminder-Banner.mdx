<Note>
**Purpose**

The purpose of this document is to provide a step-by-step guide on how the AR team sets up the non-payment reminder banner. This banner serves as a visible reminder to PMs of an open balance on their account.
</Note>

## Steps in Setting Up Non-Payment Banner

### Step 1. Log in to Userpilot

Using your Latchel email's credentials, log in to the [UserPilot](https://run.userpilot.io/spotlights/99/settings) dashboard.

### Step 2. Once logged in, navigate to the left-hand menu and click on Engagement, then select Spotlight. From there, click on Settings.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/AR-47.png)
</Frame>

In the Settings section, you will see four configuration areas: Environment, Domain, Page, and Audience. These fields are generally pre-filled and should remain as follows:

- Environment: Production Environment
- Domain: All
- Page: Only on Specific Paths

<Frame>
  ![](/images/AR-48.png)
</Frame>

### Step 3. The primary section that requires attention is the Audience tab. This section is pre-configured to ensure that the banner is displayed to all PM users, regardless of their role.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/AR-49.png)
</Frame>

### Step 4. In the example configuration, there is an active banner set up for PM-39443 (LBK Residential). To add another customer, click on Add Condition and ensure that the fields match the existing configuration. Specifically, for field #8, enter the unique last digits from the PM company URL found in the console.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/AR-50.png)
</Frame>

### Step 5. After all conditions have been correctly set, click Push Updates. Once published, the non-payment reminder banner will become visible to all PM users associated with that PM company.
