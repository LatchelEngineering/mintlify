## <strong>Purpose</strong>

The purpose of this document is to guide the management and tracking of all Accounts Receivable activities, including procedural <em>How-Tos</em> and the Calendar of Activities.

## AR Calendar of Tasks

Every month, the Accounts Receivable (AR) team creates a new sheet in the Monthly AR Tasks / Calendar to organize and track all AR activities for that month.
