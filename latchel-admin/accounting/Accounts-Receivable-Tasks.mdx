## AR Calendar of Tasks

Every month, the Accounts Receivable (AR) team creates a new sheet in the Monthly AR Tasks / Calendar to organize and track all AR activities for that month.

| Daily Tasks | Weekly Tasks | Monthly Tasks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cash reconciliation | Checking open balances for PMs with Filter add-ons | Invoice Creation |
| Respond to #accountsreceivable Slack channel | Check and update the Marketplace Customer Churn Tracker accordingly whenever a customer from any type of product (except Optional Benefits) cancels. | Monthly AR Sync |
| Respond to #service-change Slack channel | Monitor past dues | Send Past Due Communication |
| Respond to #sales Slack channel | AR Slack list check | Check the automatic payment |
| Checking billing@ email |  | Create and Terminate contracts |

<Note>
  The monthly tasks listed in the table above are generalized and do not capture every activity performed by the Accounts Receivable team. To view a complete list of monthly tasks and access the full AR calendar, please refer to [AR Tasks / Monthly Calendar](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bW7s1jozzWFSyqXp9guj_ZuoLjFRhPFsJq65K4BXKYM/edit?gid=1353544436#gid=1353544436).
</Note>
