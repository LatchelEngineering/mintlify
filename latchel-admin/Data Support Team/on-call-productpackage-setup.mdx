---
title: "On-Call Product/Package Setup"
---

The on-call rotation depends on the product package setup. If a customer does not have the on-call package, a job will never enter the "on-call" state. As a result, the on-call rotation will not be triggered for newly created jobs. This demo will demonstrate how to enable the on-call product/package setup.
