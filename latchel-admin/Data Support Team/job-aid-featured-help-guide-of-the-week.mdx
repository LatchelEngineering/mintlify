‌

### Purpose:

This job-aid outlines the steps for the Data Support Supervisor (<custom data-type="mention" data-id="id-0">@Malen Macasaquit</custom> ) to select and post a help guide in the Service Operations Slack channel based on the collected SMS conversation between the Service Operations Associates and residents. The goal is to provide valuable information to the Service Operations Associates for assisting residents effectively. 

‌

### Who are the responsible stakeholders of this project?

1. **Data Support Supervisor:** Responsible for selecting, preparing, and posting the featured help guide every Monday.
2. **Latchel Operations Associates:** Responsible for supporting the posting process, providing feedback, and engaging in discussions in the Slack channel.
3. **Program Manager (**<custom data-type="mention" data-id="id-1">@Javier Orta (Unlicensed)</custom>**):** his role is to ensure that the associates are providing the necessary help guides to the residents or customers to reduce the Average Resident Contact. 

‌

### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-2">:note:</custom> How is the featured Help Guide selected?

1. **Collect SMS Conversation**

    1. Every Monday morning, collect SMS conversations from the previous week between Latchel Operations Associates and Residents.
    
2. **Analyze SMS Conversations**

    1. Review the collected SMS conversations to identify common themes, questions, or issues that residents are facing.
    2. Prioritize topics that are relevant, timely, and impactful to the majority of residents.
    
3. **Data Support Supervisor Selection**

    1. The Data Support Supervisor reviews the identified themes and selects a help guide from the list of currently published articles in the [Help Center](http://help.latchel.com).
    2. Ensure that the selected help guide is clear, concise, and easy to understand.
    3. The featured Help Guide must be relevant to the top drivers and concerns of the residents, which are raised to Latchel.
    
4. **Post in Slack**

    1. The Data Support Supervisor must share the selected featured Help Guide in the [#service-operations](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C2R3MUKGT) Slack channel. 
    2. In the announcement, the following details must be included:
    
        1. Title of the Help Guide
        2. Published Date
        3. Brief description of the help guide
        4. The URL of the article
        5. Google link Form where anyone can provide a feedback
        
    
5. **Monitor Engagement**

    1. Track engagement within the Slack channel, such as likes, comments, suggestions, or feedback from the Service Operations team. 
    2. The Data Support Supervisor may also encourage discussions or questions related to the featured help guide.
    
6. **Review and Assess Effectiveness**

    1. Assess the effectiveness of the featured help guides by calculating the weekly **Average Resident Contact per Unit.** _**A reduced number of resident contacts to Latchel indicates a positive effect.**_ 
    2. Review and update the help guides based on feedback and needs of the Service Operations along with new product releases regularly as stated in[ Job-Aid: Review Process for Published Help Guides](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/2787442689/Job-Aid+Review+Process+for+Published+Help+Guides).
