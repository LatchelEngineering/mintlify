---
title: "How to Create a Custom View"
---

This article outlines the steps for creating a custom view in Assembly, allowing you to access all your group’s tickets in one location.

**What is Custom View?**

Custom view works as a saved filter of all the tickets assigned to your team.

### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-0">:bookmark:</custom> **The Steps**

1. On your Assembly dashboard, click **Create View** at the bottom left corner of the page.  

    ![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=97562189-ca9d-4dbc-8721-fb79c271e008&&collection=contentId-3200024653&height=730&occurrenceKey=null&width=1195&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

     


2. **Add** the **Name**, **Description**, and choose the **Type of your custom view**.  

    ![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=e574b09d-c033-4552-8bfa-bb7eaaae1957&&collection=contentId-3200024653&height=501&occurrenceKey=null&width=1642&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

      


3. Next, click **Filter**. From the filter list, select the option that best displays all the tickets you're working on. You may also select multiple filters as needed. Lastly, click **Submit** to save your new custom view.  

    ![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=d89025b4-3b57-439d-80a6-4d77d5009499&&collection=contentId-3200024653&height=795&occurrenceKey=null&width=1732&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

    



**Pro-Tip:** The best filter to use is Customer Group. However, you need to create a Customer Group first before using it as a custom view filter. Learn how to create a Customer Group by following the steps in [How to Create a Customer Group](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/3200712730).

4. Your new custom view will appear under **"Workspace"** (if you chose the **"Team"** type) or under **"Personal"** (if you selected the **"Personal"** type).  

    ![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=5b06f1bb-71cf-428e-9b62-55020b2a806a&&collection=contentId-3200024653&height=691&occurrenceKey=null&width=1050&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

    



You can now use your new custom view whenever you need to check all the tickets your team is managing!
