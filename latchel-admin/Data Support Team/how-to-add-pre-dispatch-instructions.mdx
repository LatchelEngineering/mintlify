# Objective

**Purpose**

Property Managers can provide a list of contacts that they want to be called before dispatching a service provider. For example, this could be an on-site maintenance tech or building superintendent. Latchel will contact them first. If they accept the job then we pass it off to them and close the job in our system. _Please note:_ 

1. Any pre-dispatch contact that accepts a job will make us close the job out in our system. If the Property Manager wants work to continue to be tracked then they should make the contact point a Service Provider that gets dispatched. 
2. Pre-dispatch Instructions can be configured at the company level, property level, or portfolio level. If configured at the portfolio level, this will override the company level Pre-dispatch Instructions. If configured at the property level, this will override portfolio level and company level Pre-dispatch Instructions. 

**Successful Use Of Pre-Dispatch Instructions:**

The Pre-Dispatch Instructions are well utilized when they provide:

1. A list of contacts to reach out to before dispatching a service provider.
2. Special instructions to prevent dispatching for certain types of issues.
3. Warranty information that may be required by dedicated service providers.
4. Helpful contextual advice for the team due to unique property conditions.

**Prohibited Uses of Pre-Dispatch Instructions:**

We should NOT use Pre-Dispatch Instructions to instruct specific service providers to be dispatched. These should instead be configured in the priority list for dispatching. [Click here to learn how to configure preferred service providers.](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/794198095/How+to+Configure+Preferred+Service+Providers+to+a+New+Account)

# Content

**What is happening prior to this process?**

Prior to adding pre-dispatch notes to a Property Manager's account, post the note in the #cs-peer-review channel in slack for feedback from the team. As a best practice, loop in <custom data-type="mention" data-id="id-0">@</custom> from the Emergency Team to ensure notes that will impact her team are reviewed for feedback.

1. Go to the Property Manager Profile Page:  [How to: Access the Property Manager Profile Page](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/813729656)
