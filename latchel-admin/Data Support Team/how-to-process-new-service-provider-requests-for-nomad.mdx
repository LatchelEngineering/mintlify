### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-0">:bookmark_tabs:</custom> **Introduction**

As part of Latchel’s partnership with Nomad, their owners can submit requests to onboard their preferred service providers for any of their owned properties. 

These requests come in from either a Jotform, or a Typeform in <custom data-type="smartlink" data-id="id-1">https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C04E9CLPX5W</custom> Slack channel. 

The Data Support Team, particularly <custom data-type="mention" data-id="id-2">@</custom> promptly processes these requests as soon as they are received in the Slack Channel.

‌

### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-3">:green_book:</custom> **Objective**

The purpose of this page is to provide the steps on how these requests must be processed and completed in Latchel.

‌

### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-4">:envelope_with_arrow:</custom> **Types of Service Provider Onboarding Requests**

* **New Vendor Self-Onboard** - These are direct requests from service providers to be added as Preferred Service Providers of NOMAD.

### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-5">:bookmark:</custom> **Processing the Request in Latchel**

1. As soon as the request is received in <custom data-type="smartlink" data-id="id-6">https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C04E9CLPX5W</custom>  channel, review the details in the form and record the following in the SP Onboarding tab of spreadsheet <custom data-type="smartlink" data-id="id-7">https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/18u2GsV5Wz0OGfzctcMD2Y8Vd0C2irfh2pFW5847KV5E/edit#gid=2129093277</custom>:

    1. Date of when the request was completed/processed
    2. Owner Details - this is only available if the submitted request is Owner/Vendor Onboarding.
    3. Complete details of the Service Provider Company
    4. Service Provider Skills
    5. Service Provider Territory
    6. Remarks/Action Taken - include all steps taken to complete the request. For example: Service Provider added in Latchel console and prioritize at Owner/Portfolio level
    
2. Add/assign the service provider by following these steps:

    1. Search for the Service Provider in Latchel using its company name, or contact information. If the service provider has no existing records in Latchel, create a new service provider company using the details provided in the request. Make sure to add their skills and category. Once done creating, or if an existing service provider is found, assign Nomad as the preferred Property Manager.
    2. If the request received in the Slack channel is an Owner/Vendor Onboarding form, prioritize the service provider at the Portfolio associated to the owner’s name. You may find the portfolio, in the [**Portfolios tab of Nomad’s company page**](https://app.latchel.com/admin/pm-company/18261/portfolios).
    3. If the request received in the Slack channel is a New Vendor Self-Onboard form, the service provider must be prioritized in [**Nomad’s Service Providers tab**](https://app.latchel.com/admin/pm-company/18261/vendors) at the company page.
    4. Add the service territories of the new service provider as specified in the form. You may do this by going to the company page’s **Service Provider Management**. Look for the new service provider, click the **3 dots** across the service provider company name, then choose **Service Provider Territory Settings**. Add the service territories either from existing properties or manually add zip codes. Click **Add**, then **Continue** to save the changes made.
    5. If the service provider is set as the only or the last prioritized service provider in the skill category, set it’s response buffer time to 72 hours.

3. After onboarding the new service provider in Latchel, record all the action taken in the remarks column of the spreadsheet specified in step #1. Then, go back to the request posted in the Slack channel and reply to the thread to document the action made and notify Nomad.

The **response buffer time** of the last prioritized service provider in every skills must be set to **72 hours.**

It is recommended to address the form promptly, ideally within the first 24 hours of submission, to ensure everything keeps moving along smoothly!
