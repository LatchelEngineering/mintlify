---

Currently (9/26/24), on-call rotations can only be configured at the portfolio level. 

**On-call rotations will not trigger if the following steps are not completed:**

1. [**On-Call Product Package Setup**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/PHONE_NUMBER_008/On-Call+Product+Package+Setup)
2. [**Configuring On-Call Technicians**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/PHONE_NUMBER_009/Configuring+On-Call+Technicians)**.**

**Both steps must be completed for the rotation to execute.**

**The demo begins at 1:15:** 

[https://www.loom.com/share/abcdbac39c97445c94c40a01132b94d5](https://www.loom.com/share/abcdbac39c97445c94c40a01132b94d5)### **On-Call Terminology:**

1. **Technicians**: Technicians who respond to emergency work order alerts
2. **On-call Rotation Settings**: How often the on-call rotation changes to the next technician in line and the time of day the handoff occurs. 
3. **Alert Frequency**: How often we’ll send alerts to the on-call technician
4. **Attempt (Number of Tries)**: How many times we will alert the on-call technician, before alerting the backup contact
5. **On-call Response SLA (Resident Contact Window)**: Tells the resident how long they should expect to wait before being contacted by the on-call technician (20mins - 2hrs)
6. **Backup Contact**: An email address, phone number or person who should be contacted if no Service Providers acknowledge to a given emergency alert. 

    1. 1 SMS and 1 Call alert will trigger after \[X\] minutes 
    
        1. Follows the same alert frequency time as configured for technicians 
        
    
7. **Final Contact Group:** An email or group of emails ([user group](https://help.latchel.com/s/article/How-to-Create-User-Groups)) notified if neither the on-call technician nor the Backup Contact acknowledges an emergency work order alert. 

    1. 1 email
    
8. **Administrative Email Notifications**: These emails are sent to an email or group of emails depending on the customer’s preference.

    1. Emergency work order created
    2. Non-emergency work order created
    3. Resident Requests follow-up
    4. Resident submitted a review
    
9. **Emergency Work Order alert:** the call and text message sent to the on-call technicians and backup contact seeking an emergency acknowledgement. 
10. **Emergency Acknowledgement**: The action that describes the on-call technician or backup contact confirming receipt and assignment to the work order.
