## **Objective**

The purpose of this document is to identify and create a streamlined process for Active Unit Adjustment, immediately addressing requests from customers who dispute the number of active units they possess, ensuring they will be invoiced correctly for their next month’s bill.

## **Background**

Latchel recently introduced a process where customers receive an email summarizing their current month’s unit count. The Monthly Unit Count Communication email is sent to all active PM users within the company every 21st of the month. If the 21st falls on a weekend, the email will be sent the following Monday. This new process allows customers to either manage their units through the Latchel dashboard or notify Latchel of any discrepancies to ensure accurate billing.

**Note:** The Monthly Unit Count Communication email is **not** sent by the Data Support Team. It is an automated email generated by the system and sent directly to customers.

## **Current Account Update Request Process**

Currently, the Account Update request only focuses on typical account configurations, without indicating the urgency of invoice or account receivable disputes. As a result, these requests are often treated as non-urgent and prioritized based on their ticket age, leading to customers being billed based on outdated unit counts and potential account disputes.

## **Requesting for Data Support Ticket**

A new typeform for Active Unit Adjustment request is created. All requesters are required to fill out the Typeform which can be access within #data-support-requests. These data support requests will sync to the Assembly automatically upon submission.

o   **_These requests will be highly prioritized_**, unless in the presence of an Onboarding task, and must be completed before the 10th or prior to the invoicing scheduled date of the month whichever comes first.

o   Any responses from customers to the Monthly Unit Count Communication that involve bulk updates to unit counts must be submitted to the Data Support Team via the <custom data-type="smartlink" data-id="id-0">[https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C05CGVC9FPV](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C05CGVC9FPV)</custom>  Slack channel within 24 hours of receiving the email.
