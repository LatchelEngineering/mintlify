## Step-by-Step Guide

### **Step 1. Retrieve the customer's responses to our** **[Onboarding Questionnaire](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1VjyHawGkz5vCP9sznF0KcGR5UCyOO_n9PW2Uw03mCdU/edit#responses).**

1. Go to the [Onboarding Questionnaire](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1VjyHawGkz5vCP9sznF0KcGR5UCyOO_n9PW2Uw03mCdU/edit#responses) page, click on "responses" tab and on the google sheets icon to open the spreadsheet showing individual entries
   1. Note, you can also find this file in this link: [https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/19HBq-WKJRQz2hyjb5iGszsDkZGgb2kVdMDojHYt46to/edit#gid=1714419140](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/19HBq-WKJRQz2hyjb5iGszsDkZGgb2kVdMDojHYt46to/edit#gid=1714419140)
2. Each line item in the spreadsheet will include the questionnaire answers for each Property Management Company

### **Step 2. Update Critical Notes (if applicable)**

**Definition**

Critical Notes can be added to a property, a resident, or a service provider. Generally speaking, critical notes should NOT be used for “must-do” information related to work orders. This type of information should be put into pre-dispatch instructions. Critical Notes are best for providing extra information or contextual awareness for our team. A good critical note on a property may say something like, “History of sewage backups in this property. Most clogs generally require a plumber instead of a handyman.” 

1. Information on critical notes are not collected in the questionnaire but could be provided by Property Managers during the onboarding call. If that happens, you will type them in the box below and click on "save"
   1. Note that the "PM is not at risk" button is off when a new account is created and will remain that way as this is a new account and there is no reason to believe this company is at risk of ending their subscription with us

### **Step 3. Update Property Manager Address**

1. Go to the Property Manager Account - [How to: Access the Property Manager Profile Page](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/813729656)
2. In the "details" tab, find the "**address**" section
3. Click the downward arrow next to Address, then enter the customer's address.  Click Save.
   1. Use the spreadsheet from[ step 1](#Howto:ConfigureCustomerProfileInformation\(DetailsPageinLatchelAdmin\)-step1) to get the address information

### **Step 4. Update Maintenance Communication Settings**

1. Go to the "Maint. Communication Settings" section and click in the drop-down arrow:  
2. Compare the preferred "area code" provided in the questionnaire data ([step1](#Howto:ConfigureCustomerProfileInformation\(DetailsPageinLatchelAdmin\)-step1)) with the one assigned in the Latchel Console. If the area code differs, use this SOP to edit it: [(CS/Admin) How to: Buy & Release Numbers in Twilio](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/894895478)
3. Notification groups will be set-up once members are added to this account and we understand the Property Manager communication preferences. We are not collecting this information in our questionnaire, so no action is needed until that information is available
   1. If there is a notification group created and you know what group should be selected, you can click on the box to select the group and click on "save"
      1. To create a notification group, refer to [(ARCHIVE) How to: Create a Notification Group](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/836108736) 
4. Based on the questionnaire response, select who should receive invoices collected by us. 
   1. If Property Manager does not want us to collect any invoice, toggle the sliding bar to disable invoice collection on job completion
5. This last section of the Maintenance Communication Settings will be left as is when entering the main information of the communication profile. It will be filled once this information is collected in the property data collection process. 
   1. If that information is available: 
      1. Use this SOP to learn [How to: Add Pre-dispatch Instructions](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/795116337)
      2. Use this SOP to learn [How to: Add Default Access Instructions](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/795377753) 

### **Step 5. Update Portal Settings and Upload Logo**

1. Go to the "Portal Settings" section and update it with the information collected in the questionnaire. Click Save after updating each field.
   1. Note: Application Slug is an internal field that does not need to be filled up at the account creation, so leave it blank
   2. To update the logo, click on the link provided in the questionnaire
   3. Click on the arrow down symbol to download the image to your computer. It should be saved to your "downloads" folder. 
      1.  
   4. Return to the Property Manager account in the Latchel Console (details tab and "Portal Settings"), and click on "Upload Logo"
   5. A window will open for you to select the location of the file you just saved to your computer, select the file and click on "open"
      1. The file will upload to the Latchel Console and it will show up beside the "upload logo" button

### **Step 6.  Update Escalation Contacts**

1. Go to the "Escalation Contacts" section and enter the escalation contact emails provided in the questionnaire. Click Save after updating each field
   1. Note: The "Failed Escalation Email List" is only used in the event that we can’t get in touch with any of the Property Managers escalation contacts provided in the "Escalation Contact List". In that case, we’ll send a final email to that failed email list. We will provide all the details relevant to the work order, provide an avenue for you to contact our emergency team, and close the work order in our system until we hear back from you. This is worst-case scenario.
   2. In case the Property Manager asked to have this "Failed Escalation Email List", enter the email addresses in that field. If not, leave it blank.

### **Step 7.  Update Work Order Budget Limits**

1. Go to the "Work Order Budget Limits" section.  Click the downward arrow next to Work Order Budget Limits, then enter the customer's hard and soft limits for both Emergency and Non-Emergency work orders based on the responses to our questionnaire ([step 1](#Howto:ConfigureCustomerProfileInformation\(DetailsPageinLatchelAdmin\)-step1)).  Click Save.

### **Step 8. Workflow Automation Settings**

(\*\***EMERGENCY ONLY customers\*\***) - We have the option to auto-assign non-emergency work orders/jobs to the first Service Provider on the Property Manager's prioritized list of preferred Service Providers. This will auto-dispatch any non-emergency jobs to the first Service Provider only when there is a Service Provider with a matching skill **and** they are prioritized. Additionally, there are a few conditions that apply: (1) the unit must be occupied (2) the work order will still get sent to "forward to PM" status with information on which preferred vendor has been assigned, the vendor will be assigned and the vendor will also receive a message with the work order information and instructions to coordinate with the resident outside of the Latchel portal. Latchel will not manage coordination with the vendor for these work orders. (3) There has to be an issue ID (aka an attached skill) for the work order (4) PM must be assigned to an Emergency only subscription.

Please review this video before configuring this setting: [https://drive.google.com/file/d/1ssO\_DUy2ViqqHxQNKJZ2An1Nne6YrXBp/view?usp=sharing](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1ssO_DUy2ViqqHxQNKJZ2An1Nne6YrXBp/view?usp=sharing)

To configure this: 

1. Click **Add Action Set Group**. 
2. A pop-up will appear. Search for the action "**Non-Emergency Auto-Assign**" and select it. Click **Submit** to save. 
3. You can confirm this was saved by seeing the action under the Custom Action Set Groups heading. 
4. Leave the rest of this section blank while creating new accounts.

### **Step 9. Miscellaneous Settings**

1. If this Property Manager is using Appfolio or Rent Manager, you will toggle property and tenant modification ON
   1. See step 10 below on how to notate why this toggle is ON.
2. As an admin, you can connect new customers to referral accounts by searching for the company name in the "**Referred to Latchel By**" field.
3. Leave blank the rest of the fields

### **Step 10. Add a Note on the Account (REQUIRED)**

This step is required if applicable. If you toggled the property and tenant modification ON in step 9, be sure to notate on the account why the property and tenant modification is ON so the team knows if this is ON because of and software integration, special portfolio rules, multiple accounts, per the pm's request, etc. 

Also, leave a note on the account if the customer is on a special plan (24/7 Home Assistant, 24/7 Emergency Assistant OR Pay Per Use).
