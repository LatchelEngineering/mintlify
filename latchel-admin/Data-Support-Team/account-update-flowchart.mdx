### **Objective**

The objective is to visually depict the step-by-step process through a flowchart, guiding admins in effectively adding or updating properties and residents within a customer's account.

- The flowchart should only be followed if the admin has an option to add properties and residents in Latchel manually, in such case, the customer’s account does not have an active integration.
