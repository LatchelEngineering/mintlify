‌

‌

### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-0">:bookmark_tabs:</custom> **Introduction**

RentRedi has a unique approach to property management, where property owners themselves handle maintenance requests as property managers. Accordingly, owners often submit requests for new preferred service providers they wish to be assigned to their portfolios.

‌

### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-1">:green_book:</custom> **Objective**

The purpose of this page is to provide detailed steps on how the [**Data Support Team**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST) must handle **New Latchel Preferred Vendor Request** cases/tickets received via Freshdesk. 

The aim is to make sure that the service provider is prioritized in the owner portfolio(s) indicated in the automated email.

‌

### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-2">:e-mail:</custom> **The Automated Email**

Take a look at each part of the automated email received for this type of case. These details are needed to complete the process of onboarding the service provider and to prioritize it in the owner’s portfolio.

### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-3">:bookmark:</custom> **Processing the Request**

1. Copy the **owner email address** in the automated email under the Owner Info section. Use it to search for the owner profile in Latchel. Click **People**, then choose **Owners**. 

    1. Ideally, the owner should already exist in Latchel. If not found, there is no need to proceed. 
    2. Similarly, if the owner email address is not indicated in the automated email, there is no way we can find to which portfolio the new service provider must be prioritized, unless the Portfolio Name is listed at the bottom of the automated email.
    
2. If Latchel provided search results using the owner email address, click the hyperlinked **Owner Name** to pull up the owner profile page.
3. In the **Owner profile** page, click **Portfolios** tab and find all the portfolios where the service provider must be prioritized as indicated in the automated email. You may pull up the portfolio(s) in to a separate browser tab. In case the portfolio is not listed, do not proceed. It is unnecessary to add the portfolio. Latchel imports these portfolios directly from RentRedi via webhooks. 

    



    ![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=62591b09-8c1f-475f-bdec-bc730663c3d2&&collection=contentId-3003088953&height=676&occurrenceKey=null&width=1251&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

     


4. Next, in a separate browser tab, go to **People**, then choose **Service Providers**. Using the service provider information indicated in the automated email, search for any existing service provider company in Latchel.

    1. If a record is found, click the hyperlinked **name of the service provider**. Make sure that the contact information of the service provider found is similar to what is indicated in the automated email. Then, go to **Preferred PMs** tab, and then assign **RentRedi** as their preferred property manager.
    2. If there is no search result found in Latchel using any of the service provider’s company details from the email, add the service provider by following these steps:
    
        1. Click **+New Service Provider**, and create a new service provider company profile using the vendor information in the email. 
        2. **Add all the service provide skills** as listed in the email. 
        3. Click **Save**, to save the new service provider. 
        4. Go to **Preferred PMs** tab to assign **RentRedi** as their preferred property manager.
        
    
5. After adding the service provider, go back to the owner portfolio then go to the **Service Providers** tab. Prioritize the service provider to all skills categories assigned to it. Click the **3 dots** at the upper right of the page, then choose **Prioritize Service Provider for Multiple Skills**. 

    



    ![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=8cf0d1bf-ff97-485b-a7cc-d515561d9c86&&collection=contentId-3003088953&height=683&occurrenceKey=null&width=1183&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

     


6. In search box, **type the name of the service provider company**. Once found, click it to proceed on choosing all the skills where it will be prioritized. You may also choose to click **Check All Categories** so all skills available will be checked. 

    



    ![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=b47f2170-a94d-4a6c-9e0d-3a039d408a4b&&collection=contentId-3003088953&height=587&occurrenceKey=null&width=1177&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

    



    ![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=3257a114-ad8c-4535-aa0c-2fa54d9fc12b&&collection=contentId-3003088953&height=784&occurrenceKey=null&width=1207&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

       


7. The page will then automatically scroll down to where you need to choose to prioritize the service provider either at the **top or at the bottom of all their skill category**. Then click **Prioritize** to save the service provider prioritization. A confirmation will show at the top of the page indicating that the page is refreshing to update the service provider list. 

    



    ![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=4a29535a-fe2d-4376-99d5-b6398eb2b161&&collection=contentId-3003088953&height=660&occurrenceKey=null&width=1182&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

    



    ![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=980d8271-52da-47e0-9ce2-8891b11d73a8&&collection=contentId-3003088953&height=429&occurrenceKey=null&width=1004&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

    


8. After prioritizing the service provider in the portfolio, make sure to turn off the service provider inheritance. 

    



    ![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=f4a2f208-9f4e-44a4-b91e-f61d48cb8daa&&collection=contentId-3003088953&height=371&occurrenceKey=null&width=600&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

     


9. Repeat steps 5-8 for all the owner's portfolios where you need to prioritize the same service provider.

‌

**Pro Tip:** _If you have multiple portfolios with the same list of service providers, you can choose **Copy Service Provider Settings from Another Portfolio instead of Prioritize Service Provider for Multiple Skills**. This option allows you to easily copy the list of service providers from one portfolio to another, provided that both portfolios have the exact same list of service providers. This saves you from manually adding service providers one at a time._

10. Once done prioritizing the service provider in the owner’s portfolio(s), complete the Freshdesk ticket. Place a note in the ticket that a response is not needed. Additionally, **add the link of the portfolios** where the service provider were prioritized. Lastly, **change the status of the ticket to Close**, and then click the **Update** button.
