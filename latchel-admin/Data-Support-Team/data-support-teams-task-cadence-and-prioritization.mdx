### **Introduction**

This page outlines the task cadence and prioritization for the [Data Support Team](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST). It ensures that all team members are aware of the most urgent tasks, daily responsibilities, and periodic duties. This structure aims to streamline workflow and maintain high efficiency in handling various tasks.

‌

### **Objective**

The objective of this page is to provide clear guidance on task prioritization and scheduling for the Data Support Team. By following this cadence, the team can effectively manage high-priority tasks, daily responsibilities, and monthly duties, ensuring that all tasks are completed accurately and on time.

‌

### **Data Support Team Tasks**

#### **A. High Priority Tasks**

These tasks takes precedence over all other type of tasks. If both types of onboarding are available, Leonardo 24/7 onboarding tasks must be processed first over the Non-Leonardo 24/7 onboarding task, unless the later is due earlier than the former.

1. **Active Units Adjustment Requests**
   - These requests must immediately address disputes from customers regarding the number of active units they possess, ensuring they are invoiced correctly for the next month’s bill.
2. **Leonardo 24/7 Onboarding Tasks**
   - In line with Leonardo 24/7 and Latchel’s partnership, all customer onboarding tasks that are from Leonardo 24/7 must be processed immediately within 24 hours upon receiving the request in the [#account-update-request](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C05CGVC9FPV) Slack channel.
3. **Onboarding Tasks for Non-Leonardo 24/7 customers**
   - Onboarding tasks for these customers will take precedence over other tasks, aside from the Leonardo 24/7 onboarding task, unless there are tasks that fall under or are tagged as service-impacting or critical tasks that need to be completed, with or without a due date.
4. **Service-Impacting Tasks or Critical Tasks**
   - Service-affecting tasks are identified as tasks that can impact a portion or entire operations within Latchel and/or the customer’s company if a relevant task is not executed promptly. These tasks take precedence over all other tasks including Onboarding. These tasks must be accomplished as quickly as possible to avoid any further delays in the operation and/or before the situation becomes even more aggravating for the customer.

Visit [Service-Impacting Tasks or Critical Tasks](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/863141952/Job-Aid+How+The+Task+Share+Program+Works#Service-Impacting-Tasks-or-Critical-Tasks) to learn more about these type of tasks.

#### **B. Medium Priority Tasks**

These tasks should be prioritized and will take precedence over other tasks unless any of the following circumstances apply:

<custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-1">:white_check_mark:</custom> It’s the first week of the month, which is the most crucial week for the Customer Profitability Team, as all accounts need to be updated and ready for invoicing by the 8th workday of the month.

<custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-2">:white_check_mark:</custom> There are escalation or return tasks that were previously completed but have been sent back to the Data Support team for reasons specified by the task reporter.

**In the event that there are High Priority tasks, and it’s the 1st week of the month, high priority tasks must be completed first before working on CPT tasks.**

1. **Leonardo 24/7 Account Update Tasks**
   - These are any account update tasks for any customers under Leonardo 24/7.
2. **Account Update Tasks for Non-Leonardo 24/7 customers**
3. **Data Migration Project**
   - These account update tasks are essential for ensuring smooth synchronization of customer properties and residents when activating third-party property management within Latchel. They are performed to avoid duplicate entries appearing in Latchel following the initial synchronization process.
   - This type of task is time-sensitive and has a strict timeline to follow due to the involvement of the product team; therefore, it takes precedence over all other tasks categorized under medium priority.

Visit <custom data-type="smartlink" data-id="id-3">[https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/2507866123](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/2507866123)</custom> to know more about this type of task.

If there are multiple medium priority tasks available, the associate must prioritize completing them based on due date. If the due date is not indicated, act based on the age of the ticket, starting with the oldest ticket.

### **Customer Profitability Team Tasks**

The Data Support Team has partnered with the Customer Profitability Team to ensure the timely completion of all CPT tasks alongside customer invoicing. This collaboration aims to update essential reports efficiently and evaluate customer profitability, aligning efforts toward the shared goal of enhancing customer profitability.

In line with this partnership, the Data Support Team must assist the CPT until all necessary reports and activities for customer profitability are updated before invoicing day.

**During the 1st and 2nd weeks of the month, all CPT tasks must be completed as scheduled.**

**In the event that there are High Priority tasks, and it’s the 1st week of the month, high priority tasks must be completed first before working on CPT tasks.**

All CPT tasks assigned for the day must be started and completed later in the shift.
