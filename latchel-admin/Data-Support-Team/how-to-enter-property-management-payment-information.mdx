# Objective

**Purpose**

For all subscription charges, Latchel automatically charges the Property Manager's **Subscription Payment Method** on file. To opt into the Latchel Service Provider Network, there will need to be a **Latchel Service Provider Network Subscription Payment** added for payments to Latchel Service Providers. The Property Manager can select two separate payment methods. 

# Content

**What is happening prior to this process?**

Prior to the onboarding appointment with the new customer, the Sales Engineer or Sales Development Rep has collected payment information from the customer. There are cases where the Property Manager asks the Latchel Team to update their card(s) on file. See the steps below on how to do this: 

## Step-by-Step Guide

### **Step 1- Add a New Payment Method**

1. Go to the Property Manager profile page: [How to: Access the Property Manager Profile Page](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/813729656)
2. Under the Payments tab, you will see a section on the left of your screen titled Payment Methods. Click **\+Add Payment Method.**
3. From here, enter the card details and click **Save Card.**

### **Step 2- Change the Subscription Payment Method**

1. Under the **Payment** menu on the Property Manager's profile page, locate the **Payment Settings** section on the lefthand side of the page. Where it says **Subscription Payment Method**, click the **three horizontal dots** to the right. 
   1. Click **Change Subscription Payment Method** and a pop-up will appear.
2. Click on the dropdown menu to select from the payment methods on file.

### **Step 3- Change the Latchel Network Service Provider Payment Method**

1. Under the Payment menu on the Property Manager's profile page, locate the **Payment Settings** section on the lefthand side of the page. Where it says **Latchel Service Provider Network Payment Method** , click the **three horizontal dots** to the right. 
   1. Click **Change Subscription Payment Method** and a pop-up will appear.
2. Click on the dropdown menu to select from the payment methods on file.
