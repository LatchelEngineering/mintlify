# Objective

**Purpose**

The purpose of this document is to provide an overview process on how to collect general company information

# **Content**

## **Step-by-Step Guide**

### **Step 1. Requesting Company information**

1. The Sales team will send the customer a link to the [Onboarding Questionnaire](https://forms.gle/NdjvmGPtZk8L2bK97) form
2. If a customer does not complete the questionnaire prior to their onboarding call, we can gather the information in the call by using the same questionnaire as a guide

    1. Also, refer to step 2 ([General Company Information](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/LTG/pages/791347310/Customer+Onboarding+Process+Checklist#CustomerOnboardingProcess&Checklist-step2Step2:GeneralCompanyInformation)) of [Customer Onboarding Process & Checklist](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CS/pages/791347310) for additional company information  
    

### **Step 2. Receiving their Responses**

1. Once the customer completes this form, an email will be sent with their responses to [onboarding@latchel.com](mailto:onboarding@latchel.com)
2. Go to the inbox and search for it using company name or email address of the contact person we are working with to set-up the account
3. Responses for this form can also be found by looking at:

    1. The spreadsheet located at: [https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/19HBq-WKJRQz2hyjb5iGszsDkZGgb2kVdMDojHYt46to/edit#gid=1714419140](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/19HBq-WKJRQz2hyjb5iGszsDkZGgb2kVdMDojHYt46to/edit#gid=1714419140)
    
        1. Look for the name of the company
        
    2. The response section of the form ([https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1VjyHawGkz5vCP9sznF0KcGR5UCyOO_n9PW2Uw03mCdU/edit](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1VjyHawGkz5vCP9sznF0KcGR5UCyOO_n9PW2Uw03mCdU/edit))
    
        1. Click on "Responses", "individual" button and look for the email address from the person that should be sending the response
