# Objective

Prior to the onboarding call, the customer will have received instructions to modify their [s](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdF_zrqDMA5UXc0MHh4fx_IaOFQ8GHfRfC_wgvUazuul-byTA/viewform)everity/responsibility/pre-approval settings to change to our default criteria.  It is then part of the scope of the onboarding call to discuss the customer's choices.

# Content

## Step-by-Step Guide

### **Step 1. Requesting Severity / Responsibility / Pre-Approval Definitions** 

1. The customer will book their onboarding call based on the link their sales agent provides them.
2. As part of the sales process, the Account Executive will send the customer their Onboarding Checklist. Within this checklist, the customer will have an action item to modify these settings via the Latchel portal. 
3. If the customer fails to modify the settings prior to the kickoff call, the Launch Manager will go over the steps to modify these settings live on the call.

### **Step 2.** **Adjusting These Setting**

1. The steps to modify these settings can be found in this customer guide: [https://latchel.com/pre-approval-severity-and-responsibility-settings/](https://latchel.com/pre-approval-severity-and-responsibility-settings/)
   1. _Note: The steps are the same for admins and property managers_
