| <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-4">:sparkles:</custom> About Data Support |
| --- |
| The Data Support Team is established in 2019. It was build from ground zero to up where it is now with the help of <custom data-type="mention" data-id="id-5">@</custom> , the head of Service Operations Team. It was initially part of Customer Success until December 2022, when it was transferred to Service Operations under the same management.  The Data Support Team focuses across customer escalations to identify trends and unhealthy accounts. They take corrective actions to ensure all accounts are configured correctly and data modifications are complete and accurate. |

| **Responsibilities Include** |
| --- |
| Maintain and organize the overall health and accuracy of the client’s account for exceptional customer experience. Create content on how to use our prouct in the external help center, <custom data-type="smartlink" data-id="id-6">[http://help.latchel.com](http://help.latchel.com)</custom>  Implementing and enforcing data quality standards, guidelines, and best practices to ensure that customer-provided data is accurate, complete, consistent, and up-to-date across the task share program. Overseeing data integration and migration projects, ensuring that data from various sources is integrated and migrated accurately and efficiently. Evaluating, recommending, and implementing data management systems and tools to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of the data support team. |
