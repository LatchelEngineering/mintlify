### **Objective**

The objective of this job-aid is to provide step-by-step instructions for the Data Support Team on how to create and publish help guides in Salesforce for [Latchel Help Center](https://help.latchel.com/s/) page.

‌

### **Instructions**

- Login to [Salesforce](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning) using your own credentials.

**Note:** _Once you are logged in, you will be asked to confirm your access via your phone. This must be initially set, prior to using Salesforce._

<img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=967d7a07-e553-4d84-a83e-416aec2a57a6&&collection=contentId-2634481850&height=747&occurrenceKey=null&width=573&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> ‌

‌

- Navigate to [Knowledge](https://latchel1.lightning.force.com/lightning/o/Knowledge__kav/list?filterName=00B5e00000MxX7eEAF) after logging in. From this page, click the **New** button at the top of the page. This will open another tab in Salesforce where you can draft the help guide article.

<img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=4870ce3d-0a94-4fac-a63a-514865256bd2&&collection=contentId-2634481850&height=649&occurrenceKey=null&width=1708&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> ‌

‌

- In the **New Knowledge** tab, you need to provide the following:

1. **Title**
2. **URL Name** - this automatically populates once you click the box after typing in the title.
3. **Question** - Ensure that the **Question section** of the article presents a question. While including a brief description of the article's content is acceptable, remember to include a clear question to be answered.
4. **Answer** - this is where the actual instructions and screen captures are pasted. It is the actual body of the article.

It is important to make sure that when drafting a knowledge article, you must follow the guidelines specified in this job-aid: [**Guidelines for Creating Knowledge Articles for Help Center**](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/2774532097)

<img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=04bd00ee-a97f-4cae-8a66-5edf54eeba31&&collection=contentId-2634481850&height=1080&occurrenceKey=null&width=1362&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> ‌

‌

- Once you are done drafting the article, make sure to check all boxes for the **Visibility** of the guide **under the Properties section**. This ensures that the help guide will be visible to the proper channels and people. Then, click **Save**.

<img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=b7722ee1-62e8-496b-bcc9-b011c073d819&&collection=contentId-2634481850&height=335&occurrenceKey=null&width=1173&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> ‌

‌

- Next, on the right panel of the knowledge article, you will find the **Categories section**. Click **Edit**, then **choose the correct category** of the article you will publish.

<img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=28af9de8-3701-49ba-819d-2cdbb0a74e03&&collection=contentId-2634481850&height=445&occurrenceKey=null&width=642&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> ‌

‌

- This step is crucial to identify the **viewer of the article** in the [Latchel Help Center](https://help.latchel.com/s/). There are **three categories** to choose from – **Service Provider**, **Property Manager**, and **Resident**.

Click **Save** once you have selected the Category.

<img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=e3491890-8fea-4119-9677-88275445a5c7&&collection=contentId-2634481850&height=722&occurrenceKey=null&width=756&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> ‌

- Finally, click **Publish** found at the top right corner to publish the knowledge article. A small box will appear with **two options** to publish: **Publish now** or **Schedule publication**.

1. Choose Publish now if you wish to publish the article right away.
2. Alternatively, select Schedule Publication if you want to publish the article at a later date. When choosing Schedule Publication, provide the future date when you want the article to be published.

Then, click **Publish** once again when everything is set!

<img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=4c26fadf-0099-41e9-b3e9-7a9e0cc7809f&&collection=contentId-2634481850&height=456&occurrenceKey=null&width=649&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> <img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=8833e4a4-8493-4423-88a1-23f9ae758b99&&collection=contentId-2634481850&height=418&occurrenceKey=null&width=750&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> Note: It is important to **proofread** the entire article before publishing. This ensures that the instructions are precise, concise, and easy to understand, with no typographical or grammatical errors. Remember that it must follow the guidelines specified in the job-aid: **[Guidelines for Creating Knowledge Articles for Help Center](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/2774532097).**

‌

And you're all set! Congratulations on creating and publishing your knowledge article. Head over to L[atchel Help Center](https://help.latchel.com/s/) now to see your new help guide in action!
