### Introduction:

The review process, which ensures the up-to-dateness of published Latchel Help guides, is a crucial step in maintaining accuracy. It involves systematic checks and revisions to align the guides with the latest updates and changes in Latchel's services. This process incorporates input from the product team through [#product-release](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C038Z6M0T6H) announcements and adheres to Service Operations' standard operating procedures.

‌

### Objective:

The aim of this review process is to improve the accuracy and relevance of the published help guides by aligning them with Latchel's latest product release and developments, and current standard operating procedures.

‌

### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-0">:notepad_spiral:</custom> The Review Process <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-1">:notepad_spiral:</custom> 

1. Regular content review schedule - (cadence is to be announced)
2. Monitor new developments, new feature releases, or any changes in Latchel’s current services by collaborating with the product team through [#product-release,](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C038Z6M0T6H) and change management announcements. 
3. Verify standard operating procedures by regularly reviewing Latchel’s current SOPs, and change management headed by the Service Operation’s Manager <custom data-type="mention" data-id="id-2">@Javier Orta (Unlicensed)</custom>. Cross-reference help guides with official documentation as published by the product team. Visit [\[Change Management\] Product Releases](https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/PROD/pages/2567176193/Change+Management+Product+Releases) to get updated on new product releases.
4. As new developments or changes are introduced, assess the impact on existing help guides. Revise relevant sections to reflect these changes accurately.
5. For help guides that need to be updated, a request must be posted in the [#help-guide-requests](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C04B3PNJ24V) following the process stated in <custom data-type="smartlink" data-id="id-3">https://latchel.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/TST/pages/2635333691</custom> 
6. Notify the Service Operations and Product Team of the updates made to the help guides. Receive and take action on appropriate feedback if there is any. 

    1. Once a new or updated Help Guide is published, an announcement (or possible automatic notification) will be posted to the [#published-help-guides](https://latchel.slack.com/archives/C05F57X654L) Slack Channel. The announcement may include the following:
    
        1. Title of the Help Guide
        2. The URL of the help guide
        3. Time and date of publishing
        4. What’s New about it
        5. A Google form Link where anyone can provide feedback. 
        
    2. Feedback and suggestions from all stakeholders and associates will be taken into account if provided. This process serves to maintain the high quality and consistency of help guides published for customers.
