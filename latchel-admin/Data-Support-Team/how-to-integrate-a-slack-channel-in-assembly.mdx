This page outlines all the steps to take to integrate any Slack channel you would like to manage within Assembly.

### <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-0">:bookmark:</custom> **The Steps**

1. Access the channel in Slack. Go to the channel settings, and in the Integration tab of the settings, make sure Assembly is added as an authorized app. <img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=448059f8-0147-4eb9-8a4c-01a1864d6ac3&&collection=contentId-3200188439&height=876&occurrenceKey=null&width=665&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" />
2. After onboarding and signing up with Assembly, your first landing page will be your Dashboard. In the dashboard, click **#Manage Integration**.

**NOTE:** _If you are not onboarded to Assembly yet, click this_ [**_link_**](https://www.dashboard.askassembly.app/) _and follow the prompt to create your username and password._

<img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=25a2aa57-829a-4e9a-807f-20846db25251&&collection=contentId-3200188439&height=897&occurrenceKey=null&width=1920&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> ‌

3. In the #Manage Integration page, choose <custom data-type="emoji" data-id="id-1">:slack:</custom> **Slack** under the enabled or available apps listed.

![](blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=92f15fe5-0f1b-4093-b92b-84adbf79eaad&&collection=contentId-3200188439&height=690&occurrenceKey=null&width=1899&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null)

4. Next, click **\+ Add More Channels**.

<img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=a55b5606-15d7-4f4a-adf7-709d8593aa64&&collection=contentId-3200188439&height=746&occurrenceKey=null&width=845&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> 5. In the next page, **add** all the Slack channels you wish to integrate with Assembly. Note that for first time users, it might take a few minutes to load all the available Slack channel managed by Latchel. Click **Submit** done adding.

<img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=425415b0-64dd-4608-8831-a294deb2ffcf&&collection=contentId-3200188439&height=837&occurrenceKey=null&width=1553&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> <img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=f873f5b5-c996-46a0-8eed-94fafc63499a&&collection=contentId-3200188439&height=715&occurrenceKey=null&width=1621&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> ‌

6. Wait for the page to load. It might take a couple of seconds to minutes. Once successful, you will see that your channel is included in the **Customer Channels Selected** box.

<img src="blob:https://media.staging.atl-paas.net/?type=file&localId=null&id=32ea6370-8679-4158-888d-44940786aedc&&collection=contentId-3200188439&height=752&occurrenceKey=null&width=880&__contextId=null&__displayType=null&__external=false&__fileMimeType=null&__fileName=null&__fileSize=null&__mediaTraceId=null&url=null" /> **Heads-Up:** All new messages sent in the integrated Slack channels will immediately create a ticket in Assembly. Comments within the conversation thread will also trigger an update to the associated Assembly ticket.
