### **Objectives**

- To ensure that our [Help Center](https://help.latchel.com/s/) articles are well-structured, user-friendly, and tailored to address common queries, enabling our customers to find timely and accurate assistance, ultimately enhancing customer satisfaction and loyalty to Latchel's services.
- To ensure uniformity across all help guide articles by following these guidelines and standards when creating and formatting the content.

‌

### **The Guidelines**

Here are the simple guidelines to follow when creating knowledge articles in Salesforce for Latchel's Help Center.

‌

#### **A. The Title**

- Use of **_Chicago Manual of Style 17th Edition Capitalization Rules_**:
  - Capitalize the first and the last word.
  - Capitalize nouns, pronouns, adjectives, verbs, adverbs, and subordinate conjunctions.
  - Lowercase articles _(a, an, the)_, coordinating conjunctions, and prepositions (regardless of length) unless they are used as the first word of the title.
  - Lowercase the second word after a hyphenated prefix in compound modifiers _(e.g. Pre-dispatch, Built-in, Move-in, Move-out)_
  - Lowercase the ‘to’ in an infinitive _(e.g., How **to** Update Pre-dispatch Instructions)_.
  - To easily convert titles to the correct format use the [Title Capitalization & Title Case Converter Tool - Capitalize My Title](https://capitalizemytitle.com/)

‌

#### **B. The Question**

- Ensure that the **Question section** of the article presents a question. While including a brief description of the article's content is acceptable, remember to include a clear question to be answered.

‌

#### **C. The Font and Use of Proper Punctuations**

- Use **Heading 4** with bold letters while following the correct format of title capitalization for section titles.
- Use the normal font for all other sections of the article.
- Make sure to emphasize the options to click in each instruction by making them in **bold** letters. _(e.g., Click **Places**, then select **Properties**.) No need to use quotation marks (“ “) to emphasize the options to click._
- When a "**Note**" is needed for emphasis, make the word "**NOTE:**" bold and all capitalized, followed by the emphasized sentence in _normal italics_.
- All related articles should be linked using **normal-sized bold** font. Ensure that all URLs are from the Help Center and not from the URL of Salesforce articles. Use bullets if there are multiple related articles to link.
- Use correct punctuation in each of the sentences. Use a comma (,) to pause.

‌

#### **D. Abbreviations and Correct Latchel Terminologies**

- Please avoid using abbreviations in the help guide, especially in the title. You may use abbreviations if the corresponding term has been defined earlier in the article. For example, the **PM** should be referred to as '**property manager**,' and **LCN** stands for '**Latchel Contracted Network**.'
- Use the appropriate terminologies provided in the [Latchel Glossary of Terms](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1c64SouQGL0hr4Eq20_SiOCdc1HfectI0Z2s8XYN-fpE/edit).

‌

#### **E. Drafting the Instructions**

- When constructing an instruction to follow, ensure that it maintains an easy-to-follow approach with a friendly and interesting tone.
- Use clear language, concise steps, and engaging content to make the process enjoyable and accessible for our viewers.

‌

#### **F. The Screen Captures**

- Use of **Awesome Screenshot and Screen Recording** as the official application to capture screenshots and screen recordings from Latchel.

a. Use **big head arrows** in size 2.
